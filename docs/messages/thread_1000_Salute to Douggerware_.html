Salute to Douggerware!

From: Jan | Date: 2005-05-01 22:51:28 UTC
I just thought I would share my whole hearted endorsement for our
Douggerware! Three cheers for a great product. We went to Tyler
State park today to do some repairs on my three Elvis boxes planted
there. I had a report that one had been muggled so we replaced that
one. I also knew that one had been under several inches of ice when I
checked on it in February and my report from the beginning of April
was that it was under several inches of water. Well, it was still
under water, but it was in great shape. There was a little leakage
into the box but I also want to give three cheers to Ziploc freezer
bags and to double bagging. I was just able to dry the box out and
wipe the bags off and plant it in higher grounds, logbook and stamp
nice and dry. Great products we use. The Elvis boxes are ready for
the next season of boxing visitors.

Jan of Team Little Dog
