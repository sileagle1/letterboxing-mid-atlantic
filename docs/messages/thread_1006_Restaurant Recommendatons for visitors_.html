From: Jan | Date: 2005-05-07 04:00:27 UTC
If you are going to be at Nolde Forest (where I have a box!and which
is close to my home) I can recommend Emily's Pub which is only about 2
or 3 miles away from the park. And one of Reading's better known
casual restaurants is the Peanut Bar which is a few miles further.
And one of my personal favorites is Bixler's Pub.
The ShredMTB boxes at Nolde are a good challenge with very well
written clues and a decent walk if you plan on doing all three at one
time. It actually took us three different visits to find all three.
Hope to see you here,
Jan of Team Little Dog
