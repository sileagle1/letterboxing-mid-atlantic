We finally discovered Knoebels last year and loved it.
Our Adventure Guide group is going out next weekend for a day of riding the rides.  There is a very nice campsite adjacent to the park (you can walk between the two very easily).  we'll be tenting one night.  There are also cabins available for rent.  I suspect they should be fairly open by late September.
I have a stamp I carved almost a year ago that I will plant next weekend in the park. 
Hope I can make it to see everyone in September.
Raindear
