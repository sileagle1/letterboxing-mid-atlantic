Black Hill Gather and Boch's Circus: Stupendous!

From: tempusfugitmd | Date: 2005-05-15 13:55:02 UTC
Wow! How can I possibly begin to thank all the wonderful people who
turned out to make this gather such a tremendous success?! Thank you
all for bringing food and drink and paper plates and ice; for
cleaning up so well that the pavilion looked better than it did when
I got there in the morning; for helping haul the many bags of trash
out of the park. You all made my "job" as host a piece of cake. All
I did was rent a pavilion -- you guys made it an Event. Letterboxers
rock!

Many thanks to my co-hosts and good friends, The Doubtful Guests,
who talked me into thinking a spring gather was a good idea (you
were right!). Take a bow Scarab/Phineas Boch! Your Circus brainstorm
was the big draw to this event. May it have many happy travels to
entertain and amaze the letterboxing masses around the country. A
huge thank you to all the circus stamp carvers who volunteered their
time considerable talent: there wouldn't have been much of show
without your generosity.

A final thank you to my better half, Dead Duck Chuck, who slaved
over a hot grill in the sun when he could have been out on the Bay
fishing. :)

And, yes, I would gladly do it again!

Donna
Tempus Fugit
So many boxes, so little time!
