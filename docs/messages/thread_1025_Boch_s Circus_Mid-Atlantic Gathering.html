Boch's Circus/Mid-Atlantic Gathering

From: Art Vandelay | Date: 2005-05-16 01:13:39 UTC
Wow!  What a spectacular gathering!  A really grand time was had by all.  Thanks to Tempusfugit, Doubtful Guests and Dead Duck Chuck for all your hard work.  The stamps were all amazing and I'm happy to report we found all the Circus boxes.  In fact over our 3 day stay in the area we found a total of 45 boxes and exchanged with 46 new boxers.
 
We were totally amazed at the beauty and intricacy of all the stamps.  Each box was so exciting! 
 
Once again I have to say that letterboxers are among the friendliest people I've ever met.  So glad we got to meet so many of you and to see others we've met before.
 
Thanks again for giving us a fabulous performance!
