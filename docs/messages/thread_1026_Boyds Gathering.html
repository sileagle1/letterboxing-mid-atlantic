Boyds Gathering

From: Jan | Date: 2005-05-16 09:06:39 UTC
To quote our eloquent ringmaster, Phineas Boch, "It boggles the mind".
The gathering was a wonderful experience filled with beautiful
stamps, convivial company, good food and a beautiful park. I camped
for three days at Little Bennett and was able to add 34 boxes to my
counts. Even a Saturday evening thunderstorm didn't dampen the fun,
just gave me an excuse to get caught up on some sleep after boxing
till 11 PM on Friday night - my first experience with night boxing
where I learned a few things for the next night experience. For
example, it is really hard to hold a flashlight while stamping in and
it is even harder to find a trail in the dark once you wander off of
it to find the box.
As I sit here looking at the stamps to log my finds I am again amazed
at the quality of the carvings. I have found a lot of beautiful
stamps in this hobby, but never so many in one area. Nice job to all
of the carvers.
And a special thank you to our hosts and hostesses - Tempus Fugit,
Dead Duck Chuck, Scarab and Night Owl. I can't think of anything that
would have improved the experience. I can't repeat enough how
wonderful everything was.
Thank you, thank you, thank you
Jan of Team Little Dog
