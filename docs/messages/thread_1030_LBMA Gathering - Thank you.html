LBMA Gathering - Thank you

From: Elizabeth | Date: 2005-05-17 13:37:01 UTC
Hi all,

We are home, unpacked, caught up on laundry, ect. I wanted to extend
a thank you to evryone that participated in anyway in the event. We
had such a wonderful time at our first gathering. I don't want to
name names because I don't want to leave anyone out, but thank you
to the event hostess and host, the organizer(s) of Clue, the carvers
of all the wonderful circus stamps, the amazing chefs (the food was
great), the boxers who went above and beyond to make us feel so
welcome and helped us along the way when they didn't have to, the
boxers that stopped by our campsite and made us feel a little less
like outsiders looking in, and to the planters of the boxes we
hunted for whose clues were dead on EVERY time. I feel like I came
home with great knowledge and a wealth of friendship. Our weekend
could not have been better and it is thanks to all of you that
attended.

We will certainly look forward to the next gathering. In the
meantime, I have been inspired to plant some new boxes around me in
hopes that someone will come hunting for them. LOL

Take care all,
Liz
of Penguin Patrol
