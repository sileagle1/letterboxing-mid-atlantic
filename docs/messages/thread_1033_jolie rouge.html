Ahoy there H&H,
 
As I was strolling through the woods of the Brandywine Creek this fine evening, what did I spy but yer own Jolie Rouge!  So set yer mind at rest, 'tis water-tight and all ship-shape, as it should be.  'Twas a pleasure to see the log of those who'd visited in the 6 months since I'd last been there.
 
Perhaps those who could not find it will have more luck when they try again--but they must look sharp, 'tis right smartly hid.  If they be going back soon, they may send me a message & I'll tell them where more treasure be hid on that side of the water--and on t'other....
