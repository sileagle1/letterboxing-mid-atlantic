In the words of Fleur...Where are you Boxing this weekend?

From: Lightnin Bug | Date: 2005-05-20 17:55:48 UTC
Enquiring minds want to know....

On Sunday at 8:30 am, Jan (of team Little Dog) and I will indoctrinate
the Wisconsin Hikers to the world of Hairy Leggerdom. As this is not
really a mystery, per se, the location will be ShredMTB's Nolde Forest
Series, followed by some Mapsurfing.

Monday takes us North, even by Allentown standards, to Hickory Run for
some well aged (1999) Mapsurfer boxes. Gerania (Gillian) will be
joining us to complete the indoctrination. We will be there at 11:30,
but have not chosen a starting point. Again, one box is no great
mystery to those who have ever been at Hickory run and know where the
yellow trail goes.

These will be longer hikes, but not really with much elevation gain.
So, if you are interested, do buzz in to the bug-line. Bring your own
food and water and wear sturdy shoes.

Lightnin' Bug
