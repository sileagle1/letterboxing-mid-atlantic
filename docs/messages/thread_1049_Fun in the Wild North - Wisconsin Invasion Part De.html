Fun in the Wild North - Wisconsin Invasion Part Deux

From: Lightnin Bug | Date: 2005-05-24 00:32:56 UTC
Another day, more boxes. Gerania and I joined the Wisconsin Hikers
for some Hickory Run Hairy Legging. More Mapsurfers were on the
docket, such as Hickory Trail Run and Hickory "O" Run. Both were
found and Martini Man enjoyed the Orienteering aspect of the latter
box. Perhaps a new hobby.... These were well aged boxes from 1999,
last centuru. It was almost Darmoorian!

We hopped over to Pocono International to find New Jersey Carole's
Long John Letterbox. Cool Stamp, but we were expecting something
eponymous! ;-) Picked up the Bum-Bee Hitchhiker as well.

We bopped back top Natty Bumppo's Nescopeck Butterfly in the same
park. We were second to find, after Riversol.

Gerania and I had to head back to our families, but it is rumored
that the Wisconsin Hikers were going vulture hunting.

Well, tomorrow it is back to the job. Thanks for the fun, Wisconsin
Hikers and Hairy Leggers!

Lightnin' Bug
