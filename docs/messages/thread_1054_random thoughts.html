From: mizscarlet731 | Date: 2005-05-27 02:22:47 UTC
Just got back from a week away and found 165 messages in my
in box, and before I left I had put all my letterbox stuff into digest
form. Tough week away in rural Mexico helping a friend with an
adoption. Believe it or not found time to box. There were 2 boxes
in mexico city i tried for and got skunked, one the park was
closed the other just too far away. The other 2 were in a city
called Guanahata(sp?) not to be found. As I said tough week I
tear up everytime I think of that tiny baby we left in a crib in an
orphanage not knowing if we would ever see her again. And of
all the other children that will grow up with in it's walls and never
have a family.
