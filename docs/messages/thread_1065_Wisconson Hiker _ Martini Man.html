From: Marie & Bob | Date: 2005-05-30 00:59:06 UTC
Had the good fortune to meet Debbie & John over dinner with Irene &
Maryann. Time to eat swap stories and exchange stamps. All to soon it
was time to leave. I met up again today at Tyler Park with our new
friends to do some BOB boxes while Marie stayed home. Got four boxes I
did not have and they were well on their way to getting all of the BOB
boxes when I departed.
Just about dark now so maybe they are at Issac Newtons for a little
well earned refreshment. With one more day before heading home again I
hope they enjoyed their trip and look forward to when we might meet
again.
