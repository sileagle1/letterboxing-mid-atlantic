From: Marie & Bob | Date: 2005-05-30 13:26:58 UTC
Teapot and I were up in Pike and Wayne counties PA
Found "Shohola Falls" on Friday and were then rained out for the day.
Found on Sat. 4 of the 5 "Equinunk Charge" boxes. Did not get Equinunk
UM Church. If it was there I could not find it. It seemed so simple
just step over the fence and go under the bushes. Went on to
find "Irving Cliff", "Panther Swamp" and "Promised Land" all alive and
well. Again the rain came and we headed home. Sunday with Wisconson
Hiker and Martini Man in Tyler Park for company while they did BOB
boxes. Got 4 that I did not previously do. All alive and well but
grtting harder to find due to growth of underbrush.

From: cahillymom | Date: 2005-06-01 15:54:00 UTC
We planted a new box on Dunderberg Mountain up in NY state a bit.
Fabulous, amazing, intriguing park. The guy who did the Mauch Chunk
gravity railroad way back when was doing the same here. They spent
only one summer on construction and the whole project mysteriously
died, with the railroad grades 2/3 completed. The whole place is now
overgrown, and some spots washed out, but there are tunnels, walls,
cuts and fills all over the mountain. One of those places
that "deserves" a box.
