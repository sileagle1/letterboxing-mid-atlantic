Wisconsinites report on PA trip

From: Wisconsin Hiker | Date: 2005-06-03 04:23:26 UTC
Martini Man & I have returned home after a great 10-day hiking/boxing
trip in southeastern Pennsylvania. We want to send a big THANK YOU
to all the planters of boxes! We enjoyed the wide variety of clues,
the creative stamps and the interesting parks we had a chance to
visit. I planned to send individual messages with status updates to
the planters tonight, but with the LBNA site down I can't access
the "contact the placer" feature. Hopefully I'll be able
to contact everyone over the weekend.

Here are the boxes we were unable to find:

* Mt. Minsi `98
* Indigo Turtle
* LOTR #4  Gollum (found lid of box)
* Great Blue Heron
* Catch the Wind (found indentation in ground under rock)
* Lock Ridge
* Fish in Outer Space #1
* Pinebrook Park
* Ghostly Gathering #1  Pirate
* Memory Cache (found box w/log, but no stamp in box)

These represent only a small % of those we attempted. We were very
happy to find so many other boxes and most were in good shape. On
the few that needed maintenance, we dried out and/or replaced baggies
and logbooks when necessary.

We didn't realize that PA would be almost jungle conditions! Lots
of heavy growth covering those common landmarks such as stumps, etc.
We encountered many brambly thorns, stinging nettles and poison ivy.
(Admittedly sometimes these weren't actually near the box, just
where we initially THOUGHT the box might be!) We have lots of
scratches and hopefully won't see any outbreak of rashes

As a bonus of our trip, we got to meet several local boxers for joint
boxing excursions and/or for a tasty meal. We really appreciated the
friendliness and hospitality of Lightin'Bug, Jan of Team Little
Dog (best wishes to her), Gerania93, Irene of Hikers & Hounds, Miz
Scarlet, Teapot Marie and Eagle Bob.

Thanks also to those of you who emailed us restaurant tips. If any
of you are in WI, please feel free to contact us for local info or to
get together for an exchange. If by some remote possibility you will
be in our area on OCT 1, I'm planning a gathering for that day.
You can check www.hauntedharvest.homestead.com for more info.

During our trip we did plant one box. I'll post the clues on
this board since the site is currently down, then add them to LBNA
when it is back up.

Thanks again!

Wisconsin Hiker
