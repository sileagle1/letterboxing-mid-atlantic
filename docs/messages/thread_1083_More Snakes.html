More Snakes

From: Suzanne Coe | Date: 2005-06-03 23:13:59 UTC
Last week I went out to check on one of my boxes--the county seems to be building a path to it, and I'm keeping a close eye out to make sure they don't turn in the wrong direction... & found a slow-moving garter snake in one of the bulldozer treads.  :(  Tuesday I was back at the same box with Geckospot & we ran into the live version--a nice little guy.  Then yesterday--walking the other part of the path, by the pond--heard a rustling in the grass, and YOW--here's a nice BIG snake--NOT a garter snake--with a little snake on her back.  (Cowsmart interpreted this as mama & baby.  NightmareKing suggests a parasite.  I think it was him & her, & they were busy.)  Water snakes, I think.  But I am considering changing the name of the boxes from "Snake Hunt" to something more cuddly....
