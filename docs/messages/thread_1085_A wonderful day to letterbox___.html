A wonderful day to letterbox!!!

From: divinelotus73 | Date: 2005-06-05 03:05:33 UTC
Leafcutters, Lotus and cousin The Pitman Gardener had a fun filled
day letterboxing!

First, we found "The Persistence of Winky" at the Philadelphia Art
Museum, we were glad to see that this box hung around for a few more
weeks!!

Second, we went up to Manuyunk to find "Boards and Bridges", great
hike alond the canal, found the box- but the stamp (I type in horror)
was MISSING!! So I know this box was placed by Pico, but I seem to
recall that either Reindeers or Indigo Vulture was doing box
maintence on it, so a heads up the original stamp went missing- the
log book and tupperware container were still in place, I seem to
think the stamp may have wandered off with a previous finder. Check
your bags. Also, not to leave a box without a stamp, I carved a
really REALLY simple stamp, so beware it's crude, but at least there
is an image in the box if you attempt it.

Third, headed over to the Wizardland Box, great story, fun box. But
there must be 80 gazillion trees with holes in the base!!! We
eventually found it! We also found the Global Traveller HH.

Fourth, went to play with Dude the WonderDog! I encourage everyone
to do these boxes. The stamps are awesome, and the logbook is
probably the BEST I have ever seen. It is really nice, my fondest
regards to Hikers and Hounds for their impeccable boxes!

Fifth (it was a busy day, lol) we had an on trail exchange with Team
Kelly, nice family and cute baby. They had kind words to say about
Irene! They were out doing the Brave Little Catipillar Series.
Since we had already found those boxes, we let The Gardener find
them! They were all in great shape, although we didn't find Box #4,
so I don't know if it's missing or we just missed it.

Sixth, headed back to Doylestown so that the The Gardener could find
the 4 Little Pigs box, it was in good shape, and we picked up the
Grin-N-Bear-It Hitch Hiker.

Lastly we ended the night but doing some box maintance at our Happy
Happy Birthday Box and WoM Box. Thank you to all that have found
these boxes!

Whew! What am I doing tomorrow?
Lotus
