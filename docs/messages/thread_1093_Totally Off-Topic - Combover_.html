Totally Off-Topic - Combover?

From: Lightnin Bug | Date: 2005-06-07 13:53:02 UTC
Back in the fall, I had kidded around about Mullets, a 1980's
haircut. I even went so far as to carve a HH and release it in SE PA
back in November. The mullet has been found and is apparently heading
out to Wisconsin. Anyway, I am looking for funny HH's to carve based
upon odd haircuts yet again. I was researching the "combover", choice
of the follically impared (balding dudes and used car salesmen).

Now, here is the totally bizzare thing. The US Patent Office has
actually awarded a patent for the combover to some guys in Florida:

http://coolgov.com/index.php?p=36

For those of you with Patent searching ability, the number is
4,022,227...

Happy combing over. No, this is not an obscure letterboxing clue.

Lightnin' Bug
