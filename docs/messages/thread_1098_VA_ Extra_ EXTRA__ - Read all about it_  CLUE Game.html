"Hey there Nick Danger, Mr. 'Thinks He's a Private Detective', what's your hurry, where's the
fire?"
"You're not going to believe this."
"Why, someone talk you into running the Clue Exchange Game again?"
"Nah, Mr. Boddy's been found dead downtown."
"Again!?"
"Again.  Another cousin heir.  I gotta get down there.  See ya later."
Friday, 10:13PM. 
 
I arrived at the Town Square and walked into the large convention tent where Mr. Boddy was
found dead an hour earlier.  I knew there was some kind of merchandising convention in town
but I was unprepared for what I saw.  On one side Mr. Clean consoled the sobbing Morton Salt
Girl.  Tears poured down her face.  On the other side the Pillsbury Dough Boy was in a heated conversation with the Land-O-Lakes indian maiden.  In the middle, Inspector Grogue was just finishing interrogating Cracker Jack. 
 
"What's the story Inspector?  I'd say you've got some circus on your hands here but it looks
more like the corner grocery."
 
"Very funny Nick. Always with the clever remark. Take this seriously for a change will you. 
Our pre-eminent citizen, Mr. Boddy, was found dead here about an hour ago.  The body's
obviously been moved so the murder could have happened anywhere between the Woolworth's five and dime and the KrispyKreme donut shop.  How can I ever sort this out?!"
 
"Looks to me like ya have as many possible Suspects, Weapons, and Places as the labels on A&P's shelves." 
 
"Well Nick, you think it's funny now but Mrs. Boddy has asked for you by name to help the
department investigate.  Now who's laughing?"
 
Moxie man then interrupted us.  While he complained to the Inspector about losing shelf life
or something I realized I didn't have a clue where to begin.
 
Signed,
Nick Danger
Third Eye
Private Investigations

P.s.

GET A CLUE!  Or rather bring a Clue.  Here's the scoop.  Any guy or doll can play Clue at
the VA Mystery Gather this Fall, see?  You just have to sign up for an image and bring a
stamp or two. 
 
THE THEME: Those familiar Product Logos we all know and love. 
 
Did Mr. Clean do it in the KrispyKreme with the Brillo Pad?
 
Send Nick Danger your clue of choice and we'll try to keep from having duplicates.  Sorry,
The Keebler Elf and Treehouse are already suspects.  Watch this space for information on a
database we will post of what Suspects, Weapons, and Places have been taken. 
 
It's never too early to start carving.
 
Oh, and just in case you were worried, I hear the management has some new game mechanics to prevent the little snafus we had last time.  Got me?
 
In any case everyone should leave with some interesting impressions. 
N.D. 3rd Eye
