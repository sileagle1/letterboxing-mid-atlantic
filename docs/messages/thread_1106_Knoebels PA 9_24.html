The Pine Barn Inn is a great place to stay! For about $70 (last year)
you get a nice air conditioned room with two double beds, one of which
is the quarter 'shaker' variety, color cable tv, and a lovely
restaurant that sometimes has live entertainment!
Plus there is a letterbox in the immediate vicinity, I am told.
And, they take AAA discounts there. I highly recommend it!
Enjoy!
