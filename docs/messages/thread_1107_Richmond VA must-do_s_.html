--- In lbma@yahoogroups.com, Todd White <genealogy_va@y...> wrote:
Are there any quick or must-do's near the convention center?

Check out JRP - Belle Isle - Just a short drive from the convention
center. It's one of the spots anyone visiting Richmond must visit.

Also check out Maymont (Maymont and Lucky Horseshoe) if you have the
time - It's about 4 miles from downtown and another must place visit
in Richmond.

If you get the Maymont boxes, go around the corner to get the JRP -
Pump House Boxes, another interesting spot.

The above boxes should be good.

The Carillon, Reedy Creek, Pony Pasture, and Forest Hill Park are
nearby but I think some of these may be missing.

I'll be happy to answer any questions.
