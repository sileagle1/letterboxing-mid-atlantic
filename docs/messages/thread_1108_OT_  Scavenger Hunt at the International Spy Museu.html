The International Spy Museum's Ultimate Scavenger Hunt

Tuesday, 21 June 2005; 7  10:30 pm

Have you got what it takes to beat the SpyMasters?

Picture yourself and a team of sly and talented spies, sleuths, or
detectives, on an after-hours, action-packed Ultimate Hunt behind-the-
scenes at the International Spy Museum. Bring your own trusted team
or let us identify worthy colleagues for you. Either way, your team
will test its espionage know-how as you hunt down answers to 50
mission-critical questions. Can you succeed as the clock ticks? Armed
with a top secret tricks-of-the-trade kit, you'll face cunning
challenges including: invisible messages, concealment devices, border
patrol guards, seductive agents, spies of infamyeven spies of
fiction! Only one team will triumph, but all can look forward to
coffee, dessert and awards ceremony in the clear at the Hunt
debriefing. May the spy-savviest team win!

*Minimum age of 18 required

Tickets: $45 per person or $180 per team of four  Members of The Spy
Ring (Join Today!): $36 per person or $144 per team of four  Space
is limited  advance registration required!
