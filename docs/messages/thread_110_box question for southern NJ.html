You probably figured this one out but I just checked the national
list and there's a box in, you guessed it, Bayville. If you'd like to
be away from the truck buddy picnic for awhile longer, there's one
south of there, 'Green Lily' in Tuckerton. Take route 9. I haven't
looked for either of those so I don't personally know if they're OK
or still there. There are 3 boxes on Long Beach Island. Route 9 again
but get off at Manahawkin and travel east over the bridge. I visited
those 3 boxes this Monday and they're all there/OK.
