Hi All,
The Aroostook Sleuth, Little Tag-a-long and Holster-roo traveled to
western PA last week and planted 6 boxes in the Childhood Adventure
Club series honoring the Sleuth's and Tag-a-long's childhood clubs
when growing up there. The boxes are all planted in Westmoreland
county near Apollo. If you are travelling to the area hope you will
check them out. There are also at least three other boxes in the area
that we are aware of planted by another boxer. All of these are
within in about 5 or 6 miles of each other. If you find them, let us
know what you think.
Cheers,
