Hi All,

Kirby Rider and I went to French Creek to do something mysterious and
plant 6 boxes from our Chinese Zodiac. He had carved some of them and
is getting pretty proficient with the old speedball. The region of
the Horse/Ox/Sheep does have some small wasps, so be careful. We got
nailed, once each.

Got some cool photos of some beaver stumps and some wood frogs (they
are camouflaged very well).

Cruised to Cyclonics Letterboxing Special and left behind a running
hitchhiker with a newfound phage....

The highlight of the day was when we got home and Sheba and the family
stopped by to do some exchanges. She sat in the 'distinguished
letterboxer' chair.
