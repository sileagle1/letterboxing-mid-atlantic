Warcat Country too!  Bergen County.  We have a series of 3 boxes in Wyckoff off Route 208  at the Wyckoff Wildlife Center (Mc Faul Environmental Center), 3 boxes in Franklin Lakes at the Audubon Society (also off 208) and a box in Glen Rock (again 208).  We will be planting another series of 3 this week if we get time.  This will be Rochelle Park- trying to get there tomorrow or Friday so check out the new box listings.  All of these are very close to HoHoKus and easy to find.  Some are 10 minutes, the Wyckoff boxes about an hour.

Thanks to everyone who gave me boxing suggestions for my trip. I visited longer than expected in NJ so I decided to do the 5 box series of United Methodist Churches  in PA on my way home.  I took the Hancock exit off of 17 and headed south on 191. The boxes were hidden in great spots and I recognized some of my collegues who had stamped in. Best of all, I picked up 2 HH's on the way out. One traveled to Tully area ( Tinker Falls) in NYS where I was planting a new box on the way home. The other will probably make a road trip with me this weekend. *grin* 

Thanks again for all the great suggestions. I will try to make it to some of those great Bergen County boxes!
