From: Todd White | Date: 2005-06-22 12:36:10 UTC
Hello,
There are a number along I-81 thanks mostly to Wordfried (P-U Letterbox is easy and fun and involves a great footbridge over the river), listed on both Altasquest and LBNA site. 
 
If you want a scenic view, go after Seven Bends for a great view of the Shenandoah River and valley.  Nearby is Wordfried's Woodstock Tower Seedling (which, if you find, I will try not to hate you).
 
The Table is a quick one near Tom's Brook (exit 291, south along Rt. 11 which parallels the highway).  It's mine, and only been there a short while.  Great little park for kids.
 
Easy one at the New Market Battlefield, too, just off the New Market exit called The Meeting Place.
 
For wine, go to Rappahannock Cellars ( http://rappahannockcellars.com/ ) in Front Royal.  Tell Brian Delaney I said, "Hi."  I've been bugging him to let me place a letterbox there.  Tell him to give you a discount!
 
Thanks,
FamilyMan (Todd White)
