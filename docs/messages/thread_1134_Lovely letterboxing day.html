Lovely letterboxing day

From: wpastfarm | Date: 2005-06-23 00:42:04 UTC
We had a wonderful, first-day-off-from-school, day, doing the
Hartshorne Park series in Atlantic Highlands, NJ. It is a beautiful
state park and we were able to find 5 of the 6 stamps in the series
thanks to great clues by Bookworm (we were unable to locate Purple
Mountain Majesty although I am 99% sure we were in the right spot).
My shoulders are going to feel it tomorrow, hauling around my 26 lb
toddler in that backpack for 2.5 miles, but the weather was beautiful
and it was extremely pleasant under all those shade trees.
