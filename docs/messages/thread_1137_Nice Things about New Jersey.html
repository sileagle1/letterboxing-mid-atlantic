Nice Things about New Jersey

From: dave & diane | Date: 2005-06-23 21:50:38 UTC
Hello, my husband and I started Letterboxing about 2 years ago here in RI.
We go under the trail name of the von der Insels. I joined this chat list to
keep up-to-date on the boxes just south of the northeast area - David and I
do most of our boxing in RI with jaunts north to VT, NH and Canada (search
for our Canadian boxes under "the von der Insels"). We've found a few boxes
in NJ and PA and now I'll be able to notify this list about your local boxes
that we find.
Good things about NJ:
Well, David is from New Jersey originally and is temporarilly there now in
Burlington County. Some of you may find us wanderering around the Pine
Barrens (so much like Washington County ,RI). And while visiting David on
long weekends we can take short jaunts into Pennsylvania, etc in search of
new letterboxing adventures.
Diane
The von der Insels
