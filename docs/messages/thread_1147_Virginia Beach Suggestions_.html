Re: Virginia Beach Suggestions?

From: Elizabeth | Date: 2005-06-28 14:52:51 UTC
We were there just a few weeks ago. The boxes we found were all very
quick. See below for a list:

Golden's Brainteaser #1 - Box in ok condition, could use a little
repair work but still there. Emailed owner, but got no response. Took
us about 15 minutes to find.

Golden's Brainteaser #2 - Box was missing. Trail was in bad shape,
muddy, trail signs missing. Try at own risk.

Wild Indoors - Box in excellent condition. Indoor location and hand-
carved stamp. Great shop to visit. Very fun, quick find!

Mr. Moody Goes to City View Park - Great quick 2 box series. Boxes in
great shape, large enogh to leave a HH behind in (which we did).
Stamps are very nice! Beware of thorns and brambles at Happy Guy!!!

High Roller - Box in good shape. Great park! Short 5 minute walk to
box from car! Kids will love to roll down Mt. Trashmore!

EEK's Bunny Box - Box in ok shape, logbook full, great hand-carved
stamp. Took less than 20 minutes to find and return to car. Contacted
the placer (ChilePepper) about maintainance, and they said they would
get out to it as soon as they could. Pretty quick find. A little muddy
if rain has been through recently. Be careful of snakes. We spotted
two on the trail!

Good luck at your tournament! Happy boxing!

Liz
of Penguin Patrol
