Creature of Fountainhead Park

From: Judy B | Date: 2005-06-30 02:36:08 UTC
Met up with Phynstar (NY) and Koyote (Ashburn VA) to search for the
Creature of Fountainhead Park today. A couple of boxes were found on
the return trail trip - but all but the very last (Part 6 Headless
Horseman of Halloween) were found within 7.5 hrs. Koyote had to leave
after Part 3 or we surely would have found the "horse like" tree with
his help. bummer%^( So I guess Phynstar and I lose our bragging
rights for completing in a single day. However, we celebrated anyway
with drinks and dinner and drinks at Artie's in Fairfax City. Yum!
