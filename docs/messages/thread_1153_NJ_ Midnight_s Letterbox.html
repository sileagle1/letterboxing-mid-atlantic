Midnight's Letterbox
Foothills Park
Pequannock, NJ - Morris County
Placed by Wingfoot on 06/30/05

Clues: Easy
Terrain: Easy
Time: 15 minutes

This letterbox is named after my cat.

NOTE: There is a firearms range in the park. If it is in use, you
may want to skip this box until a later date.

DIRECTIONS: From Interstate 287 get off at Route 23 heading south.
Just before the second light turn right and keep bearing right onto
West Parkway. Park is on right.

Can be done on the way to or after MOUNTAINSIDE Letterbox.

Start at Walter Hanzl's rock. Start walking at 270 degrees. You'll
come to an unblazed trail. Shortly up trail on left is tree with a
sign saying Parkland, and all the stuff you shouldn't do on
parkland. From that tree go 18 paces at 30 degrees. Behind tree, in
pile of rocks is what you seek.

Please hide box completely hidden from view where you found it after
stamping in.
