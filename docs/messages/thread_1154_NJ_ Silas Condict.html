Silas Condict (formerly known as the Shenanigans Letterbox)
Silas Condict Park
Kinnelon, NJ - Morris County
Placed by Wingfoot on 05/10/03
Updated 06/30/05

Clues: Easy
Terrain: One Easy Climb
Time: 30 minutes

This letterbox was once the Mystery Letterbox SHENANIGANS. I thought
it had never been found and I decided to make it a regular
letterbox. A geochacher did stumble across it, so it had been found.

NOTE: This is a very busy park. Please be discreet.

There are no blazed trails in this park. Just go to the highest
point in the park at 1012 elevation. There is a rock mound there.
There is a railroad tie step there. Start at the railroad tie step.
Go 5 paces at 250 degrees to a flat rock. From rock go 13 paces at
10 degrees to a small split tree. From small tree go 21 paces at 350
degrees. Behind large rock, underneath pile of rocks is what you
seek.

Please hide box completely hidden from view where you found it after
stamping in.
