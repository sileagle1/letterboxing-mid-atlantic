Waterproof Containers

From: June | Date: 2005-07-03 23:06:20 UTC
Does anyone go to sporting goods stores for containers? When I was in
Colorado last week we went to a huge one out there and they have some
pretty cool stuff. They have waterproof plastic pouches. They also
have little plastic containers that fishermen keep hooks and flys and
little stuff like that in. Some of them are waterproof. There is a
small waterproof utility box that I see can be found at
http://www.gsiodoors.com, according to the label on the box. Even the
ones that aren't stated as being waterproof would be good if the
contents were wrapped securely in a small plastic bag, depending on
the hiding place.

And if you're in the market for a bigger container, they have
waterproof ammo boxes from WWll. <s>
