July 4th weekend in NJ/PA

From: dave & diane | Date: 2005-07-06 05:25:54 UTC
I went down to visit Dave in NJ this past weekend and we did a little 'boxing.
Saturday was a bust - very frustrating. We searched for 3 boxes and didn't have much luck:
Coward? LB - no stamp, but a very damp logbook - last visited July 14, 2003!
Spy House LB  - archeology students digging in the yard, but no box found.
Rose Hill Cemetery LB - found all the clue sites but no box.
We were already depressed, so we went to visit David's old hometown.
 
Sunday, we had much better luck!
Terra-Cotta Tangram LB - Success! and what fun! A great site and wonderful stamps.
Oh, Be My Friend Mystery LB - another success and another great stamp.
Pine Barrens Box Turtle Mystery LB - Ask a New Englander to find the mill site! Where there's running water...
    We had a little problem with the compass reading, but found the box just fine - Thanks again for another great stamp.
A much better day topped off by a dinner of German food - Excellent!
 
Monday found us in PA:
Scudder Falls Bridge LB - Great directions and a cute little stamp.
Battle of Trenton - 1776 LB - A nice little series and a great spot, wonderful hand-carved stamps. We'll be back to this spot for boxing I'm sure - didn't have time to gather more boxes before I had to make my train in Trenton.
By the way, the Bowman Tower Park is closed on Mondays. Funny thing  is that the Washington Crossing Historical Park and Thompson-Neeley House was supposed to be closed Monday as well.
 
So, Thanks to everyone who placed these boxes and see you all next time.
