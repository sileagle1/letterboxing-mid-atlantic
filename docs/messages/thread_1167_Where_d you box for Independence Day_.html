Where'd you box for Independence Day?

From: Lorie Thomas | Date: 2005-07-06 19:53:39 UTC
I just got back from Vermont and was lucky enough to
drag friends into the skeeter and black fly infested
woods of VT and Mass.

At Mt. Tom in Mass we were looking for the Mt. Tom
access trail stamp but had no luck. We did kick up
the notebook for Bray Loop Metamorphosis #1 but no
stamp. I stamped in and put the notebook back but
will email the owners so they can check on the series
to make adjustments and repairs.

On the Beau Bridges trail at Mt. Tom we did find the
LB, in good condition. Not too many mosquitoes made
it through our mad slap dances. Poor skeeters acted
like there wasn't another warm-blooded meal for miles.

Monday we found the Quechee Gorge stamp and Tuesday on
the way home we found the Chicopee SP stamp. These 2
were in good shape.

Next time I'm heading for Vermont I'll have to hit a
few more right off Rt 91.

Oh, and the palmetto bug HH is on the move again.
geckospot
