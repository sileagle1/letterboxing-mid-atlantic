Fantastic boxes today

From: wpastfarm | Date: 2005-07-12 01:08:26 UTC
We did the Terra Cotta Tangram. GREAT series. What a cool place, in
a creepy sort of way, and fun to learn about the Blue Comet on our
way down there. We didn't think to take a tangram along to help us
out, so we had to backtrack a little, but it was really neat. The
last box was pretty wet inside (maybe that rain last Friday?),
although luckily no damage to the log book. We dried it out as best
we could and put the whole thing into a ziploc to be on the safe side.

Weird -- there was a HUGE cloud of moths around that site. I think
they were gypsy moths as all the trees were denuded. They were
flying in our hair, running into our faces, you name it. I have
never seen so many at one time. Truly an infestation.

We also did the "Oh Be My Friend" mystery box -- a lovely spot. We
saw an enormous Northern Pine Snake (on the state threatened list)
near the bridge, easily 3-4 feet in length.

So a great day, even if it was on the warm side. Boys are with my
parents for the rest of the week, so I get to take a little break
from letterboxing while they whip their grandparents into taking them
out. We have managed to find over 30 boxes in the 5 weeks we have
been doing this. Have to confess that I will miss it just a little
while they are gone.... ;o)

~ Kris (the B's)
