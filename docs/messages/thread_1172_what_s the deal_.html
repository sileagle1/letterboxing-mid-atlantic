From: nattybumppolbna | Date: 2005-07-13 01:53:38 UTC
It's not about the hike. It's not about the stamp. It's not about the site selection, or the
hoops I will make you jump through. It's about the 12 minutes of peace I have to write out
the clue.

It's 12 minutes that i don't have to wipe anybody's nose or change anybody's diaper. It's
12 minutes that I don't need to answer for on a time sheet. It's 12 minutes that don't
belong to the IRS or the mortgage company. It's the 12 minutes that 3 pieces of
tupperware, 3 old erasers and 3 old notebooks become... something greater.

Yes, I might stretch a lame concept from 1 to 3 boxes. But that's because I have 12 magic
minutes. And those minutes are all mine, baby.

Coming Soon: The Battle of Old Mill Run Redux!

What the groundhog took away will be - reborn! As a 4 box series.

I've been shavin' erasers like a madman. It's not much exercise, but damnit, my 12
minutes were glorious!

Natt

Depending upon how you decide to go up, the Pinnacle hike can hvae
the 1000 ft over 1 mile or two. So, it is either a 20% grade or a
10% grade. No footholds, just a good cardiovascular system
required. Once you get up to the ridgeline, watch out for some
serious rocks. Eagle Bob and I did it last August 8th.

We plan on repeating our exploits in the Delaware Water Gap aroud
the 24th of July. 2 1000 ft climbs. You interested?

Cyclonic Boxes are fun and wait until you see the pain that Team
Evil has in store for you on the AT... Bwahahahah!
