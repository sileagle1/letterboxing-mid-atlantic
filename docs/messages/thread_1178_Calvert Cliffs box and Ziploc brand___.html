Calvert Cliffs box and Ziploc brand...

From: shaneodom2003 | Date: 2005-07-15 13:41:21 UTC
Hello all,
Papa Woodwose here of Crownsville MD. We haven't posted in awhile
but I try to read a bit.
Family got out and boxed this last weekend at Calvert Cliffs in MD.
Excellent location for a box. Really the best sort of boxing. Great
Trails, wild-life (Beaver, Herons, Squirrels(The placer as well!),
Fish, crabs, and More!), Million year old shark teeth and shells,
and swimming. We had been meaning to hunt this box with a trip to
the Cliffs for over a year. The box was there and the stamp was
useable, but the contents were rather wet. Ziploc sandwich boxes
just don't hold up. They will photo degrade over time a temp
extremes. We dried out the journal abit, were able to stamp in,
carried replacement bags so double up everything. We usually carry
an extra box and/or ductape with us for just such a repair but not
this time. The next hunter, for we do certainly recommend this trip,
take time to learn and research the cliffs some before you go so you
will know how to find the shark teeth, should carry a replacement
box if the placer has not visited yet. Good, thick plastic boxes can
usually be found at dollar stores. I just want to restate that the
Ziploc brand boxes should really not be used.
Hope ya'll get a chance for this one, sure was cool.
Thanks for all for keeping up the hobby,
Peace and Happy Trails,
The Woodwoses,
Crownsville, MD.
