Hello Delaware boxers! We had a hot and steamy weekend and found
these LB's alive and well:
Rehobeth Art League
Frog Finders
Dagsboro's Quest
Jolie Rouge
Spell Check
Two in Brandywine SP may have gone missing: Brandywine Bluebird and
Future Roadrunner. Either that or the heat addled our brains. We
left behind a couple of surprises... Thanks for a fun weekend. If
you are headed north and might like to try your hand at boxing in a
"foreign land" head up to Kingston Ontario, about a 7 hour drive from
DE. We are also planning our first gathering of the region for
Memorial Day weekend, May 2006. Here's some more info:
http://www.atlasquest.com/events/event.html?gEventId=78

Happy trails! Norasta

Travelling to Canada (was Re: Delaware Boxes found...)

From: norastaa | Date: 2005-07-19 00:12:11 UTC
WarCat, At the moment you do not need a passport to drive into Canada.
We have US visitors regularly (my husband is American) and they have
photo ID (drivers License) and/or bring their birth certificates.
However, starting in December of 2006 & 2007 new U.S. regulations come
into effect:

http://travel.state.gov/travel/cbpmc/cbpmc_2225.html

So you WILL be able to drive into Canada AND get back home if you want
to participate in our gathering of May 2006.
That being said it seems
like everybody will eventually need a passport to leave and re=enter.
Well maybe not leave but the RE=Enter could be a problem. So you
may as well get a passport asap!
