From: Jan | Date: 2005-07-25 15:51:00 UTC
Hello and welcome to letterboxing.

Here is the link to the newcomers message board,
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/newboxers/. This is a board where you
can ask a lot of questions about getting started without bogging down
the regular message boards with questions that have been asked many
times before.

Also, even though not finding boxes occasionally is all part of the
game, one way that I check to see if a box is still available is to
check the last date found on the letterboxing.org website. For
example, if it hasn't been found in a year, I might guess that it is
not there anymore and decide if I want to take the time to check it
out or to look for one that has been found more recently. To use this
feature, you need to be a registered member of the website, something
that is really easy to do, just fill in the form. Then you will need
to go to the member services page and go to "set your preference" and
enable the "letterbox status - others". This allows you to see the
most recent date that a box was found when you view the clues.

You can also do the same thing on the atlasquest.com website.

Good luck and we will look for you on the newcomers board,
Jan of Team Little Dog
