From: wpastfarm | Date: 2005-08-03 00:21:51 UTC
Went to Cranford and environs today to pick up some of Rick in Boca's
stamps. Very nice stamps and we loved seeing the "granite spray on,
stick a few leaves on" effect in person. We had to give a pass to the
Echo Lake Park one, though, as the wasp nests were all over and we
were a little nervous about reaching into unknown places during this
time of year.

We also did NJ Physiography 103. We love this series and the initial
lookout was awesome -- over a huge gravel pit, with trucks going in
and out -- industry at its best but it did look like a little Tonka
truck display in miniature. The rest of the hike made us forget about
it and get back to nature though.
