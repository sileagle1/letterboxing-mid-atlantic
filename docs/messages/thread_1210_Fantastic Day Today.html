Fantastic Day Today

From: wpastfarm | Date: 2005-08-06 23:45:21 UTC
Actually had DH with us for this adventure. He has been resisting but
saw how much fun we are having and decided to join us for the exercise
at least.

Only one stamp, but it was a mystery that has not been found in nearly
three years, and even then only a handful of people have found it. I
had been to the place where I thought it was many many years before
and I had a sense of where it was -- even though it didn't appear to
be there at first, it was exactly where I had envisioned it. Odd.

Four days spent letterboxing out of the previous five. A new record
for us.
