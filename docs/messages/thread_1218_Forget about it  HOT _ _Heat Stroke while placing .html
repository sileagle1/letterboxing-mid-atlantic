Sewsewbusy and I were out in the Lousianna/Africa level heat . . .
placing boxes in prep for the Mystery Gather. Deep in a centuries old
hardwood forest... I was sweating profusely... my feet hurt... my
knees locked up... my back gave out... I was attacked by a tick....
but she was relentless... "Press on! We have to get ready for the
Letterboxers!!!"... that was the last thing I heard before I passed
out beneath an ancient oak... then Sewsew saved my life. She picked
me up put me on her shoulder and carried me back to the air
conditioned comforts of a local Country Club where the waitress gave
me cold beer until I regained my senses.

We have information about one night FREE Camping at a beautiful park
(with new Letterboxes)very near the gathering place
