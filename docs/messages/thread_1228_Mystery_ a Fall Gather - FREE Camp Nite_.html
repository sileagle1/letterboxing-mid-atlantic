One of the suggested locations for camping the weekend of the Sept 17
Mystery, a Fall Gather is at Pohick Bay Regional Park
(www.nvrpa.org/pohickbay). A non-electric site for 4 people is
$21.25 (5th, 6th & 7th persons are $2.50 ea).

I picked up brochures from the Northern VA Regional Park Authority
and saw it included a COUPON (you know how I love coupons) for
"One Free Night of camping at Pohick Bay for up to four customers.
Valid 3/1/05 to 10/30/05. Void on holiday weekends."

So if you are planning to camp and want a brochure (I have five) -
please email me OFF LIST (sowbiz @ yahoo . com) with your mailing
address ---
OR Call NVRPA directly 703.339.6104 and ask them to send you the
brochure with the coupon (they have other non-coupon brochures so be
specific.) That may be faster since I will be in and out of town the
next few weeks. But I'll mail to the first five asking by Sept 5th.

And there will be a number of boxes planted in that park! Admission
per car is $7/day for NON-Fairfax County Residents. But I am one so
I'll try and form carpools for non-campers. I would think the
admission is part of the camp fee -- but you should ask.

Hope to see all ya'll at the Gather. Keep checking the web site for
location clues update and box clues.
http://cozymysteries.tripod.com/mysteryafallgather/index.html
