From: bindlestiff_boxer | Date: 2005-08-19 19:32:13 UTC
Hi everyone,

Next month my mother-in-law and I will be coming from Mich to pick up
her Mother (Grandmom) in Bellmawr, NJ and driving to Jim Thorpe, PA to
stay at the Victoriann B&B in the Pocono's for a week.

I looked at the letterboxing.org site and don't see a single box
listed for Carbon County, PA!! Yikes... I'll be spending a whole with
2 elderly women and there are NO BOXES TO FIND!?

I've already started looking for clip art to carve a "tell me it ain't
so, Joe" letterbox to leave behind. LOL ... anyone want to adopt it
after I get it placed?

Besides all that, does anyone know of any easy to find boxes that
might not be too far off our path of our planned trip? I don't know
the exact route we'll take. So far all I've done is use map quest
route finder. (Any hints on the best route gratefully accepted!)
Please keep in mind my companions (Grandmom is going to be 95 this
year) when I say I need easy to find boxes. I can't be locking them
in the trunk too long while I go hiking down the trail. No wait ... I
can't say that in a public forum, can I? (Kidding -- I love both of
them to pieces, they're wonderful women)

If anyone lives near Bellmawr, NJ and wants to have coffee the weekend
of 9/10 and do an exchange or even this Jim Thorpe, PA area of the
Pocono's or will be in the area 9/12 - 9/16 (I think those are the
dates) let me know. I'd love to meet up with some fellow boxers. I've
got a couple of HH's I can give a big boost to and will be happy to
bring any HH's needing a ride to MI back with me.

Thanks in advance for the help. I've never been to the Pocono's and
I'm looking forward to the time in your area.

Enjoy!
---PJ
aka Bindle Babe
