From: aintnorock19 | Date: 2005-08-20 19:17:36 UTC
The best stuff in the world is called "Tecnu". It is a poison ivy
preventative that can also be used shortly after you get it.

Goto: http://www.poisonoakandivy.com/

You can get it over the counter at just about any drugstore.

For best results, if you know you're going to be around poison ivy,
you apply it liberally on exposed surfaces before you go out into it.
It will also work if you apply it then rinse off within a few hours
after you get into it.

I have used it both ways with 100 % success. I'm a youth minister and
we get a large bottle of it when we go work on mission trips where we
cut down weeds and do landscaping. I've never had any of my kids get
ivy when we've used the stuff. It's a miracle drug, and really not
too expensive.

good luck!

The irritant in poison ivy, urushiol, is found everywhere on the
plant - leaves (natch), vines, roots, etc. Did you encounter any
hairy vines on trees. That's it as well. It will begin leafing out
in a couple weeks.
