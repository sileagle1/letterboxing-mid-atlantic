Hi all,
Didn't see no snakes this weekend, but we have a major warning for Green Lane boxers.
We were hunting for Team Little Dog's "My Dream, My Reality" on Saturday.
I was with another dad, one of my girls and 2 of his kids.
We got to the expected spot behind a big dead tree.  We were surprised to see a copy of the clus sheet laying right there.  Then the other dad starting yelping and hopping around.  Followed closely by his daughter, then mine.
We were standing right on top of a yellow jacket nest.
I have never been pursued like that in my life.  They clung to our clothes for 100s of yards.
I kept swiping at my shorts, my boots, my daughter.  One went up her shirt, one in her bangs.  She got it worst, I think 10 stings.  I only got one.
Very scary.  Luckily no one was allergic.
I'd give that set of boxes a few months, maybe after the first frost before visiting.
 
On a lighter note, I replaced two store bought stamps with 2 nicely hand carved ones.
If you haven't found Golden Oak or Red Maple, check them out.
 
Be careful out there.
Raindear
