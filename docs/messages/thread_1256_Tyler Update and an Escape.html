Tyler Update and an Escape

From: Lightnin Bug | Date: 2005-08-29 13:22:28 UTC
On Sunday, Team J+R and I explored part of what Tyler SP in PA has to
offer. Our adevntures in "Mini CT" were rewarded with successful
finds of the following boxes:

Grateful Dead
Frank Zappa
String Cheese Incident
Pink Floyd Dark Side fo the Moon
Nine Inch Nails
The Beetles
SRV Texas Flood
Radiohead
VNV Nation
Schofield Bridge #1 (Left out in the wide open - please rehide
carefully) - Last logger has a red cow as a stamp
The Hop Devil

Excellent boxes, one and all and many thanks to Irene for arranging
all.

After that, we agreed that we were 'all boxed out' and decided to
head to a couple nearby wineries.

We deferred the following boxes to another day, when it is not as
muddy or perhaps the vegetation is not as dense.

The Police and Dr. Who.

I am also sad to report that Snugy Wugum, my little Chupacabra HH,
has escaped. He was last seen cavorting with a certain Fab 4....

LB

Knoebels Gathering Update - Camping

From: Nathan Brown | Date: 2005-08-29 21:45:35 UTC
Hey folks. I'm back from vactaion (a story for another post), and just
wanted to give an update as I gear up for the Knoebels Gathering on
September 24.

I have reserved two campsites (cabins were scarce) with platforms for
those interested in camping. I know Sheba, Judy and Irene are
4nterested. For those looking to split the cost of a site, please email
me privately and I will put you on the list.

For those looking for some late nite fun after the gathering, feel free
to stop by sites H-2 and H-4. You can find them on the campground map here:

http://www.knoebels.com/KcampMap04.pdf

I will update the gathering manual soon to reflect this.
