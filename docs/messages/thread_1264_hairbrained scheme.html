With gas prices going though the roof it's nice to go boxing and be able to get a few boxes
for your gas $. With that in mind on this lovley holiday week end I will be planting a series of
5 boxes. As with mmy x-mas series there willl be only one log. From my experience with my
other boxes on this trail it's a highly traveled trail, how to make finding the stamp and
stamping in more descrete? Well I'm mounting the stamps on magnets and placing them on
the bridges on the trail. You should be able to sit and tie your shoe and reach for the stamp
with no one the wiser. The log will be placed on a remote part of the trail with a nice bench
near-by for stamping. The boxes should be on the trail by llate afternoon.
