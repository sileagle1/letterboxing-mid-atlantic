Please excuse the lack of wit and humor in this post - I'm down to
serious business here.

This is a call for HELP from all Residents of the Northern Virginia
Regional Park Authority's six member jurisdictions:
Cities of Alexandria, Fairfax and Fall Church
Counties of Arlington, Fairfax and Loudoun
and anyone Camping at Pohick Bay Regional Park

There will be lots of boxes planted both in the gather park and at
other parks in the vicinity. One such park is Pohick Bay Regional
Park - with a $7/vehicle/day entrance fee!!! EXCEPT, for those
residents listed above and those Camping at Pohick.

I hope to establish a Shuttle Service from the gather to Pohick to
both deliver and retrieve boxers to the gather.

This is a 45 min donation of your time and tagged vehicle. A map for
Pohick is available at: http://www.nvrpa.org/pohickmap.html

There are six parking lots which are near boxious trail heads - only
five will be used because the boat shore launch lot usually has an
attendant collecting more fees!

To collect the names of those willing to drive I have added a
DATABASE to the LBMA List (Mystery - Shuttle Schedule). Please enter
your email, trailname, number of riders you can carry, and vehicle
make/model.

It would be helpful if there were two volunteers per time slot - so I
have records 1-10 for the 8:30 to 3:15 slots and records 11-20 Repeat
the Time Slots for backup drivers. Each Driver MUST complete the
full 45 min round trip route from gather pavillion to Pohick Bay
parking lots AND RETURN to pavillion.

At the gather there will be a sign-up board for Riders - based upon
volunteers and size of vehicles.

Obviously I need all INPUT BY Noon on Fri Sept 16 so I can make the
Rider Board.
