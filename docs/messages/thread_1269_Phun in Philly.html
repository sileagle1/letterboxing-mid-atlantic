And environs...

We tackled the rest (we think) of the Pennypack Pack Boxes on Friday,
and managed to find:

Ben Franklin: Inventor, Scientist & Founder Boxes
Mint Eagle
Pennypack Park Box

And no less than three (!!!) hitchhikers. We also dropped off one of
our newly-minted trash hitchhikers (On Your Mark, Get Set, Recycle)
at a place that really needed the help. This one was carved and
assembled by b#1, who is 9, so the stamp image requires a little
creative interpretation. Hopefully it still qualifies as art. We
had replaced the outer bag of this box so we used the old holey one
to clean up a particularly egregious mess on the way back out (we all
smelled like beer, YUCK) but there is still plenty there for the next
person who finds the box.

We also hunted unsuccessfully for the final Ben Franklin box, but I
am not sure we were in the correct spot.

Another beautiful day on Sat so we went down to Smithville NJ and
worked on Bicycle Rail Road. The first stamp (turtle turtle) appears
to be missing, as many noted in the log book for the second stamp.
We also went to one other mystery location and managed to get some
hitchhikers moving again.

Not sure if we will get to box the rest of this weekend but we are
definitely going out next week before school starts. Hope everyone
else is having a great boxing weekend aided by this beautiful weather!
