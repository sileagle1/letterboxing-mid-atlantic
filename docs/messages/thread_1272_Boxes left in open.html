Forgot to mention in my earlier rambling post that we were quite
distressed to see that Fleur de Lis's Ben Franklin boxes were *not*
rehidden well -- as a matter of fact, two of the three were just
laying on the ground. For my team, this is akin to sacrilege. There
were gasps of horror as they approached the sites and saw the boxes in
plain view. We did not recognize the previous finder's name or stamp
(that is assuming that it was a person in the log book that did this)
but it was a good reminder to us to take the time to be careful once
all the stamping in is done.
