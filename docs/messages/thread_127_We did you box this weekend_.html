We did you box this weekend?

From: Hikers_n_ Hounds | Date: 2004-06-06 23:31:58 UTC
Hikers & Hounds did some box maintenance on saturday and took our newest member, Michael to hunt the Treasure Hunt series in Neshaminy State Park. Yes, it was raining, heavily, but Michael would not be deterred from his quest. I have created a monster!
 
On Sunday we headed to Doylestown and found the Mercer Hunt series, beautiful park, wonderful trails, had a great time. Then after several wrong turns finally located Central Park and found the Kids Castle box. That park made me want to be a kid again. there is a HUGE, and I mean huge play structure that is a castle for kids to play in. Very cool little park, fun box.
 
So thanks Ed, Eagle Bob and Teapot Marie for a great day in Doylestown. Will have to head back for a couple of nearby boxes we didn't have time for.
 
H&H
