Back to School Blowout Boxing

From: wpastfarm | Date: 2005-09-08 13:31:34 UTC
We made the most of the two days left before school started today...

Tues. we went to Tyler Park PA with our good friends Team Deloro. We
started at the Aerosmith box from the Craftsmen Guild lot but quickly
ran into trouble with the Bee Gees, so we moved to the Plantation
Grove lot and after a fortifying picnic lunch, found:

Grateful Dead (what a hill!)
Frank Zappa
String Cheese Incident
Elvis Series: Hound Dog, Blue Suede Shoes and TCB
plus the Fighting Owls HH

We were unable to find Nine Inch Nails box. :o(

Wed. found us launching an all-out assault on Manhattan, with the
help of a couple of family members (inc. one savvy NYC resident). In
the following order:

Metropolitan Museum of Art
New York, New York (could not find)
Give My Regards to Broadway
Irish Diaspora (also not to be found, but cool spot)
Italian for Beginners
Little Apple in the Big Apple

Plus a new box planted, clues to be posted soon. We stamped the town
purple! And I am sore beyond belief. Half of our team went back to
school today, so thank goodness I get a little break. They didn't
make their goal of 100 boxes this summer but they came pretty close
(80+) and agreed that they had a great time trying.

Hope everyone has a good boxing weekend. Weather is supposed to be
gorgeous.

~ the b's
