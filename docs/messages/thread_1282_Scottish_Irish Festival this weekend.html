Hi LBMAers,
For those of you who will be in Montgomery County PA this weekend (9/10-9/11) there is a Scottish Irish festival in Green Lane Park.  This thing has grown a lot over the last 5 years.
There are all kinds of things to see including Irish dancing and bagpipes, caber tossing, sheep dog competition, etc.  It's FREE.
There are things for sale of course and Irish food available.
 
And there are lots of letterboxes in the park too.
