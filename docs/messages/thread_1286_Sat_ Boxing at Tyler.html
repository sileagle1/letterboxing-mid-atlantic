Re: Sat. Boxing at Tyler

From: true indigo | Date: 2005-09-12 21:32:15 UTC
I was in Tyler SP on Saturday. I am sad to say I did not log "The Police" but I certainly didn't
skip it. We got up at 4am singing all of the songs of the groups we hoped to find that day
and let me tell you "Roxanne" at 5:30am traveling along dark I 80 east does NOT sound as
good as you think it might (at least when I sing it).

We got to the park about a half an hour after it opened and dove right in, with "The Police"
clues in hand. We parked at the bridge parking and vowed to note everything along to way
hoping it would help us nab multiple boxes. I referred to my "The Police" clues (my only
ones on paper by the way) frequently. RGBisMe insisted that we go for the Beatles box
since we had a HH/cootie to drop off there.

After that things became overwhelming and confusing. So many boxes, so many paths. We
got a few, we had to pass on a few more. "The Police" was one of the ones we had to pass
on if we wanted to check out any of the other areas in the park.

We enjoyed every box we found on Saturday and are sad to not come away without seeing
them all. We make no excuses except for our own short comings at interpreting clues,
physical fatigue, and lack of daylight hours. It was a long drive (over 3.5 hours each way)
but we have no regrets. Thank you to all who have planted in Tyler Park, your efforts are
appreciated.

true indigo
