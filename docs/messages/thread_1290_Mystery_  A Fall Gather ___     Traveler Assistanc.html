From: gallant_rogue | Date: 2005-09-12 00:22:41 UTC
Got your hotel room booked?

Need driving directions??

Have a special travel situation you need help with???

Anyone coming in from out of town who needs assistance should feel
free to contact me here or by email at gallant_rogue@...

There is no such thing as a dumb question! We are here to help!

There are 50 plus event letterboxes organized and placed in "Seven
Chapters" (?)... just waiting to be found! All attendees will
receive a packet of clues for all event Letterboxes complete with
maps and a "CheckList of Event Stamps" so you can chart your finds
and keep track of what else is out there... and we will have other
area "non-event" LB clues available too! Let me know which ones you
are interested in and I'll make sure you get whatever help you need!

So you are a stamp carving "purist" . . . wait till you see our
event stamps! All the event stamps are great works of art!

Like mystery? The clues are fun yet some have clever twists(?).

Just want to hike? The location (a mystery to be solved)is perfect
and we will not run out of trails or Letterboxes! In fact we have
more Event Boxes then anyone can find in one day! (Yes... thats a
dare)

Nick Danger will lead a special "Death of a Salesman" Clue game
using those familiar grocery store icons you know and love.

Plus there are BEADS, Kiddie Events and some other special games
with great prizes...

Not into games or Mystery? Just want add 50 plus FINDS to your PFX
count? If you are a real hardcore inky fingered hiker and dont want
to hang in the pavilion to exchange or play games... There is a very
special prize for the person that collects the most event stamps in
their log by 4:00PM... remember Nobody will have any clues before
the event begins. (Even though they are close, we doubt anyone will
get them all Saturday!) Check in with Gallant Rogue for further info
on this contest!

It's NOT TOO LATE to make your travel plans !
