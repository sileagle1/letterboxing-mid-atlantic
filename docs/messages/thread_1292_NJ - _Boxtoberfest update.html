NJ - 'Boxtoberfest update

From: letterboxingbee | Date: 2005-09-12 03:28:46 UTC
Hey gang,

Just a reminder that this Thursday will be 1 month to the gathering.

There are some electrical outlets at the pavillion, should you choose
to bring a crock pot.

There are bathrooms right at the pavillion.

Pets are allowed at the park on the condition that they are kept off
all the cemented walking/runnning/track paths. Grassy areas are fine.
And of course all "litter" left by pets must be picked up by their
owners.

Anyone wishing to take the logbook making class, please let me know in
advance.

And, as I mentioned before there will be a special stamp for anyone
who brings a "non-perishable" item that I would like to donate to the
Cherry Hill/Camden food bank. This is not an obligation, but if you do
bring something to donate you will get a special stamp that I carved
just for your special donation.

Check out the 'Boxtoberfest details at:
http://njboxtoberfestgather.blogspot.com/


Thanks and hope to see you there!
