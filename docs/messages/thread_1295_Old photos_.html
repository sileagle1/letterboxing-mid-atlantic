The Gerania/boulder photo was taken just after the rock shifting
incident. On the return trip across the Boulder Field Martini Man was
in the lead followed by wi_hiker, LBug and the older woman in
sneakers bring up the rear. I stepped on a loose rock which made an
ominous wha-whoomp sound. Before he could even turn around, Rich
called over his shoulder,"Are you all right?!" Martini Man, watching

high pitched scream, she's fine"...or something like that which I
found very amusing.
