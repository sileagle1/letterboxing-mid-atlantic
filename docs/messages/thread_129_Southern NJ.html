Wow! Thats what, 4 or 5 of us now? Well partner, this state's getting
too small for letterboxers. Actually, I have 1 more stop and grab in
the area, a 20-30 minute drive and a mile(?) walk for 2 boxes. After
that the drive(usually) or the walk gets longer. I'm not complaining,
but it means that I have to have more time, 4 or more hours to pick
up a box or 2.

Here's an idea : I'll put out a group of letterboxes with really
cheap on sale stamps (like Halloween and Christmas) in dollar store
plasticware and 'plant' them in places like shopping mall bathrooms.

There's a history there. When I lived in NC my husband went on alot
of business trips. At a trade show, he had bought a 5 inch long solid
plastic cylindrical key chain for me (personal defense item). Airport
security would never allow me to go to a gate with this in my
possesion. Before seeing him off, I would go into the ladies room and
put it on a ledge over the mirror and retrieve it later. I'd probably
be arrested for commiting a terrotistic act now.

It will take quite some time to reach 100 finds.
