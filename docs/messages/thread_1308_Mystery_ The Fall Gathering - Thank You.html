Mystery! The Fall Gathering - Thank You

From: Poison Ivy | Date: 2005-09-19 13:57:13 UTC
West Viginia was also there, represented by Poison Ivy and Yuri Nahl.
Team A.D.D. (also from WV) couldn't make it at the last minute.

Anyhoo, Great Time!

I was quite sore and with blistered when I got home. (Whoever planned
my day to include a 10 hour forced march should be fired!)

Night boxing at 5:30 a.m. was quite an experience! Thanks Shell for
meeting me so early (and actually pointing out where the trail was!)
I saw a couple pairs of glowing eyes, heard some rustling noises -
and I walked through MOST of the cobwebs stretched across the path.
(Clearing the way for boxers to come.) The moon was quite large and
low in the morning sky when I started, and the sun was a glorious
orange ball nestled on the horizon during the last stretch of my
drive home.

I wish I hadn't been so obsessed, because I sat at the pavilion for
only a little bit at lunch (I had the Spam, thank you) and most folks
had cleared out by 5 when I got back, so I didn't get to chat much
with anyone.

Dead Duck Chuck and Tempus Fugit, next time we REALLY need beer - AND
it's on me in gratitude for the help with that one particular stamp.
(We did go back and get it.)

I'm not sure WHO the couple was ahead of us on the Kane's Creek
Mystery - BUT THANK YOU! (We'd catch up on them every now and then
and they'd send us back in the other direction, because we just
walked past the box.)

The Beads, the boxes, the people, the prizes, the clues, the
fun...thank you all for organizing this event.

Now, someone please just organize an event in a bar that requires
sitting.

Or is that my job?
