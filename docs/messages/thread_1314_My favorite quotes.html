From: Hikers_n_ Hounds | Date: 2005-09-20 00:02:33 UTC
A couple of funny quotes from the weekend...
 
"...but she's GREAT in the woods!" Disc Lava talking about Schizokeet.
 
Now, for the next quote you have to know Sowsewbizzy, aka Judy B. Judy is the female version of Reverend Spooner. A man known for twisting his english so badly he often made up new words and phrases that are simply hilarious. I have dubbed Judys brand of English "Judyism" and am getting pretty good at translating. Here's an example:
 
Judy - "There! Now it looks like the Bennington colors"
Schizokeet or Penguin Patrol - "Bennington Colors?"
Judy - "Yeah, you know the ads about all of the colors, the Colors of Bennington"
H&H - "Do you mean Benetton, the United Colors of Benetton?"
Judy - "No, no, no Benetton is that restaurant"
H&H - "Restaurant? Do you mean Bennigans?"
Judy - "No, isn't that Bennington?"
 
...and so it goes.
 
What a great weekend. Thanks again to all.
