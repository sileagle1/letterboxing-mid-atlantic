After her presentation, b#2's first grade teacher made a beeline for
us... to talk about letterboxing. Turns out that he had given her
(and the rest of the class, who she said were a little bewildered by
it all) an entire presentation on the ins and outs of the hobby. She
checked with all her friends, who had never heard of it, but when she
went to the website (address supplied by said b#2) she was amazed to
see it all there, just as he described. He even brought in photos of
himself letterboxing for his "what I did over the summer" poster.

Wow, the power of a young convert.
