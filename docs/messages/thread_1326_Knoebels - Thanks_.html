A Perfect Weekend.

From: Jan | Date: 2005-09-27 23:59:36 UTC
I suppose it's better late than never to say thanks, Nathan, for a
great gathering. I just got home from a perfect four day weekend and
am basking in the glow. Started out with a wonderful, wonderful time
at Knoebels- from camping there Friday night and practicing with the
neat event stamp to watching everyone emerge from the Phoenix a little
wobbly legged to meeting so many new people. I will be posting
pictures tomorrow night of the fun that we all had.
Then I left Knoebels for a day with my grandson who is an up and
coming letterboxer himself. All he needs to do is learn to walk and
we will be on the trail together.
Monday, I went to the Central Pennsylvania area, an area that I feel
is the most beautiful in the state. Barney, leader of Team Little
Dog, and I camped at Poe Paddy State Park and were the only ones in
the whole park in a driving rain. In spite of the rain, we were able
to pick up 5 boxes yesterday before provisions in the form of a
delicious hot dinner were brought in by True Indigo and RGBisMe. Then
today True Indigo accompanied Team Little Dog in collecting seven more
of her boxes. Carvings by the Crayola Posse are absolutely
unbelievable - my favorite was Air Sylph which was found on an
absoloutely breathtaking vista overlooking some of the Nittany Valley.
Terri, alias True Indigo, was an absolutely wonderful hostess and as
always was the perfect letterboxing companion.
I hated to come home to face reality again but then I got to
thinking....just a few more weeks to Boxtoberfest!

Jan of Team Little Dog
