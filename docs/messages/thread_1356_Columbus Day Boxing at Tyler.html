Columbus Day Boxing at Tyler

From: wpastfarm | Date: 2005-10-11 03:07:03 UTC
Our team has been in withdrawal -- no boxing since school started 6
weeks ago! We decided to take a chance on the weather and, toting
ponchos, hit Tyler State Park for the second time. Despite all the
mud (esp. on the way to the Police box, see post above) we had nice
cool, dry weather and picked up 11 boxes, our record for one day.
Not bad for short-legged people, and I am not just talking about
myself. Rumor has it that we might have passed the 100 box mark.
Our list:

Scudder Falls Bridge (rest stop open again, log book full)
VNV Nation
Radiohead (mistakenly labeled as "Rhinohead" in our logbook by B#1 --
he thought Radiohead made no sense, as if Rhinohead does)
MukLuk Box #2
The Police
Dr. Who #2
Texas Flood SRV
The Beatles
Across the Irish Sea
Steely Dan
Nine Inch Nails (our second attempt, hurray!)

I had to drag the rest of my team, kicking and screaming, out of the
park as they wanted to go to the other side and try for the rest.
But those, along with the Hop Devil (which we Just Could Not Find),
will have to wait for another day.

- the B's
