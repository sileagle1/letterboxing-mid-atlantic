HnH --

When you hit "reply" when you are reading a message online (this
doesn't work if you are on digest or you get individual e-mails), the
top box above the message box says

To: lbma@yahoogroups.com

Next to that address, there is a little arrow directly on the right. 
If you click on that, then it drops down to reveal three addresses:
the group, then the person whose post you are replying to, then the
list owner.  Who knew that you could CHOOSE who you wanted the
message to go to?  Hope this helps.
