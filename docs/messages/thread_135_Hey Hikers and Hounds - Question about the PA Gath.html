The park is divided, but the Delaware Canal Trail connects the two sections, or you can simply drive to the lower section.  The pavilion is in the upper section, as are the current letterboxes in the park.  There are more letterboxes near Upper Black Eddy where the campground is.

Good answer Nathan! Yes, the park is pretty darn big. Just to round out all of the discussions on the location Saturday
morning. For those of you heading south from New Hope along River
Road (route 32), there are two Bowman Hill areas. You want the first

like a fun day!
