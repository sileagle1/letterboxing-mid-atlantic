I just got home from spending the weekend with Lil Milkmonster and Out
of Sight - a weekend which started with the Boxtoberfest, and the
three of us would also like to thank Letterboxingbee and her hubby for
a really nice day. It was a bit windy which provided a little
challenge but otherwise the weather was perfect after a really gloomy
week. Bev's logmaking class was invaluable to me, I have always
wanted to learn how to make a logbook that was more than a bunch of
index cards and never would have figured it out myself. I met some
new boxers which is always a lot of fun and said hello to a lot of old
friends. A special thanks again to Rob for rescuing Out of Sight
again this year - preventing her from being stung by a bee in her soda
can. My one regret was that I didn't get a chance to find any of the
planted boxes, traveling with a baby also presents its own challenges
but we did have a wonderful time.
Thanks again,

From: letterboxingbee | Date: 2005-10-17 12:01:06 UTC
Each year this gathering gets better and better!!


I had such a great time meeting all the new boxers and saying hello to
the ones I've met before. The only down part was the winds. My
husband even said he had a good time talking with everyone. Those of
you who spoke with my sister and her family as they stopped by said
that the ENTIRE group was very very nice to her and her family. I
told her "Letterboxers are the BEST people in the world!" and she
whole heartely agreed. I enjoyed teaching the logbook class - I only
wished I could have spent more time 'explaining' things better. But
at least those who took the class can get a better understanding of
how to sew signatures together.


I will be posting the many pictures I took later today. A special
THANKS to everyone who hung around and helped clean up. And a little
thank you to "Stalking Wolf" who kept us late ones entertained for
some time. At the very end we had some bad news that a very very dear
friend of the family had passed away during the day and we left to go
console the family. In doing so, I left 2 of the event boxes on the
table. A BIG THANK YOU to "The Pirate Queen" for picking up the boxes
for me.


Thanks again to everyone for coming to the gathering. And those that
made a donation to the food drive MANY people will thank you. There
sure was a lot of food collected. I will be dropping the food off
this week to the food bank.


I had a GREAT time - now........ to make plans for next years
.....hmmmmmm...........



--Bev
