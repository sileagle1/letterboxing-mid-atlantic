From: wpastfarm | Date: 2005-10-17 00:35:31 UTC
We were so sorry to have to miss letterboxing bee's gathering,
practically in our own backyard. We were away for the weekend in the
beautiful but very soggy Hudson Valley attending the HUGE sheep and
wool festival. (Our name isn't "farm" for nothing...) We managed to
get in a little 'boxing in between sheep and wool events, but
unfortunately it was raining so darn hard on Thurs and Fri, our prime
boxing days, that we couldn't do as much as we would have liked.

We plan to do more when we head up there again next year. We
understand the layout of the area better so we can plan our trip out,
plus it looks like Talking Turtle will have provided us with an
entirely new series by then... One of our regrets was having to miss
the hike at Poet's Walk. It started to pour just after we finished
our lunch at the Pavilion and we were forced to beat a retreat to the
car. We loved what we could see and could only imagine the beauty of
the rest, which unfortunately was wreathed in fog while we were there.
