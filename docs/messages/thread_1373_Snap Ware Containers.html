I was wandering around The Container Store the other day (big surprise-ha!) and I found a container just like Lock&Lock. It's called Snap Ware, works like the Lock&Lock and seems to be just as waterproof.  You can order online at https://www.snapware.com/
 
The ones I got are about 3 & 3/4" square and a little less than 2" deep, not including lid.
 
They are supposed to be available at Target and they have a store locator.
