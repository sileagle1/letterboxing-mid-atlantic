Hi all,
I hope to have the last of the "Jack" series placed today. The series
is made up of six boxes as follows: "Shadow in the fog","Lurking","He's
watching","Jack Attack"," Montague John Druitt", and "Jack". There is
no specific order to the boxes, they were placed to have people enjoy a
day in Leesburg. Old Leesburg offers wonderful old streets with nice
shops, great restaurants, Abundant history, and haunted tours year
round. The boxes are for Halloween, but will stay permanent unless
problems start with boxes going missing. I am trying to get two more
boxes planted for halloween also, but not sure this will happen. Happy
Halloween to all!
Safe trails,
Koyote
