Ct. Clues pulled from LBNA list

From: theendgang | Date: 2005-11-02 16:20:56 UTC
Hi all,
I post this here for those that may be heading to CT. in the future.
I had Mark Pepe post this to the other lists involved. For those not
aware; a newspaper article about a series in the town included clues to
boxes. I am from this town and, unfortunately, feel that vandalism may
occur as a result. It may not, but I am not willing to take the risk.
Sorry if this inconveniences anyone from this group. The post was as
follows:

With the recent article regarding letterboxing in Winsted Ct., I have
decided to remove my clues from the LBNA list. I am concerned that with
clues posted easily in the article, that vandalism may occur and
possibly spill over into researching clues to do so with other boxes. I
realize that this may not be perceived as the best thing to do or as
a "Knee jerk" reaction. So be it. My feeling is as is and I will not
put my boxes or local wilderness areas at risk. I hope I am wrong, I am
from Winsted and hope better of it.
The clues to those boxes will be by WOM. I can also be contacted on
the LBMA group.
Again, I hope I am wrong about what this article will do. I'm sorry
for any inconvenience. The Four Directions clues will remain in place
temporarily, but will likely be effected as well.
I bid you all safe trails. Koyote
