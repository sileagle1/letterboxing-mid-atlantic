From: letterboxingbee | Date: 2005-11-02 23:29:13 UTC
The Monday following the gathering my husband took all 50+ lbs of
donated non-perishables to the Camden County food bank in Pennsauken.
My husband explained what letterboxing was to the lady who seemed
very intriged (sp?) to find out more about it. She said she was going
to mail a receipt or record of the donation and I was going to post it
for you all but so far it has not arrived yet. I was also going to
post all the pictures I took to my blog but haven't quite "mastered"
the 'blog thing' yet.

Thanks again to everyone who came out to Boxtoberfest!! And a bigger
thank you to everyone who made a donation to the food drive. We all
donated more than 50 pounds of goods. Thank you!! Thank you!! Thank
you!!
