I guess this is a good time to introduce myself. I've actually been
lurking for a few weeks now, but haven't had anything to say. :)

We live in SE PA and letterboxing is our new family hobby. I have 3
full-time partners-in-crime, my children who are 12,10,&6, and one
part-time partner, my husband. My husband was kind of ho-hum about
the whole thing until he went with us on his first hunt. We went up
to Nolde Forest State Park for Memorial Day where we only found the
first box (Double Seven). What a challenge! Anyway, he was more
excited than the rest of us when we finally found it and now he's
hooked.

We were in Green Lane today! That's what drove me out of
hibernation. I didn't even know where that was until yesterday and I
didn't even think to double check for boxes. We're going camping up
there next weekend at Boulder Woods Campground and I'm sure everyone
will by excited when I tell them how many boxes are around there.
