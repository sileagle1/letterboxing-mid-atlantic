I have to remind myself not to get my hopes up whenever searching for
any of the old boxes by Martian Maggot. She picked some great
locations, and sometimes you will find one, but most of the time
everything has changed and the clues are no longer helpful or
applicable. I see that the Cahillys, the von der Insels, SpringChick,
and Warcat76 have recently attempted MartianMaggot boxes.

Anyway, I tried and failed to find either "Somewhere in New Jersey"
box, the first at Edison Tower in Menlow Park... the clue says the
keeper of the tower knows the location of this box, but the tower is
closed.

And the second at the "War of the Worlds" at Van Nest Park near
Plainsboro -- the clues no longer make any sense.

The latest find recorded for these boxes was in 2002.
