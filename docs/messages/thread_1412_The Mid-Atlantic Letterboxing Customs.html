From: Mike Brooks | Date: 2005-11-11 19:35:04 UTC
I doubt it's a regional trait. Between planters planting containers too small to accomodate an inkpad and others just choosing not to include one, I never presume that a letterbox has ink supplied. To me, it's always a bonus when someone provides their ink for me to use.

Lately, a portion of stamps have had so much residue, I was able to stamp the image into my journal with the ink that was leftover from the prior visitor. Not even a spit shine or wiping a stamp clean on the back of your clue, let alone the other cleaning tips that have been bandied about on various forums. However, that's a rant for a later time.
