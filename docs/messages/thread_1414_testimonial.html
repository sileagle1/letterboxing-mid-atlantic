testimonial

From: Suzanne Coe | Date: 2005-11-11 23:52:55 UTC
Dear Letterboxing Friends,
 
Many of you have seen the information Lightnin Bug forwarded to us from Mr. John Pujeh.  His claims for increasing one's PFX count are almost unbelievable, and the thought in your minds must be--as it was in mine: "Does it work???"   Well, friends, I am here today to tell you the truth--yes, it DOES!  My letterboxing companion and I put Mr. Pujeh's system to the test this very afternoon, and while we were still following his directions, BOTH of our  PFX counts INCREASED by AT LEAST one!!  I could not believe my eyes, and yet, there it was.  You can only imagine my joy and excitement!!!  I decided to relate my experience to you in hopes of reassuring anyone who might be sceptical of Mr. Pujeh's directions.  If you follow them, you will indeed be rewarded!!!!!
 
Very truly yours,
 
Sheba
