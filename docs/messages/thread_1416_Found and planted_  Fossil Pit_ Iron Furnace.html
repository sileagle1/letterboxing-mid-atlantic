Hello,

Just a quick note: Fossil Pit is safe, sound, and well covered now.
Nice new logbook, too. Camp Roosevelt is closed for the season now,
but you can still park by the gate and walk in. Or at least that is
what we did.

At the other end of Fort Valley, the Iron Furnace is now waiting for
a
visitor who wants to spend a nice quiet day hiking or camping in the
mountains. At one time, there were apparently 19 pig iron furnaces
in
the valley. Find the Iron Furnace to see one--at least my best
representation of one.

If you need more boxes to lure you into the valley, take the road
west at the Fort Valley post office, up to the Woodstock Tower and
Seven Bends boxes. Down the other side into Woodstock, and the
Shenandoah Valley. Go north on 11 to pick up The Table, and a few of
Wordfried's letterboxes.

Lunch at Jalisco's in Front Royal, and go home happy.

Thanks,
