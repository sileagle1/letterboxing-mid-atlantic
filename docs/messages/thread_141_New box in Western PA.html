From: Lady Hydrangea Prisspott nee Hedge | Date: 2004-06-14 15:29:27 UTC
Dearest Ms deLis,

I was both delighted and shamed to hear of your new plant in western
PA. I grew up in that area and have a place on the Yough near
Confluence (that little town near where you started your kayak trip
11 miles upstream from Ohiopyle). I'm glad you enjoyed the area, I
love it there. Which campground did you stay in? BTW, this year is
by far the most rain I'v ever seen there. We needed it because we're
coming off a 3 year drought but we haven't had a weekend without
rain yet this year and we've seen something that I've never seen in
those mountains in 40 years; mudslides. I'm also shamed because I've
been meaning for years to plant some boxes up there as I know the
area intimately (I used to skip school and go up there for the day).
There are sooooo many great hiding spots up there. I will admit that
selfishness may have fueled my laziness as the area has grown
exponentially in tourism since I was a kid. It used to be that only
locals knew about the place but whitewater has taken off and they
keep expanding the roads and building bigger parking lots. The last
expansion of the roads really spoiled a lot of the natural beauty,
they removed half of a mountain for part of it but I guess that's
the idiot highway engineers fault not the tourists. Next time you'll
have to try the lower yough, the middle is kind of a kiddie ride and
if you really want thrills plan an Upper Yough trip. If you get back
to the area contact me and I'll give you some more tips for local
sights and local LB sites.
LP
