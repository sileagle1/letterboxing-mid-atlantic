The ADL Letterbox Clue has a secret. You find the letterbox.... then
you find a "special spot" described in the clue. The log had dozens

found an old AOL homepage with a dozen LB that are all five years
old. Long Lost Buried Treasure!

I love finding the "old" boxes. Their longevity amazes me. I started
letterboxing in the summer of 2002 (as did many DC area folks who
read an article in the Washington Post). Ten out of the first 20
boxes I found were planted by Squirrel and all had hand-carved
stamps. She must have been the only active Maryland placer up to
that time. I know she moved to a neighboring state. I haven't seen
her since the Riverbend (VA) Gather in May 2003 and hope she is well.

Darn you, Gallant Rogue. You have drawn me out of hermit-dom and I
guess now I have to respond to the post above concerning the state
of boxing in the state of Maryland!
