Tyler Update - Get Ready for the Gather

From: Lightnin Bug | Date: 2005-11-28 01:26:35 UTC
Hi All,

Star Wars Lego and I did some Tyler SP finishing today. That way we
have more time to hang with the crew at the gathering next week. At
a leisurely pace, we managed to find...

Steely Dan by Team Sudden Turkey - Cool Stamp with a Red Tailed Hawk
hangin' nearby
The Elvii by Team Little Dog:
TCB - A stamp after out heart!
Hound Dog - No more brambles (since August)
Blue Seude Shoes - Don't step on 'em.
Raindear's Cool Series:
Aerosmith in good shape - Excellent Carvings!
Bee Gees lying out in the open (rehid better)
Mystery Musician - out in the open, wet inside, ripped bags
Did repairs and hid him
Love Seed Mama Jump by Clueless - Very Floral Stamp, Cool SPOR
Pearl Jam Alive - Cool Dude - Awesome Album (will listen soon!)

All in all a fun day, but we were a little disappointed that some of
Raindear's excellent creations were left out in the open (several
feet away from trees and such). These may be party spots (I am
hoping) and the exposure was hopefully not due to careless boxers.
Food for thought.

Thanks to all of the carvers and placers.

LB
