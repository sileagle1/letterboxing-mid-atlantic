BTW, I did some boxing today!!!

From: Nathan Brown | Date: 2005-12-08 04:47:18 UTC
Well, all this talk about WOM and secret boxes didn't really matter today because when I got to Skyline Drive, I found it closed anyway.  The road was too icy they said.  Oh well.  I still managed to get nine boxes on the way down to Pigeon Forge, TN.  All were drive-bys, some very cool, others not so cool.

Traveling by Donkey - Which was hidden under a bush in a cemetary
WV Farm HHH - I had no HH, so did not take on, but got the nice box image
Roundhouse - a large and very cool stamp
The Civil War - Action at Rutherford Farm - Excellent stamp in a neat spot, and I was first finder
Iron Furnace - I more or less stumbed on this one, nice box
P-U Letterbox - This is a combo box, so it had all these trinkets in it, but that is made up for by the location, you have to cross a long, high, suspended footbridge (http://tinyurl.com/7nfxx) to get to it, very cool.  I also saw an owl (http://tinyurl.com/8s9o8)
The Table - hidden in a nice community park
Song of the Robin - Nice stamp, planted right next to an US Forest Service office, now that is guts
Heron at Dawn - Next to a large covered bridge (http://tinyurl.com/7or4f), this box was cracked and filled with water, so I replaced the box and bags

I also ventured down Rt 7 to find The Civil War - Battle of Cool Spring but was unable to find it.  I know i have the right location, but no box :-(
