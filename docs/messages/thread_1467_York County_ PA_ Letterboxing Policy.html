York County, PA, Letterboxing Policy

From: Jan | Date: 2005-12-13 09:57:10 UTC
Hello everyone,
Pink Panther and Black Swan have been working behind the scenes in York County, Pennsylvania, to get some letterboxes placed in a local county park.  I had a box in the same park which was confiscated shortly after I placed it last spring.  This team not only worked for over three months trying to contact the park superintendent to find out the policy to plant their own boxes but they actually encouraged the park system to develop a plan for letterbox planting.  They have asked me to post it here so I have added the policy to the files section.
Thanks to this team for coming up with the policy and for also
verifying that my box is still in the hands of the park officials and waiting to be replanted in the park.

Jan of Team Little Dog
