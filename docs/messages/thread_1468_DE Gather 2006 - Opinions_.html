From: Judy B | Date: 2005-12-13 21:19:26 UTC
Some folks have heard me talk about my intentions for an October 2006
gather at Killens Pond State Park, Kent County DE.

Seems, I cannot reserve one of the three pavilions until Jan 3 
and "walk-ins" are handled first. She said they have been known to
line up at 4AM, and the office opens at 7AM this one day of the year
to handle the rush.

My dilemma is I live in VA - 3hrs drive - and the summer house has
been winterized so I can't stay there.

So I am seeking alternate dates for a gather called "It's Just a
Game" that would be best handled if folks can box on Sat and come to
the gather on Sun. Details are drafted and a web site will go up
AFTER I get a pavilion booked.

FIRST CHOICE is Sun Oct 8 (Mon is a fed holiday) (Full Moon)
SECOND is Sun Sept 3 (Mon is Labor day) availability highly unlikely
THIRD is Sun Sept 10 (Full Moon)

I was trying to schedule it for after the summer crowds and under a
full moon for those nite boxers.

Would Sun Nov 5 (Full Moon) be too late in the year - especially for
anyone wanting to camp there?

Is there anyone in DE who would like to be my representative to go to
Killens Pond at 7AM on Jan 3rd?
email me off list for further discussion (sowbiz @ yahoo . com)
