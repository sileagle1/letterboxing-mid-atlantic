Jolly G Man Letterboxes

From: gallant_rogue | Date: 2005-12-18 21:20:14 UTC
Cover images for two old Jolly G Man Letterboxes are in the Photo
Album area. The more senior Letterboxers from around the country will
remember this trail name from several years ago. The Jolly G Man (now
retired) was one of the original Letterboxers in the US. He placed 63
letterboxes in nine different states. I am adopting and restoring five
of his boxes here in Northern Virginia. I'm returning the original
logs to Jolly G Man but before I do I will scan the log cover and
stamp images.

Today, Koyote and I, went and did "difficult run"...2 boxes placed by
Jolly-G-Man years ago, and abandonded by the placer..Both boxes had no

We pulled his old full books, to send to him..and need to go back and
add a new book to "dream"

Great fun w/ the Koyote, even though he was determined to break an
ankle of the way back.
