From: catbead1 | Date: 2004-06-17 01:04:35 UTC
Hi Elizabeth,

Our Michael's has carving supplies near calligraphy and paper
supplies, not with rubber stamps and ink pads. They also sell
different kinds of carving material, the Speedball white-ish stuff is
crumbly and worthless (sorry if you already know this - just want to
give warning), and the pink stuff I hear is nice to carve.

Online MisterArt.com sells MasterCarve, both thick and thin, here's
the link:
http://www.misterart.com/store/view/001/group_id/7882/STAEDTLER-
you may need to cut and paste if the link is split over 2 lines.


Hope this is helpful,
