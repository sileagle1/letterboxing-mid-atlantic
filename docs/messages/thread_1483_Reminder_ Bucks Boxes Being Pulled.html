Thanks for the reminder and for a great series planted!

We did this series yesterday and greatly enjoyed the whole package:
grounds, factory (no one was working but still neat to see the
layout), displays, clues, logbook, etc. We had to leave the last one

The Lame-O Loam Foam Lone Star Letterbox
Keith House
Ghostly Gathering (2 of 3; we could not find #1)

was fearsome.
