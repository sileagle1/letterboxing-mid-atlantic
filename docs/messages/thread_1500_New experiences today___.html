went for an EARLY morning hike, and to get "Mr Toady's
Letterbox"..and had a few new experiences.

during the hike, I came across a deer sleeping, on the edge of the
trail..When i got with-in 5 feet, it jumped up, and took off...we were
both startled, then i continue on, find the letterbox, which is a
LARGE coffee can, painted by girl scouts. I open it up, and there is a
log book, but no stamp...and i was the first finder

I am contacting them, but not sure how serious the girl scout troop
will be to follow up and add a stamp...If not, ill carve something for
it!
