From: Mike Brooks | Date: 2004-06-19 09:51:26 UTC
Two of the five letterboxes listed in this park are on a trail that is still
posted as closed. From the state park website:

"Taskinas Creek Trail remains closed because of damage caused last year by
Hurricane Isabel. It's likely to remain closed for the rest of 2004 as the
trail suffered extensive bridge and boardwalk damage."

That affects Taskinas Creek and Cardinals Rule.

The Mattaponi Trail was just reopened in the last couple of weeks. The last
time I looked for an update, it was on the closed list for a damaged
boardwalk. Apparently, that's now fixed. Mattaponi Trail LB was plundered
long before the storm and never replaced, although it would be amusing to
know if the tree itself (big one on the edge of the bluff overlooking the
river) survived the storm. LadyBug's Pupa was there right before the storm.
You'll likely be the first letterboxer to get back there to see if it still
there. One good note is that a geocache in that general area has already
been found to have survived the storm, so unless a tree fell on the
letterbox itself, it should still be there. The same should apply to Fossil.

The park itself was completely closed from the storm until March 1. They had
a lot of fallen trees that needed to be cleaned. Be ready for anything.

Mike
