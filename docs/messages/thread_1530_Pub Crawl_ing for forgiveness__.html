We are so sorry we didn't make your event Poison Ivy! Both because
it's bad manners to RSVP and bag, and also sorry for purely selfish
reasons - it looks like it was a BLAST! Spidey and I were SOOOO
excited to go, but apparently the fates were firmly aligned against
us - as proof, witness our evening...And I am not kidding about ANY
of this....!

First, Spidey had to work late, but he tore home and i jumped into
the car while it was still rolling trying to make the 5pm start. We
were tearing westward, and things were going southward - This is when
the gailforce winds and hail started. We have a little sporty thing
that apparently isn't especially aerodynamic from the sides - who
knew? Undetered, Spidey fought valiantly to keep the car on at least
one of the lanes on 7 - he was doing pretty good, when we heard the
ominous thumping from the front left tire. FLAT! We pulled over,
luckily, in a lit parking lot to change the tire, happy to see that
we could at least get a little shelter from the wind - aaaaaand
discovered that the spare was flat too. Of course. But no worries!
There is a gas station just 2 blocks away - if we can just limp there
everything will be all good. Yay! We made it! Except.... their Air
Pump is broken. (I kid you not) We limp another 2 miles down the road
flashers flashing, traffic squealing around us, to the next station,
where blessedly, the air pump works. Small mercies! This would be
around 7:15pm - and we are 30 minutes away from Leesburg on a highly
questionable spare. We face the reality that the fates are simply NOT
going to allow us to attend this event. We'd best just throw in the
towel, before the fates pull out the REALLY big guns. We were so
bummed to miss the event, we decide, to cheer ourselves up, we'd get
a nice dinner at a local restaurant. Just when we are sitting down,
with a sigh of relief that the nightmare is over - the place is
plunged into darkness by a power outage!!! Some days ya just can't
win... ;-)

It's probably just as well we didn't make it - with the kind of luck
we had that night, the bars probably would have run out of beer or
something!

Thanks for planning the event, even though we missed it - next time
for sure! :-)
