From: thedoubtfulguests | Date: 2006-01-17 18:35:17 UTC
Kudos to Poison Ivy for all the chutzpah it took to set this up,
especially after reading his report. "What letterbox?" said half the
bartenders when he went to set up.

He made a great choice for a final destination. The King's Court was
casual enough and large enough to be invaded by a gang of
letterboxers.

It was fabulous that so many people came from out of town: DE, PA, NJ,
NY, MA, Willowdale, Oz, and that faraway place Virginians seldom
visit ... Maryland. It was great to meet the Walkers, Craftymouse and
Dandelion, Yuri Nahl, and Geckospot, and to see Team Green
Dragon, "meet" the Reaper, and see Wink and Tarheel again. And all the
usual local suspects. Hikers & Hounds and MizScarlet, you're locals
now.

I stood dumbstruck watching the Brew Hounds dance for their worm. At
least I think that's what they were doing. I was awed by their
fantastic, fun, row of team stamps at the bar. MisScarlet has an ever
increasing wicked sense of humor.

What a great bunch of stamps. And I only got the last two places and
some bonuses. I am amazed at the variety of establishments.

Congrats to the win by Dead Duck Chuck and Tempus Fugit. The scavenger
hunt looked challenging, inspired and wicked. Why am I not surprised
they won?

I want to hear more from the people who did the whole crawl. Or at
least what you can remember. He sent you to a biker bar!?

Scarab of the Doubtful Guests

From: Hikers_n_ Hounds | Date: 2006-01-19 01:04:05 UTC
Ok, so here's the deal. I come to these things to have FUN. I am not at all competitive, just like to clown around and laugh. Then I hook up with sowsewbizzy and next thing I know it's a duel to the death with the other teams. We gotta win! We gotta answer all the questions! We gotta get a condom!! (It was part of the scavenger hunt, not for fun after the crawl unfortunately). So after 2 or 3 wines and Irish coffee and I think a beer at that point, and checking men's room in every tavern with no luck I was getting desperate. My eyes glaze over and I start mumbling "must find a condom balloon, must find Baskin Robbins Spoon, must find drink umbrella or face the wrath of Sowsewbizzy...". So there are these three dude's sittin' at the bar at Balls Bluff Tavern and I grab Miz Scarlet and say "C'mon, we're gettin' a condom". So we approach these 20 something pimply faced boys and say something like "Do any of you guys have a condom?". And they look at us and you can see the terror in their eyes as they shake there heads back and forth and say "No, no ma'am, no condoms here". You know what they're really thinking is "Ack! For the love of god go away you horny old broad!!!  I want my Mommy!!". (I'm pretty sure they were looking at Miz Scarlet just about then.) Anywho, we explain we're in a scavenger hunt and need the condom to blow up into a ballon so we can win. Well, nervous laughter breaks out among the young pups and they say no, they really don't have any. Beads of sweat pop out on the farheads and they are sighing deeply in relief...or perhaps remorse? I was pretty stylin' in my Greatful Dead red, white and blue tie dye, if I do say so myself!
