Incident at Downtown Saloon

From: gallant_rogue | Date: 2006-01-17 23:32:06 UTC
Despite the allegations of stamp pad ink, my blue nose tip came from
the chalk at the table in the "biker bar". After certain unnamed
crawlers stole the chalk cubes... and would not share even a nose
rub, I walked (ok ... staggered! ) from the chalkless "King's Balls"
back up the street to the Downtown Saloon where the billiard action
was hot. By the way, as I had broken my own rule by drinking in EVERY
bar... I was feeling no pain. Unfortunately, while I was very
handsome, the invisibility was not working. (Snowflakes on my
shoulders?) Seeing the joint was jumping without any hint of slowing,
I waited for the slightest opportunity to chalk my beak! I watched
the players and the crowd around the table to see if there was a
pugilists in there midst. There were none. These were Suburban
Dentist Bikers. No need for my being timid. But I did not want to be
overtly ungallant and rude. And then it came... a break in the game
action! After a spectacular crowd pleasing shot on the seven ball in
the side pocket... the shooter paused to down a long swallow of
golden beverage.... as if making a show of it for the crowd. This was
my moment! To the disbelieving stares from those assembled ... I
nonchalantly approached the table, daitily picking up the cube off
the rail... quickly chalking my pointed nose as if I did it all the
time, then feeling their eyes upon me I strolled toward the front of
the bar, grinning and nodding all the way to the sidewalk.
