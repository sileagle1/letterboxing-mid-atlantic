From: Lightnin Bug | Date: 2006-01-24 11:50:57 UTC
The Hairy Legged Letterboxers is a loose bunch of us that go out to
hunt Mapsurfer boxes (www.mapsurfer.com), considered the most
difficult letterboxes to figure out and find. We "hairy leg it" when
we are looking for the more difficult mystery boxes out there as it
often takes a group effort to figure things out and find. Our crew
hails from DE, MD, NJ, PA, RI, WI (alphabetical so as not to offend).

We recently had a couple of search and stamp missions that were
successful.

LB
