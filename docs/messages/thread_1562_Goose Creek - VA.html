Goose Creek - VA

From: Judy B | Date: 2006-01-30 00:51:43 UTC
HnH and I hit the trail this morning - going after ADG's "Goose Creek
Boxes". Unfortunately the first "Pillar of the Letterboxing
Community" was missing (at least since June 2004!). But "Rusted
Butterfly" was intact, recently rebagged, and in the most interesting
location. My guess is it use to be a pump house that carried water up
the hill -- there was a big pipe coming out of the hillside. And the
stamp was delightful. A lot of trash in some areas of the trail -
guess those rich kids from up in those McMansions got no respect!

Well, that seems to be the most I got accomplished today.
