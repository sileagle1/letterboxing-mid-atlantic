Boxes pulled in Loudoun County

From: BullDawg | Date: 2006-02-04 03:35:35 UTC
I was contacted by the park manager of Claude Moore in Loudoun VA.,
that she had basically retrieved some boxes off the LNBA site. She
was the same one that pulled the Apple Dumpling Gangs sereis of 4
(Froggy Series) a few years ago. I went today and retrieved 1 of mine
("not of the earth"), my wife's child theme box ("going on a bear
hunt") and southern lights' ("cookie monster")...We had a sit down
meeting for 20+ minutes, and things went well. I took the boxes with
me, but she wants a "park" approved box on a specific trail to promote
the less traveled areas. She would make a flyer availabe at the park
office for people to go find it. I believe me and Koyote (myself) are
gonna do something together on this...not sure

Just a heads up, if anyone plans anything in that park.
