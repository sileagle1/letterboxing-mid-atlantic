Here is our rough itinerary. Any help with easy boxes
or missing ones to avoid would be appreciated.

7/11: Drive to Washington Birthplace NM - Cattails, Jones

Fort Ward
7/12-7/14: DC area
7/15: Drive to Philadelphia - Pit Stop, Gas & Go, Carousel
Park, Brandywine Creek SP, Long Strange Trip,
Pennypack park
7/16: Tour Philadelphia & drive to Baltimore via NJ - Crow
Woods
7/17: Patterson Park, Patapsco SP, fly home

Thanks,
Silver Eagle

From: sowbiz | Date: 2004-06-23 12:38:46 UTC
Silver Eagle,

I can confirm Ft Ward (2) were there in May -- but bring your walking
stick to retrieve, they may be pushed back under the platforms to be
outta sight.
Gas-n-Go and Pit Stop both are micro boxes and the scrolls
were 'filled up' when I passed thru in early May. Contact the placer
to see if anyone has refilled. You may be of help to him/her.

I also did Mary Moore in Edison NJ (off I-95). It was great and I
was the only other stamper since it was placed in Oct 03. Just
remember a Vault is NOT a Tombstone. And bring dry socks/shoes. The
grass was unmowed and wet that morning so I got soaked to the knees.

When you say 'DC Area' does that mean VA or MD? Mary(OD) failed to
mention her "Dead Author" series in Vienna VA -- one box missing but
the rest are a real treat; as are, "Tribute to the Woods" in Fairfax
VA (also one box missing). And the "Celebration of Baseball" mystery
box is also in Fairfax (hey folks how much am I giving away when the
clues show the ZIP Code!?)and I live near by if you need a drink of
water. . . after 5PM.
Hey, if you are arriving in Balt on 7/9 did you consider driving to
the gather in Colonial Woods/Washington Crossing, north side of PA
(so about 2hr drive). Are you campers? I could borrow an extra tent
to bring up. Then you could drive back early on 7/11 (3-3.5 hr)and
resume your schedule which I see takes you back to Philly. Can you
reverse your sequence? Just a passing thought.

Judy B
sewsowbizzy . . . minding my own business, HA!
