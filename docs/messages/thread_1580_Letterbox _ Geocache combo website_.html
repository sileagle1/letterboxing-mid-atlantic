From: Mike Brooks | Date: 2006-02-13 05:24:50 UTC
Was it Boxcaching.com? For those unfamiliar, the letterboxing.org
webmaster of a couple of years ago (Wes?) changed letterboxing.org to
boxcaching.com as an April Fools Day prank. He heralded the
combination of the two hobbies, announced an annual fee for
membership, and basically drove Geocaching's site owner, Jeremy Irish,
nuts as a large number of geocachers didn't get the joke and flooded
him with questions about the merger.
