From: Hikers_n_ Hounds | Date: 2006-02-13 23:25:52 UTC
Well, we can sit around and gripe, sit around and drink, or, my favorite, sit around and gripe and drink. Or, make the best of it until our hardworking volunteers get us up and running again...again. Here's my idea. I (occasionally) get cranky at some posts that begin with "...back when I started letterboxing..." because it is often means a lecture of how great things were way back when (2002!) and how much they suck now because of a) changes b) too many new people c) too many new people making too many changes. Or maybe that's just how it appears to me. I am just cranky by nature. Anyway, I was thinking of all stress on the list over the site being down and just wanted to lighten things up.
 
So here's the thread for anyone who wants to play along until the site is up again (and then we can all try to log in and crash it again...d'oh!). With tongue planted firmly in cheek:
 
"Back when I started letterboxing, we didn't have no internet, hell we didn't even have compasses! We walked around with a forked stick until the end started shakin' and pointin' at the ground. Ya looked down, and there was a letterbox."
 
Lame, I know, so you can one-up me?
 
HnH

clear through your skull and that's the way it was and you liked it!
