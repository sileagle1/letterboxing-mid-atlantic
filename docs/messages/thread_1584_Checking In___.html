From: nattybumppolbna | Date: 2006-02-25 15:31:44 UTC
I hope we can make it. The little one is getting to the age where I think she will enjoy a
little walk in the woods. The older one has me running from karate to drawing classes to
birthday parties, so I haven't been out on the trail - until this week.

I had some free time in Atlanta this week and the nice little park down the street had 3
boxes within spitting distance of each other for a quick score. I don't know who was
poaching who, but I was able to get stamps and introduce a couple of co-workers to the
joys of 'boxing.

They were trying to be all cool and blase until it became clear from the clue where the box
was hidden and suddenly they were running ahead and moving rocks like a couple of kids.
Its fun to watch the reactions of newbies.

Then, by chance, I ended up working in a nature preserve outside of Atlanta. Lucky me.
So Dekalb County has a new letterbox - #4 in my PA Postcard Series. The nature preserve
marks its trail with cairns. I wanted to be cruel and just have the clue read: "look under
the suspicious pile of stones." But I took mercy.

I have some time coming up in Spokane and San Diego. It's funny - I only seem to be able
to 'box when I'm working.

Hope to see y'all outh there soon.

Natt-
