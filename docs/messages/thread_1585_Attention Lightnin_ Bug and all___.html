Attention Lightnin' Bug and all...

From: jsh11a@aol.com | Date: 2006-02-16 05:01:11 UTC
Well, finally! There is a new HHH in town (it's in "new"-"town," in
fact). I have been trying to get this one planted "forever," but
something has delayed me time and time again. So, today was the day!
LB, this is the box that has the gift for you in it. Look on Atlas
Ques or LbNA for the clues to "Grease Lightnin' Bug's Hitch-Hiker
Hostel" by Swallowdale. And have fun!

I have to just tell you that this was such a hoot for me to plant. It
was a very mild day, but still lots of snow in the park that hadn't
melted yet. There I was in full business attire, except that I
replaced my pumps with soccer shoes for the planting. I'm sure I
looked quite out of place in the park! It's hard to plant in a
snow-covered area, I learned! The hardest part was that there was not
the usual debris (leaves, sticks, rocks, wood chips, etc.) available to
help cover and camoflage the letterbox. I managed to find a spot that
I feel "works," though!

Enjoy!!!!!

Swallowdale :)
