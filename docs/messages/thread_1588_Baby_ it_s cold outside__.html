Baby, it's cold outside!!

From: Hikers_n_ Hounds | Date: 2006-02-20 12:29:43 UTC
But not too cold to go letterboxing! Cyclonic cracked the clues to a mystery box  and recruited a few hardy hikers to go hunting with him. Sheba, Rick in Boca, Dude the Wonder Dog and myself braved the c-c-c-cold to check out some beautiful winter scenery, and got an interesting bonus at the mystery box. And Cyclonic was right, after we were moving around for awhile, it really wasn't bad at all. After awhile we drove to Dingmans Falls and got Silent Doug's boxes there. What an absolutely beautiful place! And I was surprised at how many people were there. I am so glad we did it in the winter, that place must be packed in nice weather. Stopped and had a great meal before we headed home. A perfect day all around.
