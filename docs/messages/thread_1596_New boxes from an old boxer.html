From: trainear@comcast.net | Date: 2006-02-26 02:55:02 UTC
Hi all,
well, I pretty much dropped out of the picture for a year while trying to get re-employed.  Now that I am, I can relax a little (ha, I travel 4-5 days/week) or at least justify goofing off on the weekends.
 
So, I totally replaced the Evansburg Series today in Montgomery County in PA.
For you out of towners, this is in the greater Philly area. North and west of the city.
 
My kids had carved several Peanuts characters some time ago. I boxed them up and went out with one kid and her friend.  The trail is muddy and the course a little challenging.  Not for Hairy Leg people perhaps, but small kids might need a hand on some of the steep hills.
 
So, even if you already logged this series before, these 4 are all new.  Locations are all new.  I need to get proper documentaion into the ranger there, but go for it.
Clues are posted.
Raindear
