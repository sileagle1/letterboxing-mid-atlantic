Vacation finds

From: Marie & Bob | Date: 2006-03-01 22:19:52 UTC
Teapot & I are back from a short vacation to FL. Picked up 25 boxes
along the way. Had another 5 attempted but think they were all MIA,
still waiting to hear back from their placers. Added 3 more states and
are now up to 24. If we would only take the time and get to DL we would
be half done (state wise that is). 1 HH found in SC soon to be
released. What a pleasure boxing in 70 degree weather. Snow and time
prevented us from getting any boxes in VA. The Rusty Hinges are ready
to hit the trail soon and are scouting out some Bucks county locations.

Teapot & Eagle
