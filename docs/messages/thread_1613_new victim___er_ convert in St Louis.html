new victim...er, convert in St Louis

From: Lorie Thomas | Date: 2006-03-13 23:04:07 UTC
I just got back from 4 days in St Louis and have
turned a dear friend into a raving letterboxer.

Thursday we started w/ Carondolet Park - wet box, good
site. We took the box home to dry out and add more
paper and a baggie then returned it promptly the next
AM. Also contacted the placer.

That same evening I carved a stamp as a HH.

Friday we grabbed another friend, also a new convert
and gathered 2 from Laumeier Sculpture Park, both in
great shape. Gotta love St Louis with all their free
fun stuff!

Then off to the zoo. This box was well placed but
wetter than the first one and missing a stamp :-( We
dried it as best we could and replaced it. Gotta
contact that placer.

Saturday we attempted and did not find Steuermann,
Rayburn Park Quickie, and PASP. All 3 seemed like
questionable parks due to the amount of traffic.
Maybe we were just clueless and off track but I think
all 3 are missing. Also good experience in
disappointment for the newbie!

Finally, success w/ Mert's Dogwood and Blue Rink. For
Blue Rink we were right behind another boxer - their
first box. Someday I'll meet someone on the trail.

Then off to the art museum where we happily found
Ever-Fixed Mark - fun clues.

Total for the weekend - 1 release, 7 finds, 3 misses,
tons of fun.

geckospot & Rozebud
