They're still being sent. If your email address starts bouncing back
to Yahoo Groups too much, you'll automatically be put on "bounced"
status (but that typically affects all of your group mail, including
this one). Also, since lbox-announce is a "distribution list only,"
any reply to a list message will unsubscribe you -- so if you have an
auto-reply set up from your email address with one of those
out-of-the-office-on-vacation emails, that would get you kicked off.
You can check your membership or re-join at:
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/lbox-announce/
