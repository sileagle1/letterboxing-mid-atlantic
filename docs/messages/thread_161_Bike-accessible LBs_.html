From: Lightnin Bug | Date: 2004-06-27 22:27:14 UTC
Raindear,

You are more than welcome. The pleasure was ours. We did manage to
re-find the blue butterfly, but you may want to let folks know o
bring a stamp. We tried for th green tree frog, but got hung up in
the poison ivy zone after botching the clues (D'Oh!). Had to rinse
off at the small building on the way to the butterfly. Buzzed to the
Ladybug, but Tom was getting tired and I think we "Homered" the clues
again! /-[

All in all a fun day and we still got home in time for me to cut the
lawn and for us to go pick a bunch of blueberries up in New Tripoli.

Please let us know when you find the Pass-Set-Spike! and Rose Garden
boxes so that we can release them to the world.

Fleur - If you are looking for bikeable boxes, the Lehigh Canal
series (from the Space Travelers, if they exist - have not checked
yet) might be a good bet. Only 16 miles from Allentown to Easton,
should be no problem for somebody who did the Perkiomen trail! ;-)
Also, the Nor-Bath Letterbox/geocache hybrid near Bath is OK, if you
got a Geochache. There also appear to be some along the Delaware
Canal bike path, if you are feelign really ambitious. All of these
trails are FLAT!

You can get a ride at the Rodale Fitness park and find a letterbox
too boot! Also, Little Lehigh Parkway has 3 boxes and abotu 6 miles
of trails roundtrip. There may also be more boxes going in there in
the future.

Adios Amigos,

Lightnin' Bug
