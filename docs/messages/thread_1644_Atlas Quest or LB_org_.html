Weird me and True Indigo just had this conversation this morning.
 
I use both, I like the log features better on AQ.  I also enjoy the trip planner, which i a great feature, in addition to the ease at logging my finds and marking boxes retired (i.e. my Satellite Radio Wars series where only the bonus was retired and not the other two).
