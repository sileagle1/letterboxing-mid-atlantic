Welcome all newbies

From: Hikers_n_ Hounds | Date: 2006-03-27 23:32:19 UTC
Just in case we start getting any trickling in, just wanted to welcome everyone. I am expecting 2 to find their way here soon. While the article was being written I was in AC working a trade show. Of the hundreds of people I met, there were a few that seemed really cool and I was able to chat with a little. There was a couple from CT who had never heard of letterboxing (gasp!!!) and I haven't heard from yet, and a couple of gals from the Collegeville area who I discussed it with. I think one of them was wearing a dog pin or something to do with dogs and we started talking about dogs and I eased into letterboxing as a great thing to do with your best friend. I mentioned how much fun Dude and I have.
 
Well I got a voice mail message at work today from Sue that they went out this weekend and found some Raindeer boxes in Evansburg Park and had a great time! I called back and spoke to Nancy and pointed her to this list and encouraged them to join in the madness.
 
So hopefully they will be here soon. In the meantime, welcome all newbies. Come out of the woodwork and introduce yourselves! And welcome Sue and Nancy whenever you get here.
 
HnH
