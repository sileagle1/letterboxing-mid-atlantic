Re: [lbma] Virginia Beach Area...

From: Chile-Pepper | Date: 2006-04-02 21:53:03 UTC
That's local for us. Newport News is, depending on where you are in VB,
anywhere from 30 to 60 minutes' drive. There is a great series on the
Noland Trail at the Mariner's Museum. 5 relatively easy miles on a very
nice trail. There are also boxes in Newport News Park along another
nice trail. It's been a couple of years since we did 'em, so I don't
know their current status, but both hikes were quite pleasurable at the
time.
ChilePepper
