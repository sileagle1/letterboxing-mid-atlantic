UPDATE - As of this writing the campground has me that no one else has reserved a campsite. The sites are filling up fast! If you plant to camp out call and reserve now. Also, I have 2 extra tents that sleep 3 - 4 people if any needs to borrow one, let me know.
 
Dude and I hope to see you there!
