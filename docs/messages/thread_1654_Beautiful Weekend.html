Re: Beautiful Weekend

From: true indigo | Date: 2006-04-03 19:41:35 UTC
The lure of Bucks County called us again. We decided to focus on only the Jane Austen series.
We found the Emma, Jane Austen, Mansfield Park, Persuasion, and Pride & Prejudice boxes to
all be in great shape with stamps to match their engaging clues. Sadly, the Northanger Abby
box, although in it's hiding spot, was open and the log chewed and ruined. The stamp was
not to be found anywhere in the immediate area. We collected the remaining litter and have
dropped an e-mail to Clueless.

The locations are lovely so we were surprised at how few people have actually found many of
these boxes. This series is not to be missed.

true indigo & RGBisMe
