LBMA Rocks

From: Hikers_n_ Hounds | Date: 2006-04-14 01:03:23 UTC
Hey there fellow LBMA'ers,
 
Just wanted to drop you all a note to say thanks. Thanks for being exactly who you are. I have been so disappointed by letterboxers and lists recently that I really felt like just walking away from it all. In my humble opinion, the same 5-7 people will always try to rule this hobby, jump on anyone or anything different or new and then start singing the same old song "Oh for the good old days when it was just us and we didn't have to deal with everyone else". The funny thing is, if you look at the very first gathering movie on Mark Pepe's web site, NONE OF THOSE PEOPLE ARE THERE! The self-appointed Letterboxing Police are Johnny-come-lately's, just like the rest of us, they just came along a few years earlier. And yes, they have all worked very hard to grow this hobby and make it great, but damn it, so have we!!. The folks who were at that very first gathering, the real founders, never post nasty posts slamming anyone on any subject they want, nor do they email all of their friends off list and tell them to start mob-emailing someone they don't agree with. Nor do they contact the webmasters and try and get them involved in their little power plays and high school mentality politics. It just makes me sick. Over the last couple of years I have read post after post of people giving up on the big list because of all of this and leaving the hobby and thought "Man, they must have a pretty thin skin". Well, now I know how they feel! It's always the same folks, jumping on the dog pile, circling the wagons and using the excuse of trying to keep the hobby pure to explain themselves. 
 
Except here, on this list. I love this list because I don't think anyone thinks here they are a more important letterboxer than anyone else. And you know what, they're not!!! Each of us is equal to the other, regardless of how many years, or plants or finds you have. Whether you are the crappiest carver on the planet or the reincarnation of Michelangelo himself, no one on this list, or on any list in my opinion, is more valuable, more important than anyone else. But at least here we know that. I think we all respect each other, even when we disagree, and we realize that each and every one of us adds value to this hobby. And that's the whole thing in a nutshell. We know it's a hobby. It's not a crusade, or a cause, we're not curing cancer, feeding the poor or clothing the homeless. Those are the things to get worked up over. Not a handful of silly people on a silly list who take hunting a piece of rubber hidden inside a piece of plastic in the woods way too seriously.
 
This list is a breath of fresh air. And if it weren't for my buddies here at LBMA, I too would walk away from this hobby. If all I had as a resource was those same people trying to tell me and everyone else what to do all of the time, I'd throw up my hands and throw in the towel. The crap that goes on just turns my stomach. But you guys are so real, and so supportive of each other you make it worth hanging around.
 
You may not agree with me but you respect me enough to allow me my opinion. Thank you for that. Thanks for keeping it real, and keeping it real fun!! Please don't ever change, nor start taking yourselves or this hobby too seriously. Sorry for the rant, but I have just gotten totally fed up with the never ending nonsense and realized how lucky I am to live where I do, box with the folks I box with and have this list to use as one of my favorite tools for this (usually) fun hobby.
 
HnH
