From: Suzanne Coe | Date: 2006-04-15 20:36:26 UTC
3 days, 3 journeys....
 
Thursday morning I went to Haddonfield to walk the labyrinth.  Thanks very much to highlysung for the invitation, it was time well spent.  I enjoyed seeing her & Gwenllian again, and her stamps were lovely!
 
Friday I joined Lightnin Bug at French Creek East, where we found all of Cyclonic's 
"bunnies" in spite of the rainy weather.  The last one had a particularly cool hiding place.  We also found the Peter J McGuire Traveling Letterbox, which will be moving along shortly.
 
To finish up the week, Cyclonic, EBBebe & I took a walk on the Forgotten Road.  I've been looking forward to this one since last July when H&H and I ran out of daylight & had to give up on it half-way.  The box is definitely worth the hike (who says Mapsurfer can't carve????), plus there was the bonus of the first bald eagles I'd ever seen in the wild. 
 
Thanks again to all the placers!
 
Sheba
