From: Sara | Date: 2006-04-17 13:32:30 UTC
Like some others here, I spent Easter boxing :)

I took my parents and brother and their first letterbox outing.. and
unfortunatly.. the first three boxes we sought were naught to be
found. (They began to think I was crazy "Yea Sara.. sure they hide
tupperware in the woods..") Luckily I had several clue sheets for
boxes within Pennypack Park. We went on later to find the Mint Box,
Playmaker's Pennypack Park Box, and the Birdhouse box.

Has anyone had any luck finding the Pennypack Park Series by Joesph? I
could have swore I was in the right spot each time.. just no box..
Anyone know if they have been retired? I also had no luck finding the
Ben Franklin - Scientist box.. any one tried that one lately?

Our Welsh Terrier, Fifer, had the best time.. hes now letterboxing
hooked.. although when he was digging next to me while I was moving
leaves around.. I'm not sure he understood what we were after.

Sara
