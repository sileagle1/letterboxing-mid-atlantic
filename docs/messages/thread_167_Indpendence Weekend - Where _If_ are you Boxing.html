From: Hikers_n_ Hounds | Date: 2004-07-02 00:29:57 UTC
Well, Dude and I are heading to Washington Crossing to plant two boxes for next weekends gathering. The 2 box series is called "Big Green Monster" and it is named after an insect that you find along the East Coast. It's in two boxes because...well you'll find out next weekend! I found a dead one out hiking and thought it was pretty cool. Monday, my husband Harris will again be letterboxing with us ('ray!) and we will be hunting the Bowman's Wildflower Preserve box. This box is tough for some folks because the clues are plants and trees. Harris is a PhD botanist, so it should be a cake walk (we hope). This will be his third time letterboxing with me since January, so I guess he needs a stamp. He hiked Silent Doug's "Alder Leaf" with me and we just camped in MD and did some of the Patapsco boxes together. It's a real treat when he can come along because his weekends are all tied up with politics (he's our local Democratic chairman) but holiday weekends are usually time off from door knocking and voter registration drives so we get to hang out together like (semi) normal people. Have fun everyone and I hope to see many of you next weekend!
 
H&H
