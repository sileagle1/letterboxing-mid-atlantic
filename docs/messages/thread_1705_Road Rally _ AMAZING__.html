From: Jan | Date: 2006-05-08 10:34:15 UTC
When we finished last year's Road Rally, I thought that I could not
have as much fun at another one. The rally itself was a lot of fun
but the companionship afterwards was absolutely the best. I wasn't
sure what to expect this year, in fact, I wondered how the Rallymaster
could come up with anything even close to last year, but, WOW! he did
a terrific job. This year's hunt was more challenging - or was it
just that my team members were so intent on causing mischief that it
seemed harder?

The search really was great, Natty provided a whole new experience
this year with very, very clever hidings and clues. I still can't
believe that we photographed the "Nat Bumppo for Congress" signs but
never looked for a box around them!

But again, the gathering after we got back to the headquarters was the
big reward. We finally got to meet Pink Panther and Black Swan, and
got to visit with all of our old friends. It was hard to leave there
and come back to reality.

Thanks so much, Natty, it was a perfect day - the search, the
companionship and the weather.

Jan of Team Little Dog
