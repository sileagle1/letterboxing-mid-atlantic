Where in Philly are you staying, in the city or outside the city. When
are you going to be in town?

There are a few boxes in the city, but tons outside the city in Bucks
and Montgomery County. In Bucks County, Hikers and Hounds' series The
Brave Little Caterpiller
(http://www.letterboxing.org/BoxView.php?boxnum=11044&boxname=Kids_Series_-_The_Brave_Little_Caterpillar)
is designed for kids.

The Boxes in Pennypack Park in Philadelphia would be fine for your kid
as well. The Pennypack Park Series
(http://www.letterboxing.org/BoxView.php?boxnum=11730&boxname=Pennypack_Park_Boxes)
and the Birdhouse Letterbox
(http://www.letterboxing.org/BoxView.php?boxnum=13382&boxname=Birdhouse_Letterbox)
are all near a nice nature center.

From: Sara | Date: 2006-05-10 17:55:36 UTC
Please note that I took my family to do the pennypack park boxes,
and we found none. Joseph the placer e-mailed me back after quite
some time with this to say on April 23rd 2006:

"The Pennypack Park Boxes are gone. 1 box had the pen and stamp pad
stolen for the second time.Another of the boxes wa left open and the
contents destroyed and 1 box was taken by someone who could not
have been a boxer. I thought we had a better class of people out
there hunting our boxes, but I guess like everything else there will
be someone who has no class and resorts to this kind of behavier.
Sorry you wasted your time. Joseph"

So don't print off the clues for them, they aren't there!
