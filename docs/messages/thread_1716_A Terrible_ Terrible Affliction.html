A Terrible, Terrible Affliction

From: nattybumppolbna | Date: 2006-05-13 13:10:30 UTC
So I went back to the scene of the crime to walk Road Rally Clue # 4 to see where I went
wrong with my compass work.

I discovered a strange phenomenon which may shorten if not end my quest for Letterboxing
Champion of the World. Apparently, my body mass will cause the compass needle to move
by 20-30 degrees. The effect is most pronounced when I am facing south, but I can see the
effect to a lesser degree when facing west or easterly.

Before you ask, yes, I removed my cell phone, watch, keys and all metal except my zipper. I
also duplicated the effects (to varying degrees) with both my lensatic and baseplate compass.

Is that it for me? Am I done?

Is geocacheing all that is left to me? I'd rather knit.

Natt-
