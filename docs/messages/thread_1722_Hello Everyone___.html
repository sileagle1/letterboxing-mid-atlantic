From: nature77girl77 | Date: 2006-05-14 14:26:36 UTC
Hello, everyone! Haven't been here in quite a while, but we're back!
Some of you may remember us (NatureGirl a.k.a. Tali - blue heron
stamp - and Alexander - green train stamp) from the camping trip and
Washington Crossing Gathering back in July 2004. We've actually moved
from CT to PA now and are finally finding the time to hit the trails
and find some boxes.

Recently we've found Trexler's Tatanka in Lehigh County. We found two
of Stamp Collection - Northwest and Northeast (haven't looked for the
others just yet). We found Mothra Returns! in Bernville. At
Nescopeck Butterfly in Hazelton we picked up the Bum-Bee Hitchhiker
that had been waiting there for about a year, and we found the
Straight Arrow Hitchhiker at the Three-Eyed Fish in Wapwallopen - it
had also been waiting there for about a year!

As boxes aren't anywhere near as prolific as they were in CT, we're
making plans to put out a few ourselves. We look forward to finding
more boxes here and possibly running into some of you on the trails!
