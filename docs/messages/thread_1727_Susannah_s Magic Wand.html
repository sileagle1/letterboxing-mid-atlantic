In my own small way I am doing my part to expand the rolls of letterboxers. I got an email
from a mother who read Cyclonic's Letterboxing ad in the Inquirer and was very excited to
get her daughter started letterboxing.
Of course thje first letterbox she wanted to do was my "Harry Potter's Unicorn" in Fort
Washington. (So paying a million-five for Harry Potter licensing for that box was well worth it)
To sweeten the pot, I carved Susannah's Magic Wand Hitchiker for her first find.

The hitchiker is in one of those beach boxes (with the clamp latch and the rubber gasket) I
am interested in seeing how it holds up in the field. So if you come accross Susannah's Magic
Wand, drop me a line and let me know how the box is holding up.
