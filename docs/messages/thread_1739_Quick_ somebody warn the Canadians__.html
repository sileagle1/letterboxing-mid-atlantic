Well fellow LBMAers,
 
Six of the LBMA broads are heading up to Canada this weekend for the Rocks, Docks and Locks Gathering. Miss Moon and Jan of Team Little Dog are arriving in Canada on Friday, presumably to apologize ahead of time for the Red Hat Boxers who are driving up Friday but spending Fri. night stateside and planning to invade the Great White North Saturday AM. We are staying on a ship, a retired ice breaker named The Alexander Henry. So early, early Friday morning we cram into two cars with all our gear, tiny bladders, hot flashes and clue sheets. Sowsewbizzy has us scheduled to within an inch of our lives and the route is all mapped out. I have lobbied heavily for more frequent restroom stops but to no avail. All 6 of us will do our best to represent the best group of boxers in the US of A!!! I plan to represent all US beer drinking boxers. I promise, I'll do ya' proud!
 
So take care all and if you hear of any international incidents occuring between the US and Canada this weekend, I guarantee you it will be Judy's fault. That's my story and I'm stickin' to it...Mr. Customs Official.
