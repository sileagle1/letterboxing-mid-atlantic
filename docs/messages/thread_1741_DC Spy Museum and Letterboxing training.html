I went to the Spy Museum today. It is a highly interactive museum and
there are many tasks you have to accomplish. What is funny is that I
scored majorly high on the observation stuff. It would ask questions
like "Which places would be the four most likely drop places in the
above picture" which of course in my mind translated to "Where are the
top four places in this picture that I would hide a letterbox?" Guess
what? Thinking in terms of letterboxing got me a 100%.

Same thing with the "Name the top four suspicious people in this
picture" translates into "Which people are gonna be pitas when it
comes to rehiding a letterbox without being seen?" and again-I scored
100%. Who would have thought that letterboxing translated into Spy
training?

Just had to share the amusement. :-)
