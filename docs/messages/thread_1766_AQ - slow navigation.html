> Is anyone else having trouble navigating the Atlas Quest Website?

Alas, just the normal growing pains of a fast growing website. I've
noticed it starting to bog down a couple of weeks ago and have been
trying to figure out ways to speed up queries and fix the problem, but
it just continues to get worse.

In a nutshell, the site is being loved to death. =) I may have to
upgrade the account, but I'm still trying to find free ways of getting
the existing account to work. An upgrade would cost me another $400 or
so per year, and I'd rather use that money for selfish reasons such as
food and clothes. =)

So I'm learning about all sorts of things that might make the website
run faster, but alas, none of them have had much of an impact as of
yet. =( If I can't get to working better by the end of the month, I'll
go ahead and upgrade to a bigger and better account. But I'm trying
really, really hard to save myself the extra $400/year.

On a related note, if you try to use the site during off-peak times,
the sluggishness of the site can be greatly reduced. *nodding* Using
the site after ten o'clock at night or before eight in the morning,
you'll get better performance. Not great, perhaps, but definitely better.

-- Ryan
