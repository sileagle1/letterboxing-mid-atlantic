Howdy ya'll!

I have not been active in letterboxing for over a year now. I allowed
life to dictate what I could and could not have time for.

I am currently taking on a project as part of a course I am taking. I
decided to challenge myself to become active again in the
letterboxing community by taking on this project. But I cannot do it
alone. The project involves creating several series of boxes for some
local Girl Scout Camps. Yes these would be private boxes targeted at
this community as a way of introducint the joys of letterboxing to
girls.
What I am looking for are folks to help carve stamps, folks that
would be willing to go to a camp and help plant the boxes and write
the clues, and people that would be willing to be an "expert" in the
fall to introduce letterboxing to girls at troop meetings.

Interested? Please contact me off list as I do not read the list
often and may miss a reply here at samitchell@.... If you want
to do any one part to make this project a success, your talent and
time will be well spent.
Thank you for listening... and I look forward to getting back out on
the trails seeking boxes and running into you!
