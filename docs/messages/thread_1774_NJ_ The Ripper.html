The Ripper
Somewhere in New Jersey
Placed by Wingfoot on 06/04/06

Clues: Somewhat Easy
Terrain: Easy
Time: 15 minutes

U built it for M, or maybe it was it Y for P,
It's been so long, it's faded from memory.
From the parking lot go west,
Cross the lawn, that would be best.
Now locate the picnic table,
Find the avian's home, if you're able.
Starting from that spot,
Go 9 paces directly north, it's not a lot.
At the base of the tree, is what you seek,
Make sure no one is watching, so they don't get a peek.
The tiny stamp is in a fake piece of granite,
Take out your logbook, so you can stamp it.

If you find this letterbox please make sure to log it on
letterboxing.org or atlasquest.com as I have no way of knowing if
you found it or not unless you do.
