Well when LBMA throws a party, spelled G-A-T-H-E-R, it is bound to be
fun and not without surprises. Since HnH hosted this casual affair,
whodda thought she'd have this mysterious boxer actual come out and
be unveiled!!!

The merriment went on with visitors from all 6 LBMA states and CT.
It was a great turn out . . . even if we only had two authorized
parking spaces at the gather/camp site. So RedHat Cab was making
runs to and fro from the satellite parking for day trippers. At
least those quick to react got their $5 entrance/parking fee back!

Meeting so many new folks keep the exchanges going for some time
while the delectable chicken grilled - thanks Irene/poultry princess
and Rusty/grill master.

Sunday breakfast was an early AM affair in order to break camp.
Hopefully the Swedish Egg Coffee kept the drivers awake for the trip
home. . . I should have had a 3rd cup or more than 3 hrs sleep.

Sat Lois Lane met and hiked with us in preparation for a freelance
Games Magazine article. HnH had strict guidelines about what could
and could not be in an article. I just hope LL didn't lose her job
at the Post or get a speeding ticket when her keys were left in my
car and I was on high seeking a true rara avis. Fortunately a
Gallant Rogue happened by on his descent and retrieved my keys in
order to release LL's keys from the car. I suspect her first points
of Letterboxing will be wear proper attire and don't lock the keys in
the car. Thanks to MizScarlet LL even had a trail stamp.

Guess I better go review my pics and exchanges so names and faces are
linked. And then I'll get some posted.

Thanks Irene for a great gather - at just the right speed.
