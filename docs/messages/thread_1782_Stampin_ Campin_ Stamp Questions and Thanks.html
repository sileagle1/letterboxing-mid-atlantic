From: Judy B | Date: 2006-06-12 23:45:57 UTC
PI

Miles of Smiles is a ssb traveling box. Where I travel it
travels . . . until I leave the Pirate from Every Port in Sept, then
it gets dumped off in Norwalk CT . . . now if I can find the
Pepperidge Farm HQ in Norwalk, hmmm.
It started in Kingston ON because the gather was on the water. Fish
and Multi-Colors you can figure out for yourself. Then it went to
Wandering in Wonderland, Stamp'n'Camp'n', and next Pirate gather.
The box should be pretty well beat to a pulp by then.

Swedish Egg Coffee is a "table treat" - both in a cup and in a log.
Anything sitting on a gather table is what I call a "table treat". I
don't know if there is an official term. You'll need to query Spring
Chick.

Let me know if there is anything else I can't answer.

JudyB
