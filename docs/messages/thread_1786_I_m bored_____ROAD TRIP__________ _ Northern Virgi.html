OK...I've wanted to do it and now I'm going to. I'm bummed I missed
the stampin' and Campin' Gather!! My Father's Day wish was to take
off for the weekend and box. Wish granted. I want to hit Cacapon
State Park/West Virginia area and/or head down Shenandoah Valley
along Rt. 81. I can't leave until 7 a.m. on Sat. morning since I am
on call at the hospital until then. Winchester would basically be my
center of operations. I leave from Leesburg, Va.
So...I put it to this group to give itinerary suggestions. This is a
slash and dash idea, so nothing grossly long, like say... some of my
boxes or Bulldawg's. I have to plant a box in Upperville first thing
in the trip, then I'll head toward Winchester. Cacapon State Park is
ripe for boxes and I would like to head there, but I need campground
ideas. Shenandoah is beautiful, and I love that area. Lots of
options! I've been looking up stuff throughout the day, and thought I
would ask for input. No hotels-just a simple campsite. All ideas are
welcome.
If Bulldawg goes, it would be an opportunity to physically verify
that we are not one and the same if you wish to meet for an exchange.
Until then...the question still begs for some.
Since this is a very specific area I'm looking at, responses will
probably be best sent to me off-line if preferred. I will post my
journey after the weekend, though it is my custom not to report what
boxes I find except to the placer, unless told to do so. I wish you
all safe trails.
Koyote
