Re: Looking for those small ink stampers

From: Lightnin Bug | Date: 2006-06-15 13:28:26 UTC
The place where I get my 1" square pads is online:

http://www.scrapbooking-warehouse.com/

The manufacturer is Tsukineko:

http://store.scrapbooking-warehouse.com/tsukineko.html

1" Versacolor square pads:

http://store.scrapbooking-warehouse.com/versacolor.html

I also buy them locally at the Dick Blick that is a 15 minute walk
from home. That is where I happened to get the daubers. I also
purchased some of the refill ink for my personal stamp online so that
I would not need to purchase new stamp pads every time I ran out of
my colors.

They are all pigment, so they will not run if wet.

Hope this helps,

LB
