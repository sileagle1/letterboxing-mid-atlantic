Howdy LBMA'ers,

Just wanted to let you know that Team Lightnin' Bug expects to be
there (and not too square!!) on Saturday, July 10. We have taken a
tour of the Southland (Nashville to Western KY) and have had a chance
to exchange stamps and HH's with She Who Plants of Nashville. We

circular (created by the creative She) and one with a riddle that

maybe next time!
