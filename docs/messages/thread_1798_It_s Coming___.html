that Sangria is the PERFECT Summer drink.

Here's my recipe:

1 bottle DRY red wine (Baco Noir is lovely)
1 Orange (sliced,seeded & squeezed into mixture)) ( A juice Orange is perfect.)
1 Apple (sliced & seeded)
1 Pear (sliced & seeded)
1 Lemon (sliced,seeded & squeezed into mixture)

Here's where your personal preference come into play

1/2 cup Sugar
OR
1/2 cup Pear Juice and
1/2 cup Apple Juice

and if in a more citrus-y mood

1/2 cup Orange Juice

A variation I call Evening Sangria adds 1/2 cup Rum to the mix.

Stir and allow to set in the refrigerator overnight
Serve over ice.

Sangria is an artform. Experiment with proportions and wines. Try spices. The great
thing is you get to drink your mistakes.

> Here's my recipe:
>
> 1 bottle DRY red wine (Baco Noir is lovely)
> 1 Orange (sliced,seeded & squeezed into mixture)) ( A juice Orange
is perfect.)
> 1 Apple (sliced & seeded)
> 1 Pear (sliced & seeded)
> 1 Lemon (sliced,seeded & squeezed into mixture)
>
> Here's where your personal preference come into play
>
> 1/2 cup Sugar
> OR
> 1/2 cup Pear Juice and
> 1/2 cup Apple Juice
>
> and if in a more citrus-y mood
>
> 1/2 cup Orange Juice
>
> A variation I call Evening Sangria adds 1/2 cup Rum to the mix.
>
> Stir and allow to set in the refrigerator overnight
> Serve over ice.
>
> Sangria is an artform. Experiment with proportions and wines. Try
spices. The great
> thing is you get to drink your mistakes.
