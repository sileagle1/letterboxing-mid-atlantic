Knoebels Kampin' Gatherin' UPDATE!

From: Nathan Brown | Date: 2006-06-23 18:11:06 UTC
Gathering date has been CHANGED, and is now Saturday, September 9th.
The following is the offical announcement.

Come on out to the second annual Knoebels Gatherin', held at Knoebels
Amusement Resort in Elysburg, PA. We will be gathering at 9AM in picnic
pavilion T this year for some good times with friends old and new. At
noon you can go ride the rides, or sit back and enjoy the day.

Admission to the park is FREE, as is parking, so come just for the day,
or better yet, spend the entire weekend kampin' at the campground right
next to the park.

For campers, there will be a special nighttime letterboxing series for
you to enjoy. Reserve your site by calling 570-672-9555. The Gatherin'
Camp HQ will be at site T-15, along Texas Ave, so be sure to call early
to get a site nearby, while there are still plenty left!

A Gatherin' Guide will soon be avalible at
www.penncoasters.com/letterboxing.htm where you will find clues to all
the local "known" letterboxes (there are some mysteries out there) as
well as schedules and directions.
