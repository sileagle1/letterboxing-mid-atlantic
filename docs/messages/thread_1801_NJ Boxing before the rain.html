NJ Boxing before the rain

From: the B's | Date: 2006-06-24 02:37:02 UTC
Given the weather forecast for the weekend, we thought we'd better
get it in today. I was informed today our goal is 100 boxes by
Labor Day.

Started at Holmdel Park & Arboretum in central NJ. Great place,
especially for kids. Longstreet Farm, a working 1890s farm, is
right next to the Arboretum. Who knew pigs were so BIG? An original
Martian Maggot box is your reward on the Park hike. It has been
there 3 years but very few visitors since planted. Dunno why,
because it is a nice walk.

Also sought out Rick's Lost Boxes. Great stamp! Cooled off
afterward in the sprayground in the same park. For anyone boxing
with kids in the summer (or maybe just borrow one or two), bring the
swimsuits along so you don't miss this. A whole area filled with
dumping buckets, sprayers, fountains, you name it, and best of all
it's free.

Happy boxing to all this weekend -- hopefully it dries out a little
on Sunday so all is not lost.

-- Queen B (the B's)
