Okay here's one for you all...

Life Imitates Boxing

I have this stamp that I carved a year ago, but never found a good
home for. It's a kite and one of my best carvings to date. Too nice
to lose to HH-dom says I, so I plan this little journal-like scenario
for clues about wanting to go to the beach and fly a kite, but it's
too late in the day to drive, go I somewhere along the way. Find a
nice spot, and the kite soon gets eaten by a tree, at the roots of
which would sit a yet-to-be-carved "Kite-Eating Tree" stamp.

Wouldn't you know, I went boxing last week and took the stamp and box
along just in case there was a good place to hide what I had. One of
the brood asked to bring a kite. Took the carving kit to play with
while we admired the kite in the air. The above scnerio played out
exactly as I what dreamed it up, except that there were no places to
put a box. Went home and carved stamp number two. It must be fate!
