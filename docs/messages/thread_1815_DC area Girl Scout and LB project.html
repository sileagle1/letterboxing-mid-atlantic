What I am doing is heading a project to put 49 boxes out in the
Girl Scout camps(properties) of this council (7 in each of the 7
camps). This is through a class I am taking that requires a community
project. I figured why not combine 2 of my favorite communities -
LBing and GS! The boxes will only be available to GS that are in the
camps. They will not be on the LBNA or AtlasQuest site. This will be
a way of introducing this fun hobby to Scouts in a safe and familiar
environment. Think of them as Event stamps that will stay in place.
There will also be a tailored description of what letterboxing is all
about available to have them "taught right".

What is needed are stamps that are carved, boxes prepared, folks to
hide the boxes and write the clues, and a "this is letterboxing"
article written. This would be done by the end of August. Lastly I
want to have "experts" on call to teach and get girls excited about
Letterboxing at troop meetings or at the camps when they go on troop
camping trips. Do you see any way you could fill one of these roles?

Do you have any stamps that you carved that you are not using?
Slightly flawed attempts, or retired boxes that you be willing to
donate to the cause? Or would you like to carve a few stamps to be
placed in the camps? Wanna spend a day out in a GS camp writing fun
clues and hiding boxes? Wanna compile a "how to" article? Or do you
want to do it all????? just kidding on that last one!

Really, I am just looking for support. I figure there are lots of
boxers out there that have retired stamps, slightly flawed ones or
just are willing to carve a few to have the girls learn about the
joys of LBing. Or would you be willing to be on an "on-call experts"
resource list to attend a GS troop meeting to get the girls excited
about LBing?
