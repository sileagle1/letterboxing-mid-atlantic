Boy, looking at the local paper today (Allentown Morning Call), it's
really scary what it's like out there. If any of you are directly
affected by this flooding, please know I'm keeping you in my prayers.

If it's possible (and legal), I plan to head into Easton tomorrow to
check on my Xak Promar series. I think a few may be gone. I then
plan to (again, if it's possible and legal) head down Rt. 611/32 into
Bucks County, take some pictures of the river, and seek out some boxes
in Point Pleasant.

Would anybody like me to check on your box while I'm traveling down
the river? Let me know.

The loss of a letterbox is insignificant compared to what the river
communities are going through, but somehow I feel called to do this
tomorrow...who knows...maybe I'll end up in the right place at the
right time to help somebody.

Peace,
Myke (Indigo Vulture)
