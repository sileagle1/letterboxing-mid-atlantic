Northern Virginia Flooding

From: BullDawg | Date: 2006-06-30 19:21:03 UTC
..went out for "The Doubtful Guests'" Audobon 2 box in Reston VA
today, and the hike follows a stream most of the way. It had risen to
a level in the past few days that had trashed the area, bringing the
water above all 3 of the foot bridges you cross. The last foot bridge
(#43) is fairly large, and was taken down stream about 40 feet til it
lodged itself on some large trees. You could still walk on it to get
across, but it was at an odd angle, and rocked depending on your
weight. Me and the kids had fun cossing it, and took a few pictures
that i will post in the coming days of us on it. Was cool, but still
the area was in shambles.
