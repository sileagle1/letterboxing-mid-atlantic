From: Jan | Date: 2006-07-05 00:11:38 UTC
Today we cruised to the State College area in the little red car, top
up due to the intermittent rain, and found seven really neat drivebys
and picked up two hitchhikers (yes, I said I picked up TWO HHs). When
we got home, I ran down to the river to check on my Happy Pawlidays
but the trail is now closed due to the flood. I also checked on my
After the Mystery box which is nearby and was happy to see it is safe
and sound. It is now also the temporary home of a visitor I brought
home from Canada. Then I moved on a bit and found a good hiding spot
for a box that I have been planning but wasn't sure that I could find
a good location for it. Mission accomplished.

It felt good to be out since I have been in a self imposed
letterboxing exile due to the fact that I am not crazy about ticks,
mosquitoes, poison ivy, gnats, black flies, heat, humidity, etc. Maybe
this weekend will bring more than drivebys my way.

Jan of Team Little Dog
