delaware river trek

From: Myke Scholtes | Date: 2006-07-08 23:25:50 UTC
Message
So today I headed to Easton, PA, and drove downriver all the way to Point Pleasant.  (I tried to do this last Friday, but as it turned out, that wasn't...err...legal at that point.)
 
Anyway, I'm happy to report that the following boxes are still alive and kicking, and LEGALLY findable:
 
Xak Promar: 2, 3, 4, 5
Play on Perspective
Doctor Who # 5
The Devil Made Me Do It
Kayaker's Dream
Night Flight
Ties That Bind
Recycle, Reuse
 
All above boxes are in great shape, with one exception (and even that one is passable).  I'll be emailing that placer off list.  What's really astonishing to me is that Xak Promar 4 & 5 made it.  The outside of both boxes were covered in river mud, but they were unmoved, and the contents were perfect.  Wow.
 
In sadder news...
"Xak Promar 1" is no more.
The same fate seems to be true for "Have Bike, Will Travel".
 
And finally...I tried to find "Lock > Rock", but the trail is closed there, and I was in really clear view of the road...and I didn't feel like explaining to some passing officer why I was on a closed trail.  So I bailed before I got to it.  So I don't know about its status.  Either way, it's not legally findable right now.
 
All in all, a pretty fun day.  611 and 32 are mostly open through those stretches.  It was really sad how many people had dumpsters on their properties, though.  This yearly muck-out and cleanup must really put somebody living there to the point of despair.
 
Anybody check on Schuylkill or Perkiomen boxes yet?  I hear there was some nasty flooding in Perkasie and Sellersville...I'll have to get back down there to check on my Lost Cats sometime soon.
 
Peace,
Myke "Indigo Vulture"
