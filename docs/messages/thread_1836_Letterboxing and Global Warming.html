Letterboxing and Global Warming

From: nattybumppolbna | Date: 2006-07-07 00:10:29 UTC
So this "global warming"-thing seems to be hitting the big time. It has it's own movie now.
And despite a comprehensive plan of denial and neglect by the current administration, global
warming seems to be killing polar bears pretty efficiently in the Arctic.

What does this mean to Letterboxers? I don't know. Maybe it's the new Y2K. Or maybe we
will start seeing more and more flooding - particularly here in the East where we seem to be
paving over all of the land that used to absorb much of the run-off that's flooding our
neighborhoods and parks.

My 2 cents is, if you like your boxes to stick around for a while, you might want to rethink
planting them in locations so close to water. As so many of our parks are near creeks and
rivers that becomes a challenge.

Thoughts?
