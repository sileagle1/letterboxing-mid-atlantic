Berks County Flood Report

From: Jan | Date: 2006-07-07 20:11:04 UTC
I know there must be some boxers out there who have been anxiously
awaiting the weekend to go boxing in Berks County, PA, so I thought I
would give a little update based on my box checking this week. First
of all, Nolde Forest with its zillion boxes is in good shape. The
streams there are much higher than normal for this time of the year
but I found both of my boxes there safe and sound. From what I can
remember about the location of many of the other ones, they should
also be safe. There is one mystery box that I am doubtful about
though. So come to Berks county and head for Nolde.

I also went to Blue Marsh to check on my boxes there this afternoon
but was not surprised to find that the park is still closed entirely.
I was able to slip in to check on Silent Doug's Capitol Tile:
Cardinal and it appears to be gone. I was amazed to see that the
water line was way past this box which is a good forty feet away from
the lake itself.

The Exeter River Trail is also closed. Both Silent Doug and I have a
box on that trail and I will check on them as soon as the trail
re-opens. This trail was waaaaaayyyyyyy under water so I don't expect
to find my box there anymore. Doug's was much higher and may have
survived.

So if you are coming to my neck of the woods, avoid Blue Marsh and
head for Nolde or French Creek. I haven't been to French Creek but I
haven't heard any local rumblings about any kind of trouble there.

Jan of Team Little Dog
