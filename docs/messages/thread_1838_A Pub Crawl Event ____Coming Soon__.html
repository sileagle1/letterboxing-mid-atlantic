A Pub Crawl Event ..."Coming Soon!"

From: gallant_rogue | Date: 2006-07-08 22:18:16 UTC
My good friend Sewsowbizzy and I hiked about six miles in Prince
William State Forest on Monday. We collected the active NPS series
logging all but the missing "Fools Gold". As we walked we combined
our considerable intellectual resources and brainstormed a Pub Crawl
Event for Old Town Alexandria Virginia.

Visit this site for Maps & Hotel & visitor information:

http://www.funside.com/

We are not ready to release specific details because we are working
out details and want to provide some surprises... but looking at
the LBMA Calendar and vacation schedules for local letterboxers we
are counting on to attend... Saturday NOVEMBER 11th is the date we
are looking at. This is a three day weekend for govt. employees

There is no way we could duplicate the Magnificent Ivy's Magical
Leesburg Pub Crawl so we will not try to imitate rather we will
borrow some of his great ideas. and add some ideas from some other
successful events and add a new idea or two and produce a new and
different product that will uphold the high standards of LBMA.

Why November? There is simply too much to do to hold it in July...
too many vacations to compete with in August... Knoebels II is in
September, "It's Just a Game" is in October... so The Old Town Pub
Crawl Event (name to be announced later!) will be the weekend after
Halloween... while the weather is still reasonably good... and on a
Full Moon Weekend...

This will allow our letterboxing associates from around the country
to make their travel arrangements!

We will release the event name August 1st !
