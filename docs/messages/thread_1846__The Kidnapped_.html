From: June | Date: 2006-07-12 12:57:43 UTC
If it's a clean break, maybe you can turn it into a hinged stamp. Place the
two pieces together firmly and place tape on the back, then cut off the
excess tape around the edges. I've only done it the other way, placed the
intact stamp on tape and then cut almost all the way through the stamp so
it can be folded over. I use 3-4 layers of clear packing tape. Some people
use duct tape. When it's cut cleanly you don't see any line at all in the
stamped image. It might work,depending on the break.
Good luck.
missmoon
