Extreme Letterboxing

From: fleurdelislb | Date: 2006-07-17 01:43:07 UTC
I was out in Portland, OR last week for a work conference. While
there, I knew I had to try to get a Der Mad Stamper box. So during Day
2 of the conference, I snuck away from one of the forums and found my
way into the woods. It had rained that morning, so the ground was
damp. I saw three snakes (eek!) and was running along the trail trying
to find the box before anyone realized I was missing.

How is this extreme, you ask?

Because I was in a skirt and open-toed, 3-inch-heeled shoes.

When I finally located the box, I had to walk a ways through thorns,
poison ivy, rotted stumps, and other overgrowth. I kept telling
myself that it was okay, that it's a good story, and that I'm
obviously out of my mind to be looking for a box dressed the way that
I was. I was also petrified of a snake slithering over my mostly bare
foot!

I did get the box - first person in more than a year to stamp in - and
I think that I'll stick to shorts and sneaks from now on.

FdL
