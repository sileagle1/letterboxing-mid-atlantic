Alexandria Pub Crawl Accomodations

From: gallant_rogue | Date: 2006-07-23 22:41:02 UTC
A few people have begun talking about accomodations for the
Alexanria Pub Crawl . . .

The Holiday Inn Old Town is on the Pub Crawl route... there are
cheaper and there are more expensive places nearby... but nothing is
closer! That's where I will be staying.

Other nearby hotels are:

Travelodge
Embassy Suites
Courtyard Inn
Radison
Hampton Inn
Holiday Inn
Best Western

If you want Five Star... The Morrison House is walking distance too.

Before you book..... map it and see how close you are to KING STREET
and the Potomac River... factor in a cab ride.... Please do not
drink in drive in Alexandria Va...

Those people that are staying in Old Town can close the bars ...
then meet for brunch!
