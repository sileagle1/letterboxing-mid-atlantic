update on Kinder Farm Park boxes

From: wood thrush | Date: 2006-07-24 00:20:49 UTC
Just returned from boxing in Anne Arundel Co., Md., and have some sad news about two of
the boxes in Kinder Farm Park, "Kinder Park Treasure" and "Kinder Farm Park." Both were
vandallized when we found them. A lid was all that remained of one of them, while the other
had an intact container with two empty baggies inside.

While the lid-only box was in a fairly high-trafficked area, and showed signs of partying
nearby (beer cans, etc.), the intact-but-empty box had been hidden in a very non-obvious
spot that one really would have needed the clues to access.

Sad stuff. There is also a fairly new box in the area, which was in fine shape; our guess is the
vandalism happened before it was planted.
