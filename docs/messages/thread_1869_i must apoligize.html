From: team_new_hampshire | Date: 2006-07-27 03:13:36 UTC
it has come to my attention that i may have said some things that
may have been taken the wrong way. let me begin.

first, let me say that Pennsylvania is a wonderful state. i look
forward to visiting/invading PA. some of you may have taken some of
the things i have said about your state to heart. i never meant for
this to happen. this whole invasion thing that i have been planning,
is a game i hope goes over real well. i only have been saying some
of these things to kinda get some people to notice and have fun with
it. i have visited your state 2 times in the past and have never had
a better time then in the keystone state. your state if full of
history and that might be why i love PA the most. this time around,
i had hoped to meet some of you LBers while there. so far i have met
hikers n' hounds and i have no doubts that the rest of you are any
less kinder than they are. i was lucky enough to have met with irene
on more than 1 occation, and look forward to meeting her and all of
you, on your turf.

second, have always been pretty good about croo-posting. i don't
think it has every really been a problem with me until resently.
with this invasion game coming up, i just want to make sure that
everyone that can play, knows about it. after i head to PA, there
will be maybe 1 more cross-post. this will state a winner.

a winner? i guess now would be a good time to explain this invasion
game that i have planned. when i had planned on invadaing PA, it had
nothing to do with aliens. after awhile, it sounded like a cool
thing to do. then i got into it a bit more and it was on.

here is the deal:
--the invasion starts on august 12th of 06'. destination french
creek state park in beautiful pennsylvania. i will be LBing in NH,
MA, CT, NY, NJ, DE, MD, VA, PA, on that day, collecting at least 1
box in every state. boxes and est time range will be posted saturday
and listed for the 2 weeks heading up to the invasion.
--finding us should be rather easy. be at the location in the time
range posted, and look for me and 2 little aliens running around
looking for a letterbox. we WILL try to look as normal as possible.
do not be afraid to walk up to us and try and stop the invasion. our
spaceship has already been posted on
http://thealieninvasion.blogspot.com already so you can look for us
before getting out of the car to look for us.
--now don't think you won't get anything out of this. the person who
stops us from invading PA will, and i really mean will, get a prize.
i made something specially for this invasion and i want to reward
the person who figures out how to stop us. i just not going to say
right now how to do it. you'll have to wait and see.

so i don't know if i have left anything out. feel free to ask me any
questions about this game/invasion. i will do my best to give the
the answer your looking for. hopefully this invasion thing goes over
awesome. if so, i plan on invading somewhere else next year.

let the good times roll in the granite state.
david (team new hampshire)
http://teamnewhampshire.blogspot.com
http://thealieninvasion.blogspot.com
