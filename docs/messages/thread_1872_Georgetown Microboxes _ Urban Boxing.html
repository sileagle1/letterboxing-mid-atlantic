#2:
A Word About Urban Boxing...
When searching out and retrieving these microboxes, you have to be
patient. Night time boxing is probably the best solution when a
reduced number of people are around. Establish yourself in a given
location and take the time to retrieve and then to rehide/replace
properly. DO NOT ATTEMPT A LOCATION IF YOU ARE IN A HURRY AND CANNOT
REPLACE CORRECTLY. The survival of this series or any urban series
relies on you to be patient, take your time and put the stamp securely
back where it was planted.
