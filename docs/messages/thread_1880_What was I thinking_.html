In my business we have a saying:
"It's better to beg forgiveness than ask permission." As long as you are careful, responsible
and not out to hurt anyone, it works pretty well. That's state highway property. What do
you think they would say if you asked about hiking? How about leaving a box there?

I once had to get permission to videotape traffic on the blue route, It practically took an act
of the state legislature. PENNDOT sent out 3 people to supervise us and it took two hours to
get everyone together in one place. A few months later I needed traffic footage for another
job. We pulled over and shot it. It took five minutes. When the state police stopped us, we
told him what we were doing, apologized and went on our way.
