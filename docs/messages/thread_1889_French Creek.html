French Creek

From: Jan | Date: 2006-08-04 11:02:27 UTC
Our morning paper, the Reading Eagle, posted this story today.....I am posting this since I know that we are being invaded next weekend and hope everyone takes precautions by leaving nothing of value in their cars.

A rash of daylight break-ins of vehciles belonging to visitors at French Creek State Park have occurred in the last two weeks, park rangers said Thursday.

Thieves smashed vehicle windows to gain entry to seven vehicles since July 23, Chief Ranger Chris Nangle said.

The break-ins occurred in both remote and heavily visited areas of the recreation area, which straddles the Berks and Chester county border.

No witnesses to the crimes have been found.

Nangle said it appears the thieves drove into areas where numerous vehicles were parked, then made a fast getaway.

Credit cards taken from the vehicles were immediately used for gas purchases in Birdsboro and at Boscov's stores in the area, investigators said.
