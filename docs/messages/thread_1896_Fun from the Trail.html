Old funny:
Walking on the trails of Cape May Pink Panther (PP) and Black Swan
(BS) spy a very deceased frog on the trail, belly up.
PP, in her best scientific voice explains she knows exactly what
happen to the frog - he croaked.

New funny:
BS & PP walking the trails of beautiful Berks County find a very
lively large frog on the trail.
BS: Wish I had an aquarium at home to take him home too.
PP: That's not right. You know "Leave only footprints, take only
memories"....
BS: His name is Memory.

What's your funny from the trail?
