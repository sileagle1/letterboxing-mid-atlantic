W orthington State Forest Adventures

From: Lightnin Bug | Date: 2006-08-06 23:05:46 UTC
Indigo Vulture and I had a great day over in W orthington State
Forest in NJ yesterday. This is also known as the Delaware Water Gap.

We were able to find Riversol's Vision Quest box, last found in Dec
2004 by our Jedi Master, Silent Doug. Lephantman's Lost Clues
obviously pointed to a lost box. Oh well, fool us once!

Wingfoot's W orthington State Forest series was well worth the hiking
and climbing we did. We managed to snag both and observed the
guardian of the Sunfish Pond box slither and rattle as it made sure
we were not muggles. I did manage to put a photo in under
miscellaneous. They should be the last ones. I took it on a cell
phone, which made the rattler rattle. Other than that we were not
perceived as a threat.

We also saw a porcupine (first time in the wild for us for one of
those rascals) on the way up to the summits.

Saw many areas for potential future eevil and normal boxes...

Cheers,

Lightnin' Bug
