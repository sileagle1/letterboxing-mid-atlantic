Spent the weekendin the lower three counties...

From: Hikers_n_ Hounds | Date: 2006-08-07 02:44:16 UTC
Howdy there fellow LBMAer's,
 
I sat in traffic for 4 hours yesterday but finally made it down to DE to meet up with Sowsewbizzy and check out some sites to plant for her gather in Oct. Found some really cool places I had never been to before and helped plant a few boxes too.
 
I hope you are all planning to attend. This gather looks to be a blast. And what a great way for Delaware to shine! Even the natives will visit some new places I think.
 
I got a sneak peak at some of the stamps and they are just incredible! But you'd better be wearing your thinking cap to find a few of them. Judy plans to give your grey matter a real workout!
 
Oh, and I FINALLY CARVED A STAMP! Two of them in fact, and Judy said they were pretty good. So I went ahead and started re-carving some of my 4 missing stamps. Treasure hunt should be back up soon, as well as Big Green Monster. I got a missing report of Disc Golf but haven't confirmed it yete. Miz Scarlet and I have to put a few more boxes in the Battle fo the bands sometime in the fall. Led Zeppelin has to be replaced, she is adding Cash and I am going with Hank WIlliams.
 
Last weekend Dude, Mr. Irene and I were in Schoharie, NY and got "Vromans Nose" whaich was a very cool hike, about 1,200 for above sea level, but I think only 700 or so from the vally floor. Felt about 7,0000 but we made up and back and had a great time. A hike worthy of LB et al or Bull Dog. Went after a couple of boxes in nearby Shaorn Springs but think they're gone. Cool place with a neat history though so we thoroughly enjoyed our visit.
 
So anyway, mark your calendars for Oct and see you all in Delaware if not before!
 
H&H
