From: Lorie Thomas | Date: 2006-08-09 23:39:49 UTC
On AQ type in Wilmington, DE.
Any idea what parks you'd like to hit?
HNH's series in Valley Garden is lovely, a more formal
park.

Brandywine Creek has lots of boxes, including several
of Sheba's, on both sides of the creek. I like this
park a lot.

Carousel Park is nice if you also want to see horses
or the dog park.

Brandywine Springs park is free, has several boxes
planted, is fairly small, and has the historical bonus
of having been the home of an old amusement park.
It's being refurbished but historical boards are neat
to read.

I haven't been to Rockwood yet but understand it
contains another series. Another more formal park w/
mansion. It's also the site of this year's
Shakespeare Festival - Much Ado About Nothing. Very
good play, esp. the evening show, and only $8. And
there is a show tomorrow night! I recommend a picnic!
http://www.delshakes.org

Happy hunting.
