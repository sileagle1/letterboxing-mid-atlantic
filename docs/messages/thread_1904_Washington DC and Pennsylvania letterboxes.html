I posted this on new boxers, but it is relivent here as well:

First, do a search for letterboxing.org on the Way Back Macine on
www.archive.org. You will see a whole list of dates that the site was
archived. Choose the most recent.

Now, often the Way Back Machine does not arcive the entire site, but
just the most popular pages, so clues to individual boxes may not be
found. In this case, Google is your friend.

Google the name of the box you are looking for (it often helps to put
the word "letterbox" in the search field too). When you come up with
the results, most likely the box you want will be right at the top, look
just below it and you will see a link that says "cached" and click on
that link, and is will have the most recent saved page for the box.

Now remember, this will not always work, if a box was brand new it may
not have been cached anywhere. Also, the cached pages are older, so
clues may have changed. But, heck, it is better then nothing, and 9
times out of 10 you will find what you are looking for.

____________________________________________________________

As for boxes down by Independance Hall, sorry, there are none that I
know of. There are a few in nearby Fairmont Park and in Northeast
Philly in Pennypack Park. There are a ton in the surrounding suburbs,
Bucks, Montgomery, Chester and Delaware counties.
