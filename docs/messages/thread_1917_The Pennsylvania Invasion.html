From: Jan | Date: 2006-08-15 09:02:47 UTC
Lil Milkmonster and I were happy that we had the opportunity to take
part in the Pennsylvania Invasion at French Creek by Team New
Hampshire. After all of the pre-event publicity, I was a little
apprehensive about it but the aliens proved to be very friendly and
Milkmonster and I had a great time meeting them. I placed a few
pictures of the Invasion gathering on my blog at
http://letterboxingmimser.blogspot.com/2006/08/invasion.html.
