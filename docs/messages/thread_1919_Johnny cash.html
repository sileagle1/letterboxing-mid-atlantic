Johnny cash

From: mizscarlet731 | Date: 2006-08-15 22:07:58 UTC
On a recent visit to the DE shore I stopped by the Bottle and Cork to pick up a napkin so I
could have a good image to carve for Judy. Well while I was there I ran into my friend Jim
who used to own the "cork he said he had run across some stuff while cleaning out his old
office and I might be interested in it. Not many people know that Johnny and June Cash
spent alot of time in Lewes with thier friends, John and Michell. On one of these visits they
spent an evening at the cork to hear ayoung band they had heard about, Love Seed
Momma Jump. Well the guys in the band were pretty excited about this so on the spot they
came up with a song. They only played it the one time and nobody wrote down the lyrics.
Except my friend Jim, he scribbled them on a napkin and tossed it in his desk. The napkin
had been in the desk quite a few years and was hard to read I don't know if I got it all
down but this is close as I could come.
I keep a close watch on this box of mine
I keep my eyes wide open all the time
Because it's mine i walk the trail

I look for trees to carve my line
First ones full I'll try the second
Because it's mine I walk the trail

You got to go a way off the trail to the south
16 steps should be enough
Because it's mine I walk the trail

I'm standing on the middle of 3 big rocks
I'm facing my home in the east
Destiny is at my right foot
Because it's mine I walk the trail

It's no Walk the Liine but I kinda like it.
