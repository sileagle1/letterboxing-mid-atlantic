Ben Franklin series

From: Silent Doug | Date: 2004-07-12 13:05:08 UTC
On Friday, Jennifer & I found all five of the Ben Franklin series in
Philly. Great stamps and logbooks in a great park (we were actually walking
in that park a couple of weeks ago and lamenting the fact that there were
no letterboxes there, so it was nice to visit again and find a bunch of
boxes!). We rode our bikes, too, which made returning to our starting point
much easier. We were the third to complete the series (though some people
had found one or two of the others).

Fleur de lis, the first three containers are already cracked, and any outer
baggies that were still on any of the boxes were all torn. Those Gladware
containers just don't stand up very well in a letterboxing environment (and
neither do outer baggies), so you might consider replacing them soon with
something sturdier. I like the Milan Classic (by Holiday) boxes that you
can get at Dollar Tree for $1 in various sizes. They stand up really well
and are waterproof -- and are inexpensive.

Thanks for the letterboxes!

Doug
