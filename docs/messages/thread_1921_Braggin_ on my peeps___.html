From: Hikers_n_ Hounds | Date: 2006-08-16 01:26:41 UTC
Hey folks,
 
There was a post on El Pepe's website about the lack of new mystery boxes in the Northeast. LB and I were braggin' 'bout how that seems to be the reverse trend here in LBMA land. Even though I rarely hunt mystery boxes unless a friend drags me along against my will, I really do read most of the clues you post. Is that wierd?  I love r4ading clues, even if I have no intention of ever hunting the box. I just love them! I have absolutely no idea what they mean, but just love the creativity that you put into them. And I am so impressed when someone actually finds them! How'd they do that!?!? I ask myself, what did they glean from the jumbled words, cryptic quotes and little squiggles that I didn't see? You guys amaze and humble me, really.
 
I still laugh out loud every time I think of one of Cyclonics clues, I forget which one, that are in morse code. I can post that here because I posted to the list "Hey Nate, something's wrong with your clue, it's all messed up" and someone else posted back "Ummm, Irene, that's morse code"!!! LOL!!!! 
 
You guys rock, you guys roll.
