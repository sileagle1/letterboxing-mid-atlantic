From: shaneodom2003 | Date: 2004-07-12 14:01:02 UTC
Hello all,
Sounds like the folks who attended the wknd event had lots of fun. My
family and I went out here in AA Co. to hit some boxes just in case
they get pulled. Hope they don't though, so if ya have a box in and
AA Co. park, please contact them for a permission letter. (See my
post about Anne Arundel Co.)Over the 2 days we went after 6 total
boxes with the following results.
Downs Park-Amazing park with nice view. We took two friends and
introduced them to boxing. Lots of fun. Found 2 for 2. Both in nice
spots and good area. Our friends really enjoyed them and will
continue boxing. I hope the placers of the boxes get permission and
maybe more boxes in the park. It is a perfect local.
Arbology-Went after them on Sun. with just us.(The Woodwooses). These
boxes are in White Marsh Park in Bowie MD. Prince Georges County so
not threatened with removal. This park is by far our favorite boxing
experience so far. Amazing series of trails in a non-rural area. The
woods of White Marsh are great. Unforuantely! :'( the first two boxes
had been found by someone not nice!
#1 was totally missing, and then we noticed the rain soaked strips of
the journal. Found part of its cover.
#2 box missing, journal distroyed and striped of its pages. We found
the cover and the STAMP! thrown in a bush. I will contact the placer
and see if I can mail it to them. This was obviously done by a
person. The way the journal pgs were strew down the path made it
certain. I am assuming that the first one was found by accident and
had the clues to #2 in it.
#3 We found it. Hidden abit better than the first two and I think the
trasher of the boxes gave up. (Mama Woodwose and Lil'Dragon couldn't
at first and called in me as reinforcements.) Nice box in good shape.
I would say that it's my opinion that the little glad sandwich
containers don't make the best boxes. We put it back tight with a
little duff strew over it and added double bagging and info flyers to
it.
We also went after the first of "Who let the Horses out." We found
certainly the right spot and looked hard. Poking behind trees and
such but no luck. It could be there. I'm sure these are good trails
here for the right boxers. But I don't recomend them for the family
with young kids.(As the clues clearly stated)Even the 1st box is a
little rough area, and the the trails are also used by some shady
folks. Lots of liquor bottles around and what looked like a homeless
squat.
Still a great time and getting us into new areas. Esp. White Marsh
Park, we decided that these are our favorites so far.
Peaceful Trails,
Shane "Pathmaker" Odom
Papa Woodwoose
