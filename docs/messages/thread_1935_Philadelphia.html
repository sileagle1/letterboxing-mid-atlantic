From: mother_duck_n_brood | Date: 2006-08-22 18:10:53 UTC
The one in MD is near CALVERT Manor. It's a good drive-by.

There is quite a concentration of boxes in Rockwood Park, just north
of Wilmington, DE, and right off I-95, about 45 minutes from Center
City Philadelphia. There are at least 5 boxes there and another in
the adjacent Bellevue State Park. Both parks are pretty small and
finding all the boxes in Rockwood should not take more than a couple
of hours, depending on how long you linger over the log books.

There is also a box outside Iron Hill Museum, in Newark, DE, which is
also very close to I-95 and a quick find.

There are boxes in Pennypack Park in Philly. I'm not sure if they
are close enough or safe enough to walk from Independence Mall as I
get lost in that part of Philly myself.

By the way, I enjoyed your boxes in Buxton last year and plan to
spend next week looking for all those new ones!

Mother Duck,
Wilmington, DE

...um yeah, what he said below. I couldn't remember the name of my own box! I just got a find report so I know it's there. Thanks for the plug!
 
If you're going to be in Philly then I've got to put a plug in for Tyler State Park, about 30-40 minutes from Center City. I-95 North to the Newtown-Yardely exit, left at the top of the ramp. Stay on that road for a few miles (4-5?) Tyler entrance is on the left. There are ton's of boxes there. OR drive past the main entrance, pass the Community College, left into the Schfield Ford Covered Bridge Entrance, 1/2 ton of boxes there. On your way back to I-95, pass the main park entrance now on your right, right at the next light. Turn right into Fisherman's Parking lot entrance. 1/4 ton of boxes there. Perfect place for a boxoholic.
 
just my two cents.

As a semi-drive by (not too far off I-95 but a nice respite from
driving), we highly recommend the Bridges of the D&C Canal series.
South side is in DE, north side is in MD. Great stamps, great
location. I will never forget my kids' faces when they made a
signal to honk the horn to an oil-barge towboat -- and he did,
nearly lifting them off their feet.

boxers have our work cut (carved?) out for us.
