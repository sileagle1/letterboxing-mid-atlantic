Finally catching up

From: Nathan Brown | Date: 2006-08-29 16:15:15 UTC
I know, its been a little quite around here, huh? Well, I am back from
vacation, so watch out ;-)

Between all the coaster riding, driving, coaster riding, baseball,
coaster riding, camping, oh, and coaster riding, I did manage to get a
little bit of boxing in.

I found a few boxes in Indiana, one was a nice little box across the
street from Holiday World called Santa Coaster, the other, Abe-The
Formative Years, in a patch of posion ivy at Abe Lincoln's childhood home.

In Reedsburg, WI I discovered the wonderfully carved Herbie.

Crossing over to Michigan I found Snug Harbor in Muskegon, MI. While
there I planted Keystone Traveler: Bumping Around Michigan
(http://tinyurl.com/ewdwg), a four box drive-by series. I would like to
sometime return to this park, as it is nice and there are many more
boxes to find there.

My first Canadian find was London Calling, a clever box outside London, ON.

And then I was at the WNY Gathering, which was a blast and I would love
to do again. I didn't do so much boxing as I did hanging out, and
watching others try and figure out Houdini's Great Escape. That
Saturday Morning I did plant a box in Darien Lakes State Park, Heart and
Soul, which will but public soon.

I cannot convey just how much fun the gethering was. Sprite, sans
Highlander who was off testing his edumacation, put on a great show, and
there were tons of boxes to seek out if you were so inclined. Everyone
was wonderful, and the camping was great. The weather even held off
long enough to get some D&D in that night (letterboxers and D&D, there
is a scary combination).

Sunday I poked around and did get about a dozen boxes in the area. I
stopped by Sprite's place and meet Highlander.

All and all a great time all week, and I am beat now.

Oh, one other thing, LB, the Borg Cube has now assimilated 6 new HH's,
while dropping off all that it had brought along.

I look forward to seeing each and everyone of you (except Judy, you have
a good excuse) in a few weeks at Knoebels. I am now fired up and hot to
trot to get things done, so watch out, I've got some neat stuff in store
for you all.
--
