Re: Skullduggery (Alexandria Pub Crawl Event) Sept 01 Update !

From: gallant_rogue | Date: 2006-09-01 23:13:09 UTC
We have two months to get organized! But I've already received a
couple of emails about this: Please form your teams ( I know there
are some Red Hatters already scheming!)... then if you are less the
seven...please be willing to accept solo crawlers on your team...
We have some brand new letterboxers coming out! I will assist placing
anybody that does not have a team... but we wont do that untill after
the Delaware Event: It's Just a Game

(I figure by then you all will have finalized your team!)

BullDawg has graciously constructed a Guestbook feature on the
Skullduggery site he built... I'm advising people to use the Guest
Book on the website to advertise their team needs...

Those of you that have ever been in a Golf Tournament may be familiar
with the term "Shotgun Start"... that means from the Detectives
Meeting... each team will be heading to a different pub to start the
crawl. That way we wont all be in the same bars at the same time...
very little overlap... then...those lucky people still standing and
able to "slove" the Skullduggery Kryptogrph... will be able to attend
the Mysterious Skullduggery Ball (aka The Last Pub!) We will all end
up at the same place!


(There will be no clues or letterboxes planted near the Lyceum)
