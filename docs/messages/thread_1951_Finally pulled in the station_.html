After lots of hard work shoveling coal and uncoupling cars, we are
happy to announce New Jersey's first "train by" series of
letterboxes.

That's right -- no need to drive from box to box. Relax in the
comfort of train cars while you are chauffered from box location to
box location, for less than the cost of one gallon of gas.

Enjoy!

Boxes Pulled in Franklin Lakes NJ

From: Art Vandelay | Date: 2006-09-16 19:13:58 UTC
Hi!
Warcat 76 has been out of commission for most of the summer with family problems and then a wonderful cruise.  Today we finally had a chance to check on our box that had been reported missing in Franklin Lakes.  Sure enough it was gone.  Then we decided to stop in the office at the center to see if they had a lost & found and sure enough they did and there was the box.  It was a little wet but I'm glad we found it because there was also a hitchhiker in it.  That was wet too but we'll try to dry it out and get it moving again.  So we decided not to rehide the box and also pulled the 2 others in the series.  They were cheap little store bought stamps anyway so no great loss. Thanks to the people who alerted us that the box was MIA.
