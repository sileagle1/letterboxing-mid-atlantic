Oh it's definately camping! All tent and tarp, in the middle of a valley surrounded by those gorgeous Wv Mountains. No bathrooms, Porta Pots about 1/8 mile away. The only reason there is wifi and ham radio service is that this weekend sees about 2,000 cavers from all over US and CA get together for an Old Timers Reunion. They set up an "OTR Radio" mini-station and all kinds of other cool stuff to make us all feel connected. Plus many of them are just "gadget guys" who love to play with their toys. But it's pretty primitive camping, believe me. 
Anywho, wasn't able to check my Spruce Knob boxes as the weather was too bad to get to the elevation. Did checked Windmills Of Backbone Mountain and Infinity Knot and both are fine and have been found by our own NJ Carole! Plus planted Rich Mountain Battlefiled and checked out the local cemetary for some ideas for next years plant.
 
Rich Mountain was a tiny little battlefiled on top of a mountain but was an interesting place that is well maintaint. Signs cleary explian the 4 hour battle and take you from spot to spot, there are several big limestone boulders in which local survivors of the battle camp back and carved their fallen comrades names, etc. Pretty neat. And the stamp came out well if I do say so myself.
