From: theendgang | Date: 2006-09-03 15:09:16 UTC
Hi All,
The 37th Bluemont Fair (www.bluemontfair.com)is being held Sept. 16th
and 17th. I would like to invite people to come out for an informal
meet and greet on the 16th. Ditsyquoin, who is the postmistress for
Bluemont post office, will be stamping a special postal cancellation
stamp at the EE Lake Store. She'll stamp your log and you may get an
exchange along with it. She has also graciously offered the back lawn
of the post office as a meeting area for boxers to meet for exchanges.
I have carved a special "event only" stamp for the fair, and this stamp
will be retired after the fair. Clues to the box will be available at
the fair and probably by password only. I'll post the info just beofre
the fair.
I may try to get another box or two planted as well, but there are
numerous other boxes nearby. The fair looks to be a great time for all
and a great way to check out the numerous country fairs that are coming
up for the Fall. Let me know if you want to meet at a certain time or I
can just state a time and plan on that. I will be there most of the day
on the 16th. Hope a few of you can make it out and welcome Ditsyquoin,
who is a new boxer in our midst, to the hobby. Safe trails!
Koyote
