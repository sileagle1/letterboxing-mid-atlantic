EB-Bebe and I went on a boxing adventure in the Lower Three Counties
today. We were accompanied by North Star and her dog Fin who is a
hyperactive ball of fluff.

We first hunted down Oh, The Irony. As stated, it is amazingly close to
the trail, and very clever as well. We rehid it well, and it was
immediately tested by a couple who walked by. They stopped right by the
tree so the woman could take a call on her cell. She stood there
talking and was looking right where the box was hidden, but was none the
wiser.

Afterwards we went for a ride before hitting the trail again in search
of the Possium Hill Explorer series. All store bought stamps, but a
nice hike. Funny, the planters called the first three boxes micro
boxes, though they turned out to be the size of my regular boxes. I
wondered what they considered a regular size box, and found out with the
last box, which was gargantuain.

All and all a nice day, great weather, some nice wooded hikes, and a
very tired Fin.
