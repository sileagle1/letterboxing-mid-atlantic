"Missing" box is there!

From: wood thrush | Date: 2006-09-05 13:25:18 UTC
Was out boxing in Harford County, Md. on Saturday, and in addition to
finding the very nice Ma & Pa Box and Jerusalem Mill Froggie, I took a
chance and drove to Havre de Grace. The Concord Point Lighthouse box
had been listed as missing for some time, but it is definitely there.
Last finders before me were in mid-2005. The clue is no longer on
LBNA, but you can still find it on AtlasQuest (that's where it is
listed as "missing".

It's well worth checking out. What a pleasure to find it, and in good
shape, too.
