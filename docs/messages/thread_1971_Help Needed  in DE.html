Mea Culpa

From: otisfriends | Date: 2006-09-11 15:41:37 UTC
We screwed up. We left a few cards for stamping in at Along the Way"
with a message to e-mail us for cards with the old stamp when what we
should have done was to leave stamped cards there, or better yet,
given an alert. Spring Chick suggested planting an entirely new box
in a slightly different location for people who already found the old
box, but the spot for this one is nearly perfect. In nearly four
years in a spot very close to the water (after four seasons of winter
storms), the log book was still bone dry. Long story short, there is
no stamp there currently (just a small temporary log), but we have
several cards with the old stamp for anyone who can identify the name
on the campaign poster at the old bait shop and/or the color of the
pencil we left behind. Will replant in the same location with a new
stamp as soon as we get the chance.

Otis' Friends
