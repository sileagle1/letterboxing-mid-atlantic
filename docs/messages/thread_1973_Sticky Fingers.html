Sticky Fingers

From: tightlinefishing | Date: 2006-09-12 03:34:34 UTC
I appreciate all the recent traffic to my first letter box, Red Sox
Nation - A in Wilmington, DE. I went to check on the box today to see
if everything was in order. What I found was that someone had stolen
the two red and blue ink dot stampers I put in there and the practice
stamp pad. I don't even know if the thief is a member of this group,
but I've got to say it's pretty pathetic to be so selfish as to steal
from someone else's letterbox. Are you that cheap or lazy that you
won't go out and buy your own supplies? The ink dots are like 4 for
$3 at Micheal's and the scratch pad was 25 cents at the drug store.
The two different inks are there for a reason which you know if you've
found the box. Yes, I replaced them and I would appreciate it if the
culprit would grow up and maybe get a paper route to pay for his/her
own supplies!


Fishman
