From: Suzanne Coe | Date: 2006-09-15 01:22:25 UTC
Let's see--2 days in Chester & Delaware County.  If I have a good map & can do the math, Chester Co. Hat Trick.  Brandywine River Trail, both boxes, maybe visit the museum.  South on 202 over the state line to Grapes of Wrath?  If I'm that close, spend $6 & a couple of hours for Into the Woods (in DE @ Brandywine Creek SP).  If I spent the $6, might as well go to the other side of the park for Brandywine M&M and (very discreetly) Le Jolie Rouge (it's almost Talk Like A Pirate Day, after all).  Zenith is close, practically a driveby.  North to Ridley Creek SP--if I can only do one box, Silent Doug's Ridley Creek box (looong hike but worth it).  Also the Bird Series if there's time.
 
Tyler's in BUCKS Co, but there IS excellent boxing there.  If I can only do one box?  The Police.  After that, everything's good.
