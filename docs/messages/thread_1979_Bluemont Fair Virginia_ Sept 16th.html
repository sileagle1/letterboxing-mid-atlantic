Bluemont Fair Virginia, Sept 16th

From: theendgang | Date: 2006-09-14 20:12:42 UTC
Hi all,
I and the rest of "The End Gang" will be at the Bluemont Fair as
planned on Saturday, September 16th. Hikinghams will also be attending.
Bulldawg and his crew are also likely to attend, though not until later
in the day. I am meeting Hikinghams at 10:30 am at the EE Lake Store.
Ditsyqouin has been busy and I am not sure she will be actually the
person doing the postal cancellation stamp. I have carved a fair stamp
but it is not planted yet. I will use Saturday as the day to plant and
will post the clues that evening. If you catch me at the fair, you may
stamp in before the plant, or I will tell you how to find it. The stamp
came out better than expected, so I will plan on leaving this as a
limited time box somewhere in Bluemont. It will remain out for one
year, at which time I will retire it and plant a new fair box for next
year. I am still playing with the time frame, so forgive me if I
suddenly pull it ( will give notice). Hope some more people are able to
come out. It looks to be a great wekend and the fair has lots to do.
Safe trails, Koyote
