A day in Bluemont, Va

From: BullDawg | Date: 2006-09-16 23:45:18 UTC
Sorry more didnt come out for a small meet-n-greet at the Bluemont
Fair today, but thanks to Koyote and his family for coming out and
setting it up. Great to meet new faces and locals Hikinghams, and
Ditstquoin. The fair was awesome as well. After chatting with
Hikinghams about a local box by Gallant Rogue, I had to go get it.
So the wife, 2 kids and I left about 1:30...went to Bear's Den..and
did Gallant Rogue's "Spirit"..which had been on my list for awhile.
Nice hike, took longer than expected(2.25 hrs), but that was due to
kids and a few more breaks than normal. "Spirit" is a great box.
Awesome hike, and challenging...perfect for my needs..great stamp,
and got the opportunity to hike with 3 through-hikers for a bit on
their 3rd day of a week long hike, pretty cool. The AT is a great
place. I just left it with a sense of accomplishment, and was so
proud of my 2 young daughters for sticking through the long hike,
and the climb back. Was one of those "dad" moments, teaching life
lessons, and toughness. Thats what I take from "spirit"......its
not about the stamp, but that trip will always be with my family.
Its not the count, but moments like this..thanks Rogue, Im a fan.

For those thinking about doing it......do it..It tougher that 95% of
letterboxes but the feeling is worth it..I left a one of a kind
Hitch-Hiker that i finished this morning to get people out there.
Every time i hike the AT, i wonder why i am not on it more. Who
knows, maybe I left another box out there today... ;)

thanks for all those that made today a great family
day....especailly my wife, she is such a trooper..and always smiles.
