Re: Our First Plant

From: JJ Rhinehart | Date: 2006-09-18 02:47:58 UTC
<<Yes, yes--and where did you go????>>
 
Our weekend was wonderful.  We started in Valley Forge and did Mt. Misery in the misty fog on Friday afternoon.  Damp and slippery and treacherous, and absolutely joyful without kids in tow.  :)  The number of deer we saw was amazing, as was their obvious acclimation to humans.  (It still baffles me that there are people who can stare down such a beautiful creature and then take its life for sport, but that's another topic for another board...!)  From there we went on to Wayne and did Mizscarlet's great Walkin' The Dog series.  Tim loves the serenity of the woods; I love the challenge of being sneaky on a busy walking trail.  We both got our kicks on Friday.  :)
 
We had to make side trip (delivering a baby guinea pig to its new owner) Friday night, and by then it was dark, so we had a lovely, romantic anniversary dinner (pizza!) and then tackled a few more boxes on Saturday -- Rose Tree Park, Havertown's Grange Estate (I do so love Harry Potter) and the cute Princess box in Newtown Square (sorry our 6yo missed that one).   By Saturday noon we had found a total of 14 boxes and had hiked nearly 10 miles -- not too shabby!  We headed home early with grand plans to do Ridley Creek, Tyler and the north-of-Phila area (Ft Washington, Horsham, Ambler) as soon as the in-laws will agree to more nights of babysitting madness.
 
Thanks to all for the tips!
-4Rhines
