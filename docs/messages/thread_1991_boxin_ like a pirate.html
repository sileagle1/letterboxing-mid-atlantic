boxin' like a pirate

From: Suzanne Coe | Date: 2006-09-19 16:39:43 UTC
Avast, me hearties! Indeed, sharks of all kinds be fittin' conversation for the day.  None such did I see, but I did weigh anchor and sail a bit up the coast, by the commodore's bridge to a place where I have found plunder before.  'Twas my thinking that some scurvy bilge rat had stolen away t'other morsel, but I looked sharp and spied it out.  A tasty bit of cheese indeed!
 
Apart from finding meself becalmed for the best part of an hour on the voyage home, 'twas a grand morning!  And it mayhap that by the time the moon sets over the yardarm, ye'll hear another tale of treasure....but ye must seek that one for yerselves.
