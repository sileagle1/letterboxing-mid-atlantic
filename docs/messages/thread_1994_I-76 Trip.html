From: the B's | Date: 2006-09-21 02:02:04 UTC
We are planning a cross-PA trip in the near future, and thought
letterboxing would be a great way to break up the tedium of the PA
turnpike. (OK, so it's just a good excuse to find boxes outside our
normal stomping grounds.)

We are driving the PA TP from start to finish. Atlasquest's trip
planning feature suggested visits to boxes in Carlisle, Murrysville
and Zelienopie, as well as one at a rest stop along the turnpike.

Any suggestions for other boxes along the route (esp for the western
part of the state) that we might be overlooking? We have to pick up
some boxes on the return trip as well, of course.

Please e-mail me off list with any thoughts. Thanks!

-- Queen B of the B's, whose head is swimming after looking at the DE
clues today
