Scenery on Bull Run

From: Shelley Shearer | Date: 2006-09-27 22:28:02 UTC
We decided it was too pretty to work today so we went off to do the boxes on Bull Run
Conservatory. What a HIKE! Beautiful trail, but getting up that last bit of it was
killer. Just to add to the fun, while we were packing up and getting ready a jogger came
up the trail. We first thought to make sure letterboxing stuff was put away, but then
realized the man was completely nude and lost track of what we were doing. Just sneakers
and socks, jogging away. What do you do? Nod like you would to other hikers? politely
ignore?? He was in our field of vision for some time so we just found other things to
stare at. Perfect end to the day. : P
