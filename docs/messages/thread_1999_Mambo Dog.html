From: mizscarlet731 | Date: 2006-09-23 10:44:22 UTC
I will be bringing by 13 year old son to the Games in DE, many of you know him as Mambo
Dog. To make him feel more involved in the day and keep his nose out of his PSP and
engaged with real humans I have come up witha plan. Mambo Dog's Letterboxing Super
Store, for all your letterboxing needs. He will have Dougerware, Lock n lock, carving
material and many other letterboxing related items. If anyone has stuff they would like to sell
let me know and he might do it for a small commission. This enterprise is not about making
money but rather to keep my son busy for the day, we won't be making much profit on any of
these items. Let me know if you think this is a good idea.
He will also be offering a carving class for kids. Using non-sharp gouges.
Mizscarlet
