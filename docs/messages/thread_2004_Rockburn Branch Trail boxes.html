Rockburn Branch Trail boxes

From: wood thrush | Date: 2006-09-27 13:14:26 UTC
Recently I've been seeking out the older boxes in the region. I've had
my eye on the "Rockburn Branch Trail" series of three, originally by
Go Against the Flow, then adopted by MD Orphan. They were placed in
Patapsco Valley SP in the Avalon section in Elkridge, MD.

Hit the trail and couldn't find any of them; in fact, the landmarks
also seemed to be gone. The clues are more cryptic once you're on the
trail than they appear to be on paper; indeed the original placer gave
the boxes a rating of "moderate to difficult." I'd assumed this was an
indication of the difficulty of the hike, because of the very steep
and long start to the trail. But now I'm not so sure. Perhaps it is
the clues that are difficult.

Anyway, long story short: This is a beautiful, but lightly used trail,
and so I am inclined to think that the boxes may still be there. LBNA
shows only "founds," with the last being in May 2005. I'll gladly hike
the trail and try again. But I'd appreciate communication offline with
folks who have found the boxes in the past, especially if you found
them rather recently. Just send me an email!

Many thanks, wood thrush
