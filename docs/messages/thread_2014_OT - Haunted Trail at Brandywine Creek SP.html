Brandywine Creek State Park's annual Haunted Trail will be on 10/20
and 21, 7-10 PM each night.

under 12 years are generally scared off before they get their $8 worth.

for the first time.

traveler - just find me (heh heh heh) and tell me the state in which
the original, black-and-white "Night of the Living Dead" was set.
