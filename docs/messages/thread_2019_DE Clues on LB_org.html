DE Games clues have been posted to LB.org to record your finds.
Note that the boxes at the pavilion park have been posted twice - once
under placer "DE Games" to comply with the state park application and
again under placer "sewsowbizzy" with links to Tripod site. Please
post your finds under sewsowbizzy's boxes - which also follow the
titles used on Tripod.

I cannot post to AQ until after the gather because there are multiple
series with multiple carvers. So each box will need to be listed
individually - but several will link to the same Tripod clue.

This is the part of letterboxing that wears me out!

First, the bad news.  The owners of the property where the Western Maryland box is busted me retrieving the box after dusk and asked that searching be limited to daylight hours.  They are very nice people but apparently there have been break-ins in the neighborhood.
 
I wondered where that school bus was.  I didn't see a trace of it.
 
Now, the better news.  Although I poured about a cup of water out of the sailboat box at the boat ramp near Mispillion, the bags held and I added a freezer bag inside the box over all the others.  It looks like there's been high water lately.
 
Now, the really good news.  All of the other boxes I found were in good shape, though you probably gathered that from TLD's report.

Re: Box updates

From: sewsowbizzy | Date: 2006-10-02 14:43:29 UTC
Wow - thanks for the box maint and sit reps.

I had posted (late in the day) on AQ Event details that the School
Bus got mowed and TLD retrieved the shreds of what was left. Go to
AQ for TRAIL info/action.

Without giving away the location of the box "M*D*" it is now noted
that it is not an after dark box to get. But I feel good that you
found it!! Glad I got permission to plant it so they at least know
folks will be poking around. That is not always the case with other
boxes.

As for the SailBoat - I thought those boxes were water tight (can't
remember if it was Dougerware or LnL). Guess I should have checked
the high tide tables BEFORE I went a plantin'

I never said I was good at planting boxes - so of the possible 77
stamps planted, 2 were MIA & were recarved, 1 MIA (Bird & Flower)
eliminated from RoadTrip, and now 1 mowed (School Bus).

Appreciate any other sit reps -- but don't give away the locations if
it is a mystery or the name of the box if it is a MonyLoop or
exCLUsion. Best to list the clue title and which box in the series.

Box On Folks
Judy B
