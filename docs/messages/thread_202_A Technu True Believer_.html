A Technu True Believer!

From: Hikers_n_ Hounds | Date: 2004-07-15 12:02:53 UTC
Well, it's been 6 six days since I unknowingly squatted in poison ivy several times during the night while camping out last weekend (it was dark, there was alcohol involved, etc). When I realized in the morning what I had done I grabbed my brand new bottle of Technu that I bought just for the weekend and followed the directions. And I do not have one speck of poison ivy anywhere in my squattin' area! Nada, zip, zilch, zero. I had forgotten to treat my feet and shins and did get some there but again, I followed the directions and it is gone.
 
I heard about Technu during one of the "what do you carry in your pack" strings either on newboxers or the main list. So far this and a whistle were the two new things I went out and got...and just in time. I picked the Technu up at CVS for about $6? Worth every penny I spent.
 
Thought I would share....
 
H&H
