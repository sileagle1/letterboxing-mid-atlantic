From: Debbie Kotlarek | Date: 2006-10-12 01:58:21 UTC
We definitely recognized those 5 posts in your photos! Luckily by the time
we went there on Monday the water had receded and no swimming was required
to retrieve the box. The only hazard was the mud that Martini Man got on his
shoes. It's amazing to see how much water covered the box (for how long?)
and that it survived just fine. Luckily someone had double-bagged
everything in that box (all game piece baggies and logbook/stamp baggie had
been placed in another sturdy outer baggie). I'm a strong believer in
double-bagging and I'm sure that's what helped the contents survive, since
there was a 1/4" of water inside the box when we opened it.
