One more for Valley Garden Park

From: mother_duck_n_brood | Date: 2006-10-13 02:04:19 UTC
Finally hiked through Valley Garden Park last week and found Rosebud's
new "D&K's Wedding Box" and the two out of three from H&H's "What A
Long Strange Trip It's Been" series. I was so impressed with the
carvings and inspired by a spot I found on a side trail, that I added
my own today. It is by far my most detailed carving endeavor to date
and I hope that it contributes to a trend for a very artful park.

http://letterboxing.org/BoxView.php?
boxnum=26344&boxname=Under_the_Fairy_Moon

I can't wait to see what H&H plan to add to the series and to see the
retun of box#1's stamp!
