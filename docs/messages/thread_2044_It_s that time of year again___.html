Yup, just a friendly reminder that it is hunting season here in the
Commonwealth of Pennsylvania again, so wear yer orange if you are in
parks or on gamelands that permit hunting.

While there is hunting year round in Pennsylvania, deer season is the
big one, and there are a lot of hunters out there, so there are some
guidelines your should follow.

Archery hunting has already begun.

Muzzleloader season begins this Saturday and the full open season opens
on October 19.

I also suggest not boxing at all in the permitted areas on October 14 or
19 or any Saturday during the season. Again, there are alot of hunters
out there.

Hunting is still illegal on Sundays in Pennsylvania.

Other states have differeing laws regarding hunting, so be sure to check
on them before heading out.
