brains have left

From: Myke Scholtes | Date: 2006-10-14 21:40:57 UTC
So I went back to Bucks County today to check on some of my early
plants. The Ring-Ding-A-Ding series, while quite poorly carved, is
still in good shape.

However, "Brains," my first (and perhaps most challenging) mystery
box, is no more. But the good news is...I found the empty container
and Ziploc bags strewn around. Well, that's not the good news. The
good news is I also found the stamp. So I'll be reinventing Brains as
a hitchhiker. I've got loads of obscure classic rock lyrics to use as
a name for the box... "Thinking is the best way to travel" or "Take a
giant step outside your mind" or who knows what else. Brains, while a
cool clue (and a much better stamp than Ring-Ding-a-Ding), was in a
completely dumb spot. I'm not surprised it was muggled.

So my brains have finally left me.

My parents always knew they would.

IV
