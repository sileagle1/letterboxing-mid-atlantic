I sure don't. I think I may have formed a permanent relationship with that guy in the Eclispe in front of me and we will be announcing our wedding later this year. All are invited and there will be a special Wedding Event Box in the shape of a GW Bridge Toll booth.
 
Thanks again to lboxingbee and her ever-lovin' husband for doing a fine and wonderful job as they always do!

'Boxtoberfest

From: letterboxingbee | Date: 2006-10-23 15:23:33 UTC
  <just starting to come out of the fog>
 
WOW!!  
 
What can I say.  'Boxtoberfest keeps getting better and better each year thanks to all you lovely people.  I was worried that the location may be a little too far for some of you to travel to, but thankfully there were plenty of 'boxers that did make it.  Thank you to everyone that came out on Saturday.  From the boxers that arrived early and stayed late to the 'boxers that came but could only stay for a few hours many many thanks for making the event.  I had sooo much fun speaking with "oldbies" and was soo happy to meet all the "newbies".  I was soo glad that Miss Moon decided to make the trip down here instead of staying home and "being mad at her self"!  :) 
 
Congrats to D&A for winning the raffle and congrats to Von Der Insells for winning the 50/50.
 
I'm sorry that there wasn't much "'going on" (carving class, log book class etc) but I find "going with the flow" to be much more casual and easier than trying to cram everything in, in only a few hours.  I'm sorry this year there wasn't a group photo either.  I had hoped and tried to keep that on schedule but as you all know there wasn't one.  Thankfully I took lots of photos and plan on posting them real soon. 
 
Sometime this week my hubby will be taking the food to the South Jersey food bank and I will keep you posted as to how much you'all donated.  It sure felt like more than last years 91 lbs. of food. 
 
I also wanted to say a special thank you to everyone that exchanged with my niece, "Princess Ann" (Bobbi Ann) <the little girl with the makeup on>.  I've been telling her about letterboxing for what seems like forever and this was her first "official" gathering and her first "real" exchange with other 'boxers.  She had soo much fun and before I knew it she was sitting at the exchange table and asking people for exchanges.  I was so proud of her - I think she's an official letterboxer now. 
 
Well, thanks again to everyone that came out and celebrated the day with us.  I had a blast!!  I can't wait til next years gathering.  Now... where... to hold it.  I'm taking suggestions for next years location (I had 2 ideas given to me at the gathering)  Put you're suggestions in for next years site <I can't guarantee I'll use them though>.  I'd really like to stay in New Germany but may consider a site in Eastern PA (close to Philly). 
 
 Thanks again to all the attendees.  'Boxtoberfest gets better and better each year.  I'm off to start thinking up some 'evil' for next years event. 
 
 
going back to lurkdom,
 
"letterboxingbee"
~Bev
