Yes ! Alcoholic Drink at Every Bar... (It;s a Pub Crawl!)

From: gallant_rogue | Date: 2006-10-28 23:49:19 UTC
Nobody HAS to do anything they dont want to do... you can participate
as a "non-competitive" team but... to qualify for a prize in a
drinking event... your team has to drink! Intoxication is one of the
obstacles or handicaps to overcome. As Poison Ivy (the founder of this
event) once said.... "this is a pub crawl... alcohol will happen".
Sure non-drinkers are welcome.. but the designated drinkers will have
to take up the slack... at least 25% (two or more people) of each team
must consume an alcoholic drink at every bar. The 25% can be the same
people over and over... or different people each time... but at least
two members of each team must consume an alcoholic beverage at each
location... and everybody must make it to the finish line to stamp in
the event log book. (Of course if you are a desinated drinker you
should not be a designated driver) It might be a good idea to share
expenses as a team.
