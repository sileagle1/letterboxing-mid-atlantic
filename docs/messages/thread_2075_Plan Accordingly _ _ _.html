Lurkers out there reading this fun missive, please be advised that
the "smack" talk about who will drink the most may SEEM reckless and
irresponsible... The Elbows, the Crawlers...Hikers, Hounds, BullDawgs
& Koyotes have all made plans to stay in Old Town and absolutely will
NOT be operating motor vehicles. I commend their responsible choice
and strongly urge that all those participating plan ahead. Stay over
in town and have brunch with us in the morning! Yes you will get
arrested and lose your license if drink and drive... but our real
concern is your safety and the safety of your family.

The Old Town Hotel 480 King St., Alexandria, VA 22314 is in the middle
of the Pub course... a seven minute walk from anywhere... $129 plus
tax ...Here is a link to their website: http://www.hotel-ot.com/


PS - Gallant Rogue will drink the most!
