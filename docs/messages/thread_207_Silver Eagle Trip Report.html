Silver Eagle Trip Report

From: Silver Eagle | Date: 2004-07-18 22:03:44 UTC
Well I'm back from my trip to Washington D.C., Baltimore
and Philadelphia, and I need a vacation from my vacation!
Boy there is a lot to see in these areas and my family tried
to see them all. I did manage to squeeze in some letterboxing
and ended up finding 27 boxes and placing 6 (one in each state
we visited, including D.C.). The clues have been posted so
I hope you will find them soon. Here are the boxes I attempted:

Scott's Cove(2) - Alive & Well (7/9)
Rocky Gorge(3) - A&W (7/9)
Supplee Lane(3) - A&W (7/9)
George Washington - A&W (7/9) (this box was placed by my
brother Baby Bear and I just had to find it)
Rock Creek Park(2) - A&W (7/10)
National Zoo - Not Found (7/10)
Stack's House - A&W (7/10) With bonus stamp
Huntley Meadows Park - Frog Not Found, Dragonfly A&W (7/11)
Silver Spaceship - NF Found, but not sure where to look (7/11)
Jones Point Lighthouse - NF, but fishermen kept me from looking
too long (7/11)
Radio Days - A&W (7/11)
Inventor - A&W (7/12)
Orion - A&W (7/12)
Mr. Jefferson - NF (7/12)
Skull & Bones - NF (7/12)
Lion, Witch & Wardrobe - Lion A&W (7/14) (I ran out of daylight
so could not look for other two)
Pit Stop - A&W (7/15)
Gas-N-Go - A&W (7/15) (also found geocache nearby)
Future Discovery Road Runner - A&W (7/15)
What A Long Strange Trip It's Been(3) - A&W (7/15) (the second
one had washed down into
a gully so I replaced it)
Ben Franklin Series - Ran out of time after placing mine
70 Million Years Ago - A&W (7/16)
Pinchy Pinchy - A&W (7/16)
Patterson Park Pagoda - NF (7/17)
17 Year Ick - A&W (7/17) (Ran out of time to look for others in
this park)

Thanks to all of you for making my trip even better! As a
suggestion, I recommend somebody place a box in the National
Arboretum. It is beautiful, free and not run by NPS.

Silver Eagle
