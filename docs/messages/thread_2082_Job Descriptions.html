Job Descriptions

From: gallant_rogue | Date: 2006-11-01 04:51:04 UTC
Spokesperson - Represents the team and performs the official
communicating for the team with event organizers.

Historian - Maintains notes and records. Ideally is the team member
somewhat familiar with historical landmarks and locations in Old Town.

Accountant - Responsible for team funds, making payments on behalf of
the team.

Evidence Technician - Properly handle, transport and manage evidence
collected preventing contamination that would ruin a case and harm the
chances of conviction.

Navigator - Tracks location of team at all times. Provide guidance for
most direct routes between destinations.
