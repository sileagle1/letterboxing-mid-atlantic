Hitchhiker protocol

From: leabhar314 | Date: 2006-11-02 14:28:43 UTC
Well, I finally found a hitchhiker (after all this time I was starting
to wonder if they were really any out there).

So my question is whether there is any protocol/tradition/accepted
preference for how far it should travel before I plant it again. It
was in one box of a series, and I figured putting it in another box in
the same series would be kind of lame, but should I wait till I'm
traveling out of state? Is across town good enough? Would anyone
really care as long as it gets planted again?

I've sent a message to the placer to see if they have a preference,
but I'm wondering what the general feeling is.

~ Leabhar

Re: [lbma] Hitchhiker protocol

From: john@johnsblog.com | Date: 2006-11-02 14:45:27 UTC
I think that across town is fine. You might check to see
that you don't put it into a box that it's recently
visited, but otherwise just go for it. The most important
thing is to not let it settle to the bottom of your
letterboxing pack where it can be forgotten! :o)

RE: [lbma] Hitchhiker protocol

From: Pete Mandeville | Date: 2006-11-02 15:18:51 UTC
I think it depends where you are. Being in Wilmington, DE, if I get a
hitchhiker, I like to get it across state lines, MD or PA are very close. I
also try to consider the traffic to the box. If it's an obscure letterbox
that gets visited once every 4 or 5 months, I'll hold it and find something
that's visited more frequently.

Fishman

Re: Hitchhiker protocol

From: otisfriends | Date: 2006-11-02 15:37:48 UTC
Same series is lame. Across town is fine if you won't be going farther
soon, but I do like to move as far as I can. I used to go for high
traffic boxes, but now I like putting them in boxes that have a higher
degree of difficulty (mysteries or physically demanding boxes) as a
kind of reward. As long as you take good care of them and don't wait
too long I don't think it matters much.

I tend to agree. I go out of state quite a bit, so I like to make them jump

them in a mystery or WOM box just to make victory a little sweeter for the
next person. But that's just me. :)

Oh, and I always contact the placer of the HH and tell them where I found it.
I don't tell them where I left it unless they ask. :)
