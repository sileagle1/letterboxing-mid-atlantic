Day in DE

From: Suzanne Coe | Date: 2006-11-06 03:17:19 UTC
Well I am sorry I missed the crawl, but we had a great day in DE.  Nathan & my sisters Wendy (Shutterbug) & Lynn joined me for the orienteering event at White Clay Creek SP.  Fortunately we were not trying to set any records, because we stopped to meet & greet along the way--we met the Murphys, a family of 'boxers who were writing clues to a new box for the park!  They told us how to find an unlisted box they had hidden about a year ago--it had been found by a lot of geocachers, but we think we were the first letterboxers to stamp in.  We also visited the Wolf Box, 2 of my boxes (I just posted the clues for one of them, which was previously WOM), The Crag!!!, and Psychiker & Syner's Ticking Tomb box, "Listen."  A beautiful day to be out in the woods, with good company!
