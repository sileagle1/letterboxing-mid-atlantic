Throughout the day today I found myself smiling at funny remembrances
of the weekend (Poison Ivy's charm, Bull Dawg's dry sense of humor,
Shell's giggle, the Hedge's zest, Scarab's kindness, George's unending
energy and enthusiasm are just a few of my lasting memories). The
experience definitely left me wanting more. For me the coolest part
was the people that I met and making a team out of strangers. It's
wierd to finally meet some of you after reading your posts, finding
your boxes and passing along your hitch hikers for the past year.

The crawl was a great way to celebrate my first year as a letterboxer.
It was quite a distraction form my surburban housewife/mom life that
I live. I am fondly looking forward to another event somtime soon.

Until then I go back to the things that make me "Mother Earth"...my
kids, my pets, my garden and my Girl Scouts (not the ones on Captain
Ken's boat).

God bless you all,
