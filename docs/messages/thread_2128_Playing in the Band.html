Playing in the Band

From: Hikers and Hounds | Date: 2006-11-07 12:05:49 UTC
"Playing
Playing in the band
Daybreak
Daybreak on the land"
 
Saturday, Dec. 2
Tyler State Park, Newtown PA
 
Well, the days are short and the leaves are dropping, my time of year. C'mon out to Tyler State Park in Newtown Park for a relaxing little gather where the Battle of the Bands still rages on. Tyler has evolved into just what I hoped it would when I started the BOB, an ever growing collection of a varitey of boxes. Many are based on peoples favorite musical groups, but there's also a Dr. Who there, Beer and all kinds of cool stuff added since we were there the last time. Bring your favorite band to plant if you haven't already done so. I've got 3 more to plant myself before the gather. Plus a disc golf course if anyone is interested in playing.
 
So Dude and I will be there from about 10:00 - 3:00, then whoever is left can join us a Isaac Newtown's Pub for some great beer and heat!
 
Dude will be serving grilled cheese and soup, coffee, tea, hot chocolate. Mark your calendars if it sounds like your kinda thing and dress warm. I'm going to look into getting the pavilion with the fire pit, but if not we'll have a fire in the grills again and I'll bring my propane heater.
I'll post this on AQ sometime later today/tomorrow with the exact location in the park. 
 
Have a great day and don't forget to vote!
