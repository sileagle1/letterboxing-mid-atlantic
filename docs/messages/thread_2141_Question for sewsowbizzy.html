From: Judy B | Date: 2006-11-15 18:09:21 UTC
Roz- I bought a real pond liner from Home Depot (about $60) and cut
it into about 2ft squares - large enough to drape over the box so
rocks/branches could be laid around edges to hold in place.

Then I bought all the 4oz bottles of rubber cement at ACMoore when it
was on sale - took about 3/4bottle for each square - I spread
liberally. Then sprinkled with that wonder DE sand soil. Tried
leaves but they either didn't stick or crumbled away. Don't try
painting it will just flake off. But if you add enough debris over
box that should suffice.

So you might ask at a nursery or landscaper if they are replacing
someone's pond liner and ask to buy (free?) the old leaky one. Sorry
I didn't think of that before but I was on a mission.

ssb
