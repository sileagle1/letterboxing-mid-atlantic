From: nattybumppolbna | Date: 2006-11-25 00:45:15 UTC
I am a frequent visitor to Franklin Maps. What a place! They have a great selection of
USGS maps of PA and maps of all types of everywhere else in the world. Their travel/
hiking/backpacking book selection is also very nice. You can plan your next 20 stamp
series while browsing their collection of "Great Hikes in__________" books.

The staff...eh, not so helpful - knowledgeable, but busy on the phone. It's worth the trip
even if you're not going to buy. I often crib titles of books I didn't know existed and buy
them on half.com.

I'm surprised there is no Mapsurfer box there. Wait! What did I say?
