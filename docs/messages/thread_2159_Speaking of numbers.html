Speaking of numbers

From: Nathan Brown | Date: 2006-11-25 05:30:13 UTC
A few were complaining about the stance of counting New England boxes as
½ and CT boxes as ¼. So, today the hairyleggers
have come up with a friendlier way of counting finds. The new formula
is as follows:

All CT boxes count as 1
All other New England boxes count as 2
All boxes in other states count as 3
All international boxes (except those in Dartmoor and Canada) count as 4
Canada boxes are 3
Dartmoor boxes are 1
All Mapsurfer boxes count as 10

Please recount you finds accordingly ;-)
