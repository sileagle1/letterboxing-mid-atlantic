Any color is easily camoflauged with Camo Duct Tape -- but be forwarned I have found Walmart is carrying either a different brand or Duct has ruined their product.  Do Not Buy Camo Duct with Oak Leaves on it.  This does not tear and the scissors quickly get gummy.  Also it Doesn't Stick!  I must have hit 5 Walmarts before I found an old supply in DE and bought out the shelf (of 5 rolls).
