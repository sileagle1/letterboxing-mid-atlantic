What a great day!

From: Hikers and Hounds | Date: 2006-11-27 00:11:03 UTC
Well Dude and I were planning to do some maintenance today locally. I am still in the dog house with him after leaving him to go to CT for a couple of days. My s-i-l is a very nice person but doesn't allow dogs in her home. Which is why this is only my second trip in 10 years!
 
Anyway, I promised the old man we'd get out today. Cyclonic called and asked what we were doing and said he'd tag along. I was replacing box 3 of Treasure Hunt, planted Black Beard (Arrrrg!) found a cool little surprise under some SPOR, and headed to his HHH when we noticed there were some pople in the area. Turns out they were new boxers, the Rosens! Very cool. We did an exchange and chatted for awhile. By the time I got everything done it was already 3:00. The plan was to hit Neshaminy, Tyler, then Allegheny Art Supplies in Newtown before 4. Cyclonic said let's ditch Tyler, hit the art supply store and finish at Isaac Newtown, my favorite pub. Say no more! I shouted.
 
Deb from Team Radetoan told us about Allegheny Art Supplies and they had some cool stuff, and carried Master Carve and Speedball Carving tools but no "Pink Stuff", my favorite. So I see Cylconic testing out his latest pick up lines on a comley young red head named Margo. I asked her if she carries any "pink stuff". But, alas, both of us get shot down! But she did say if I call her with the correct name for it, she'll try to order it. Don't know if Cyclonic got any "follow up" instructions as well, LOL!
 
So we hit the pub and camped out and drank some great beers, ate some awesome food and here I am sittin' at my PC feelin' all fat and sassy. What a great day!
 
H&H
