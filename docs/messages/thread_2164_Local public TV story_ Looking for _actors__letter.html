Local public TV story. Looking for "actors"/letterboxers.

From: thedoubtfulguests | Date: 2006-11-27 18:06:54 UTC
I have been contacted by a local public television reporter who
wants to do a piece on letterboxing. I know a lot of people are
afraid of publicity. But I think public television, and only local,
is a good demographic for some letterboxing publicity. I wouldn't
agree to help if it was a regular network news show.

They want to film a couple of people hunting for the Orion letterbox
in DC. No problem with permission since it is my box.

They are looking for letterboxers who have not already found Orion
so they can film them finding the box. The tentative shoot date is
next Tuesday morning, December 5th. This is subject to weather and
schedule changes. They do not need experienced letterboxers. Newbies
might even be better. They say it is not a "family" show so they are
looking for adults and not kids. They would prefer to film a couple
of people working together.

Another possibillity would be for me to rehide the box and alter the
clues, so that even if you've found Orion before you can do it
again. (I plan to carve a new stamp.)

If you are interested, or have someone to recommend, please write me
off list. I know this is a weekday and that is difficult. If you
know someone willing but only available on a weekend let me know
that too.

Scarab of the Doubtful Guests
