From: brendan_hickey13 | Date: 2006-11-29 18:02:19 UTC
Hi, all

On Sunday, 4 February 07, I will join the Lewes Polar Bears for the
11th consecutive year. At 1:00 PM, we will hit the Atlantic Ocean at
Rehoboth Beach, Delaware.

Yeah, it's probably a bad idea - I have run through snow and ice in
previous years, tend to turn pink from head to toe, and feel cold for
most of the rest of the day.

It's a bad idea for a good cause, though. Last year, 2390 Bears
raised over $420,000 for Special Olympics Delaware.

I'd much rather run into 35-degree water than ask for donations, even
for a great cause like this, so this is my pitch.

Here's my official donation Web site:

http://www.firstgiving.com/brendanhickey


Syner and I will be at the "Playing in the Band" gathering this
coming Saturday, and I will offer a stamp to anyone who contributes
any amount to Special Olympics Delaware, at the gathering or at
another time.

Thanks much!

Psychiker
