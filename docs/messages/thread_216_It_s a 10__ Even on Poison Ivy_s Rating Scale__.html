Fellow boxers,
 
I just found tonight after work (the addiction is getting worse and worse...this was after an 11 hour day no less) two of the best boxes I have ever seen! Fleur de Lis "Ben Franklin - Scientist" and "Ben Franklin Stateman" are just wonderful. The logs are so cool, and the stamps are excellent. I only was able to get the first two and was running out of light as Dude and I were running out of the park. I can't wait to find the rest!
 
Fleur, my only suggestion is I think you have the name of the road wrong. The entrance is on Winchester and Old Newton. I believe the clue says Woodward or something? I didn't notice that Winchester changed names anywhere but I could be wrong.
 
Oh, and wear long light pants if you have them. Lot's and lot's of stinging nettle for Scientist.
 
Great boxes!! Can't wait to get the rest, stinging nettle and all!
 
Irene & Dude - H&H
