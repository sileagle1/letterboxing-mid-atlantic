New boxer in my area!

From: rozebud.rm | Date: 2006-11-29 19:32:30 UTC
When I got home from my trip to DE for the Games, I was in the
office at lunchtime logging my finds. Kelley, a teacher who works
with the Shakespeare Festival on our education programs, stopped by
and asked me about my log, so I told her about the hobby. She was
interested, and asked me to email her some links.

I didn't hear back about it from her, but then last week, when we
were talking about a study guide she is preparing for us, she told
me she had bought a carving kit and downloaded some clues and was
taking her hubby and 2 kids out over the weekend to box! She'd made
sig stamps for herself and the kids, and they were all excited about
it.

She called again on Monday to say that they had a blast! The kids
are already bugging her about going out again, and even her husband
(grudgingly) admitted to having a good time. PLUS - they planted a
box!

So I ran out yesterday morning before work to snag it...seems she's
a better boxer than I, because I couldn't find it! Couldn't figure
out the last little bit in her clues. I need to call or email her
and tell her that she is smarter than me...well, either that, or
it's gone missing already. The spot where I THINK it was planted is
a pretty exposed area (a rocky hillside right next to a parking
lot). We shall see.

Here's the link, in case you are interested... fun clues; needed a
little googling around to fill in the blanks.
