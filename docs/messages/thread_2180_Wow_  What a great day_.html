We just wanted to say thank you to Hikers and Hounds for a fantastic
day at the Playing in the Band gathering in Tyler State Park,
Newtown, PA. It was so good to see many of the same people we met at
Boxtoberfest IV and a whole lot of people we have never met before.

great...need I say more?

Irene, you really did a wonderful job! The whole family enjoyed it!
It was nice to meet Mr. Irene in person too.

Dude, thanks for letting my kids take you for a walk. All I've heard
this afternoon was how much my kids want a dog, but I think we'll
just come and play with you once in a while if that's okay.

Special thanks to Mambo Dog who was extremely patient with my son
Tommy. He really enjoyed working through your "Joker's Hand" clues
(thank you for your help) and loves the stamps. Tommy has already
asked if you will be at the next gathering. He had a lot of fun with
you this afternoon.

And of course it was great to celebrate two special occasions today.
Happy Birthday Jan! (Team Little Dog) Also, Happy 1st Anniversary to
Psychiker and Syner! And many, many happy returns to all of you!
Thanks for letting us share in your special day.

Hope to see you all again real soon!

Debbie (Radetoan)

As special thanks from Dude to all of his little boxing buddies. It's great when there are kids at a gather who want to walk him. Usually we get in our car, drive to a gather with him panting in the back seat all the way from excitement.Tthen we get there and he has to lay around a pavilion all day while his Mom socializes and maybe does a little boxing if he's lucky. So when there are kids to walk him he has a much better time. Thanks to all of the younger boxers who made such a fuss over him. He was king for a day!
 
Next year, same place same weekend. but I am going to look into maybe renting the hostel for the night and have a sleepover to whomever wants to stay for a second gather I think I'll call Twightlight at Tyler.
 
Thanks again all,
 
Irene, Dude and Mr. Irene - Hikers and Hounds
