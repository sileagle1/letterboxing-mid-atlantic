Never too cold to box

From: Nathan Brown | Date: 2006-12-08 19:28:55 UTC
Sheba and I had a fine morning boxing in Bensalem this morning. It was
cold, and windy, and cold, but we found all that we searched out.

We started with the Neshaminy HHH. I let her follow the clues and I
just tagged along.

Afterwards we headed over to Bensalem Central Park and the Super Hero
Series, all of which we found in good shape. The last box of the series
was a bit tricky, and were it not for Sheba, I don't think I would have
figured it out.

Off to Armstrong Park, we picked up the Creepy Crawlers series. The
boxes were all close together, so we grabbed them all at once, stamped
in in the car, and then replanted them. Of course, we had to stamp the
tick in blue ;-)

Deb, there was a big spider in the last box, but don't worry, we killed
it for you.

All of the stamps are very nicely done. Guess I better do some work and
get some more boxes in the township.
--
