There seems to be some confusion regarding the link for the voting
booth on Atlasquest. The information for the link has **NOT** been
posted on the front page of AQ so anyone just going to the site won't
know about it the vote.

The voting booth is located under the link for your own individual
page on Atlas Quest, provided you are a registered member. Hover your
cursor over "My Page" and you will see it listed along with your
logbook, mailbox, profile, etc. It is listed at the bottom.

Please go to the voting booth and make your vote heard.
