Hi,

The boxes are mine and were taken off the website when I received
reports that one was missing rather than disappoint (my Carlisle
Indian School Box went missing at around the same time.)

I checked on them today- was feeling pretty good-- boxes 1, 3 and 4
are still there, box 2 is missing--which is very disappointing as it
was probably the better of all the stamps there carved just about 5
years ago when I was a new boxer.

There is another box in the same place as 2 was--in fact under the
same stone--planted by a group of 30 preschool bible school kids
whose teacher I had tried to disuade from bringing a group that large
and that young to letterbox. (?????) The box they planted does not
have a stamp in it--only a log book-- and doesn't look as though it's
listed anywhere.

I've cleaned out the geotrinkets left by the pre-school group and
emptied them of some water. The remaining boxes appear to be in good
shape. I don't know if I'll relist them or not, but the clues L'bug
found are good. I did pull them a couple of years ago for a bit
after some vandalism in the area.

Box 4 shares a spot with a geocache so if you've never geocached you
can take a look to see what they look like.


Hope that helps.
