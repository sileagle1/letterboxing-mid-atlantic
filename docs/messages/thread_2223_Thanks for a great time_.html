Thanks for a great time!

From: brendan_hickey13 | Date: 2006-12-31 01:22:40 UTC
We spent the afternoon at Sadsbury Preserve, where we met HikerDad,
Sir Ty, and HikerDad's father (HikerGrandDad??) - very nice folks, and
we kept thinking that HikerDad looked like Joaquin Phoenix, were
thinking autograph instead of exchange. :)

It was about 3:15, winter sun and unfamiliar park and an unknown
amount of hill between us and the RAV4, when we decided to push it.
We found 4 more boxes and swept the preserve before heading to Victory
Brewing for dinner.

Syner and I just wrapped up our first journal. Since 9 Apr, we are
P10 F234 X50 E5, and mostly, we're grateful for folks who carve such
incredible toys and then tell us stories so that we can share them.
