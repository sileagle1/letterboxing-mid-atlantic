Letterboxing Goals for 2007

From: Nathan Brown | Date: 2006-12-31 19:27:51 UTC
So, I've been putting some thought into what I want to do in 2007, and
come up with a few goals:

Many more plants. I have about 40 or so now, I would like to get that
up to 150 by the end of the year. I have so many ideas, it is time to
implement them. Here are some of the boxes you can look forward to in
the new year:
- 7 Wonders of Bucks County
- More Bunny Hunt boxes (finally)
- More Bucks County Haunts boxes
- Alice in Wonderland series
- Zelda series

Update my website. I don't expect it to be a fantastic, interactive
site, but just get some thoughts, ideas, and information up there for
folks. Inofrmation like exacto carving, what to look for when planting,
and how to build a micro box. Things that I am stong at and can help
others with.

More finds. I really slacked off at the end of the year and have not
been out much. I want to get into better shape, and one way to do that
is to get out there and find some boxes. I have to time, now I just
have to use it.

So, what is everyone else planning for the upcoming year?
--
Nathan Brown

So, I've been putting some thought into what I want to do in 2007, and
come up with a few goals:

Many more plants. I have about 40 or so now, I would like to get that
up to 150 by the end of the year. I have so many ideas, it is time to
implement them. Here are some of the boxes you can look forward to in
the new year:
- 7 Wonders of Bucks County
- More Bunny Hunt boxes (finally)
- More Bucks County Haunts boxes
- Alice in Wonderland series
- Zelda series

Update my website. I don't expect it to be a fantastic, interactive
site, but just get some thoughts, ideas, and information up there for
folks. Inofrmation like exacto carving, what to look for when planting,
and how to build a micro box. Things that I am stong at and can help
others with.

More finds. I really slacked off at the end of the year and have not
been out much. I want to get into better shape, and one way to do that
is to get out there and find some boxes. I have to time, now I just
have to use it.

So, what is everyone else planning for the upcoming year?
