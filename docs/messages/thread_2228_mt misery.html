From: Suzanne Coe | Date: 2007-01-03 05:22:20 UTC
generally the "reply to placer" does work, in that the email goes to whatever address is on the account--doesn't always mean the placer will respond/care anymore, and even if they do sometimes it takes them a day or three (life getting in the way). 
 
I'd say if you added baggies to a box & emailed to tell what you did & offered to do more, you've definitely done your part. You could always sub out the box if the old one was too cracked for repairs & risking damage to the contents.  Or add an additional logbook if the old one was soggy.

Re: [lbma] Re: mt misery

From: MaryAnn Lockard | Date: 2007-01-03 10:57:32 UTC
There is log your find and the contact the placer
option. It makes me nuts when people only use the log
your find. I get a message the box has been found or
not found but no more info. I like to know the box
conditon, how the clues worked ect. With the just log
in option you don't get that. A lot of boxers who
don't read the lists may not know about the contact
the placer option. I always respond when someone
e-mails me even if just to say thanks.
