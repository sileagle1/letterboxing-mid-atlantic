From: thedoubtfulguests | Date: 2007-01-08 03:25:18 UTC
Night Owl, Tuxie, and I went out and picked up Rudolph at Red Rocks
just before it was pulled after the holidays. A fine warm day, but oh
that mud everywhere. We saw a group of geocachers. Then we spotted a
family with a big notebook searching around some logs. I kept wanting
to meet up with them but we were on different tracks. Then they passed
us while we were stamping in. Finally we met up at Mr. Toadys. It's
always great to run into boxers on the trail and do a trail exchange.
Nice folks. Busy day in the park.

The Rudolph series was in a great park, a nice tour, fun, story clues,
and really cute, well carved stamps. Catch it next year if you can.

Scarab
