Had a great weekend and much success boxing. I was 9 for 9, found a
mystery box, a hitch hiker and dropped off a hitch hiker . . . yet I
still have yet to come across a single boxer on the trails! I must
have been SO CLOSE on both days b/c by the log dates on LBNA

strange was that I was logging in at this one box and I saw a man with
white hair approacing me from about 40 yards. I put my head down to
fisnish signing in and when I looked up again, he had turned around
and walked off. It was SO STRANGE b/c there isn't even a trail
running through where I was!
