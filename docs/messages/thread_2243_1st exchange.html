Hey everyone,

I had my first exchange on Friday when Cyclonic stopped by the Visitor Center Museum. It was great to put a face to a name.

It was pretty funny because I greeted him like any other visitor and when about my business. When I saw him I thought he looked like an avid hiker but we get a lot of those.

Later he hung around the guestbook and then left. My colleague happened to glance at the guestbook a couple of minutes later and says... " Mark, I think this might be for you." I check it out and Nathan had left his stamp. I run outside and he is gone. I'm kicking myself for not realizing who he was.

He came back later and I am 99% sure it was him. He asked if I was Mark and we talked quite awhile.

We had quite a nice chat and exchanged his great sig stamp for my just carved very rudimentary family stamp.

Anyway, it was very exciting. Sheba, sorry I missed you on Tuesday Hopefully I'll catch you another time.

Mark aka M of Team PM JET
