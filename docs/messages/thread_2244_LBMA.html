I think of LBMA as more of a letterboxing state of mind than an actual geographic region. Really. While the bulk of us might be from PA,NJ,DE,DC,VA,WV and please don't forget Miss Moon up in NY! And what about Team New Hampshire, who drove all the way down here just to hang out with us. We have folks from all over. I sometimes think of us as east coasters but then we'd be leaving out Rozebud, Wisconson Hiker and Martini Man.
 
So we're really from all over. People who love the hobby, try not to to take themselves too seriously and just try and squeeze as much joy as possible out of the short time we have on this planet.
 
To me, that's LBMA.
 
H&H
