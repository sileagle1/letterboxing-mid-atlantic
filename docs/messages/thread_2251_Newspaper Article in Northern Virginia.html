Newspaper Article in Northern Virginia

From: BullDawg | Date: 2007-01-11 23:13:07 UTC
..Ok....so I accepted an interview for our local paper here in
Northern Virginia.."The Loudoun Times Mirror"..The interview will be
out on Wednesday Jan. 17, 2006. Before I get some backlash on the
interview, I'd like to let you know why I did it. The interview was
offered to a lot of folks via a random email to members from
letterboxing.org. I'd like to think I am a respectable letterboxer,
that promote the hobby, and nature etc. I was afraid that If I didnt
take the offer, someone else might. I also, hope the interviw I
provided them, will give some insight to the hobby, that we all want
to focus on, and I'd like to see some new faces here in the area.

With that said, I should be able to proof-read the article before
release to make sure it protects us and this hobby from others. I
also, should have a clue to a box that is only available to those that
buy this newspaper. So if you want a one time only clue, be sure to
have it on hand.....the clue might be straight foward, or knowing
me..it will be in some kind of code in the interview. If it is
straight foward, Im guessing locals will go find this box of the
published clues...If it is done this way, I will make the box, with
info on the hobby in the box, and further instructions as to what it
means to us, and guidelines etc.

Just want to keep things as they are, but welcome in a few newbies
over time.

Thanks, and let me know what ya think!

BD....there will be pic of my 2 kids letterboxing in the paper!
