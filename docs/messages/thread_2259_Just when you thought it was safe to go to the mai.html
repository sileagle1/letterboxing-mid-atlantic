Just when you thought it was safe to go to the mailbox.....

From: Hikers and Hounds | Date: 2007-01-19 12:39:54 UTC
So Mr. Irene is bringing in the mail this morning and says "Yo hon, you got something here from the Klingon Language Institute" to which I reply "Yeah, right". Mr. Irene fancies himself a comedian and is always saying things like "Yo hon, Ed Rendell called and wants you and Dude on the governors letterboxing advisory council" or some such nonsense. My standard reply is "yeah, right". So this morning he replies (to my reply...stay with me here) "No seriously, it's from the Klingon Language Institue. And who knew they were in Allentown, PA?" SO I am thinking a) after the Klingon homeworld of Praxis blew up they must have had to relocate. Allentown, while not an immediately obvious choice, actually makes sense. I mean, have you seen the Allentownians? A Klingon could easily blend in. b) Lightnin Bug. Hmmm..After inspecting the package I notice right away the Qapla' , the Klingon word Success!, is mis-spelled. I mean, even someone with almost no Klingon language skills at all should know how ot spell and pronounce Qapla' !! I feel there is a bug at work here. Be warned! And thanks!
 
H&H
