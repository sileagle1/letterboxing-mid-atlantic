missing "Positiviely New Jersey" box with a slight change in spot to
keep it from being kidnapped again. I'm up for others as well (at
least some of the one's I've solved the clues for) but I think it
would be fine to have a group effort. Although I have been e-mailing
Rick regularly, I have yet to meet anyone on the trail or otherwise
and hope to do so in the future. I'd be glad to hear your comments
and will check back often. Thanks, John.

couldn't miss it!

We nabbed Long Beach Island Shark and Starfish with no problem, though

buried beneath mounds and mounds of sand, if it is there at all.

in plain sight. Could not tell who the previous finder was as they

fell).
