Nice Surprise

From: virginiawaterbird | Date: 2007-01-25 17:17:28 UTC
I went out this morning to plant a replacement for Muskrat Ramble II, which vanished
last Fall -- Muskrat Ramble 2007 being the new one. I suspected MR II might have
been dragged off by animals, and on the way to plant the new one I stopped to search
one last time for MR II. To my great surprise, there it was! Sitting under its tree and
now highly visible in the green bag I had put it in last summer! I don't know where it's
been, but it's back. It's now wrapped in fall colors (I have no winter ones) and hidden
under leaves and bark. I went on to plant MR 2007 a little farther along the trail. So
you all can get 2 for 1 with a short hike!

Waterbird
