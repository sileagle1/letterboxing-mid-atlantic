From: Judy B | Date: 2007-01-25 21:42:47 UTC

When I made the event log for DE Games I followed the instructions from Carol Duvall on HGTV
http://www.hgtv.com/hgtv/cr_home_accessories/article/0,,HGTV_3255_3992464,00.html

It will give you instructions on a method of stitching the bound edge starting at Step 10
I used duct tape in lieu of bookbinding tape - which is expense
You can get wax to wax any heavy duty thread at a fabric store (or maybe in the sewing notions area of Michaels/ACMoore).  It eases the pulling of the thread thru the holes.

That is the extent of my book binding.

sewsowbizzy
