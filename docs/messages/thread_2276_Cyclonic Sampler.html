From: Nathan Brown | Date: 2007-01-25 18:53:33 UTC
OK, it is not a box, but it could be!

Irene has already related our adventures last Sunday across the "Great
Divide" to the land of New Joisey, so I thought I would report on my
findings thus far:

http://www.cyclonic.smugmug.com/photos/125400542-M.jpg

I think I liked the Blueberry Ale the best, followed by the Wanderlust,
the Milk Stout and finally the Raison D'Être.

Irene raved about the Raison D'Être, but I fould it a bit too woody for
my taste.

The Milk Stout was, well, interesting, certainly different. The
Wanderlust is a lighter ale that I think would be real good in the
summer, it went down nice and easy. I liked the sweeter taste of the
Blueberry, since I do have a bit of a sweet tooth.

I have others to try and will report once the research is complete. I
may have to go and obtain more samples.
