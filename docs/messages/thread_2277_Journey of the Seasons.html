Awesome, I'd love to have H&H in on eof my logbooks. The stamp is
for the third box in the first series of my outlaw country series.

The First 3 are going to be : "Outlaw country series set 1: Willie,
Waylon, and me." with the "me" being DAC.

The second three are going to be "Outlaw country series set 2: Three
men with broken hearts"

The third three are going to be "Outlaw country series set 3: The
new breed" With probably H Scott Biram, 16 Horsepower, and Ryan
Adams.

If you're into real country and not the pop crap, the 3 in the third
set are worth finding. H Scott Biram is very Hillbilly, 16
Horsepower is very Appalachia Mountain Death stuff, and Ryan ADams
is great folk country.

I can't wait to get the boxes out there, I'm workin on Willie right
now, it's a huge stamp, and then this set will be finished. I'll
probably plant each set of three in a different park around the area.
