I've done a few hashes with H4 - not recently and not enough to have
a hash name. It's a fun, welcoming, rowdy group - definitely not for
the kids or those with tender ears.

H4 hashes tend to be around 3 miles, lots of beer, can get into some
nasty bushwhacking (called "shiggy"), but each group has its own
personality. The Ben Franklin Mob in Philadelphia tends to longer
runs with less beer.
