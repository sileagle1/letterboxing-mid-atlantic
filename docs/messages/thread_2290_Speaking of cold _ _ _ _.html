Hmmm, was it really cold? This was the first time that Syner and I
have been to Cape Henlopen without donating voluminous quantities of
blood and sections of assorted body parts to the local insect
population. Basically, we went looking for some of the DE Games
boxes that we missed back in October - lots of good stuff still
alive and quite well.

Otis' Friends rocked today. Sure enough, the crowd headed for the
surf a few minutes early, but he hit the waves and got his head wet,
which makes him among the few, and was extremely brave for a first-
time Polar Bear.

Too early for official water and air temps or a rough guess as to
number of Bears and money raised, but will post when I see it.
Thanks to everyone who cheered us on or who contributed to Special
Olympics Delaware through this event - I still have a stamp for
you. :)
