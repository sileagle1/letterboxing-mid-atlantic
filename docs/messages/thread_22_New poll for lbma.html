Keep in mind that I only carve using exacto knives. Some materials may
prove to work better with carving tools.

Comments are below:

> What is your preferred carving material?
>
o Master Carve - Carves like butter, and is just as soft. Fine for
most work, but I think it is too soft for finer detail work.
o Speedy Stamp - "The Pink Stuff" Great carving material, it is
easy to get, carves nicely, and solid enough for most detail work. It
can be soft for finer detail, but if you carve in the right order
(details first), then it is very nice.
o Speedy Cut - "The Crumbly Stuff" This stuff stinks. The only
good it is for is as a practice material, as it is cheap.
o PZ Cut ORANGE - Never carved with this. I assume it carves just
as nicely as the white stuff. One thing I don't like, it does not seem
to take ink well.
o PZ Cut WHITE - My favorite. It is solid, but easy to cut with the
exacto. I've done my very best work using this stuff.
o Other - The are a few others, Mars erasers, wood, plumbers gasket.
Dick Blick makes a gray stuff that carves pretty well, but I think the
matirial is a bit inconsistant.
