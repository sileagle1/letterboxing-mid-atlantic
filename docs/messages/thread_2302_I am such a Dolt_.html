I am such a Dolt!

From: mstoeff61 | Date: 2007-02-11 22:50:06 UTC
Rushing home after a fantastic day of letterboxing with my dear
friend (Janila) and I had to stop suddenly. After the road cleared I
proceeded on my journey home. Tired but happy with a wonderful
letterboxing adventure and great day!

Reaching for my water bottle

mmm Where did all the water go? Did I drink it? Floor of the car is
not wet Oh well must have been thirstier than I thought.

That night Black Swan figures out the bonus clues Janila and I could
not fathom at the moment on the trail (I think we were both in lunch
mode, brains not functioning right with hungry bellies!). Swan and I
make plans to return to the scene of the crime on Sunday

Sunday morning, running late to hit the road for the adventure, I go
out to warm up the car and wait for the Swan. I usually pull forward
a bit, but why won't the shifty thing move into drive?

Ah, now I know where the water went! I guess freezing weather makes
water FREEZE-20 minutes later and some time with a hair dryer,
finally on our way!

Yes, we did accomplish our mission and a few to boot!

I am such a dolt!
