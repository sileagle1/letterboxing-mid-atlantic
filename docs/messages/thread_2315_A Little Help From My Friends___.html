A Little Help From My Friends...

From: nattybumppolbna | Date: 2007-02-19 20:12:44 UTC
The original Police (Battle of the Bands) stamp which was washed away by Neshaminy creek's
frequent swellings was actually found and recovered and returned to me by a 'boxer with a
heart of gold.

The replacement stamp is nestled all snug in it's aerie. But I did notice something about my
old stamp: Apparently some kind hearted person - in an effort to help out later 'boxers -
marked the stamp with an arrow indicating the "Up" position. I have no doubt thta this was
done in the spirit of helping others. Letterboxers have continually renewed my faith in
humankind. So please take my petty complaint for what it's worth. But the kind hearted
person put the arrow on the wrong way.

So if I may offer a word of advice... if you're not sure...

Sorry 'bout the bitchen

- Natt
