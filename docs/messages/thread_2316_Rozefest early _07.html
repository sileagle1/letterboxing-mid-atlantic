Rozefest early '07

From: geckospot2000 | Date: 2007-02-21 01:49:29 UTC
As soon as we left New Castle County we also left behind the snow and
ice. Our Sussex county roadtrip was a resounding success, we got
every stamp we looked for!

We whetted our whistles at the Smyrna Reststop w/ Northern Spy then
headed to the shore where we found:
Indian River Lifesaving Station
Sydney Opera House
Emeril
Mail Stop of Locomotivation - We were confuzzled by the clues for
Loco at this point, and on a tight schedule, so we skipped the next 2
of this series.
Poultry
Vintage Car (V8?) - but the shop was closed so no inside treats.
Lenape Tribe

We skipped the boxes that required walking since we only had 4 hours
to do the series and get back home, and yes we were an hour late
getting home anyway but we certainly couldn't stop when they were all
so close! Also, we were first finders since the games on most (all?)
of these boxes. Several boxes were out in the open but unmolested
though they probably would have fallen victim to the first mower of
spring had we not replaced them a bit more securely.

Poor Rozebud almost lost a finger when the gecko's blood sugar took a
dip and she suddenly became very cranky, but an immediate late lunch
saved all from further bloodshed. Matter o'fact Rozebud was kind
enough to offer another finger after the initial outburst ;-)

It was a very cold but wonderful weekend, great for drive-bys, and we
are in the process of converting another friend. One of us, one of
us, one of us...

geckospot
