Who Needs a Theme? Gathering

From: Nathan Brown | Date: 2007-02-22 19:45:36 UTC
Well, I figure now that I have some of the location details worked out,
it is time to offically announce the "Who Needs a Theme?" gathering.

It will be held on May 19 (not the 5th as previously planned) at
Washington's Crossing State Park in Titusville, NJ.

The theme of the gathering? Well, it is anything goes. a potluck of
boxes are to be planted in this large park, and I am looking for
donations of more to be placed. Boxes will be needed by May 5th to be
placed in the park, so drop me a line and send along any old stamp you
have laying around, doesn't matter what it is of.

The gathering itself will begin at about 10 at the Nature Center
pavillion. Bring food. This will be my typical laid back type of
gethering, with a few event stamps going around, but lots of boxes in
the woods.

Gathering not enough for you? Stay that night at the park's campground.
Site D has been procured for after gathing fun. There WILL be night
boxing as well.

You can sign up at Atlasquest:
http://www.atlasquest.com/events/event.html?gEventId=321

More details to follow as well.
--
