Time Warp - Valley Forge

From: tightlinefishing | Date: 2007-02-26 01:03:59 UTC
I braved the elements today to hike up Mt. Misery to grab the 3 boxes
within 50 ft of eachother :) I swear I was back in the Colonial Army
with the way the snow was coming down out there. I kept waiting for a
Hessian scout to jump out from behind a tree and jab a bayonet in my
face.

Then I made the fatal and frustrating mistake of making my first
attempt at some Mapsurfer boxes. Have people actually killed
themselves out of pure frustration while searching for any of these?
I did get to see many great parts of the park while wandering around
aimlessly though. I think this is actually part of the intention of
his cruelty and I got some great pics of deer feeding in the snow.

So between pulling 14 fish through the ice Saturday (Yes, the Fishman
fishes YEAR ROUND!) and a great hike through parts of Valley Forge
Park, it was a great weekend!

Fishman
