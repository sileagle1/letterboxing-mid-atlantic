It is with much regret that I announce that I will be retiring my
first planted letterbox, Red Sox Nation - A. The box will officially
retired Sunday, April 1st . . . no seriously, it will. The Wilmington
Blue Rocks reverted back to being the Kansas City Royals Single A
affiliate some time this past fall. So, technically the Red Sox are
no longer affiliated with the Blue Rocks now, but I'll wait until the
first game of the Sox regular season to pull the box.

I plan to make another plant in Rockford park soon to maybe attract a
little more traffic to it before it gets the ole "86" . . which is
just a bad number for Red Sox fans in general.

GO SOX!
