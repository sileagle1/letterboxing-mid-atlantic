Road Rally Alternative

From: Lightnin Bug | Date: 2007-03-20 11:06:54 UTC
One of the things that I considered for this spring was to hold
something akin to a 'land rally' at French Creek. It would use the
permanent orienteering course, have alien themed stamps and be
called "French Creek Alienteering." I decided not to so as not to
conflict with Natt's Road Rally, Cyclonic's late May gather or Hikers
and Houndz' June gather, but perhaps there is an opportunity. Also,
as the course runs near the pool at FCSP, I hoped that it would be
run prior to Memorial day. The thing is, I only wish to hide the
boxes and pull together the course. I personally do not wish to do
all of the auxiliary gathering extras such as organizing food, etc.

If we can collaborate on creating some stamps and have somebody else
pull together the refreshments, amusements, etc. I can certainly put
together a boxing/orienteering course.

Your thoughts?

LB
