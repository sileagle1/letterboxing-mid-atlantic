From: Judy B | Date: 2007-03-26 14:22:19 UTC
evilmommy -
the clue did not make any reference to the Camp giving permission for the letterbox being planted!  It referred to the Camp allowing hikers on the property.  There is a big difference between hiking and planting letterboxes.  Unless a clue specifically states "owner has given permission for planting this letterbox" it should never be brought to the attention of the owner. 

As a newbie, you need read

http://www.letterboxing.org/GettingStarted/Letterboxing101.pdf

especially the section on Page 2 about Trouble Finding a Box  that starts with
"Do not ask the authorities about a letterbox if you cannot find it."

You might also find it helpful to join:
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/newboxers/

Keep in mind this adventure is suppose to be discreet --- unless we are partying at a local establishment !!

You have learned a valuable lesson, and there may be others out there learning along with you.

Good luck on contacting Raindear -- but do not contact the camp again, please.

sewsowbizzy
