French Creek Alienteering perhaps a reality...

From: Lightnin Bug | Date: 2007-03-30 23:48:56 UTC
Thanks to the kinds offerings of help from Hikers and Hounds, Pink
Panther and perhaps the McCleln Clan (hope I spelled it right), I
think we can pull off the French Creek Alienteering event.

Right now, the date that is least conflicted with other mid-Atlantic
events is 17 June 2007. No conflict with NJ, VA of MD events. This
will also give those who may wish to head up to NY some time to make
it back if they feel it worth their while...

PP and H&H are looking into meeting places, etc.

I hope to plant the boxes on Memorial Day weekend, thereabouts. So,
if you'd like to see one of your "Alien Themed" letterboxes become
part of this fun course/event, please let me know (via email). It
can be amusing aliens (like from the Far Side cartoon), scary aliens,
whatever floats your alien's boat. My only request is that I receive
them prior to 24 May 2007 so that I can plant them that weekend.

We will eventually get this up on AQ; I shall post this when the time
comes.

Stay tuned as things maybe develops. Remember to reach me offline if
you wish to contribute your view of what Alienteering is all about.

No aliens were harmed in the creation of this post.

LB
