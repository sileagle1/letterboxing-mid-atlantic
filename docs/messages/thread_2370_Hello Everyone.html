From: momma22and2 | Date: 2007-04-01 03:10:01 UTC
I have been a lurker for a few days and thought I should speak up and
introduce myself (and hubby). We are new to letterboxing only having
discovered this in the last few weeks and found our first box placed
by Woodthrush, Bay bridge/Sandy Point box 3/25 and our second box
today along with two attempts. Seems boxes were missing. I know we
were in the correct area and should I had done some more research
seems both have been attempted before me. But a beautiful day spent
outside and having fun nonetheless. I have been reading all I can
soak up about letterboxing and have gather some really good
information so far. I am looking forward to carving a stamp and
placing my own box, hopefully this summer, and attending a gathering
so we can meet some people and find out even more about boxing in
general. Hoping we can go out again tommorrow and find a box that
illuded us today (we just did not have right directions to area)and
enjoy another day outside.
We have just moved to Annapolis from Delaware (there for 6 months)and
up from South Carolina before that. I sure wish we had known about
this before relocating from Delaware, there are lots of boxes in the
area where we were (Bear area) and we did not even know it. But the
good thing is we can revisit the area and play catch up as it were.
Hope to meet some of you in the future and sorry for posting such a
long message (hope I am forgiven)

Hopelessly addicted,

Cannonball
Pam(/Steve)
