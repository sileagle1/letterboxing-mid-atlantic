Re: Richmond this weekend

From: Mother Earth | Date: 2007-04-11 15:27:17 UTC
I am out of town but picking up the Yahoo group posts. I live in
Richmond and have placed several boxes myself. The Clue Series is
awesome but VERY time consuming and covers 4 parks that are spread
over the entire metropolitan area. I would suggest trying that when
you have a lot of time. Last year my kids and I spent a good part of
our Spring Break searching for the boxes.

I loved Scuffletown Park; it gets you into the old city parts near
Strawberry Street Cafe-great lunch or dinner spot! The Hollywood
cemetary boxes are good too. You will visit Presidents' graves
there. There are three there: Olde Arbour and An Irish Lass, View
of Richmond. The cemetary is a neat spot.

I live in Short Pump, near Glen Allen. Bobfoot has some good boxes
in Glen Alen. The Mountain Road ones are historical. I have a
series called "It started with a Tavern" and a friend of mine placed
one at Deep Run School on the same site. There are 5 there all
together. I had one at the Barnes and Noble but it recently fell
behind a book case and I haven't retrieved it out yet. Maybe it will
be back up nect time you visit.

Have fun!

Mother Earth
