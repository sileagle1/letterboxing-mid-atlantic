Another Letterboxing Romance

From: Nathan Brown | Date: 2007-04-12 05:13:59 UTC
Alas, another letterboxing babe is taken, as the devious Celtic Quinn
has popped the question to ever so cute Kitten Without Mittens.

The Kitten recounts:

"ok ok ok ill tell....after i kill wandering star.

no im not pregnant
no we didnt adopt
yes it involved letterboxing
no not a rock
yes you have to thank all the wonderful family of postal letterboxing

in january the Valentines Day Magic Ring was listed, and me suddenly
feeling very much sappy, both myself and celtic quinn to carve a stamp
together for it- instead we carved to seperate ones- but the downfall of
that was that they were a complete secret from one another. We didnt
speak of it, we ignored each others logbooks, and it was very hard.
after a month of this plotting the ring finally began and our boxes went
out- mine in one package and cq's in another.
fast forward to today.
we finally recieved them home, after the four other lovely participants
recieving our packages- and gracefully not telling either of us what
they thought of them, we fianlly got to open them.
wandering star being on-site took pictures (she also tomented me all day
saying that i know what it is and you cant have it...but anyways)
so cq loved my postcard letter that i had written a loving sentiment on
and included a stamped image of myself, and when i finally got to open
his three box package with two cameras staring me in the face (see im
building up tension here)...(DUH DUN DUUUMMM) so the first had a lovely
poem that he wrote for me and a matching sentimental stamp, the second
lines from a love song, and the third..well...

ok ill tell- it was a stamp that had a ring and around it "will you
marry me" and attached to the stamp holder was a ring box with ring inside.

so everyone in that ring knew- ALL my friends knew- even my friends
mothers knew- and i JUST found out my mother knew!!!

and i said yes."

Awwwww... ain't it sweet.

Let me be the first from LBMA to congratulate the two of you.
