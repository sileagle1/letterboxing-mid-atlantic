From: Silent Doug | Date: 2004-07-31 12:56:14 UTC
I decided on XTC, to add a little post-punk, New Wave, British neo-pop rock.

If you end up with too many boxes to plant in a single park, you might
think instead of planting them along the Delaware Canal State Park. It's 60
miles from Easton to Bristol, and I think there are only four boxes on the
canal path so far. There are lots of places to enter the path and a couple
of parks along it (like Tinicum). Maybe it wouldn't end up being a series
that people could hike and find in a single day, but it could be part
driving tour to start at one end and then drive and walk to find them all
along the way.

Here's the park map and web site:
http://www.dcnr.state.pa.us/stateparks/parks/delawarecanal.aspx

Also, the Delaware & Raritan Canal parallels the canal on the NJ side for
30 miles, so there are more options there.

It's just an idea -- planting 15 boxes in a single park might be asking for
trouble, since they'd be so concentrated and we might have to use less than
optimal hiding places. (I know they do long series like this it in CT, but
still....)
