Shroom with a View - B. Aug 06, D. Apr 07

From: brendan_hickey13 | Date: 2007-04-15 22:37:49 UTC
"Rest in Plastic" to our first plant, Shroom with a View, recently of
Anson Nixon Park in Kennett Square.

The box is completely gone and the root hollow cleared out, the likely
implement (a stick) being left leaning against the tree. The
informational sign directly across the trail has been torn off, so this
goes as a probable fatal encounter with a fuggle (not to be confused
with the species of hops).

Yeah, it happens and yeah, it goes with the hobby and yeah, there are
bigger tragedies in the world. We're just a bit ticked here. That
little bit of plastic and paper and rubber wasn't bothering anyone out
there in the woods.

We'll decide if there is to be a "Shroom with a 2" when we feel a bit
better.

Psychiker

now, and hey there was a tree hollow a bit further down the trail...

When I found it there was about 2 inches of ice on it. I really had to

down that trail in that whether.

When you rehide, look for a spot further from the trail, and face the
box away from the trail. Hiding boxes is a constant learning
experiance, I know I am still coming up with ways to better hide them.
