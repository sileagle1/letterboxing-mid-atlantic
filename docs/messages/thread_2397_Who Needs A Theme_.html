Who Needs A Theme?

From: Nathan Brown | Date: 2007-04-19 18:21:54 UTC
Well, it is just a month away now. I know some of you folks have been
meaning to sign up, so get to it. An bunch of new folks have already
signed up, and I am looking forward to meeting you. I know there are a
bunch of you folks over in Joisey, so come on out and join in the fun!

I have gotten several wonderful box contributions that will be planted
this weekend. This is on top of the 15 boxes that were planted last
week, with still more on the way.

It looks like there will be a whole bunch of event boxes to keep you
busy as well.

If you would like to camp, that is no problem. All you have to do is
show up with your camping gear and a contribution of just $1.00. No
reservations are needed. The site is large and within walking distance
of the gathering.

For campers or those that just wish to have some fun after dark, there
will be a night boxing series that will be easy and about 10 boxes, some
new, and some old, but all fun.

The gathering should be a blast! Bring a friend. Bring two friends.
Heck, bring a whole gaggle of friends!

Remember, Washington Crossing State Park in New Jersey, May 19 at the
Nature Center Pavilion.

Sign up on Atlasquest:
http://www.atlasquest.com/events/event.html?gEventId=321

--
Nathan Brown
