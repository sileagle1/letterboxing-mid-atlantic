Hi Fellow LBMA Members!

I have long admired Fleur de Lis' wonderful letterboxing site.
Inspired by her, I decided to create my own site to chronicle my
thoughts and experiences in letterboxing, and whatever else is going
through my mind!

Since Eric and I are expecting our second child in December, I
thought it would be fun to include an "awaiting baby" link to
chronicle those thoughts separately. Maybe family members tuning to
see baby news will get hooked on letterboxing, too!

Anyway, the door's open, come on over and pay a visit!

www.highlysung.blogspot.com
