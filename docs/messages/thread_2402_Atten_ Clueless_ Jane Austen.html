From: Clueless | Date: 2007-04-24 23:26:30 UTC
Yep, I know about it.  Let's see...a newspaper in Doylestown did an article on letterboxing and included mention of my Jane Austen box.  A member of the Jane Austen Society of North America (JASNA) read the article and contacted me to see if I knew about them.  Yes, I was a member, but had never been to an event, but she got me out to one.  She became the president (or equivalent title) of the local chapter, and asked if I would talk to the group about the hobby, which Doug and I did (we got a Jane Austen action figure as a speaker gift).  Though she hasn't seemed to me to be much of a boxer (unless I've somehow completely missed it), she does seem to think it's fun.  She got her brother involved, and subsequently put together a Jane Austen event at Winterthur with a letterbox involved.  She invited us, but we were unable to attend.  That's all I know.  I was under the impression it was an afternoon box, though never actually followed to be sure.

For those interested, already on my calendar for 2009 is the fact that Philly is hosting the national JASNA convention.  Been watching for outfits on ebay.
