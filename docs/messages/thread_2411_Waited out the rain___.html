Th link to the "Pina Colada Song" was good. So, I decided to take
some liberties with it and while listening to the muzac version of
the song -I re-wrote the song - as shown below. Play the link from
the previous e-mail while reading this version - they go togther
well. -John

Escape - "with me" (The Letterboxing-allota Song)

This version is definitely not by: By Rupert Holmes
Lyrics partially borrowed and butchered w/o permission but with a
good sense of humor by: The Jersey Trailblazers


I was tired of my partner, we'd been together too long.
Like a worn-out recording, of a favorite song.
So while she lay there sleeping, I read a magazine in bed.
And in the Activities column, there was this letter I read:

"If you like Letterboxing-alotta, and getting caught in the rain.
If you're not into geo-cashing, if you're a little insane.
If you like carving stamps at midnight, of all kinds of weird shapes.
I'm the trail partner you've looked for, write to me, and escape."

I didn't think about my partner, I know that sounds kind of mean.
But me and my old partner, had fallen into the same old hobbies
routine.
So I wrote a letter to the editor, about the activities ad.
And though I'm nobody's poet, I thought it wasn't half-bad.

"Yes, I like Letterboxing-alotta, and getting caught in the rain.
I'm not much into orienteering, and I've been told I'm insane .
I've got to meet you next August soon, and cut through all this red
tape.
At Doug's workshop at the Appalachian Mountain Club, where we'll plan
our escape."

So I waited with high hopes, then she walked in the place.
I knew her LbNA patch in an instant, I saw her compass in place.
It was my own lovely lady, and she said, "Oh, it's you."
And we laughed for a moment, and I said, "I never knew"..

"That you liked Letterboxing-alotta and getting caught in the rain.
And the thrill of being first finder, and that you're a little insane.
If you like carving stamps at midnight, of all kinds of weird shapes.
You're the trail partner that I've looked for, come with me, and
escape."
