Unfortunately the zoo is not in the best of neighborhoods, but do not
let that discourage you. Not far from there you can find the much
talked about Freedom Trail series (which are still being planted it
seems). I can't tell you exactly where they are, but they are not that far.

There are several boxes in Wissahickon , a section of the city you will
sort pass on your way there. They include Wizardland, Body Parts, A
Quaker Yarn, and Monastery. I have not gotten these boxes yet (I know,
I know, I should have), so I have no idea how long they will take, but
they are in a wonderful park. The Wissahickon Trader series is nice,
but may be a bit too long.

There is also a Geocache hybrid along the Ben Franklin Parkway called Be
Prepared. Also there is The Persistance of Winky. While it was
supposed to be removed several years ago, it seems to still be there and
is a very nice, very easy stamp.
