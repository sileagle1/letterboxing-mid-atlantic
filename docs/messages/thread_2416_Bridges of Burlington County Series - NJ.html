Bridges of Burlington County Series - NJ

From: Dave and Diane | Date: 2007-04-30 13:32:58 UTC
Hi all! Dave and I went out to do maintenance on the series yesterday.
We had had reports of a very damp logbook for box 2 and full logbooks
for all other boxes.
Well, Box #1 and box #2 are gone. I figure they got swept away with the
torrential rains this week; in fact, the second bridge is tilted at a
very strange angle. The rest of the series is fine, just walk on by the
first two box sites and we'll replace as soon as possible.
We brought my sister-in-law along for the hike and gave her a stamp.
Don't think she's hooked enough to go out on her own yet, though! Her
stamp is a small flower and her trail name is Senior Secretary. She
works for the Ocean County Library system, hmmmm could be a place for
another indoor box.:)
We also checked on other area boxes and they are fine. Thanks to
Gerania for keeping a watch on Batona Trail Surprise. What's the
surprise, you ask? Can you find your way back to the trail from the
box!:)
See you all the next time I'm in the area - back to RI tomorrow.
Diane
the von der Insels
