From: wood thrush | Date: 2007-05-09 18:52:17 UTC
Minwartburg,

Boxes in libraries can be hidden inside fake, "altered" books that the
placer creates at home and then places on the shelf alongside real
books. Here is the clue to an especially clever one I found here in
Maryland.

http://www.letterboxing.org/BoxView.php?boxnum=2052&boxname=Bookworm

Sometimes they are hidden under a metal library shelf in a micro
container attached with a magnet. The first box I ever found was like
that.

Sometimes the librarians know about them and sometimes they don't. The
clue will often tell you which is the case. But as a precaution, do NOT
ask the librarian. Some libraries actually welcome boxing on the
premises; it gets people interested in books and into the library.

wood thrush
