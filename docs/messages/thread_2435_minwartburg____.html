From: MaryAnn Lockard | Date: 2007-05-12 10:27:40 UTC
Ah, the homage stamp. I think one of the original
boxers who perpetrated this should step up with the
trail story. It is now the stuff og legend, we sit by
campfires at night and tell the tale to newbies who
listen with rapt attention the firelight gllinting off
their eager faces...
Anway an homage stamp takes the original signature
stamp image and does somekind of wordplay on the
boxer's trail name the first victim of this was Silent
Doug who became Silent Dog or was it Silent Duck?
Other victims include Lightning Bug became Lighting
Slug, lighting Mug. Lighting Doug. There are many more
Cyclonic has a list of them on his web page. We then
will stamp these into boxes we think the victim might
find. They have no idea who the carver was and it
makes them crazy. I can't wait to get my hands on Miz
Harlot!
