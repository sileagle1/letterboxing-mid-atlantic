Hi all,

After a few reports that some trees had been cut down near my Snoopy box in Evansburg State Park, I finally got around to checking it out for myself. 

Holy Cow folks!

I am very sorry for those who have attempted this box in the last few weeks.  It was extremely muddy.  I nearly fell a couple of times.  But worse than the mud was the poison ivy.  I try to be pretty careful about limiting fellow seekers' exposure to that plant.  Obviously this box was placed in late winter.  Poison ivy (and mud) were not there.

 

Although I got some good logbook feedback on the location (despite 100% initial wrong turn attempts) I have completely relocated the box.  I wanted it to be near the Mill and that cool bridge, but it's just not a good location.

Snoopy is now near the Beautiful Butterfly, back in the main part of the park.

New clues are posted.

Thanks for the reports.

Tim
