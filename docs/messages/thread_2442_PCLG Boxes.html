PCLG Boxes

From: jerseytrailblazers | Date: 2007-05-14 01:48:09 UTC
Hello all, decided to finally get to some of the PCLG boxes I haven't
been to before today. For those that have already been there and those
who haven't- here's an update. "A Mooving Tale" is still there. But,
the box was wet inside and although I dried it out, the stamp has
sustained damage and is starting to fall apart. I have had recent
coversations with Brain from PCLG (who moved out of the area) and I
will update him on this box and the others. If there is a good carver
in that area that might want to try and replace that stamp - it may not
last a lot longer as it is. It wasn't easy to get a good image as it's
starting to come apart in places. "Sneak Attack" is still in place, is
in good shape and is at the end of the 2nd log book. Next person out
might want to bring an extra as I did not have one. Also went to look
for "Signature" and it appears to be missing. I found the spot where
the "field stones" cover the box as stated in the clues - but the box
was not there. Overall - 2 out of 3 classic boxes in an 8 hr drivng
day is not a bad day at all. The next PCLG I will be going to
is "Messenger of Peace". (And hitting some other peoples boxes in the
area) I know where it is - but if anyone can advise what shape its in -
I'll bring supplies this time if needed.
Thanks - John.
