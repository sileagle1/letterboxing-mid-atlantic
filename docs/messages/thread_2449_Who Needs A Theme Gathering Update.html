Who Needs A Theme Gathering Update

From: Nathan Brown | Date: 2007-05-17 14:09:41 UTC
Hmmm... Seems my laptop lost the last email, so here we go again,

Who Needs A Theme Gathering, May 19, 10am
Washington Crossing State Park
Titusville, NJ
Nature Center Pavilion

Directions:
From I-95, take New Jersey Exit 1 (Rt 29)
Route 29 North about four miles to the first light (Rt 546), turn right
The park entrance is about half a mile up on your left.
Once in the park, make the first right and follow the signs to the
Nature Center

The park is open at 8am

Camping:

A site has been reserved in the group campground. We are at Site D.
All are welcome to join us for just a few hours or the whole night.

Directions to Camp:
Exit the park and turn left
At the first light, turn left.
Turn into the Phillips Farm Day Use Area.
Parking for the campground is at the end of the road. The site is at
the far end of the field in the woods.

I am looking to get a head count as to who will be camping. So far I have:

Hikers and Hounds
Sheba
Gerania
MizScarlett
SSB

Anyone that I am missing please let me know.

Night Boxing:

There will be a night boxing series. It will begin at the campsite
after the sun goes down. Directions will be available at that time.
Important, flashlights ONLY, no head mounted lights please.

I think that is pretty much it. Any questions? Ask away.

--
Nathan Brown
