Another friend falls victim to letterboxing

From: Chris Bamberger | Date: 2007-05-22 13:38:38 UTC

I was chatting with an old friend last night, and she told me about how she was riding her bike more on the local rail trails.  Oh, and by the way, she’s been letterboxing, too.  What?!?!  I was stunned.  Before she went out to ride the trail, she decided to see if there were any boxes along the way.  There were, so she found them.  Then there were boxes at another park she visited.  The rest of the family doesn’t enjoy it as much, but she’s having a great time with it.  In case I didn’t know already, she wanted me to know there were a whole bunch of boxes in the town where we grew up, too.

 

If you haven’t guessed already - she lives in Connecticut!

 

And yet another of our friends falls victim to the hobby…

 

 

Chris (Scattered Showers)
