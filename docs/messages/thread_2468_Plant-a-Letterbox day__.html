Actually, I did plant my first letterbox yesterday with the help of my
mother. I got her interested in letterboxing, but she prefers to
letterbox by proxy -- sending her stamp and logbook with me and my
family when we go out. Well, when we started doing this, she was
caring for her elderly mother, so I went along with it. Granny has
since passed away, so when we headed out this spring and she tried to
pass off her boxing kit to me I told her -- "No more boxing by proxy!!"

This letterbox was her idea, but I put it together, and she planted
it. It was announced on the lb-announce list yesterday. I'm
currently working on another one. I don't know if we will get it
planted on the "official" day, but hopefully this weekend!

midlandtrailblazer

person changes every year...
