Weekend letterboxing

From: JuleBee | Date: 2007-06-11 13:35:52 UTC
I finally got a day to letterbox, so I headed out to Bombay Hook for the Pierre's Vinyl Cut series.  I stopped in the vistor's center to see if the Parson's Point trail was opened and it was.  No eaglets this year.  The lady warned me that there were reports of flies.  I thought I was ready because I had brought my 25% deet spray.  When I hit the first trail, I got about 30 feet before I could not take the flies, so I got back to my car, put on a long sleeve shirt and resprayed myself.  I have never seen so may flies they kept getting between my eyes and glasses but I kept on.  I found 2 of 3 of the boxes and really enjoyed the tower on Bear Swamp trail.  The breeze kept the flies away.  It was a good thing that there has been so much discussion about tick removal, as I was covered in deer ticks.  So much for Deet working.  I may have to be a cold weather letterboxer, because I really hate ticks.  But it was fun to get out and box. And towards sunset my husband and I went back out and there was so much wildlife, it was amazing.  That's what I love about letterboxing, there's always an adventure.
