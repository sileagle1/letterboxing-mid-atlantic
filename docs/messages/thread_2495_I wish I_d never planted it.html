I wish I'd never planted it

From: Audrey | Date: 2007-06-07 14:20:49 UTC
What's your most annoying plant, maintainance-wise?

Mine's the NJ Physiography series. I love it and hate it. What was I
thinking?
The initial planting was fun. Carve a map of NJ, cut the stamp into 5
pieces, plant 5 boxes all up and down the state. Simple.
First, box 101 reported missing. More than once. Carve one piece of
map for replacement box. Go to plant - box NOT missing.
Next missing report: 101 again, since I have an extra stamp? Nooooo,
it's 105, chewed up beyond hope. Carve another map piece, replace.
Next: 103. Carve replacement stamp. Make charming hand-bound book,
this time, too. Box NOT missing.
Next: 101 or 103, since I have replacement stamps? 103's book, since
I have a charming new one? Noooo, it's 104's turn. Carve stamp, slice
out first page of charming book and stamp second page, replace box.
Last Saturday - note the day.
Next: This Tuesday - note the day. 102. Gone.
AAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAARGH!!!!!!!!!!!!!

Thank you.
We now return to your regularly scheduled civil discussion of dry,
safe boxes and satisfying finds.

Aud
