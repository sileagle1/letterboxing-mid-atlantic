Re: [lbma] Another Enjoyable Gather . . .

From: Nathan Brown | Date: 2007-06-11 14:35:28 UTC
Yup, another successful gathering.

We really had a good night Saturday, despite the park Gestapo. They
seemed to hold off after about ten or so. I think I finally went to bed
at about 2:30. I know Green Dragon and KPI were still up. Boy, were
they having a ball.

It was great seeing you Southern folk. Green Dragon, Bulldawg and I had
a very lively discussion that I really enjoyed. I kept forcing Scarab
and Night Owl to stay later and later for just one more stamp. When KPI
and I went on a beer run they left Pirate at the site (in the capable
hands of H&H of course) and he says don't worry, he was going to go play
with the girls ;-) Kids are great!

Schizokeet, Sheba and I went for the Blackburn Branch boxes in the
morning. They were wonderful, and only Pioneer Spirit had beat us to
them (that man can get around!), but it was nice that some of the boxes
had their old logbooks and we got to see some really old signature
stamps (including an early Scarab).

Schiz and I later went to the Oriole's game, where the team we were
rooting for, the Rockies (She is the actual fan, I just figure I've got
to root for the National League team since my beloved Phils are such),
crushed to O's 6-1. Camden Yards is a really nice ballpark, and that
makes it the #5 major league park I have been to.

Sheba and I stopped at Pony Boxer's home on the way back and had
dinner. The chicken was excellent.

Another fun weekend, and I am happy the night boxing went over so well
once again. I am going to continue to do it here and there, and change
out stamps each time so folks can get something different each time.
Hmmm... Maybe Alienteering?

--
Nathan Brown

AKA Cyclonic
Penncoasters.com
