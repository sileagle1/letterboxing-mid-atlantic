Unfair Hiking Practices

From: Rick from Boca | Date: 2007-06-12 06:34:38 UTC
Today after work I enjoyed the Salt Air and Carnival of Souls
letterboxes on the Great Salt Lake, but the entrance to the Audubon
Society's "Lee Creek Area" was a cattle gate no more than 1' wide.

This is blatant discrimination against people who are a little more
than a foot wide. I held my breath and sucked in my stomach, and while
squeezing through wondered if I would ever make it back through on the
way out.
