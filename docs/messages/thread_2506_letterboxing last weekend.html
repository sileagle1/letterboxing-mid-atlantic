From: Pamela | Date: 2007-06-13 19:46:10 UTC
Handyman and I, Cannonball from Annapolis, MD went out boxing this last
Sunday. We eneded up leaving at 9:30 am and headed to Lewes and
Rehoboth Beach to find some boxes. We are also hosting on of the boxing
buddies and took him to find his first box(es). We also hit Ocean City
and found one box there, In all we found 6 boxes including "The
mariner" and "shiver me timbers" as well as "Anglers shark fishing" in
Lewes. The finding of the boxes was not without incident though, The
first box we reached was at the "cannonball house" in Lewes and is in
a "garden" outside the museum. I am not giving up any info as this is
all in the posted clues, anyway there was a bird which had fallen from
his nest, a blujay, and the mother flying overhead visibly distraught,
Well you can guess what happened while I am wearing a WHITE sweater
shirt... Handyman thought thise was hilarious, and as we know blujays
are fond of berries so hence two large purple spots. GREAT! so we
proceeded to the other two boxes and decided I did not know these
people anyway... who cares. Who am I kidding this was bird doo...
anyway had to sneak into Walmart and buy a new shirt for the rest of
the day. We were two hours from home and I had come to see the beach
and letterbox. Now I know to take at least a spare shirt. We also
nabbed, Greyhounds reach the baech and dogfish head in Rehoboth and
Girls gone wild #1 in Ocean City. After leaving at 8:30 pm to head the
two and 1/2 hours home we finally arrived after 3 accidents and traffic
delays at minight. (11:45) We had a great day despite the bird and
traffic. Thanks to all who planted these. We had a great time.
