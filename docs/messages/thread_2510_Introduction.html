From: wildhunt3 | Date: 2007-06-14 11:50:14 UTC
I've been boxing for 3 years, having heard about it on the Dog Scouts
website. Central Illinois was pretty desolate, as far as letterboxes
go, when I started, but this past year has seen a letterbox explosion
in the area (it's a good thing I'm not working in July, so I can get
all those boxes before I uproot). I box with my two dogs, Yuki and
Claire - we'll be easy to spot on the trail because I bet they will be
the only mutts in the area sporting Great Lakes letterboxing patches
on their backpacks.

As to the lack of boxes in the Chestertown area, I'm bringing at least
two of my series with me, and every one I've talked to about housing
has recommended a park to me for exercising the dogs.

Katy
