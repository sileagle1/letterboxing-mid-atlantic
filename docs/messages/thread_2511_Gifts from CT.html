I'm still in Salt Lake City. There was a series planted here
called "Gifts from CT" where a bunch of carvers from Connecticut sent
various carvings of Utah's state animal with Butterfly to the 2004
Gather. A couple of the stamps were carved by Chuck Straub, one of the
originals. Very nice carvings, and a great way to seed letterboxes in
Utah.

I really liked this idea. These are special boxes.
