A Father's Day Tribute

From: Mary Erickson | Date: 2007-06-16 16:57:11 UTC
I was after a box yesterday in an urban setting, and
the paved path went through a large freeway underpass.
Many barn swallows were nesting in the nooks and
crannies. Suddenly one of the swallows flew right by
my head. Whoa, I thought, looks like he wasn't paying
attention when he swooped out of here to go get bugs.
But by the third strafing run I realized that I was
being squarely TARGETED. Seeing how popular this path
was, I bet by the end of the day Daddy Swallow was
pretty exhausted with all that vigorous defense of his
babies up above.

[Okay, I suppose it might have been the female bird,
but I'm taking poetic license in order to say...]

Happy Father's Day to all those who are vigorously
providing for, protecting, and defending their
families!

Mommo
