Team Skullduggery is ready! OK I admit it... I'm excited about
getting to be a participant in another Letterboxing Pub Crawl!!!
Putting on an event like this is a huge amount of work. I spent four
months getting Skullduggery ready and still needed Sewsowbizzy to
carve stamps the night before (Thank you again!) Our BullDawg has
been scheming and making trips to Old Town Alexandria for seven
months... and if any of you have hunted his letterboxes you know the
the degree of thought and effort he puts into them (the guy's AQ
alias nickname is "Psycho"!)... so I am so very psyched about being
in this, his first event . . . that I will bet a mortgage payment
this will be absolutely hysterical ( typical BullDawg
style ! ) . . .and there is no place better for a Pub Crawl then my
very own hometown : Old Towne Alexandria. Please allow me to
apologize in advance for anything I might say or do in the excitement!
Yeehaw... can't wait till Saturday!

Mobey Dick tightey whiteys?....wow I must have forgot all about those!   
 
as for the crawl..well the past 7months or so have involved about 8-10 trips to old town, and countless calls etc.  I have basically put all letterboxing plants and finds on hold to focus on this.  For all those that read this, I must say I has been a blast planning this.  I have exhausted a lot of my clue ideas doing this, and hope everyone is happy...oddly I am worried about pleasing the masses.  I was so sure that this was gonna be awesme, but like with anything you always second guess yourself near crunch time.  The locals here have been a blast, and was the reason I did this.   Gotta say, after doing this, I appreciate more Rogue's and Ivy's first 2 crawls...as I now do for any event.  But this type of event takes SOOOO much work, but I dont regret one bit of it.  My only letdown is the number of folks signed up.....but remember there are suprise guests, and things.
 
I have exhausted my efforts on this, I have nothing left to give, except some more hidden clues between now and Sat.......

From: Hikers and Hounds | Date: 2007-06-20 01:30:13 UTC
Hey there BD,
 
Don't stress!! I am sure it will be wonderful in it's own unique way. I used to stress about my gathers a lot, to the point where I didn't enjoy myself at all! But I have attended many and realized that each gather, no matter hbow big or small is special. I really mean that. Letterboxers are some of the best folks on the planet I always enjoy my time spent with them. I have been awed and bowled over and oh-so-appreciative of the larger, themed gathers. And I just love the smaller more intimate gathers. So no matter what, people will enjoy themselves. I love Abe Lincolns saying "I believe people are about as happy as they make up their minds to be". If someone comes to a gather expecting to have fun, they will create their own self-fulfilling prophesy.
 
So don't sweat the small stuff, try and ejoy yourself if you can! I can't wait and plan to kick everyone's butt, literally or figuratively.
 
H&H
