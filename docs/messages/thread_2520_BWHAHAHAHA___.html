Well, the elusive Red Hat box in Archbald Pothole State Park is no
longer elusive. Pink Panther and I were in the on Saturday and she came
up with it in ten minutes. It works well when you have the right tools,
namely a woman to think like how a woman might hide a box. Kinda like
if you study while drunk you should take the test while drunk.

Amazingly, that was not the only box we found with little or no clues
this weekend. We managed to find Golden Goddess, a Lock Wench creation
with little more then a recollection of the clue, and two Crayola Posse
boxes with no clues at all.
