Pinnacle/Pulpit Rock Hike this Weekend - Any Takers

From: Lightnin Bug | Date: 2004-08-05 23:04:51 UTC
Greetings LBMA'ers!

I've been granted permission by Mrs. LB to persue two newly restored
and sought after letterboxes this weekend. These are none other than
the Pinnacle and Pulpit Rock. But wait, the plot thickens. There is
an observatory up there where I shall stash a letterbox, giving us 3
to hunt for. Or, you can credit for a plant. The stamp is carved
and ready to go! I'll also be unveiling a new improved Lightnin' Bug
stamp!

What I am looking for is a hiking partner or a few who want to
partake in this rather strenuous hike. It can be anywhere from 6-9
miles, depending upon which shortcuts you take (each one shaves about
1.5 miles from the roud trip). There is also about a 1100 ft
elevation gain. I'd urge that little kiddies be left at home and
sturdy shoes be worn (boots, not sandals). This is one of the most
spectacular views in eastern, PA, so bringing a camera or binoculars
is a must!

Lotus (Cherish), Zack and the Leafcutter have expressed interest in
partaking, but I have not heard from them lately.

Either Saturday or Sunday is open, but be prepared to be on the trail
3-4 hours total.

Please respond here or email directly.

Thanks,

Lightnin' Bug Itchin' to do some seriously fun hiking!
