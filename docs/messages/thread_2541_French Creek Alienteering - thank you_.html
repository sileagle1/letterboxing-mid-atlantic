Re: French Creek Alienteering - thank you!

From: 4 suits | Date: 2007-07-01 21:19:37 UTC
ABSOLUTELY A BLAST! Thank you SOO much! What a great mixture of
exchanging (stories, stamps, histories, ideas...!) & boxing & playing
for all of us. We left early this afternoon - after finding a few
more boxes that we ran out of time for yesterday & being stumped by
the bonus box (more on that later, LB).

We had a great time with our new friends, the Keystone Khans - it IS
more fun to box with more kids and more brains! Sameera is
definitely more observant that I am (ketchup), and Farhan was
definitely taking advantage of the more gullible among us. I threw a
few muggles off by loudly referring to "feeling better now?" as the
boys & I came back on the trail (kudos to Peter for tossing the box
into the bush so the muggles couldn't see it; we'll update you on the
poison ivy count when we next log on). The kids got along so well,
it was like a dream.

As for night activity, we can confirm the rangers are keeping their
eyes on those families with young children up too late - no loud
noises by those kids, people; get them in bed, er, bag.

We are all exhausted, both our boys are on the couch behind me passed
out cold - they might be in trouble w/ the rangers again tonight. :)

Liane of the 4 suits
