I have most of the pictures from our trip ready for you to look at but I want to wait until I get Judy's CD so I can include some of them. She has some good ones that I didn't get. I'd like to have them all together rather than two bunches to look at. I took a tripod so we have both of us in some of them.
 
Sooooooooo, in anticipitation and to keep you interested, I posted a map of Alaska with our route on it and the miles we traveled. You should have an idea of just how far we traveled. And we met lots of people who took packaged trips on buses. This way was MUCH better!
