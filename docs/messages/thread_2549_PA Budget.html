PA Budget

From: Jan | Date: 2007-07-06 22:46:37 UTC
Just a word of warning to any out of staters or local people who may
not be up to date on the news and who may be traveling to
box in a Pennsylvania State Park next week. All state parks will be
closed as of midnight on Monday until Pennsylvania passes a budget.
This includes my local state parks, French Creek and Nolde Forest, so
before you travel to letterbox, you may want to check whether our
parks are state or county or local parks so you don't waste a trip.

We are a state who brags about the fact that there is a state park
within 25 miles of every Pennsylvanian so these closings could be a
big blow to us. Hopefully, the budget will be signed quickly and no
one will have to suffer the loss of our 61 state parks for any length
of time.

So box till you drop this weekend and enjoy our beautiful parks while
you can,

Jan
