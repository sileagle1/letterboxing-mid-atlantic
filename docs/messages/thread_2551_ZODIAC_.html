From: gallant_rogue | Date: 2007-07-09 03:36:58 UTC
When I found the Zodiac's "Murder at Bull Run" Box I hiked alone. The
times that I can go are limited. So when I have time to go on a nice
hike with a new box... I have to go. I was mesmerized by the Zodiac
series... and yes. . . a bit spooked. The theme kind of psyched me
out. I tried very hard to get someone to go with me... but none of
the usual suspects were availlable. So I went alone. No big deal. I
do it all the time right? Plus I knew the trail. I was at the trail
head very early. There were no cars and there is only one trail head.
No activity. It was a cold spring morning. I had the whole mountain
to myself. (or did I?) About one mile up the trail... I stopped to
get rid of a little coffee... son of a bitch! . . . there was someone
coming up the trail behind me! About two hundred yards below hiking
at a very quick pace was a large white male... dressed all in black!
Black pants, black hooded jacket and sunglasses. I couldn't believe
what I was seeing. I thought it was the Zodiac himself... laying in
wait in the old mill ruins... just waiting for someone to come find
the letterbox. Well obviously I survived... but I hiked up that hill
for a half hour looking over my shoulder at the mysterious hooded
hiker coming up the hill. As I got toward the top I took a side trail
that I knew would loop back and allow me to get a good look. I re-
emerged on the main trail just as my "stalker" appeared. Turns out it
was just another hiker... enjoying a brisk morning hike. Nothing
more. I allowed him to pass, went on to find the box (First finder of
a Zodiac Box) then went about the rest of my day. But for thirty
minutes my imagination allowed me to hike with a serial killer coming
up the tril behind me.
