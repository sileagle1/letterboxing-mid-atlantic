Karma . . . . BELIEVE IT!

From: tightlinefishing | Date: 2007-07-11 17:02:44 UTC
So back in February I received a message from a boxer telling me
that my AM-2 box in Rhode Island had apparently been muggled. All
they found was a soaking wet log book on the ground nearby. I
placed the box in November and it hadn't even recorded a find yet.
I was SO MAD! So, when I got a chance, I recarved the stamp, put
together a new box and this past weekend I finally got a chance to
go up to RI and replant it, in a different location of course.

Thinking of how I found Sheba's Red Heron box launched into the
woods 30 feet from the site, I decided to return to the crime
scene. I started walking out a spiral around the site to see what
may turn up. About 20 feet out, I found the box bottom but nothing
turned up out to about 50 feet. I'm walking back towards the site
and I see this rock the size of a piece of bread resting between the
roots of a tree. It was in a typical location that you might see
some smaller boxes planted. On a whim I looked behind the rock and
there it was . . . the stamp! Some JACKASS had taken the time to
destroy parts of the stone wall I had hidden the box in, throw the
contents about in the woods, but then deliberatley hide the stamp in
the roots of a tree . . . ? WTF?!!! I really don't get people
sometimes.

Anyway . . . the point being, I must have done SOMETHING right along
the way to have the luck to find the stamp. Karma . . . it's
watching you!
