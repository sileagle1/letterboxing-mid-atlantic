Maybe it wasn't muggled !

From: sewsowbizzy | Date: 2007-07-16 20:34:46 UTC

We all dread having our letterbox muggled, but the same goes for geocachers I'm sure.

  As I visited with Lord & Lady Dunnabunk this weekend, they told the tale of fate for a geocache planted under the pier on Tybee Island (just out side of Savannah GA ).  A wedding was in progress when someone noticed a suspicious item under the pier that looked like a pipe bomb.  The police were called and the bomb squad showed up.  The wedding took a back seat to the unfolding drama.  The pipe with screw caps on each end was removed, planted it in the sand and then exploded.  Imagine their surprise to find a collection of trinkets . . . the closing comment by the police was --- they really should plant these in clear plastic containers!! 

  A reporter's version is found here

http://www.savannahnow.com/node/303163
