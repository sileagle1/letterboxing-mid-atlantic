Pencil Pusher: another LaChola box restored

From: wood thrush | Date: 2007-08-01 18:56:04 UTC
A few days ago Whisper Foot mentioned that the Arborology series
originally planted by La Chola has been restored with new clues and
locations (and new carvings by La Chola!) Hats off to Whisper Foot of
the Historic Letterbox Restoration Group for her hard work.

That reminded us in the HLRG of another La Chola box we recently
restored but forgot to tell you about---Pencil Pusher.

http://www.letterboxing.org/BoxView.php?
boxnum=6260&boxname=Pencil_Pusher

This one retains both La Chola's original choice of a hiding place and
her interesting and cryptic clues. We changed only the very end of the
clues and located the box within two feet of the original placement. We
have recarved the stamp, based on the very kind jpg images sent to us
by (if I recall correctly) Tempus Fugit.

We hope you will enjoy this restored box, another blast from the past.
