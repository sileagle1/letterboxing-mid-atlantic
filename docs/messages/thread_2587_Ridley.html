Went hiking up at Ridley with a friend who is new to boxing to show her the ropes. What
great weather! Re-found the Early American Farm by Trax, box was last found in Oct., it was
out in the open a few feet from it's hiding place and a little wet inside. Re-hid and dried out.
Trax is no longer boxing? I hate to see good boxes dissapear so I will try to keep my eye on
this one since I'm up at Ridley a good bit.
Checked on my Pulp Fiction box and sorry to say it is gone. Looks like hunters were back that
way. It was one of my favorite images, debating wether to re-carve.
