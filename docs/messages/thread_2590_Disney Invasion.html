From: Otis' Friends | Date: 2007-08-04 11:15:28 UTC
To have any longevity, this will have to be in a spot where the thousands of daily small visitors won't happen upon someone retrieving the box.  Already a tough nut to crack.  Then take into consideration that Disney hires people just to police the grounds removing litter.  Add in that some of the usual tools of the urban boxer (the sprinkler that suddenly appears from nowhere, the false post, etc.) will likely be noticed by people edgy about terrorism threats.
It'll have to be a mystery, but even then I wouldn't count on it lasting TOO long (though Natty seems to be doing well with the zoo series).
