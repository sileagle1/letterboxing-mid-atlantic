While out planting Mambo Dog and I encountered something really weird. We were on a
deserted trail in a local park when as we crested a hill we came upon a large structure. It
would appear it was a home made log cabin. It was large maybe 15 feet long and with several
rooms. It was not a square or rectangle but more free form.
Mambo was a little freaked out(so was I, but didn't want to alarm son). I was afaid we had
stumbled on a secret drug hideawy or drinking hangout. Mambo was afaid it was a homeless
persons house(do homeless people have homes?)
We were way out at the highest elevation in the park and far from any road or trail head.
There was not alot of trash around, a little broken glass. I did plant a box not too far away, I
planted it before we saw the structure.
