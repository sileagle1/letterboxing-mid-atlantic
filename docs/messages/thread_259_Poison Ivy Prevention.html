From: marybeth_mc | Date: 2004-08-09 00:17:31 UTC
I have recently come down with a horrible case of Poison Ivy/Oak. It
is such a bad case one of the doctors I work with told me I look like
a leper and prescribed me some steroids. In order to avoid this
again in the future I have picked up some 'Ivy Block' at a local
CVS. I had never heard of Ivy Block before, but came across it on an
internet search, but will certainly be adding it to the backpack
tonight. The backpack is beginning to look more like a travelling
medical kit. With the abundant poison ivy in this region it may be a
good idea for others to pick up some of this wonderful lotion.

With you so susceptible to poison ivy also consider the physical things you can do to prevent it.  Like wearing long pants, understanding that you can get it from petting a dog, and you can get it from touching the outside of clothes that came in contact.  I have heard of some people who carefully peel off their clothes after being in the woods and wash them right away.   The problem is it is an oil that can get on stuff and can then transfer around.  I think there are more such hints out there.
 
I bet the block will help.
 
Glad it isn't stopping your boxing.
