From: Heather Siple | Date: 2007-08-11 01:32:33 UTC
There aren't a lot of drive-bys in Northern Delaware, but Lil' Red Racer is always good.  Ditto for Don't Panic!, which is within sight of I-95.  Just Passin' Through is very close I-95, but requires a short walk, as do all of the Rockwood Park boxes.  Rockwood has fun clues, if I do say so myself, but three of the five carves are done by kids.  The Yummy!  box is the best carve in that park and a nice box, too.  There is at least one Delaware mystery that requires no hiking, is a couple of miles from a major highway and can be solved before you come by doing some web searching, but I'll not tell any more about that!
 
Mother Duck
