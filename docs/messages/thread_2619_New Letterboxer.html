Re: New Letterboxer

From: Michael Scholtes | Date: 2007-08-13 10:40:33 UTC
What part of Bucks and Montgomery? Here are some ideas of good
beginner boxes, if memory serves (it's been several years since I
found some of these).

Upper Bucks:
LoTR Series
Giant Peach
Ring-Ding-A-Ding

Central Bucks:
Who is the Author?
NPIV Compass Points
Kids Castle
Henson's Friends

Upper Montgomery:
Most boxes listed as "Green Lane" are good beginner boxes.

Some of these boxes may only be listed on letterboxing.org. Hope
that's helpful.

Indigo Vulture
