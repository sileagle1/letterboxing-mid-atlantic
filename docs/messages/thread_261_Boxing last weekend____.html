From: Poison Ivy Hedge | Date: 2004-08-09 13:01:21 UTC
I went to Ohiopyle State Park this weekend and got Kayak and Yough
River Trail II.

Kayak is an easy jaunt into the woods that left none of the smokers
gasping or clutching their side.

Yough River Trail II, However, is another story.

The clues can be found in the Letterbox Basement group.

Find some place to rent a bike - it will go much better for you.

We drove as close as we could and ended up walking a mile downhill
and a mile flat.

Then a mile flat back then started the mile trek uphill and hitched a
ride with a bus that carries rafters up the hill.

The box is fine - but only about 5 people have stamped in before us
and it's been there for 2 years. The stamp is okay - and a 5 mile
bike ride along the trail would be very pleasant. It's a flat gravel
path that goes along the river.
