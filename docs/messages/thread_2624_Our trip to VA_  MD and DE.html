Our trip to VA, MD and DE

From: Dave and Diane | Date: 2007-08-13 18:55:11 UTC
So, Dave got a pass for a long weekend. We went to the other
Jamestown this time! We spent Friday at Busch Gardens (very hot
temperatures!). Then Saturday we went down to the Jamestown Village
area. Very neat setup. No letterboxes but I did get acouple of
elongated pennies. The next morning we headed for NJ by way of
Hampton and the Eastern Shore. We decided to get breakfast at Marker
20 and met the planter and her group out side the restaurant. We were
about to go back to the car for the clue sheet, when PWalker noticed
the LBMA patch on my fanny-pack! We got an exchange a very good
breakfast and a Letterbox! Not only that she told us that the Blue
Skies Gallery was just around the corner. Got that one,too. On to the
bridge/tunnel and the Eastern Shore boxes. We were running out of
travel time so we limited our searches to Crucified Crab, YinYang and
Sunset. Picked up 2 more HH's! On to Maryland State Crustacean and
another HH! Wow, 9 stamps in 1 day! We haven't had #'s like that
since Dave's been on Active Duty! Especially HH's, they've been few
and far between lately - guess we have to carve some more.
Dave has an actual Leave scheduled, so we're going north this time.
At least two of our boxes need maintenance. Perhaps we'll find more
HH's!
See you on the trail!
Diane
