Re: Giving Hints

From: jbzfarm | Date: 2007-08-14 00:14:32 UTC
I've had folks never respond to my emails. I may ask for a hint but
clearly state that we do not want an answer, just another clue.

Our challenge is with those who email and expect a less than 24 hour
notice with exact directions to a box that has cryptic clues
(sometimes not so cryptic too). Some are map-challenged, and no
matter what help we give, they never find it. Fortunately, this
hasn't happened too much.

We have found it incredibly helpful to have friendly alumni LBers
who routinely find our boxes. If they can find it without much
trouble, then others can find it too. This helps us verify that our
clues make sense and the box can be found. We haven't done any
extremely difficult mystery boxes yet, but I am sure we will.
