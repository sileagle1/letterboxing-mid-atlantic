Yes, there's a new letterbox for all you Yinzers out there in
Pittsburgh. Clues for AM - 3 have been posted. It's the 3rd and
final box in my Alma Mater Series. AM - 1 and AM - 2 are both in RI.
Unfortunately AM - 1 had to be temporarily pulled due to
construction. AM - 2 was muggled before a single finder. I've since
recarved the stamp and replanted. I'm just too damn lazy to get the
clues together. Ironically, while doing a sweep of the original area,
I found this rock leaning against a small tree root outcrop about 40
feet from the planting site. On a 'boxer's instinctive SPOR whim, I
lifted the rock and there was the original stamp! What kind of
a$$clown would trash the box but rehide the stamp? Probably some
geocacher! ; ) KIDDING!

Good luck!
