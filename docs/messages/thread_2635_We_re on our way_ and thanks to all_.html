From: Shelley Shearer | Date: 2007-08-18 16:51:52 UTC
For those that have tried to get to the website for updates - just a note that I was
having trouble accessing it lately but the gather is moving forward! We have about 30
stamps in or coming in. (more are always welcome!) The historical society and many
shopkeepers are excited about taking part and the Castle B&B has a Murder Mystery Dinner
theatre lined up for anyone interested in an evening event.

Gather food will be some halloween treats and snackage as well as chili to combat any
cold weather.

I know its a trek but I hope to see lots of you make it out there! If anyone needs help
with hotels or arrangements please feel free to email justplaingeorge AT yahoo.com. The
Castle B&B still has one room available. Right now I think the place is all
letterboxers. :)

Also, I was recently told that a letterboxing murder mystery I'd written was accepted
into an anthology coming out probably around January. It's called Chesapeake Crimes III.
