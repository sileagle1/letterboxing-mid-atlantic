Greetings, 'Boxers...

Walker & I will be making a brief forray into New York State, camping our way to Oswego,
along the Lake to Niagra Falls and back through Corning and the Route 15 corridor of PA.
Pray, tell me O well-traveled friends, what boxes be there along the way which are of
particular interest?

It's a little like asking for a recommendation for a good place to eat, isn't it?

We will be leaving a series to mark our tour as we pass, following our dictum, "TAKE only
stamp impressions. LEAVE only small environmentally-friendly weather-sealed plastic boxes
hidden in places unlikely to make a negative environmental impact on the surrounding flora
and fauna." Good words to live by, but makes a lousy tee shirt.

Your help, as always is most appreciated.
