There are several boxes at the top of Mt. Misery there. Psyker
mentioned the Wanda and Pete box, but there are others as well. Look
for clues for the following:

Mount Misery (yes, this is a second one)
Treason!
Another Covered Bridge
Shall We Dance

All of these boxes are within 500 feet of each other at the top of the
"mountain."

The box with the statue picture is Baron Von Steuben, and sadly, it has
gone missing. There are plans to replace it. The clue can be found here:
http://www.penncoasters.com/letterboxing/vonsteuben.htm

Good luck, it is a really nice hike with some terrific boxes at the end.
