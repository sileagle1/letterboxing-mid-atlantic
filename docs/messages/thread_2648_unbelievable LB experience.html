unbelievable LB experience

From: wildhunt3 | Date: 2007-08-26 20:16:11 UTC
Having finally moved in and gotten mostly unpacked, I decided to head
out letterboxing with the two dogs on Friday.

In the parking lot, after our concession to defeat at the hands of the
mosquitoes, we were approached by a fawn. Did I mention I had two
medium sized dogs with me? About 6 feet from the dogs, the fawn
paused. I thought it would run, but instead it walked up to my dogs
(both roughly the same size as it) and tried to sniff them! Being
dogs, they went ballistic, which did finally scare the fawn into
running away. The dogs tried to follow, dragging me several feet
across the parking lot before the metal clip on Yuki's leash snapped.
He disappeared into the woods in hot pursuit of the fawn.

Thankfully, Yuki is my dog with excellent recall - if Claire'd snapped
the leash, I'd still be looking for her. And thankfully, the park
ranger saw the whole episode, not just the part where my loose dog
emerged from the underbrush, so he didn't cite me for having an
unleashed dog.

Any one else had any odd encounters with wildlife recently?

Katy
