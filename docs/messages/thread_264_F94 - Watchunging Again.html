From: cahillymom | Date: 2004-08-10 00:18:09 UTC
So now Doug places boxes like fine wines and lets them age a year
before posting clues? Got the Rifle Camp box today. The little
laminated cards are... do you have a job, Doug? WHERE do you find
the time to go so over the top? I am continually amazed at the
amount of effort put into these boxes. And to keep finding, too!

Anyway, for anyone who cares, and the rest of you hang in there and
be glad this is not the big list and coming from clear across the
country, that makes 12 published boxes along the Watchung Mountains
now. I know some like to know what's along what road, etc. This
would make a similar set.
From N to S:
1-5. Down Under Series of 5 on High Mt. - the northern end of the
2nd Watchung
6. Rifle Camp - on Garrett Mt. where the Passaic River cuts through
and takes a big, big Fall
7. Gabriella's Flag - Eagle Rock Reservation
8. Boy Scout Area Waterfalls - South Mountain Reservation
9. The South Mountain Reservation letterbox from the basement - I
KNEW I'd been there earlier this year!
10 & 11. Watchung Reservation and Stables boxes
12. my NJ Physiography 103 box by Chimney Rock
?. mmmmph, mmmmph, mmmph (that's me trying not to tell you what
other boxes might, just might, be part of this "set")
So. I am now done with the Watchung Ridge. Where next?
Aud

I have learned that if there is a spot in NJ that deserves a box, it

Of course, there's a story behind the length of time that it took me to
post the clues, a series of unfortunate incidents as well as the plans I
had in mind for a more interesting clue. Finally, I tossed it all aside
since the box had been sitting in the park all that time, completely
clueless, and threw together the zen minimalist clue version instead.

Jennifer and I had been lamenting our recent lack of box planting recently
(though we did restore four "heritage" boxes, so that should count for
something), but we have plans in place (and even some boxes ready to go
that I've been carrying around waiting for the right location to jump out
at us), so sometime after we get back from our trip to CT this weekend we
will start planting again. Or maybe we'll wait until it's too cold to
letterbox this winter. Or maybe I'll be better able to focus on new boxes
after I get to F500... Or maybe....
