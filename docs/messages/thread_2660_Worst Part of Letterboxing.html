Worst Part of Letterboxing

From: nattybumppolbna | Date: 2007-09-03 18:27:37 UTC
I know... How about a discussion topic: Letterboxing.

What's the worst part of Letterboxing?

Me? Start? Well, if you insist...

For me, the part of Letterboxing I dislike the most is the encounter with those folks who
have a proclivity for seeking carnal gratification in the outdoors. (subtle enough?)

Maybe it's me. Maybe I draw that kinda crowd. But I'm getting a little irked by the guys
that follow me into the woods from the parking lot. [The last time I just yelled to the sky,
"I'M NOT INTERESTED!" and he finally went away.]

I also get a little weirded when Walker and I walk around a bend in the trail and run into
two guys who are desperately trying to look invisible.

I know I hit strange parks at weird times - maybe I should stay away from the urban/
suburban parks altogether, but it also happened to me in a little county park in the Middle
of Nowhere, NY. I have no problem with what they are doing, just where they are doing it.
Have these people never heard of Motels? Cars? Alleys? Bakeries?

I know there is a price to be paid for Letterboxing The World, but JEEEZUS!

I am considering something like a cow bell they use out west so they don't surprise the
bears. I need a noisemaker that says "HETERO COMING WITH POSSIBLE CHILDREN IN TOW"

Any suggestions?

Natt-
