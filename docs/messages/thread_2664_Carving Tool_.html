RE: [lbma] Carving Tool?

From: JuneMcAllister | Date: 2007-09-06 22:37:32 UTC
Unless I have a really larger area I want to carve out, in which case I use
the #5, all my carving is done with a #1 speedball carving tool. I still
use the one I got in my original carving tool kit I got when I first
started. They should be at any large art store or online. I think you get 5
or 6 tools, I forget.

I know someone who has made her #1 even smaller by heating it with a flame
and gently bending it inward as it softened up. I tried doing it with a
pair of pliers and no heat but didn't like the result. It seemed to drag. I
haven't tried the heat method yet.

I also do all my carving with a magnifying lamp. Can't do squat without it.
And yes, you do push it, not pull. You gouge the line out. Glad you liked
them!!

MissMoon
