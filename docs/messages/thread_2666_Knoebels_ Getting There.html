Knoebels: Getting There

From: Nathan Brown | Date: 2007-09-06 18:26:22 UTC
Since Mapquest really stinks at giving directions for getting to
Knoebels, here are several ways you can do it.

From New York/ New England area
- Get yourself to I-89 west
- Take exit 241 for Rt 11 south
- Turn left onto Rt 487 South
- You follow Rt 487 to the park. Watch for signs, as the road makes
several turns in Catawissa, including turning right at a Y after the
town that is easy to miss. The campground is after you get to the park.

From the West
- Take I-80 East to exit 524 and take Rt 54 South
- Follow Rt 54 to Elysburg
- Turn left onto Rt 487 in Eylsburg
- This will take you right to the park. The campground is before you
get to the park.

From The Harrisburg/Lancaster area
- Take I-81 North to exit 116
- Turn left onto Rt 901 North
- Turn left onto Rt 54 North (If you are going to dinner Friday night,
turn right here to go into Ashland)
- Turn Right onto Rt 487 North
- This will take you right to the park. The campground is before you
get to the park.

From Baltimore Washington
- Take I-83 North to Harrisburg and follow the Harrisburg Directions above.

From Philadelphia
There are several different ways to take
Quick route
- Take the Northeast Extension to exit 95
- Go straight at the light and onto I-80 West
- Follow directions for New York/New England

Scenic Route (Take if you are going to dinner Friday)
- Take Rt 422 West to Rt 222 North
- Exit for Rt 61 North
- Follow Rt 61 to Ashland (watch for several turns, they are well marked)
- Go through Ashland and stay straight onto Rt 54
- Follow directions for Harrisburg/Lancaster

Going from the park to Devitto's
- From the park or campground, turn left onto Rt 487 South
- Turn left onto Rt 54 South
- This will take you into Ashland. Devitto's is on the right just
before the end of town.

I think that should cover it. Email me if anymore directions are needed.
