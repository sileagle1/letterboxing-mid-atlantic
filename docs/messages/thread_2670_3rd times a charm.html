It was fantastic. I hope to do a thorough follow up on my blog in the
next day or two, but I must say I am amazed.

Thanks to everyone that came out. There were an amazing amount of folks
for a gathering that has so few boxes.

Thanks to JBZfarm for the wonderful boxes they planted. The stamps are
spectacular.

Thanks to everyone that camped. We had a huge turnout for the camp,
which means one thing, I have to set it up better next year (and bring
more lights!) If you have not camped at this or any of our gatherings,
you are really missing out. It is one thing that I think really sets us
apart, our camp outs are a great time for everyone.

Thanks to everyone that cleaned up afterwards, great job!

I think you can expect more of the same next year, only a better
pavilion and some better site placement.

So pencil in September 13th 2008 for the forth installment!

From: jbzfarm | Date: 2007-09-10 12:03:19 UTC
Knoebels Gathering Rocks!

A big thank you to Nathan for setting up this fun event! We were so
glad to re-connect with some dear friends and very happy to meet some
new ones. This is one event that is already on our calendar for next
year.

Thank you to everyone who found and replaced our boxes. Rainbow Girl
survived! That is quite a feat, knowing its placement and the
traffic. While The Lighthouse was muggled, we were glad that 3 of
the 4 made it through the day. We very much appreciate the care that
was taken by many of you in replacing these high trafficked boxes that
required such stealth. We hope you enjoyed being sneaky. For us it
was great fun as we knew where we could go in the park to find boxers.
We peeked more than once to see who was stamping in. It was a real
treat that is rarely experienced on the trail.

Thanks for sharing in the fun.
