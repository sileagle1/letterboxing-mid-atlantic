Thankfully I think the 150 number is inflated. Though not nearly as
many boxes that is little comfort to those whose boxes were taken.

boxes and clues were pulled to protect them, and some offers to
recarve and rehide boxes. It was good to see the community circle
the wagons and support each other.

keep us down. And most agreed. Many have been replanted and/or
reposted.

They're Crazy in CA

From: Suzanne Coe | Date: 2007-09-15 15:06:29 UTC

OK, so we're in sunny California (me, Becky, Nathan, Bob), having a wonderful time.  Went out to dinner with a few of the local folks  and afterwards we walk over to the supermarket with Wassamatta_u & his 6yr old daughter to get some beer.  Yes,  they have beer in the supermarket.  How enlightened....or so we think. 

After making our selections & picking up a few other things (3-pack of Locknlock knockoffs for 2.49!!), we head for the checkout.  Nathan scans his beer, the register prompts for a cashier's approval.  Cashier wants to see his ID.  No problem!  He whips out his driver's license (the one he used to get the rental car that we've been driving all over the state in) & the cashier looks at it & shakes his head.  "No, that's no good.  You got California ID?"  Nathan says no, he's from Pennsylvania.  Cashier looks at me.  "YOU got California ID?"  No, I'M from DELAWARE....  We try to convince him that we are clearly of an age to consume alcohol, that we will never come up with California ID, etc....

We end up having Wassa buy our beer for us.  By this time the cashier is so tired that he doesn't bother to check Wassa's ID.  Which is a good thing, because he'd left it in the car!
