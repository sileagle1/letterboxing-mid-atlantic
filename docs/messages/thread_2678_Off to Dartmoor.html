From: nattybumppolbna | Date: 2007-09-13 20:38:04 UTC
I have never been to Dartmoor. But I have always wondered what it's like. I was working with
a British film crew and asked if they had ever heard of letterboxing. No one had. I asked if
anybody had been to Dartmoor. A few of them had worked on a film in Dartmoor Park where
crew members would occaisionally come across pill bottles and such hidden under rocks.
They also said they had a problem with people with compasses who would wander into the
shot - completely oblivious to what was going on.

So in that way, I imagine Dartmoor is a lot like Connecticut.

Have a great time.
