From: Hikers and Hounds | Date: 2007-09-18 06:37:48 UTC
The trip went well, I have fallen in love with England! The villages down here in Cornwall look like something from a Thomas Kincaid painting. The moors are stark and beautiful, the ponies and sheep wander all around you as you hike. The pubs are incredible and the kind of place you can just stay in all day. Well, I can't hog this computer so this will have to be brief. Oh, several of us played trivia last night at the Prince of Wales pub and came in second place!  The boxing is fun but I do like the American version a little better, but it's a privilege to be here.  You might hear all kinds of wild stories about me or see weird pictures posted on the Pepe's site about me having to duct tape my pants on the moor, but it's all a pack of viscous lies!! Well, can't hog Silent Doug's computer. I miss you all!

H&H

Looking forward to seeing you all in man-asses next weekend, don't forget to ask me about my new personal traveller!
