Last I knew the Lighthouse box is gone, and possibly the Phoenix, but
the others are in place, as far as I know. Knoebels Grove is under a
pile of gravel. It may still be there, but bring a shovel to get it out.

As far as area boxes, both your Ashland boxes are in decent shape.
Mother could use a new box, the old one is cracked. But it was dry.
Piece-a Pizza has a new box and log and looks great, so stop in and say
hi to Tom and his wife.

We did not check on the box in Centralia.

Waiting is in fine shape in Danville

Pineknotter Nuptials looked good in Sunbury, and be sure to get up and g
et Hey Mose as well, neat clues, but not as neat as the clues for the
mystery box in the same park. Those boxes looked good as well.

Hope that helps, and have fun. Hope you don't get the ride nazi on the
Flyers and can get some good snapping in :-)
