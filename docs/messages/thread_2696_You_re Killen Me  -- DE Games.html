From: sewsowbizzy | Date: 2007-09-24 15:13:12 UTC
OCTOBER 2007 - the DE State Park Application expires this month. So I
am pulling ALL Killens Pond SP boxes and will submit new applications
for replanting the exCLUsion boxes in this park. May not happen until
Spring 2008 but this clue will be updated when it happens.

If I'm recovered enough from jolly ole England tommorrow morning I may
head up to Killens Pond and do the retrieval. Otherwise later this
month.

The non exCLUsion boxes from the park will be replanted around DE
(unless the carvers wanted them back) next year.

Also, of the exCLUsion boxes, 1 Chef 1Food 1Restaurant will not be
planted in KPSP. Anyone finding all the others and guessing the
correct who/what/where will be given clues to find them somewhere else
in DE -- again next year.

sewsowdizzy from all the travel

Re: [lbma] Re: You're Killen Me -- DE Games

From: JuleBee | Date: 2007-10-03 17:02:27 UTC
Just wanted to let you know that I got out to Killens Pond on Friday (28th).  It was a beautiful day and I had a great time.  I may not have found many boxes, but I sure did find the chiggers.  Is there anything to use to prevent getting them???  Here is the rundown:
exCLUsion - Food #1 - found - this was a really cool 2 sided stamp.
exCLUsion - Chef #2 - not found - there were runner on the trail and I quick slipped back behind the dogwood tree after they were out of sight but didn't spend any time looking as there were a lot of people out running.
exCLUsion - Chef #1 - found
1930 decade stamp - not found
MonyLoop - Resort Bonus 2 - Not found
exClusion - Food # 2 - not found - this one may be there but since my back was still giving me problems I didn't really try too hard on this one.  Plus I had no idea what Kruger's knuckles looked like.  Had to ask my daughter when I got home.  Pretty sure was at the right place, but not exactly sure of where to look.
Mony Loop - State #2 - didn't even try for this one, after 3 hours roaming around, my back wouldn't let me go any further..
