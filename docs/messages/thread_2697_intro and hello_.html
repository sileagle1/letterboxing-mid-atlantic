intro and hello!

From: Victor E. Ramirez | Date: 2007-09-24 19:44:45 UTC
Hi! My name is Victor, and my gf & I are new letterboxers. My trail
name is Ratinabox and hers is Abita. We live in Montgomery County,
MD, bordering Washington DC. We started letterboxing about a month
ago (intersting story behind that, but I'll save it for another
post); we've already found six boxes on five sets of clues (one was
a two-box clue), and just last Saturday we found our first
hitchhiker.

(To say we were excited was putting it mildly. If I could do
backflips, I probably wouldn't have. Ground wasn't really
level 'round there.)

She's eager to plant our first box; we've already scouted out some
locations and are looking for two more places to plant them, since
she's decided on a three-box clue. It's a clever theme so I don't
blame her, but the place we want to hide them is around a community
lake, and I fear the chance of vandalism is rather high.

I hope I can learn a lot from the more experienced 'boxers on this
list...and that I hope you don't mind hearing from a new letterboxer
every now and then.

--Victor
