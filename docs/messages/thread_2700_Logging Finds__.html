I think they should have to stamp in if a logbook exists.

But unless it's a series of boxes with a logbook in only the last one and
they know that going in, I'm not sure if it's right to have the stamp and
logbook in different places. I'm of two opinions. That's obviously not what
we think of as a traditional box but I know you are trying to be different
and "change the game". Part of me says that's cool and part of me says
there should maybe be certain basic elements that remain the same in order
to prevent things like this from happening. OR.......If you're going to
change the game, I think you have to consider ahead of time if something
like that happens....think it through to all the possible outcomes and
decide how will it be handled before it happens. There are some urban boxes
that have no logbook at all because of the location they are in but I
consider those finds.
