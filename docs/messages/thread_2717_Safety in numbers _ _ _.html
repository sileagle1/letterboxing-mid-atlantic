Re: Safety in numbers . . .

From: Victor E. Ramirez | Date: 2007-10-03 12:15:04 UTC
After having read the clues on AQ for a particular box, where the hider
lifted up the rock and found a snake before doing maintenance, I
usually have one of these with me:

http://www.rei.com/product/745686

It helped me keep my footing while finding the Brood X box last week
(though I still slipped on all the nuts on the ground--those things are
like walking on ball bearings) and I figure I can poke it into any
suspicious holes w/o it breaking. Plus, since I usually have a camera
with me (so that I look like a photo geek instead of a letterboxer),
the little cork ball screws off and becomes a camera monopole.

OTOH, you have to be *real careful* extending the pole. I broke two in
REI before I figured out the trick (and the trick is to be *real
careful*).

--Victor (Ratinabox)
