Re: [lbma] hunting season

From: Heather Siple | Date: 2007-10-02 16:34:04 UTC
Deer and dove season started September 1 in Delaware.  We went out to the canal a few weeks ago and were startled by a guy in camo with a cross-bow, in the only bush in the middle of a barren, 200-yard wide sand pit.  I think he only made his presence known because we were making a ton of noise and scaring away his quarry.  He warned us that we should think about going away because it might be easy for us to get shot by a stray bit of ammo, but somehow I think that as we were out in the open, loud and obvious and he was so well hidden that we didn't see him until he said something right next to us, that it was not our safety he was concerned about.
