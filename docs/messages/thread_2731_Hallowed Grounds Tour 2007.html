Since some of you aren't too far to join us (NYC is only about 6 hours
from Western NY), I thought I'd let you know about our Hallowed
Grounds Tour on the 20th of October. We're doing a driving tour of the
some of the famous and not so famous cemeteries in Orleans County, NY
that'll end on our (The Sprite & the Highlander's) doorstep. The clues
are finally done and are posted on our website. Either click on the
website at the event page here:
http://www.atlasquest.com/events/event.html?gEventId=453

or go to the AQ page for the boxes and the clue link will take you
right to it:
http://www.atlasquest.com/lboxes/showboxinfo.html?gBoxId=57018

Again, if you can make it out our way, it promises to be a fruitful
venture. There's 31 stamps in the series, plus several on the wa that
you can grab to make an even 40 or more. Plus, you'll get to climb up
inside a spooky stone tower and hang out in the Highlander's Halloween
decoration explosion at our place! Just sign up so we know you're
coming and we'll give you our home number in case you get lost. I
uploaded the map to a page as well, but if you want a better view, get
a Western NY Region map from Mapworks...it's the best!

Hope to see some new (and familiar) joining us!

The (spooky) Sprite & the (haunted) Highlander

P.S.--The boxes are permanent, but the event stamp that will make the
