Among the 40 brewerys and microbrewerys pour... Gallant Rogue's
favorite Rogue Ales will be participating. Rogue Ales are made with the
finest hops and barley malt, free range coastal water and Pacman top
fermenting proprietary yeast. Rogue does not pasteurize its products.
Rogue Ales are bottled using an oxygen absorbing cap, brown glass for
better shelf life, and plenty of malted barley and hops to provide
stability. I may volunteer for the moring shift so I get free admission
and other free stuff... including beer!
