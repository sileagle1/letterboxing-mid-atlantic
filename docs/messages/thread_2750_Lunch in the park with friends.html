Lunch in the park with friends

From: Hikers and Hounds | Date: 2007-10-19 02:16:13 UTC
A litte late posting this but Wednesday it was my pleasure to greet the Von Der Insels when they came into Philadelphia for a visit. We got to spend my lunch hour eating cheesesteaks in my favorite little park across from Independence Hall and they got my special Philadelphia stamp. The good news is they didn't get nearly as much sight seeing in as they had planned and will have to return. By then I should have "My Beautiful City" series done for them (and others) to find. After my Dec and Jan gathers. Which are coming along quite nicely I might add!

From: von der Insel | Date: 2007-10-24 19:54:15 UTC
That was a great day!
We saw the major sights of downtown and left letterboxing until another trip, especially since we came in by train from Trenton and all the boxes are on the outskirts of town - it seems! But there is so much history to take in and I took way too many pictures!
After the Liberty Bell and Independence Hall we made our way down to Carpenter's Hall and then on to the City Tavern for a well deserved libation and dinner. Train travel does have its perks! We'll have to catch the carriage rides next time - they stop running between 4PM and 6PM  - wussies. What's a little rush hour traffic! ;)
Diane
the von der Insels
