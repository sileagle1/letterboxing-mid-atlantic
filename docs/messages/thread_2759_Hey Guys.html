Howdy Everyone,

I'm 1/6 of Hart x6. We live in Illinois on the boarder of Iowa in
what is known as the Quad Cities.

We've been boxing for 11 months now, generally in the Illinois/Iowa
area. This past June we drove 2,700 miles to Oregon and back to visit
with my folks -- it was our first long haul vacation with the
knowledge of letterboxing, that really helped break up the trip.

We have 4 kids. Skeeter age 5, Bug-a-Boo age 3 and twins who will be
2 in January. Skeeter and Bug-a-Boo are our little hikers, they love
to box and their only complaint is, "Daddy, I get bored when we don't
find the letterbox."

Anyhow, the reason we joined this board is that sometime next year
(probably late August) we will be making a family trip to the East
Coast and spend most of our time in WV, VA & NC.

I want to take the next several months to get to know you guys over
the board, because I enjoy finding boxes much more when I have some
sense of who placed it (even if its just name recognition).

I will probably have a few boxes to place while out there, so I may
recruit some helpful tips on a park or something where we could hide
a box. Also, once we nail down the itinerary some, I will probably
ask some advice on boxes to find.

But that is all months in the future. I just wanted to say, "hey" and
I look forward to getting to know you.

Hart x6
