Bunkhouse Chili

2 pounds lean beef for stew cut into ½ cubes
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 medium chopped onion
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 can (16 ounces) chili flavored diced tomatoes
1 cup bbq sauce. Something hickory or spicy
1 to 2 tablespoons of chili powder
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon salt
¼ teaspoon pepper
2 cans (15-17 ounces each) red kidney beans
- Possible to add 1 ½ cupes of beer as well. We didnt in this one.
Brown beef in oil. Add onion and garlic; sauté. Stir in tomatoes, bbq sauce, chili
powder, oregano, salt and pepper.(add beer now if using it). Cover and simmer for one
hour, stirring occasionally. Add kidney beans, simmer uncovered for 45 minutes.
Sprinkle with cheese when done.
