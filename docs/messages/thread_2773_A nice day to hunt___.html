A nice day to hunt...

From: jerseytrailblazers | Date: 2007-10-29 16:30:08 UTC
Hello all, I haven't posted in a month or so, been busy working too
much ..but kept up boxing in between. Just recently I had the chance
to go boxing with Rick_in_Boca. We hadn't gone in awhile so it was
good to meet up again. We decided to hunt some PCLG treasure. To
sum it up, we found the box, "Uprising" which was still in place
since 2002 and in good shape. Good clues (make you think) on that
one. We were only about the 6th or so to find it. Last finders were
early in 2006 - I think it was A.Cahilly. We also recently looked
for "Huts". The box had not been logged on LBNA since 2004 as being
found. We seemed to have found the spot -(another good clue set) it
matched up well to the directions and lo & behold we found a
box...with its top chewed off by some sort of animal...with plastic
bag remnants but no stamp or log or anything else. Most remanants
were covered in leaves so we spent some time checking around the spot
for any contents scattered about - but none other were found. We are
considering rejuvenating the box for future finders.... but of course
that is always a topic of debate among some people.....and just came
up on AQ again where I basically jumped back into the same (martian
maggot)conversation that took place here back in the Spring. I had
been conversing with Brian of PCLG a few months back and was in the
process of waiting for him to decide which of their boxes I could
adopt (since as many know the group disbanded and moved away). He
was receptive and then......the conversations stopped. I have no way
of knowing for what reason. Tried again recently to contact him but
have still not heard back. So the boxes remain un-adopted by me. I
will not attempt to adopt without permission.....not getting stuck in
that hornets nest...but as the boxes survive (if they do) I will help
to maintain whenever I am in the area(s). Getting back to "Huts",
both Rick and I really want to maintain that great box with clues as
is..... so we'll see how it goes. I'll defer more to him on this one
as he's been down this bumpy road before... Hope to meet up with
more of you on the trails some time. -John
