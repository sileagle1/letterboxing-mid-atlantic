Hehehe, love the trail name.

There is not too much downtown, but if you know where to look you will
find some.

Email Hikers and Hounds, she has a special stamp if you meet her for
lunch. She works in a building right next to Independence Hall.

If you search THIS yahoo group you will find some word of mouth boxes.
Search for Freedom Trail and also Chaos at the Zoo.

A hybrid box, "Be Prepared," is still alive and kicking, and in a great
location. You may want to check out "Persistence Of Wink" as well,
though I don't know if that is still there.

That is about it. There are some boxes in parks further out in the city
and tons in the suburbs.

Let us know when you will be in town and likely there will be some folks
that can meet up with you for exchanges.
