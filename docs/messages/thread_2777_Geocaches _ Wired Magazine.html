Re: Geocaches & Wired Magazine

From: Lightnin Bug | Date: 2007-10-31 16:10:07 UTC
Geocachers that I know in the eastern PA area had this happen to
them a few years ago (2004 or so) - a particular individual was
targeting/stealing caches that were stored in ammo containers. They
had their suspicions as to who the perp was (but could not prove
anything concrete) and a couple of them had a talk with the person
they thought was responsible. Local caches are far more numerous
and much more frequently visited than letterboxes, so it was only a
matter of time before the local GC community was able to notice
something was amiss, monitor and report on suspicious activity and
act.

No pitchforks, etc., but still action.
