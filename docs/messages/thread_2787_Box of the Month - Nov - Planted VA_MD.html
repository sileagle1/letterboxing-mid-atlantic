Box of the Month VA/DC Nov - Fluttering Around by Flutterby

Planted in Potomac, MD at Carderock. Easy, short walk along the river.

http://74.200.76.117/lboxes/showboxinfo.html?gBoxId=59592

Penguin Patrol has this crazy idea. Instead of just a postal ring, why
not send plantable boxes? Twelve people from all over have created a
box and mailed it. We will plant them for one month for anyone to find.
Then pick it up, mail it on, and plant the next one in the same place
for the next month.

The first box' stamp is beautiful and easy to color: Fluttering Around
by Flutterby of CT. (Fluffy Angel got excited when she saw it in my
log, but I couldn't tell her what it was then.)

I wanted to plant it at a place easy to get to, right off the Beltway,
close to both MD and VA. I did. Ok, maybe I shouldn't have planted it
in this particular park but I couldn't resist the nice views of the
river. I think it is a spot worth visiting again and again each month.

Let me know what you think.
