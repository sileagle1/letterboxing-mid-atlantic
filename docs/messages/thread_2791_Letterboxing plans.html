From: alteredtome | Date: 2007-11-11 18:30:14 UTC
I am new so I hadn't thought about a plan, yet.

Find: so many boxes so little time; just to get everything that is close by that I can take my dog with me.

Plant: Do my first plant [which will be at Frying Pan Farm Park, Herndon Virginia (Fairfax County)]. I have a stamp carved, log book made; scout out the "drop" location yesterday and wrote the clues; but need to locate a good container. Haven't been able to locate Lock and Locks at any of the recommended store so I may have to turn to rubber Maid. But  we were out surfing the internet last night and found the greatest box by Pelican, their Micro case Mossy Oak series. It had an "O" ring seal and the outside was printed with leaves. A box  $21.00 to leave in the woods seem over the top, particularly since I hope to do at least one plant for every 10 finds.

alteredtome
