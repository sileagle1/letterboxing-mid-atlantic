RE: [lbma] Planted my 1st LB

From: JuneMcAllister | Date: 2007-11-12 21:36:41 UTC
Use your own judgement, depending on the location, how close it is to you,
how busy the location; do you think it has a chance of going missing
easily? If you think it's safely hidden from prying hands, eyes, kids,
animals, weather, just wait to hear from a finder for the time being. And
remember that a box doesn't necessarily get found immediately. Some boxes
go many months between finds, depending on the location, clues, etc. Some
boxes get lots of traffic.

missmoon
