DC Letterboxing Weekend. Classic getting out there.

From: The Doubtful Guests | Date: 2004-08-15 22:59:13 UTC

We had a classic "getting out there" afternoon letterboxing.  Took my 87 year old folks out letterboxing for drive byes.  They live in a semi-assisted facility now so they really enjoy getting out.  Despite Charley and overcast skies, it was a beautiful afternoon. 

Found Bastion finally after not finding it on a previous outing.  You do have to do the obvious research sometimes.  I love architectural type plans. 

Went after Cornucopia.  Really enjoyed strolling and shopping near this urban letterbox.  Too many people were hanging out by it so we went for ice cream and sat in a sidewalk cafe enjoying the day.  Upon return I was able to snag it while the two old guys hanging out there never looked up from their paper.  Enjoyed the place and the cute stamp. 

Did I find 20 letterboxes today?  No, but we all had a perfect summer afternoon out, in an area we have not been to in too long.  My folks also really enjoyed it. 

Thanks for the fun,

Scarab of the Doubtful Guests
