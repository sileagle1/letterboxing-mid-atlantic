Not sure how many people have already seen these but they are perfect
for my boxes. Versafine by Tsukineko has a small stamp pad which is
about 1 5/16" square and 13/16" deep. It comes in a variety of colors.
I found it at a local rubber stamping store near my home in Virginia,
not a big chain craft store.
