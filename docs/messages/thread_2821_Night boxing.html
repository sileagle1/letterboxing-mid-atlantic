I tend to warn away from headlamps for night boxing. They tend to shine
in other people's faces, throwing off their night vision. Stick with a
small, low power flashlight and keep it low on the trail. The moon is
in its last quarter, and the cloud cover should light things up pretty well.

From: brendan_hickey13 | Date: 2007-11-30 19:09:48 UTC
Not bad to carry both. A red light or a red filter - some
flashlights and a lot of head lamps come with them - is a big help
because the red light won't kill your night vision and isn't quite
so obvious to others.

Beyond that? Expect cooler temps and dew, so waterproof boots or
trail runners help. A walking stick is a good idea, as is a
thorough reading of the clue and your map before setting out.
