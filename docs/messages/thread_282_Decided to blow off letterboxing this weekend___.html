Decided to blow off letterboxing this weekend...

From: Hikers_n_ Hounds | Date: 2004-08-15 22:14:51 UTC
Had way too much to do. Got a big camping/caving weekend coming up and am boxing all next Sunday in Lancaster with the Red Hat Letterboxers (as the Mad Hatter). So I decided to take a break from this addiction and try to remember what my weekends used to be like before boxing.
 
Oh, except Saturday AM we met Zed Boxing and Little Stinker at Philly Int'l to welcome them to our area and do an image exchange. And I had my work friend Mike Smith with me who doesn't have a car so usually only gets to box with me. Since we were so close to Delaware we decided to hunt for just a few boxes because I told Mike I really didn't have time to box this weekend. So we found Just Passin' Thru, Yummy, Brandywine Bluebird and Future Discovery Road Runner. Got home around 8PM!! Stopped and bought some wine and decided to spend just a little time on my log. Around midnight after way too much wine crashed out with Mr. Irene. Woke up today with the "bottle flu" and was glad I was REALLY not going to do any boxing today. Until I read my email...The Raindears wanted to meet at Nockamixon to plant our co-project, a Lord of the Rings series. So, had to wake Carvin' Carmen, he and I threw the boxers together and Dude and I boogied up to north to mmet the Raindears. Got the boxes planted and exchanged with them again (we didn't all get each others images at Leap Day). Saw a Dollar Tree on the way home, ran in and spent about $30 on boxing stuff (Silent Doug, they really own you a paycheck for the advertising you have given them). And just got in to start my non-letterboxing weekend stuff. Sure glad I decided to blow off letterboxing this weekend....
