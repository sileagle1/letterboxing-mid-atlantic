Hey LBMA folks - this is fun idea for the cold winter months -- not
that weather stops LBMA'rs from hitting the trail!!

For those that don't already belong to GL -- here is the gist:
Organizer has created a BINGO Card Format, instructions, and list of
block entries (in lieu of numbers) in the following categories:

Letterboxes
Trail Names
Events
General Terms

There are 125 choices for 2008, laid out in columns B.I.N.G.O

Player prints a card (table in word) and puts 25 entries (in
appropriate column) on their playing card which is mailed into the
organizer along with $1/postage and a prize contribution.

The hitch is: to list a letterbox, trail name or event -- you have to
have found, exchanged, or attended!! Which is why I can't play the GL
game %^(

Organizer posts one of the 125 'words' each morning and if it is on
your pre-entered card you daub it (BINGO lingo).

The usual winning categories apply (Full card, 4 corners, straight
line, "X", perimeter).

If this is of interest to LBMA -- I'm game for setting it up to run
beginning Feb 1. As a first time - we could skip the mailing bit and
you just email me your block choices before Feb 1 for verification
when you call BINGO. In fact in the spirit of DE GAMES - I would call
it TRAIL %^)

As for the prizes, in lieu of organizing a gather in 2008, I would
contribute 4x6 pink stuff for each winner -- only you would have to
meet me at a gather in 2008 to retrieve it -- that stuff is heavy!

To be real fun, we would need at least 25 players. So I will set up
in a few minutes a POLL - "LBMA TRAIL" - just check YES and your name
will appear as having 'voted'. If there will be multiple players per
'family' - I'll include some number choices - just 'vote' for the
number playing. When 25 have 'voted' - I'll generate the details.

NEED votes by Jan 7 so there is time to prepare and for you to
select/email your card choices.
