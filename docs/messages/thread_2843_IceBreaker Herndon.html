HOHOHO!!! ;-)

Sorry to be late on this, I am still recovering from a wonderful weekend
with my wonderful Schitzokeet, but that is another story for another
time and in another place :-D

It was a fantastic time, with many new faces along with the old, and
even some we have not seen in years. The Breeze played a fantastic
host, food was excellent and the service was beyond awesome. Did anyone
catch the name of the waitress, I would like to send a nice thank you note?

Everything was very casual and laid back,and there were many that stayed
the entire time.

All I did was make a reservation and carve a stamp. The people that
actually showed up are the ones that made the gathering happen. That is
what I think a gathering is really all about, getting people with a
common interest, or even passion, together. Event stamps, table treats,
they are all just bonuses, it is all you folks that took the time to
come out that really make it all come together.

I hope we can do it again, and I am already planning for next year, it
will probably be on the 28th of December, not sure yet.
