I, personally, would like to see a tree fall in the forest. Once,
though, is good enough for me, and certainly from a distance greater
than a few inches.

I did see a large branch fall from a tree. Coincidentally, it was
near where Abita and I were pulled over by the police, on a
different day. We both heard the rather loud cracking and turned
toward the sound in time to see a huge branch falling to the ground.

I thought it was pretty cool; Abita thought it was pretty scary. I
pointed out it's cool from a safe distance and would definitely be
scary if it were directly above our heads. And I resolved should I
ever hear a sound that loud again and I can't see what's causing it
in front of me, it must be above me and the best course of action
would be something along the lines of Mr. Bumpo's, only without the
screaming like a little girl. I plan to substitute cursing like a
sailor.
