Hi all,

I don't post much but thought I'd give my thoughts on this. I think
most of you are right in that LTC's, Postals, etc... have nothing to
do with Letterboxing.

But in reality, what do personal travelers, special stamps that you
share with friends, etc., have to do with letterboxing either? I
mean, other than traditional letterboxing, it's all just icing.

And I think that's my point: Sure, none of this extra crap really
is letterboxing, and yeah, AQ gets silly sometimes, but who cares?
There will still be people, plenty of them in fact, who just put
boxes out there in the woods. I would quit this hobby if there
weren't. But why get in a huff about the folks who want to do extra
stuff with stamps? LTC's are a way to share art, as are postals and
the other stuff. If they're not for you, cool. If they are,
whatever.

I'm glad I happen to be part of the LBNA retinue where there are so
many skilled planters. If I were stuck somewhere where there was a
bunch of people only in it for the icing I'd quickly fall out of
this hobby. I think we should count our blessings rather than point
out the wierdness that other folks do. (That's a grammatically awful
sentence but I'm unwilling to change it right now.)

Mr. Yuk (4 LTC's and some postals to my credit, but would rather
figure out a Mystery any day.)
