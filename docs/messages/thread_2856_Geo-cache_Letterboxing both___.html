Re: [lbma] Geo-cache/Letterboxing both???

From: Jim Fiorentino | Date: 2008-01-04 00:41:25 UTC
I do both geocaching (since 9/04) & letterboxing (since 8/07). I actually got started in LB'ing when I found a Letterbox Hybrid geocache in a I-84 rest area in Ct. 
 
I enjoy them both and you can have wonderful adventures and meet people from all walks of life doing both. Both geocaching and letteboxing bring something to the table and can be quite enjoyable when combined on a hike or a vacation. Don't rule out one for the other..explore and enjoy...then make your choice (if needed).  
 
In geocaching the 'search engine is you'...as it is in letterboxing...do what you feel is right for you and your family. Try them both and enjoy.
 
See you along the trail.
