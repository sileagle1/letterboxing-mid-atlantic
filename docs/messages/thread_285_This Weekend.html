This Weekend

From: Kathryn Wolfe | Date: 2004-08-16 20:46:37 UTC
    So, this is about my third weekend of letterboxing, and this past weekend my F count rose to (dun dun dun) ... 8!  My mind boggles when I see posts from people roaming around Connecticut reporting 30 finds in a day. 
 
    I actually ended up doing lots of searching with only a little finding, but the searching was so enjoyable that I really didn't mind.  I looked unsuccessfully for Radio Days (I found a dirty wifebeater, but no stamp!), and the rain cut short my hunting for Pandamania here in downtown DC.  Similarly, stymied on Boxing After Dark, though I stuck my hand in many drainpipes and looked up many street signs for the elusive box.  I also couldn't find the VA letterbox that had been hidden in Glencarlyn Park, though the walk was so nice that I didn't really mind.  The brook is beautiful.  However, I did manage to find Mr. Bow-Wow in Virginia, none the less for water-wear, hiding coyly behind a tree.
 
Enjoy,
 
Longhorn
