Lookin' Good For The Pub Crawl

From: Nathan Brown | Date: 2008-01-07 19:20:45 UTC
Well, things are getting well in order for the Pub Crawl this coming
weekend in New Hope, PA. Hikers and Hounds, Miz Scarlet and I were out
and about this past Saturday confirming pubs and planning boxes and
clues. Nearly all stamps are ready and will be in place shortly. H&H
has been hard at work on the trivia, and it should be a blast. I was
worried, but everything is lookin' good, and now I am very excited!

The crawl will be very laid back, and you don't have to drink to join in
the fun. Just one person per team has to order something from each of
the locations, and it does not have to be a drink. So even if you don't
drink, consider coming on out and joining in the fun.

You can sign up on AQ if you like, but you are not required. Just meet
us at noon at Triumph Brewery, things should get fully underway by one.

http://www.atlasquest.com/events/event.html?gEventId=282
