Idea for disabled son to be able to carve, at the
beauty supply store there is this little tool used for
cuticles, it looks like a little gouge but is not
really sharp but will cut soft carving material. I
used it for my Cub scouts. Even the kid with hemophila
was able to carve with no trip to the ER.
As for the trading card thing, no one will ostrasize
you for it but my point is it is not letterboxing.
They are ATCs (artist trading cards) done by boxers.
And yes I carve stamps for other purposes that can't
be considered boxes, exchanges, event stamps ect. And
it's all in good fun.
