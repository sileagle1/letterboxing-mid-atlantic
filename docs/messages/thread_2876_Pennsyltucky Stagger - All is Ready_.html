Pennsyltucky Stagger - All is Ready!

From: Hikers and Hounds | Date: 2008-01-12 01:11:33 UTC
Well the boxes are planted, beer is being brewed (I highly recommend Triump's Coffee and Cream Stout) and the pubs are as excited as I am! Special thanks to Triumph Brewery for giving us a free room upstairs to start and finish in, but all of the pubs have been great. Mother's is running a drink special just for us "Spicy Mama" or something, and Ricky the bartender at Marsha Brown's is really looking forward to meeting everyone. All of they pubs are. Please treat them well.
 
LODGING - There is still lodging available at The Logan Inn and The Inn at Stoney Hill. I've been getting a lot of grief about loding, but these are both very small villages and all there is are B&B.s Most are about $200 a night. but if you double (or triple) up like the Red Hats do it's more affordable. I promise you, it's a lovely place and well worth it. Sowsewbizzy saw them for the first time today and is already talking about when she can return!
 
RULES - Teams are a good idea but not required. One team member MUST buy something at each pub, alcohol is NOT required. But I do hope you will all spend a little freely and tip well. YOU DRINK AND CRAWL AT YOUR OWN RISK. You're an adult, take care of yourself. If you drink too much and can't drive let me know, we will have sober folks to help get you home. If you live too far we'll have sleeping bags and my car seats lay flat. I'm serious.
 
START AND FINISH - Crawl starts at NOON at Triumph. Everyone will be back to Triumph by 6PM - NO LATER. Trivia will start promptly, if you miss a round, you miss the chance to win!
 
PARKING - The best deal is at the lot at Triump. $6.00 for for 9 hours, you'll need ones and I'll explain it to you. Triumph Brewery is located at:
 
Triumph Brewery
400 Union Square, New Hope , PA
Ph: (215) 862-8300
 
WEATHER - Looks good! Fifty and sunny during the day, mid-30's at night, little or no wind. Purrrrfect.
 
Hope to see you there!
