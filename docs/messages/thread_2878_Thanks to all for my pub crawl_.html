Re: Thanks to all for my pub crawl.

From: brendan_hickey13 | Date: 2008-01-13 23:14:59 UTC
Thanks, Hikers-n-Hounds, Sewsowbizzy, Mizscarlet, Cyclonic,
Schizokeet, et al for this excellent event.

We made the rounds this morning and found the boxes that we couldn't
get last night, getting to exchange and hang out with Grizzly Girl &
Shadow Dancer a bit (sorry about that cootie, though in fairness, you
tried to cootie first LOL).

The bartender at Lambert Station told us that she chatted with staff
from a few other bars last night and they would have liked to have
been involved with the crawl, so it sounds as though we were
appreciated. River Horse saw over 50 visitors yesterday, which is
unusually high for them.

Syner and I rescued the Indian this morning. When we reached the
spot, the bench was occupied by a man and his dog, so we killed some
time in the store but he was still there.

Finally, we started to look for the box anyway - then the
conversation about a "scavenger hunt" and he pulled the stamp out of
his pocket. It had been in the open, on the sidewalk, so he picked
it up - no idea why he decided to take it or what he planned to do
with it.

When we left, man and dog were gone, Indian remained (maybe best to
wait for the movie to come out).

Psychiker
