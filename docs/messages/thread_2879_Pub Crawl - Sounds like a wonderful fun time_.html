You were sorely missed, and I gave a  tip 'o the pint to you in my trivia game. One of the Pot-Luck questions was "In the LBMA - The Milan Holiday Classic food storage container is better known by what name?"
 
The 2 days were wonderful, thanks to all for their thanks. Thanks to Miz Scarlet and Cyclonic for the pre-crawl "research" trips and help, and thanks to Sowsewbizzy for stamps and a six pack.
 
Most seemed to have a very good time, to those who didn't...oh well, I tried! I had a great time this morning finally getting to crawl myself with my red hat gals. A lovely way to spend a Sunday morning!
 
I really, deeply appreciate everyone who showed up, but was especially tickled that Space Traveler came out of the woodwork and brought her husband Mike with her! Back when I started boxing she was the only planter in Philadelphia (that I knew of anyway). The fact that they teamed up with Mars88 is just too perfect to be a coincidence! And they were an excellent trivia team, almost unstoppable. But the Constitution round was "aced" by the Stacked Deck and they wound up winning by 1 point!
 
I had a blast, I appreciated everyone who came and missed my buds who couldn't make it.
 
Two people were quite insistant I have to post all of the clues on AQ...ugh! I love Ryan and Amanda but am lukewarm on AQ, however I do LOVE the events function. I will get everything up eventually so that those of you who count using AQ instead of your logbooks can do so. Eventually...
 
Thanks again to all, it was a lot of fun.
