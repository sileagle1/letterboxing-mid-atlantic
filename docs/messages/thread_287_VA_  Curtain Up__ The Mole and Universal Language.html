VA: Curtain Up!, The Mole and Universal Language

From: genealogy_va | Date: 2004-08-17 01:52:52 UTC
Hello,
Just a status report on these 3 that I tried tonight.

Universal Language was a lot of fun to translate, and then quick to
find. Nice little park too. Box had standing water in it, and the
front half of the logbook was soggy--lots of ink spread on some of
the older entries, unfortunately. Baggie with stamp had water in
it. I replaced both baggies, dried out letterbox, I pulled out 5-6
soaked, blank pages to help cut down the moisture content. If more
people try this one over the next few days, more moisture will be
released...

Curtain UP! Concert tonight, so I had to work around the clues a bit
to get around the closed off area at the Filene Center. I found the
area where the box should be, and had no luck locating it at all.
From the look of the area, they have had some flooding, and I think
this is lost.

The Mole: Same story. I found the spot, I'm pretty sure, but again
lots of evidence of flooding. I invested a lot of time, blood and
dignity (fell in the mud) to find these last two!

Anyway, it was a fun night, and lots of interesting parks in NoVA.
If anyone else has seen the last 2 recently, please advise and I'll
look again next week, but I believe they are history.

Thanks,
FamilyMan
