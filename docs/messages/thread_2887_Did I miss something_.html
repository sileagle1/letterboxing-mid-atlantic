From: Indigo Vulture | Date: 2008-01-16 12:15:09 UTC
A variety of reasons. In my case, I've had my clues posted on both
LBNA and AQ for several years, and over the past few months, I've
started to find that cumbersome.

The real question for me is not "why did I pull my clues," but "why
did I have them double-posted in the first place?" It's because I was
just so excited about new things. I sometimes have a problem with
"ooh...shiny."

So why leave them on LBNA, instead of AQ? I'm becoming a Luddite,
starting to shun the shiny, and I prefer the simplicity of LBNA.
Atlas Quest is fast on the way to becoming an internet portal, it
seems, and I *personally* don't like that right now. To each his own,
and to me LBNA!

Thanks for asking.

I.V.
