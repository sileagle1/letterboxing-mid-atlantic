I am hosting a mini-meet on February 2. You can get most of the
details at AQ:

http://www.atlasquest.com/events/event.html?gEventId=541

As far as I know this is the first meet in WV. It is near Charleston,
WV. It is going to be Groundhog themed, and we are even having a vote
on whether French Creek Freddie (the WV groundhog) will or will not
see his shadow -- the voting is being conducted through postal
letterbox.

So far we don't have many signed up to come. I know I will enjoy
meeting with the ones who do, but it would be nice to see a few more.
There doesn't seem to be much activity in WV, and I was hoping this
could get things jump-started.
