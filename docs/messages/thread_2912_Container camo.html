Depends on where it will be.  Apparently weathered silver duct tape takes on the look of concrete.  I'm a big fan of textured finish plastic spray paint (Rustoleum), but not everyone goes for it.  Tape can attract moisture. Some people just use Sharpies.  Try something and see how it works.

From: sewsowbizzy | Date: 2008-01-30 22:57:59 UTC
You can also water test by putting in a piece of paper towel and then
plunging up and down in a bowl of water. If the towel stays dry you
are home free.

In the woods we use a lot of the camo duct tape -- the old rolls that
can be torn not the new ones that are so plastic they don't even stick
to themselves.

An urban box would probably need to blend in with something other than
leaves and most paint on plastic doesn't last. The Fusion spray for
plastic probably would do better.

Use of plastic bags should be limited to the inside and not used as an
exterior covering.

Good luck and let us know what you decide. Maybe it will be the new
idea of the year!

sewsowbizzy
