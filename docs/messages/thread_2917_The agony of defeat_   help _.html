Midnight Boxer, I'm pretty much on "ignore" for Hidden Harvest too.
Although it really eats away at me, wondering what the Mobots had in
mind with their cryptic references. I did find two of their boxes a few
years back, "McKeldin Library" and "Testudo's Hideout." I tried for a
third, "The Stinkeye," and couldn't come up with it. That one involved

of the "icky" sort. Given the busy urban location, it has to be long
gone.

I really liked the Mobots' clue style and wish they were still around.
Their boxes are a nice piece of letterboxing history.

Speaking of which, I checked on the "HLRG" restoration of Pencil Pusher
