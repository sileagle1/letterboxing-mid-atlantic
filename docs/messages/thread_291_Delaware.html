Deer flies are NO fun! I had a couple tormenting me on South
Mountain in Allentown last weekend while I was planting the South
Mountain Series. No blood drawn. Sometimes you've got to outrun
them (or outwalk them). They are persistent pests. I've found that
wearing a baseball hat with duct tape on the back will frustrate them
(they like to attack from the rear). That will get me into the "ugly
hat society!"

Lightnin' Bug
