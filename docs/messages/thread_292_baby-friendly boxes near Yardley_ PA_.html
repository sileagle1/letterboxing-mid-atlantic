***************************************************
I have planted several boxes near Yardley that would be very baby friendly.  The easiest would be the Canal Bridge box (http://www.penncoasters.com/letterboxing/canal%20bridge.htm).  The box is right on the trail, and is very stoller accessible, even wheelchair accessible with a little help.
 
The Bowmans Hill Tower box (http://www.penncoasters.com/letterboxing/bowmans%20hill%20tower.htm) is just slightly off trail, as in less then a 30 second step off the trail to get it.
 
Finally, Scudder Falls Bridge (http://www.penncoasters.com/letterboxing/scudder%20falls%20bridge.htm) is in the rest area at Yardley, and is pretty easy to get to, also just off trail.
 
As I recall, Winahawcawchunck and Battle of Trenton, both close by, are also easy and just off the trails.
