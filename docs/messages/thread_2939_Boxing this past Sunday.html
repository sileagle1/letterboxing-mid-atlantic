From: Lightnin Bug | Date: 2008-02-12 11:59:06 UTC
Silent Doug's site:

http://www.letterboxing.info/

is probably the best, most concise site for e new oxer to learn most
of the lingo and "how to's" of the hobby. He wrote it when he lived
here in the LBMA zone, so we feel a little bit of pride by
association.

www.atlaquest.com also has a glossary that can be accessed by the
toolbar (on the left hand side). In some ways they overlap, in some
ways they do not. It is also a good place for a new boxer to
generally learn.

Cheers,
