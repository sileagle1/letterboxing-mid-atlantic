Hello!

I don't post often, but I do read :)

Just thought this would be an appropriate announcement for Valentines
Day and an even more appropriate place to announce it :)

David (the male half of LBing Team HappilyEverAfter) proposed to me
(the female half of LBing Team HappilyEverAfter) two weeks ago and he
used letterboxing to do it!!

Since only a few of my friends and even less of my family has been
letterboxing with us, no one seems to understand how exactly David
proposed to me, and how absolutely cute it was. They think we are crazy.

The whole story is here: http://ringonmyfinger.blogspot.com/

If you love letterboxing, you'll love his proposal (i think.. I did!)

Thanks for giving me somewhere to share something "outsiders" of
letterboxing don't understand!

Sara

TeamHappilyEverAfter
