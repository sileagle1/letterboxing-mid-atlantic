From: Hikers & Hounds | Date: 2008-02-23 16:51:56 UTC
Hey gang,
 
Mars888 (I think I have the correct number of 8's here) are hosting a gather in NE Philly in the spring. It's posted on AQ but thought I'd copy it here as well.
 
 
We had an idea for an event & have posted to reserve the day & to see the level of interest in our idea.

We hope to involve letterboxes, art, history, outdoor fun, good scenery & company into a fun day. This will be RSVP only.

THE CONCEPT WOULD GO LIKE THIS:
11 AM meet & greet at a Pennypack Park landscaped hilltop, at an equine ring site overlooking an historic 1800s large animal rescue farm (Ryerss Farm) for clues, brief history, & stamps. Then event or other activities all day as you choose.

About 2 PM a brief guided tour of a nearby stable, where a rescued & other horses offer programs for therapeutic riding (hopefully) and/or a stamp overlooking this Park stable (or other activities as you choose). THIS TOUR IS NOT PET FRIENDLY.

About 2:30 PM a free guided park walk on historic Burholme hilltop with (on clear days) 30 mile views (or self guiding walk (at any time) if you prefer) or other activities as you choose.

About 3 PM a free tour of Historic Burholme Mansion & Ryerss Art Museum or other activities as you choose. This tour allows children w/ an adult. NO PETS IN BUILDING.

About 4 PM last chance for Event Letterboxes in Burholme walking area.

About 5 PM tailgate lemonade or iced tea, stamps, a few prizes, etc. atop Burholme Hill.

This will involve a drive of about 3 miles to the 3 park areas w/ event stamps. Plus any drive to other area stamps or activities you may choose.

A lawn chair would be a good idea, as would a sun shade or rain fly if you want.

We will go rain or shine w/ the basic idea & at least 6 horse/history stamps if anyone at all is interested (i.e. signs up).

There at many fine restaurants & diners etc in Burholme area.

We are opened to & hoping for suggestions to improve or expand this concept.
