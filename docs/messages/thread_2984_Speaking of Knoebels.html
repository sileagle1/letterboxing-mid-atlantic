Speaking of Knoebels

From: Nathan Brown | Date: 2009-07-26 23:44:15 UTC
Since I am thinking about it, and am now ready to really get this thing
rolling, here is the information on Knoebels this year.

Once again we will be returning to one of the best amusement parks
anywhere, Knoebels Amusement Resort!

*The Gathering*

This gathering is totally free, as you don't have to pay admission into
the park. Rides do cost, but you don't have to ride if you don't want
to. If you do, you can get ride tickets or a wristband to ride all day,
which you may want to do as the park has some of the best rides
anywhere, including the top rated Phoenix rollercoaster.

We will again be returning to Pavillion P-3, which is a cool spot near
the creek. The gathering starts early so that everyone can have a chance
to enjoy the park later.

*Camping*

Once again we hope to have some great fun at the campground after the
gathering. There WILL be night boxing this year near the campsites.

Camp HQ is in a different location this year, site Q-X in the Quebec
circle. There are lots of sites left, but the campground is filling up
fast. call the park today to reserve a site:

1-800-487-4386

As usual, no sign-ups are required, just come on up for some fun. This
is a mostly social letterboxing event, a chance to meet and greet some
great folks, share stories and laughs. You don't have to buy anything,
you don't have to give anything, just bring your logbook, sig stamp, and
a willingness to have a good time. You can bring along the whole
family, including the dog, cat or rat, they are all welcome.
