The Clan Jiggs and I were privy to one of the very first cheese races
held on the Left Coast in Richmond, CA in Sep 03!

As I recall, Sir Balthazar and Jugglermouse (both sadly long removed
from our illustrious hobby) were the initial driving force behind the
races!

They took the time to show us that Kraft single seemed to have
superior plastic, and that one would generally want to avoid the
cheaper, store-brand variety!

At the San Luis Obispo gathering in Oct 03 we again had the chance to
try our luck, and this time, the Jigglets were victorious against such
luminaries as Amanda and Ryan, among others.

The travelling event boxes refered to in an earlier post were
originally entitled "Yes On Cheese Racing" and "St Brie: No To Cheese
Racing" and were planted as traditional boxes. When Sir B and
Jugglermouse removed themselves from the scene, the boxes were pulled
and converted into travellers, and have appeared from time to time
since then. The last time we saw them I think was the Ithaca NY
gatherinmg in Jun 05... St Brie is still listed, but the Yes box is
not.

Might be time to track 'em down!

Jiggs
