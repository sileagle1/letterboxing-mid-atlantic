on LB.org you want VA - Northern VA - Fairfax County and within that area:
Annandale (9-11 mi East - 15-25 min)
Burke & Burke Lake Park (6 mi South - 10 min)
Chantilly (9mi SE - 15 min)
Vienna (8 mi North - 25 min)
Springfield is a big area so some may be close enough, others 30+min
Dunn Loring (9 mi NE - 20 min)

have to look up each of the clues (unfortunately the titles aren't
marked MIA)
