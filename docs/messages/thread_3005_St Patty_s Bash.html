St Patty's Bash

From: Nathan Brown | Date: 2008-03-03 14:51:26 UTC
Come one, come all, and celebrate how the Irish saved Western Civilization!

The St. Patty's Bash is on, and all are welcome to join, young and old.
This is a nighttime mini gathering being held in Bristol, PA. You know
there are boxes down here you have been pining to get. Well, now is
your chance!

The gathering starts at 7PM, but you may come in earlier if you like.
There is some great boxing in the Bristol/Bensalem area, so I encourage
folks to come out early and find some of them.

The will be food for all, including hamburgers, hot dogs, all sorts of
snacks, and home made ice cream (some interesting flavors too). It is
BYOB, but coffee and soda will be provided. Your young leprechauns are
welcome to attend as well, as video games and movies will be set up for
them.

If the whether cooperates you will be able to enjoy our large deck and
fire pit.

Sign up is NOT required, but if you wish to do so, you can here:

http://www.atlasquest.com/events/event.html?gEventId=659

We are just five minutes off the Turnpike and I-95, and I can see Joisey
from here, so it is easy to get here. A webpage will be set up soon
with directions to make it a bit easier for you though.

Hope to see lots of folks out here in less then two weeks!
