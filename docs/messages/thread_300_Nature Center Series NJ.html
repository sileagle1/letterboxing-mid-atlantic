Very late box report here. I went there a few weeks ago!? I took my
Mom. The mile+ walk was more than enough for her, bless her. It was a
very nice day and a lovely walk through the forest.

The clues mention numbered trail markers but some of them are
missing. This effects the search for one box more than the other, at
least for right now. I asked a guy in the office about it and he said
that previous managers of the property installed the markers. Audobon
is there now and they are not going to replace the missing ones and
have removed some as well.

Both boxes were intact but one was visible from the trail -looked
like a wash out- and the second wasn't well covered. It was wet
inside too, but the log was OK.
