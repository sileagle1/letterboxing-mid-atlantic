Still No Theme Gathering

From: Nathan Brown | Date: 2008-03-07 16:42:43 UTC
I know, I am a bit behind on this, but here are the details to this
upcoming gathering.

This year's gathering theme will be the same as last year's, no theme.
We don't need no stinkin' theme to have a great time!

The gathering will start at about 10AM at the Greene Pavilion, which is
just inside the park's gate. You are welcome to come earlier if you
like. We will have the pavilion area all to ourselves, with plenty of
parking, bathrooms, and even a playground for the little ones.

The park already has more then 30 boxes hidden it, but you can expect
more boxes of all types to find their way there. Also expect plenty of
table treats and event boxes, bring as many as you like.

Food will be total pot luck. Bring your favorite dish to contribute.
Flatware, dishes, cups, soda and water will be provided.

Sadly, the gathering will not include a camping element this year. The
group sites are all filled up at the moment, though this may change. I
am working on possible alternatives as well, but right now it looks like
there will be no camping.

Sign-up is not required, but if you wish to do so, you can here:
http://www.atlasquest.com/events/event.html?gEventId=528

--
Nathan Brown

I did investigate some other options. The problem with NJ State Parks is if you want to reserve a site, there is a minimum of two nights required, except for the groups sites, which is why Wash Crossing worked so well.

Bulls Island State Park is just up the rive and has about 70 sites, so with, some without electric. There are some private campgrounds in the area as well. I am also considering looking into Tohicken Valley Park, a county park in Point Pleasant that has a camp ground. All these locations are about half an hour from the park.
