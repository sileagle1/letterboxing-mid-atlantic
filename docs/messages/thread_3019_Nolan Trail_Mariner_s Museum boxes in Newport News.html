I moved to Newport News a while ago, have been adopting some of the
abandoned boxes here and am planning to take on this series.

I've been doing some research and have concluded that the stamp for
box #1 went missing pretty early on, and the box #4 is probably
forever lost. These boxes were planted before Hurricane Isabel
devastated the park and in the cleanup the paths at that end of the
park were changed a bit. Also, as far as I can tell, the trees that
were one of the main clues are no longer there. (in hurricane
country you can't count on trees being permanent landmarks). BTW -
there is a message on AQ that describes some history and previous
maintanence.
http://www.atlasquest.com/boards/message.html?gMsgId=17172

I was hoping to get images of the original stamps and replace stamp
#1 and replant #4. Did anyone on this list ever find box #1 while
it still had the original stamp? and has anyone EVER found box #4?
