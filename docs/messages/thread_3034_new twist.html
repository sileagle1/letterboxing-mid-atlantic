new twist

From: Heather Siple | Date: 2008-03-11 23:23:49 UTC
At Little Cat's suggestion, we are trying something slightly different in the letterboxing world.  Our stamp for "A Christmas Gift" was damaged and quite out of season, but still people came to find it, as recently as two weeks ago.  I liked the hidey-hole and decided to replace the broken stamp with something soon-to-be-out-of-season instead, namely, an Easter stamp.  Little Cat suggested that we rotate the stamps in this box every so often so that we have a relatively up-to-date stamp related to whatever holiday is on the calendar, regardless of the time of year.  Kind of like a HHH, except I'm changing the stamp every couple months or so.  Those who like to box to see what has been carved can check back occasionally to see what is new.  The same log will remain in the box until it is full.  There is plenty of room in the box for HHs, too, as I've heard rumblings that there is a lack of good HH stops in Delaware.
 
Right now, there is a little something extra for the next finder.
 
Let me know what you think!
 
Mother Duck
www.sipleart.com
