From: Nathan Brown | Date: 2008-03-14 04:28:08 UTC
I have uploaded guidelines that were posted for the Farifax County, VA
parks. I think these are new.

Basically, you may plant in any of the listed parks given you follow the
guidelines for planting, which are pretty straight forward. If a park
is not listed, you must get written permission in order to plant in the
park, though it doesn't really tell you how to go about doing that.
