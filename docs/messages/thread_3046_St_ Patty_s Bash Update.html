St. Patty's Bash Update

From: Nathan Brown | Date: 2008-03-14 17:19:57 UTC
Well, I think I am pretty much ready for this little gathering. Here
are the final details:

I won't have an event stamp for the gathering itself, just been too
busy, but there will be plenty of surprises and other goodies.

I'll have plenty of food, including home made ice cream. Please BYOB.
So far, the selection I have been getting from folks has looked pretty good.

Party starts at 7, but you can come earlier if you desire.

Directions:

From I-95:
Take Exit 41 from 95 for Rt 413. Turn right at the top of the ramp.
Move into the left lane, you will pass over Rt. 13 and under a RR bridge.
Go straight at the light and then veer right at the blinking light (that
is the tricky part)
Turn right when you see the sign for Mill Street Parking
Park in the lot and walk up to Mill Street
225A is in the middle of the block on the opposite side, look for the
decorations.

From the PA Turnpike:
Take Exit 357, Delaware Valley
After the tolls, take the ramp for Rt 13 North
Stay right and turn right at the first light (Green Lane)
Go all the way to the end and turn right (Radcliffe St)
Take this all the way to the "Do Not Enter" sign and turn right (Market St.)
Take the first left and follow down into the lot.

You may find some parking right on Mill Street, but it is usually busy
Saturday nights.

A clue for those coming:
http://www.penncoasters.com/letterboxing/celticflower.htm

Enjoy, hope to see some old friends and well as some new folks!

--
Nathan Brown
