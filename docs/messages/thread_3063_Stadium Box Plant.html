Re: [lbma] Re: Stadium Box Plant

From: von der Insel | Date: 2008-03-19 15:55:10 UTC
In these times, it is best to either stay out of public gathering places or get permission.
Think about what our boxes look like to the uninformed!
There are too many stories of misunderstood LB's.
One geocacher here in RI was stopped by police coming out of the woods, because he was observed by a concerned citizen going into the woods of a small park with a "package" . All of the caches in that town were pulled by the police. This was just before their huge 4th of July parade.
Then there is the story from Boston that made the national news of a radio station's publicity stunt that involved a search for hidden boxes in the City!
I would rather not see Letterboxing get a bad reputation. Let's go hiking in the woods.
Diane
the von der Insels

Re: Stadium Box Plant

From: brendan_hickey13 | Date: 2008-03-19 19:24:17 UTC
I think that the concerns about how a letterbox might look to the
uninformed are valid and a good reason for caution. Members of
harrier groups have been arrested for marking urban trails with
flour prior to a run, being taken for toxins or signals for
assailants.

There is also the practical issue of discovery - how long would the
box even last, especially in a place that (should) receive regular
cleaning and is subject to a lot of vibration.

You might be able to do this in a gift shop with permission or you
could head for the weeds in the parking lot.
