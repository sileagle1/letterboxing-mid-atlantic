Dangerous Creatures

From: Hikers_n_ Hounds | Date: 2004-08-23 22:00:12 UTC
Wow! Snakes, bears and chipmunks, oh my! The most dangerous creature I have encountered on the trail is easily myself. I have stabbed myself in eyes, legs, ears, butt (don't ask), stepped on my own feet, nearly broke an ankle in Pennypack about 3-4 weeks ago and STILL have the pain and the bruise. And I am not safe to hike with either! I have stepped on all four of Dude's feet, his tail, I stabbed my son Carmen in his sandal clad foot 2 weeks ago with my metal-pointy tipped hiking stick as we were hunting along the canal. The Red Hat Boxers don't know how close they were to a mortal wounds yesterday! If you ever see me and a bear on the trail and have to choose which way to go, do the bear a favor and grab his paw and run like hell in the other direction!
