From: Shelley Shearer | Date: 2008-03-23 02:09:00 UTC
Hi all,

I just found out that a letterboxing short story I wrote is finally available. It was
chosen as part of a mystery anthology based on the Chesapeake area. Only about seven
pages, but it's all based on letterboxing. Part of Chesapeake Crimes III.

https://wildsidebooks.3dcartstores.com//Sisters-in-Crime_c_674.html

There's the link to it on the publishers site and it will be available on Amazon in about
two weeks.

SHOULD you buy it and should you like ANY of the stories, please post a positive review
on Amazon when possible. Those things really do help. The authors get no profits from
the book as they all go the the Sisters in Crime chapter, but the more notice it gets the
better for all of us in getting more chances to be published again.
