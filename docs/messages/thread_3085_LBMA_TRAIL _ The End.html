And the final winners (again) are:

Straight Line : MidnightBoxer on Feb 29 (65) wins 1 Pink Slab

4corners & Middle : LightninBug on March 18 (121) wins 1 Pink slab

Big "X" : LightninBug on March 19 (124) wins 2 Pink slabs

Perimeter : Psychiker on March 20 (129) wins 3 Pink slabs

100% : on March 22 (142) 4Suits - just under the wire takes home 3 Pink
slabs, while Psychiker was 1st to call and only gets the glory.

Now you all just have to find me at a gather to collect!! Then you need
to carve and plant and email me the box name after it is posted to
LB.org -- so next year it can be included on the list of choices.

I'll definitely be at CELEBRATE - another Camp'n' Stamp'n' 2008 on May
30 - Jun 1 Tuckahoe SP near Denton MD. Will you!

Thanks to everyone who played, I hope there is more competition next
year! There certainly won't be as many (unvisited?) un-choosen boxes in
such far away locations as Virginia (LB you need to get out more!)
