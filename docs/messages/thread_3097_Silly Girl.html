Silly Girl

From: Nathan Brown | Date: 2008-03-31 22:02:37 UTC
She is still laughing about it. Becky and I were hiking the Rainbow
Falls Trail in the Smokies this morning.

On the way down, she decided she had to answer nature's call, and I mean
now. We looked around, and with no one in sight, she scooted a little
bit off the trail, as much as can be done on the side of the mountain,
and did her business.

Suddenly, around a corner come two other hikers! They, however,
promptly turned around and decided it was a good time to take in the
view of the beautiful valley below.

It was a good hike, and the Tennesseans are getting a box out of it.
