FINAL SCHEDULE & NEWS 3/31:

THE EVENT: All Stamp hides have interesting history & scenery. This
is an outdoor letterboxing event most stamps will be traditional
letterbox hides. Stamps will be in place 9 AM Sat. to 6 PM Sunday. 3
will be relocated as long term letterboxes by Fri. 4/11/08 check for
new clues. 8 or so event stamps in Burholme Park, @ Krewstown, &
Pegasus Stable area. The rest are within 1200 feet of Burholme Park.
Most do not have logs, please sign or stamp any logs you find. The
HT2 series of stamps do not have ink, bring some. Most of all enjoy
the sites & have fun. Clues @ Event page on Atlas Quest. Check `em
out. 1PM to 4PM HOST TABLE phone 215-685-0544 or -0599 leave a
message for Jay, we will call you back. Emergency events cell # now
in hands of all AQ registered attendees.

11 AM meet & greet at a Pennypack Park 8600 Krewstown Road NE Philly
19115. Take road to Right away from house to stable area. Follow
signs 300 yards. NO REST FACLTIES AT THIS SITE. Wheelchair
accessible, grassy & paved areas. No CARS on Grass. EVENT HOST TABLE
HERE: 10:45 to 11:20 Nearest park restroom @ 8600A Verree Road
Philadelphia, PA 19115
Map & Web page: http://www.fairmountpark.org/KrewstownRoadHouse.asp
EVENT HOST TABLE HERE: 10:45 to 11:20
Nearest park restroom @ 8600A Verree Road Philadelphia, PA 19115
rest room webs site & map
http://www.fairmountpark.org/PennypackEC.asp


At 12 noon at 8297 Bustleton Ave.. Phila. PA 19152 a brief guided
tour of a therapeutic riding stable THIS TOUR IS NOT PET FRIENDLY.
Park on Bustleton or Strahle Steets. In lot parking for HP tags ONLY.
Restrooms available. website & map:
http://www.fairmountpark.org/Pegasus.asp
EVENT HOST TABLE HERE: 11:30 to 12:45

Breakfast or Lunch on your own: Many fast food, chain & a few family
restaurants near Roosevelt Blvd & Grant Ave, & Welsh Rd & Blvd. A few
diners & small restaurants on Grant Ave. or Welsh Rd. route to
Krewstown Rd. & Many fast food, chain & a few family restaurants in
Fox Chase/Burholme area on Oxford Ave from Pine Rd. SW to Cottman
Ave W. of Oxford.

1PM to 5:30 PM EVENT HOST TABLE AT BURHOLME HILL TOP, by mansion in
Burholme Park 7300 Central Ave. Phila. PA 19111 HOST TABLE can be
reached here by phone 215-685-0544 or -0599 leave a message for Jay,
we will call you back.

2:15 PM a free guided park walk on historic Burholme hilltop with (on
clear days) 30 mile views MEET on porch on Mansion. Wheelchair
accessible, grassy & paved areas. Playground. Rest facilities until
4PM, (accessible). Location 7300 Central Ave @ Cottman Ave. PA Rt. 73
@ Montomery County Line. Map & webpage:
http://www.fairmountpark.org/RyerssMuseum.asp

3 PM to 3:50 PM a free group guided tour of Historic Burholme
Mansion & Ryerss Art Museum. The Museum is opened 10 to 4 PM & free.
Accessible. THIS TOUR ALLOWS CHILDREN W/ AN ADULT. NO PETS IN
BUILDING. Rest facilities until 4PM, (accessible). website:
http://ryerssmuseum.org/

4:15 PM tailgate lemonade or iced tea, stamps, a few prizes, etc.
atop Burholme Hill.

??? PM One or two tables of us will be dining off the menu at The
Blue Ox Bistro near Burholme in their 1761 building. IF YOU WANT TO
JOIN US SIGN UP by 3PM AT HOST TABLE. Their website is
http://www.blueoxbistro.com/
Appetizer, & dinner menu at website. Easy access with 2 or 4 steps.
Lot Parking for 100 cars. 321 year old building. This will be an
informal set up of tables in a dining room @ Blue Ox. We expect us
14. plus any who may SIGN UP BY 3 PM SAT. @ HOST TABLE. We will call
for a reservation Sat. from park. WE WILL SET THE TIME Sat. @
Burholme Park.

7:20 SUNSET @ Burholme.
