I know this won't be popular, coming from a non-resident and all, but think of it as the objective view of an outsider.

I define North Jersey by exclusion; i.e., I first figure out what South Jersey is, and everything else is North Jersey.  From my perspective (entering New Jersey from the Delaware River and the Philly area bridges), I know that I am most likely to visit South Jersey on a day trip, and unlikely to visit North Jersey unless it's an overnight.  Thus, everything from Fort Dix south is South Jersey.  Everything North of it is North Jersey.  Your map may vary.
