From: maydaylillies | Date: 2004-08-25 02:26:46 UTC
So a few weeks ago the two of us planted our first letterboxes, both
in Essex County NJ. Gabriella's flag we have heard back from a couple
of people about, stating that they found it and its in good shape.
However the other box, Boy Scout Area Waterfalls, has not been as
successful. I recieved an email stating that the stamp had gone
missing, and a family before them replaced it, however it was missing
again. I could understand someone thinking they were cool and taking
the whole box or something like that, but just the stamp...and twice!
Has this ever happened to anyone else?

Re: [lbma] Missing Stamps

From: The Doubtful Guests | Date: 2004-08-25 13:43:34 UTC
I have heard of geocachers finding a letterbox while searching for a geocache and taking the stamp thinking it's a geocache trade item.  The problem with geocaching is the GPS takes you to the general area and then you have to look all over for the cache.  If there is a letterbox in the vicinity it may likely be found.  I wouldn't say it happens often that stamps go missing like that but it is worth checking. 
 
You can easily check on www.geocaching.com to see if there is a cache near your box.
 
Scarab of the Doubtful Guests
