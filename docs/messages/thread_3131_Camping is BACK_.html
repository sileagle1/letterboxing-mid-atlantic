Camping is BACK!

From: Nathan Brown | Date: 2008-04-18 17:59:56 UTC
Yup, camping is back for Still No Theme, but it is on a very limited
basis, so get in now!

Right now I have two sites reserved for May 17 at Bulls Island
Recreation Area. The sites are #57 and 58. Many thanks go to Mark (PM
JETS) for arranging this.

Bulls Island is about 25 minutes up river from Washington Crossing State
Park, right on Rt 29.

I just spoke to the park, and they, at the moment, have a bunch of other
sites available, however, if you wish to reserve you must reserve for
two nights. If you pull in that day you may be able to get a site, but
there is no guarantee that you will find an open site.

Everyone is invited to make their way up to the campground for a
campfire and night boxing if they would like. Visitors will have to
park in the overflow lot near the park entrance (don't worry, it is a
small park).

I figure the gathering is going to wrap up at about 4PM, and I will head
up to the camp at that time.

For dinner, folks will be on their own, but New Hope is not far away,
and you know how much fun that can be ;-)

I hope folks are interested. I like to present a camping element, and
am glade to get something together this time.
