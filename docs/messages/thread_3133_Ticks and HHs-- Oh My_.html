From: Heather Siple | Date: 2008-04-21 17:30:09 UTC
Long socks and long pants even in summer, coupled with lots of bug
repellant! Do not use citronella-based repellants because they only have a
useful life of 12 minutes, according to our family doctor.

But, make sure to leave the bug repellant at home if you go to New Castle,
Delaware because there are no mosquitoes or ticks around those boxes, but
the biting flies are attracted to it like BBQ sauce.

Generally ticks get on you by dropping from the trees above you or
hopping a ride from bushes you pass.

Wear long pants, sturdy shoes and socks. Tuck your pants inside your
socks. Also, wear a wide brimmed hat. Wear light colored clothing so
you can more easily spot them when they get on you.
