From: gerania93 | Date: 2008-04-23 03:40:46 UTC
I think everything is illegal in NJ, I should know, I live here. I'm a
bit wary of walking alone in city and suburban parks. I carry a cell
phone, whistle and sometimes pepper spray. I have been assaulted, but
never while letterboxing. Standard rule, the further away from people
that you are, the fewer people there are to bother you. I've hiked for
hours in the woods and rarely run into another person. No people, no
problem. Day hiking in state parks has never been a problem.

Read about the area where you are going to letterbox. Has there been a
rape/assault in the area? Is a serial rapist lurking? Go somewhere else.

Keep your eyes and ears open, use your common sense, play it safe and
you should be fine.
