From: Wisconsin Hiker | Date: 2008-04-22 16:43:02 UTC
Hi!

Only 4 weeks until we'll be in the Mid-Atlantic area! We're flying
into Philadelphia late on May 21 and hope to do lots of hiking &
boxing during the 5 days we're there.

As many of you know, we like to find some challenging hikes and/or
areas such as a large park where we can hike all day without doing a
lot of driving around. We're just starting to do some research and
are thinking we'll probably stay in the area north of Philly, maybe
into NJ and the southern part of NY.

It's been 3 years since we were last in the area. During our
previous trip we did a lot of Battle of the Band boxes, Mt. Minsi,
The Dr. Who series, Pulpit Rock, Nolde Forest, a funny B&E dog
series, and many others.

We're hoping some of you might be able to give us some
recommendations for some good hiking spots to hunt for either your
own boxes or others. You can contact us off-list if you'd like.

Thanks!

Wisconsin Hiker (& Martini Man)

From: Lightnin Bug | Date: 2008-04-22 16:58:19 UTC
Hi Guys,

A lot has been added over the past 3 yrs. Many longer hikes can be
had in French Creek (if you plan it out right, you can find 20+
boxes and hike 20 mile), Middle Creek (where I hope to join you) and
even the AT and of course all of the new stuff up at Pulpit Rock.
The DWG has some newer stuff on the Joisey side and may conceal some
mysteries. South Mountain will be getting some new stuff for a
quick blitz in Allentown as well.

Indigo Vulture has been busy up in the Scranton Wilkes-Barre area
and I think there are some leg stretchers there.

"No drive by's for you!"
