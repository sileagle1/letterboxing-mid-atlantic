From: gallant_rogue | Date: 2008-04-25 01:00:53 UTC
Just posted 40 pics in Yahoo Album : "Gallant Journey". Weather has
been great so far... Asheville / Hot Springs are amazingly beautiful.
Hot Springs reminds me of Topanga Canyon,,, the rustic hippie commune
in the coastal mountains above Malibu California... except the
Appalachian Trail runs down Main Street with markings on the sidewalk.
Campground is very cool... Fat Cat runs the check in station... buy him
a "pepsi"! Max Patch... wow! So far ... the town is full of AT thru
hikers excited about TrailFest... this is a gathering of the most
dedicated hikers in the country... I'm so pleased to be here...
and "ahem"... I see nothing foolish about this venue Nathan!
