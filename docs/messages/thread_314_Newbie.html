From: Hikers & Hounds | Date: 2008-03-27 10:15:29 UTC
Welcome! My only advise is take yor time and let your personal style develop. We all box differently and each get different satisfaction fromt eh hobby. Some love to carve gorgeous stamps, some love to write impossible clues, some love the hunt, some love to plant. some love to gather with other boxers and hike or socialize, some are more solitary in their enjoyment of the hobby. That's what I love best about it, you really can make it suit your personal style. It's your hobby too, make it your own.
 
Hope to meet you at a gather soon, check out the Events page on AQ, there's a couple coming up. One in NE Philly, a camping event in MD and some other things.
 
Welcome and enjoy yourself and congrats on the wedding!
 
H&H
