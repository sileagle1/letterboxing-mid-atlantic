Dreams of letterboxing

From: fleurdelislb | Date: 2008-04-30 12:47:02 UTC
Maybe it's because I hit my head on the yoga mat yesterday, or maybe
it's because I have one of these every 6 months or so, butI had a
'boxing dream again last night. I found one in the main branch of the
Philly Library. It was hidden in a hollowed out book called "Umberto
Eco". It wasn't hidden very well, for as soon as I started to get the
book the stamps all fell out. There were about 10 of them, and none
of them made any sense, there was no theme, and I was all confused. I
was trying to stamp in as the security guards were trying to get me
out before they closed at midnight. When I rehid them, I put them back
in a hollowed out ball of yarn thinking that that would be much more
inconspicuous on the library shelf than the half-open hollowed out book :)

FdL
