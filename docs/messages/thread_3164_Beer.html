Beer

From: brendan_hickey13 | Date: 2008-05-01 20:54:14 UTC
The Friends of Brandywine Creek State Park will host "Beers of the
Brandywine," a beer tasting and educational event on Friday, 5/9
at 7:00 PM.

Peter Cherpak, the founder of Beer Appreciation, LLC, and a noted local
beer educator, will discuss local beers and beer styles and
environmental issues related to beer.

Guests will have the opportunity to taste beers from nearby Twin Lakes,
Victory, and Iron Hill breweries. Door prizes will include a one-
day kayak rental from EMS in Concord Mall and a Victory growler with 2
fill certificates.

Tickets are $20 for Friends of BCSP, $25 for others with a year's
membership in the Friends included at no additional cost.

Tickets are sold at the Nature Center, if you find yourself looking
for boxes there or nearby, or you can email me and I will put you in
touch.
