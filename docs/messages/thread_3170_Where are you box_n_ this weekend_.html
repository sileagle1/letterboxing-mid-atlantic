Agreed, it does, so I will go no further except to say that it is in an
interesting location, so there is a positive.

Back to the topic at hand, Schizokeet and I had a very nice weekend boxing.

We headed to Dorney Park on Saturday and enjoyed some rides, and then
headed up to Locust Lake to camp. On the way we stopped and got Mr.
Moneybags, a box she has been wanting to get since it was released.
Very cool location, I recommend this indoor box. On the way to the camp
we found Mail Pouch, a nice bug box, great stamp.

It rained all night, but in the morning the sun came out and it was a
wonderful, warm day. We had planned to go to Knoebels, but after taking
a walk to find a certain mystery box in the park (concerned parties will
be interested to know it is in fine shape), we decided to instead do
some more boxing before heading home.

So, we rode over to Tuscarora State Park and found two of the three All
Animal Alliteration series. We believe we know where the first box was,
but it is now long gone. Then we found, after some persistence, Itch In
PA, another Bug box. It had been a good 9 months since it was last
found, and was a bit damp inside, but none worse for the wear. It was
well dried out before replaced.

Nathan,
I don't work on the weekends so I missed you both when you came for my letterbox.  I would have loved to see you.  Schizoceet must have printed out the clues before I changed the name to my box, so you might have a hard time finding it online to log in. 
 
 I was at a letterbox gathering and told someone about the stamp I was carving.  They said ,"Oh, Mr. Moneybags!"  I didn't know the name of the character so I thought, "Great! Now I know what his name is."  After having 2 people email me an tell me that they were at the game Moneybags (which has nothing to do with a MR. Moneybags) and couldn't find it I thought I would do a search online to see how hard it would be to figure out if you didn't know the name.  Well I was surprised and a little embarrassed to find out that his name was Rich Uncle Pennybags.  His name was changed around 10 years ago, but I couldn't put the new name because that is a dead giveaway to the name of the game.  I think the old name has more character anyway.
 
Glad you enjoyed the store.  It's a fun place to work.
