FIREWOOD

If you are attending Celebrate  another Camp'n' Stamp'n'  please NOTE

Visitors to Maryland state lands will be asked if they have brought firewood from home. If they have, both Park and Forest Service staffs will require campers to immediately burn any firewood being transported to a DNR property and direct visitors to local sources of firewood. Repeat violators may have their camping permit revoked.

There is a problem with the emerald ash borer, so please do not bring fire wood from home.  If you have scrap interior lumber from home projects which has not been stored outside, feel free to bring that to feed the fire ring.

It is requested that each odd number site bring a bag of charcoal and those in even number sites purchase a $3 crate of fire wood from the park office.  In anticipation that some campers may be arriving late on Fri  - I will have several crates pre-purchased and you can reimburse me.

TABLES

The fire ring will be located in the "Parking for Canoe Launch & Overflow" between Sites 9 & 10.

There is a fire ring and two fire places with grates.  There are no picnic tables  so if you have a folding table please bring it. If you have a shelter or vary large tarp bring it too, just in case of rain.

ICE

If you are not driving over until Sat to camp, please bring frozen blue blocks if you have them.  The closest town is Ridgely (10 min) and so bagged ice can be purchased. 

MENU

The menu is as follows and will only be available to those who have signed up on AQ for the event.

PLEASE BRING YOUR OWN BEVERAGES (beer is allowed).  Cup,plates, etc will be provided.

Fri Dinner -

Hot Dogs, buns, catsup, mustard, relish, onions  cook your own over the fire  bring a stick!
Chips & Pickles
S'mores

Sat Breakfast 

Swedish Egg Coffee, ½& ½ , sugar (bring your own artificial packets)
Omelet in a ZIPLOC  you add beaten egg, extras like grn peppers etc., zip & put in boiling water to cook
Brown & Serve Links
Bread to toast (better have a second stick if you toasted marshmallows last nite!), butter, jam, PeaNutButter

Sat Lunch 

You are on your own - I will take SubWay orders/money by 11AM and bring back about 1PM.

Sat Dinner 

Cold Veggy Pizza  i.e. no cooking involved
        Crescent Rolls cooked flat (I'll do that on Thur)
        Spread with Cream Cheese/Ranch Dressing Mix
        Top with lots of veggy stuff (I'll have that chopped on Thur)
Soup (large cans of Campbells  unless someone wants to volunteer to bring home made for 27!)
Dessert  undecided  maybe a sheet cake.

Sun Breakfast 

Since we have to pack up  I'm just doing a run to Dunk'n Donuts for box coffee and bagels - or you can place breakfast sandwich orders Sat at dinner time.  I'll pick up for delivery about 9AM at the fire ring.

Hope this makes packing for camping easier. 

sewsowbizzy
