It sat there teasing me all week. I was working in Wernersville PA. and every day I had to drive past a park which (gasp) had No Letterbox!
I had an idea for a new series inspired by an offhand remark on the hairyleggers. I cut the rubber Wed night and prepared to swoop in on my way home from work.
But we finished after dark and the local Smokey had taken up residence in the parking lot after sundown.
Today, it rained. And rained and rained and rained and rained. Normally I wouldn't be such a wimp, but I had a 3 hr drive and a dinner meeting that would go better for me if I didn't show up smelling like wet dog.
So South Mountain Park still doesn't have a box. But I will be back next week...
