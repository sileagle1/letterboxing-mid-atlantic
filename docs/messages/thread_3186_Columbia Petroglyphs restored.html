Columbia Petroglyphs restored

From: wood thrush | Date: 2008-05-11 20:35:46 UTC
Back in March WimpyGirls gave the Historic Letterbox Restoration Group permission to adopt
their boxes in Maryland. We completed the restoration of their Columbia Petroglyphs series
this weekend.

If you were one of the many people who tried this series in recent years and were frustrated
by missing boxes 1 and 5, and the stampless box 4, we'd like to invite you to stop by again.
All three recarved stamps are faithful to the WimpyGirls originals, and we have changed the
locations for boxes 1 and 5, which will hopefully give them longer and happier lives. Also
note that the contents of usually soggy box 2 are now housed in a nice Lock n Lock. Enjoy!
