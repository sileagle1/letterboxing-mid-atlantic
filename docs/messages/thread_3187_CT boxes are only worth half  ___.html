It is just the running gag that we have had for several years now.
Boxes in CT are SOOOO plentiful that you trip over them when you walk
out the door, have to dodge them in the streets, and there is one under
every SPOR in the woods there ;-)

It is all in jest though. There are plenty of little boxes, but there
are also plenty of challenging, unique, wonderful boxes as well.

formula which would multiply the "F" by 50% if you entered "CT" or "Conn" in the State
