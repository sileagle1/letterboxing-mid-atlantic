From: samanark | Date: 2008-05-19 19:55:08 UTC
There is another hike like this in China...
My stepmom sent me the photos:


http://www.amusingfacts.com/forums/view_topic.php?id=925&forum_id=1

The email saying that there is a restaurant a the top that will give
you a free meal if you hike up is a hoax, but the trail is real. I
found more information online:

"The passage shown in the pictures is located in Mount- Hua
This particular section is called Chang Kong Zhan Dao, which
translates into Vast Sky Stack Road.

here's the official site, it's in Chinese but if you look to the
bottom left of the page, you can see another picture of it.
http://www.huashanlj.com/Article/ShowArticle.asp?ArticleID=110

The road was built during the Yuan dynasty by a person named He Yuan
Xi and his students. It took them 40 years to complete the whole
road. It was build as a passage way for them to reach a more
quiet/peaceful place to practice their religion.

Pretty intense!"
