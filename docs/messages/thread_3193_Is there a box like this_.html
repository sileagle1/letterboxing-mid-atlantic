I recently planted a box in Ocean City to promote an event that took place there. In my clue I promoted the cause it represented and instead if the normal things in the box I left a stamp and ink but no book only a stack of papers with email address for donations.

 

They may not ever get any direct donations from it but it does idenify the issue and remind people.
