1st connecticut box!

From: four.suits | Date: 2008-05-29 01:09:56 UTC
Hi everyone! I just had to share my pleasure from this weekend.

We visited married friends of ours who have recently moved from Europe
to CT and we were able to squeeze in our 1st CT box (so many to choose
from - 24 within 5 miles of their home!) They joined us & through
discussion of the hobby, discovered that one of them (British) knew the
history of letterboxing & had found the one in Dartmoor while trekking
many years ago. We were so psyched to introduce them to a hobby that
could help them discover more about their new surroundings (new places
to hike!) & provide a link to his homeland.
