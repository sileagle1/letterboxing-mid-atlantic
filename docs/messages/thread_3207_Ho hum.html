Ho hum

From: sewsowbizzy | Date: 2008-05-29 01:12:43 UTC
since no one is posting I may as well ramble on.

Went to $Tree for supplies and always check the plastic contain
inventory.

There was a LnL knock off -- clear, blue "Sure Fresh" label.

Bought one and subjected it to the ol' p-towel dunking test.
Aaaccck! Even with a blue gasket the water seeped in!!

It looked like the gasket was loose, so I pop'd it out and ponder what
to use to enhance it.

3 rubber bands cut and stuff in the gasket channel.
Went to reapply the gasket and realized it had originally been applied
narrow width down...hence its looseness and uselessness.

Took out the rubber bands and reinserted the gasket fat width down...a
bit testy as it tends to stretch -- so it had to be eased back in
(that's a sewing term.)

Voila! Another dunk test and the P-towel stayed dry. Yipppeeee

So that's $1 for a water tight contain (some adjustment required) that
measures 4" x 6" (inside).

Now I'll have to wait until after 10AM to leave for DE so I can swing
by and purchase the rest of the inventory.

Then I can sit around the campfire reinserting gaskets.
ssb
