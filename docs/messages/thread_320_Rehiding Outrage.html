Rehiding Outrage

From: Art Vandelay | Date: 2004-08-27 23:37:46 UTC
We just got back from checking our Arboretum Treatum box and we're very upset.  The box was rehidden very well, but the logbook was not in it.  We found the logbook in its double plastic bags (which were partially open) about 10 feet away out in the open.  The logbook was ruined- all the stamps washed away.  The fact that the box was rehidden makes me think it was a letterboxer who's at fault.  We will replace the logbook this time but if it happens again, we will have to pull it.  Again, PLEASE make sure you are rehiding carefully and check to make sure everything is back in the box.  It ruins the experience not just for the planter but for others who come along later and cannot stamp in.  I'm posting this on the Newboxers also so please excuse the double post.
 
Warcat76
