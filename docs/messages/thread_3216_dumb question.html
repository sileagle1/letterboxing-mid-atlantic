While we are setting rules for boxes, I think I have a few of my own:

- You must be able to enjoy beer to enjoy one of my boxes, and Bud and
Miller don't count. Plying me with beer may help however.

- If you can't find the box, I may or may not help. See the rule above.

- All boxing buddies visiting my boxes must be flesh and blood, stuffed
animals need not apply. Buddies must have a pulse, but a brain is not
required.

- You may log, unlog, dislodge, disassemble, post, print, and ply my
box, but just put it back the way you found it in the end.

- You may use any and all resources, conventional and unconventional, to
find my box, and you may brag about it to me. See the first rule, again.

- You must carry a mallet with you while hunting my boxes in order to
smash any purple, accordion playing, polka dancing squirrels, unless the
squirrel is playing "O Dem Golden Slippers" or giving out Jenny's number.

- You must think outside the box.

- You must enjoy the box.
