Well, I had been wondering how a HH that I released on 12/27/07 in PA
was doing...since the box it was dropped in was logged several times
since then but no word on the HH. Of course I realize that it's only 6
months later and that some people do not log finds of boxes or HH's -
but I still wondered how it was doing. Today I found out. It turned up
in a box in MI. It was alone in the baggie with no logbook. Guess I
wrote the HH name on the baggie (I really don't remember) but the
finder was able to find me. The HH was a tribute to another boxer whose
box I found destroyed by some NJ floods. The stamp was salvaged and I
made an HH out of it. So, it is not pleasant to hear of its current
status. I suppose I could run the list of who to blame....the person
who moved it, newbie or not, an errant geocacher or perhaps one of
natures logbook eating creatures that don't like the taste of stamps or
baggies...but it doesn't really matter.I'm just annoyed..... ....as many
have been before me...and many will be after me.
