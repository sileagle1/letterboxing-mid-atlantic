OT - Funny book based in the Lehigh Valley

From: Hikers & Hounds | Date: 2008-06-04 16:29:48 UTC
Yo from Philly folks!
 
Yes, this is Off Topic but for a change it's not about beer! I started a bookshelf at work for folks to bring in books that we can all read and return. Most of the books in there are the ones I brought in but there are a few folks who have started donating. Anyway, I was desperate for anything new to read and even though it didn't sound like my sort of thing I picked up a book described as a "screwball mystery". Now I love mysteries, they're my favorite, and I come from a long line of screwballs, but I don't usually enjoy them together. The title of the book is "Bubbles all the Way" by Sarah Strohmeyer and I have to say I really enjoyed it. It's set in the Lehigh Valley and if full of references to  the entire area. It's definately "fluff" reading, but those of you who live in or just like the area might like it. The main character, Bubbles Yablonsky" is a hoot.
 
Anywho, just thought I'd share. I recently came across another auther form FL, Carl Hiaasen, that I've been enjoying as well.
 
If anybody know some great mystery, court room, or spy writers, let me know off-list please. I've read almost all of the more popular authors and am running out of books to read, thanks.
 
H&H
