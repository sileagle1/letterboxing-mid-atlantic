From: lunastrixae | Date: 2008-06-08 16:46:05 UTC
Hey folks, just checking in to say hi! It's been a crazy spring for
this newbie and I haven't had a lot of time for boxing. I have another
small University city series pending, though.

Friday I took a day off and went to Ridley to find some boxes. I'm
always limited on where I can go, since I'm not a car person. But I
can take the R3 to media and it's only 3 miles to the park. I think
I'm going to start putting public transit tips with my clues when
possible.

I thought I could find more in a day (8 finds, 13 attempts), but that
means I have to go back! (Thanks again to mizScarlett, missmoon,
LightninBug and Sheba for the boxes).

I don't think I even got poison ivy, which I think is the Ridley park
official plant!!
