Sadly I must report that another of the Non-Existent Birds series is....well...non-existent.  I went skeet hunting yesterday after work and discovered that the pipe where Green-Billed Skeet made its nest was full of nasty water & the box was gone.  I will return at a later date to see if I can find another of this endangered species in a different location....
