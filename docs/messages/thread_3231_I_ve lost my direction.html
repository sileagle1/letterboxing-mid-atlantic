From: nattybumppolbna | Date: 2008-06-15 02:22:39 UTC
Take an analog watch.
Point the hour hand at the sun.
Halfway between the hour hand and 12 is due South (in the Northern hemisphere)
Turn around to face North and you can easily find:
1 o'clock 30*
2 o'clock 60*
3 o'clock 90* (E)
4 " 120*
5 " 150*
6 " 180* (S)
7 " 210*
8 " 240*
9 " 270* (W)
10 " 300*
11 " 330*
12 " 0* (N)

It's not foolproof (because of the amazing variety of fools in the world) but it works better
than moss.
Cloudy days in the woods are tough, but you can usually get pretty close.
