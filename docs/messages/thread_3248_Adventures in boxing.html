Adventures in boxing

From: mizscarlet731 | Date: 2008-06-26 10:41:06 UTC
Ventured up to York county yesterday, only found one box but found other treasures of the
summertime forest. Not including poison ivy and a deer tick.
While walking back from box spotted a beautiful blue bird, not a bluebird. Tried looking it up
in bird book but couldn't find it. Bird was a deep peacock blue, chest white with a red ring at
the throat. Sounds like Eastern blue bird but color was much deeper and the feathers on
chest were in red ring not the red blush that covers the chest and blends in on a bluebird.
Any ideas?
Also while walking back I saw these jummping bugs on the trail. On closer examination they
turned out to be little tiny frogs! Hundreds of them! Spent some time hunched over on the
trail just watching in amazement.
