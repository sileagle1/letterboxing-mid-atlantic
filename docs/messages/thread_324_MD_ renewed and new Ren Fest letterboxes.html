Johnny Fox & Brass Dragon Ren Fest

Placed by: Scarab   (contact the placer)
Placement date: Sep 6 2003
State: Maryland
County: Anne Arundel
Nearest city: Crownsville
Number of boxes: 2

Clues

I have replanted Johnny Fox and added the Brass Dragon

These are temporary microboxes for the duration of the Maryland Renaissance Fair season. Check local listings, or Google their website, for opening hours and fees.

Johnny Fox,

sword swallower extraordinaire, has been entertaining at the MD Ren Fest for over 20 years. The first time I saw his show I was so shocked, I laughed so hard I cried and I hurt. "Water from India," protects him from harm. I'll let you figure out whether he really swallows the swords or not.

After the show buy some snacks from Johnny Fox's Almonds booth. Then turn left past the School of Juggling and find an interesting triple tree with a trunk smaller than the other two. Behind it is a zig zag path going down the hill. Halfway down find a long curved bench. Count 4 support posts from right to left and sit down next to the 4th one. Reach under the bench and between the supports for what you seek.

Brass Dragon

Stroll up Kenwood Lane. Note the "King of Swords" shop. He is my family's armorer. We have five of his fine wooden shords and knives. Every year he repaints them for us.

Find the Brass Dragon. Be sure to look carefully as you will recognize one of them later. Count the number of dragons above the rain gutter. Multiply that number by 60 and head off in that direction. Stop at the stage sign.

From there head straight south to the two big chairs. Yea, they are pretty important chairs. Stand behind the bigger one. Multiply the same number by 28. Head off in that direction. Pass horns hanging on your right. Pass a place of many unnatural objects.

Up ahead you will see an imposing yellowish building on a hill. Count the number of flying buttresses. Count the number of winged creatures in the roof. Add those two numbers together and multiply them by 5. Stand inside by the back door of the building. Process out the front doors for that number of steps. Act grand as you walk.

Multiply your original number of dragons times 7. Add that to your footstep count and head of in this new direction.

Pass turkey legs. Pass a tall toad. Pass the first of two trees "in the middle of the way". Pass ancient mysteries and prophecies. You seek a place with magical potions. They line the walls on shelves. Which one would I find particularly interesting? How many sides does the container have? Multiply that number by 3 to get X. From the magic corner count the number of double posts along the rail. Between the Xth posts, under the rail, in front of a big tree with a fat poison ivy vine (be careful), find the Brass Dragon.

Scarab of the Doubtful Guests
