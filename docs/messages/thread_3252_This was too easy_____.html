Really all depends. Fresh & local is always better, but in general,
beer with higher alcohol content will last longer (truly the only
reason for drinking it - other than the obvious and best reason).
Lagers don't do as well with temperature fluctuations (power outage)
and no beer does well with much light exposure (clear bottles are a
bad idea).
