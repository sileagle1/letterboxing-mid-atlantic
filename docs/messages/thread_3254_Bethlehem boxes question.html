From: Indigo Vulture | Date: 2008-06-29 17:33:02 UTC
My "Bethlehem" series doesn't involve any long hikes into the woods,
but you will have to do a bit (just a bit) of work ahead of time.

South Mountain Disc Golf course has several boxes. See Joy's "South
Mountain" series is there. (There are more there as well, but they
are...mysterious.)

Lightnin' Bug's "Minerals on Lehigh Mountain" is pretty close to
Bethlehem too.

Willing to drive a bit further? Allentown and its environs are
infested with Lightnin' Bug's boxes.

I used to live walking distance from St. Luke's. I miss
Bethlehem...lovely city.
