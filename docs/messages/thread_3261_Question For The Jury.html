Re: Question For The Jury

From: sewsowbizzy | Date: 2008-07-10 19:05:30 UTC
You should always ASK the host/ess 1) Do they want a stamp 2) will
they plant the stamp or will it just be on the table 3) Do they want
a log & box and what size 4) will it be perm or temp and send postage
for return if just a temp or state it is a 'gift' and you don't want
it back.

A host/ess receiving unsolicited stamps or incomplete stamps (no
box/log) is not obligated to complete the scenario or return the
stamp (unless postage was provided). If they do plant it later
somewhere else, Clue and Log should give credit to the carver.

Those are the 'rules' I play by.

sewsowbizzy
