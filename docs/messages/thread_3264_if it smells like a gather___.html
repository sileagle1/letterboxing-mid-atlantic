if it smells like a gather...

From: Heather Siple | Date: 2008-07-11 13:11:17 UTC
A friend of mine and I were pondering the demise of the Rockwood Park Ice Cream Festival here in Delaware, which got killed by the county due to financial reasons.  Unlike the re-opening of PA state parks, this one did not get revived after its first absence.  So, we decided to call out all the folks we could to come make our own.  The first stamp placed in that park, which was the first one I ever found, by a now-relocated Neverenuff, honored the festival.  Sounded like and excuse for a gather to me, as this will likely be a rather small celebration muggle-wise. 
 
Henceforth, anyone out there who is free this Sunday from about 12:30 to whenever is welcome to come eat, play, exchange, look for the 4 boxes in the park, etc.  Bring your own food and lawn chairs.  We're supposed to have a grill,  a cooler of dry ice for anyone's ice cream and some old-fashioned games (croquet, bocce, an out-of-practice juggler who's happy to share her toys).  If anyone says they're coming, I'll see about carving up a quick event stamp.  I'm probably not posting this to AQ, because it is a last-minute, disorganized project and I don't think anyone who is not near Wilmington, DE will  be likely to show up on such short notice. 
 
The park's web site is http://www.rockwood.org/home/webpage1.asp in case you want to get directions.  It's easy to get to.  It's right next to a ramp for I-95.
 
Let me know if anyone thinks they might be there.
