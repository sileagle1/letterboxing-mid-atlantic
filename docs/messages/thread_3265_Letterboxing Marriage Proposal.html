Hi everyone, I know it has been awhile since I posted last but I wanted
to let everyone know that I just got engaged. I decided to use
letterboxing as my way of asking. I created a pirate themed letterbox
with a stamp that said will you marry me and had it planted near the
Lake Huron coastline in Michigan. After she stamped the box stamp and
saw what it said I pulled out the real ring and asked her. I am proud
to say that she said yes.

Ryan
The Cichlid Guy
