I don't think a series needs a logbook for each box. I didn't mean a series. I just meant one, individual box, that the organizer and donater both know is going to be hidden outside somewhere and the organizer has not specified anything other than that. That's all I meant. Personally I think one logbook for a series is great. Or one at each end if it's a big series, one at the beginning and one at the end. I don't think every box needs a logbook. My Poor Clark Kent box here in the city is just a magnet stamp, nothing else. I put that in the clues so people can skip it if they want a logbook to stamp into. Since it's urban and in a busy spot, just having the stamp makes for easier hiding.
