Mount Tammany Report

From: Otis' Friends | Date: 2008-07-14 02:19:50 UTC

The little one and I made it to the top of Mount Tammany on Saturday, taking the red dot trail up and the blue dot trail down.  Some good news and bad news.

 

First, the good news:  We survived, and added two new boxes, one near the summit and one much closer to the bottom on the red dot trail.  The views are truly amazing from the outlook, and one thing we didn't see was venomous snakes.  The logbook for the Mount Tammany letterbox was almost full but dry.  We added another logbook.  We didn't get caught in bad weather.

 

 

Now, the bad news:  The stamp was missing.  I think I might have heard something about that a few months ago and searched but didn't find it.  I had a stamp with me that I dropped in so that people making the climb will have something to use, but it's a throwaway effort and not deserving of such a demanding hike.  If someone with carving skills is up for the climb, the box could use a better stamp.
