From: Lillian Haas | Date: 2008-07-23 16:24:12 UTC
Collingswood's 3 R's is easily accessible by PATCO, and Haddonfield
Hadrosaurus is less than a mile from the Haddonfield station. (And
PATCO can take you fairly near a mystery box, too.)

There's a whole series of boxes, Freight Train, that involves riding
the RiverLine up through Burlington County; you can pick up the
RiverLine from the PATCO station in Camden.

But that's about it for public transportation in South Jersey. Don't
even try to take a bus.

From: Heather Siple | Date: 2008-07-23 18:43:38 UTC
There are three active boxes about 5 minutes by bike from the Wilmington
train station, but I must not name them. About 1.75 miles north of the
station is Brandywine Park, in which you will find quite a lot of active
boxes, including the FotB series and I left My Hearts in Brandywine Park.
Brandywine Park connects to Rockford Park/Bancroft Mills/Alapocas Run Park,
where you can find the most excellent Cliffhanger series, among other
things. There are a number of mysteries strewn for four or five miles from
the Zoo all the way across through Alapocas. A quick trip it is not. I
have ridden my bike happily from about 2 miles northeast of Alapocas all the
way to the Zoo in Brandywine Park, but to go through the greenway you may
have to walk even a mountain bike in one or two places. It is a great trek
for those who want to be out for the hike and bike! Those who are not into
Kamikaze biking might want to take their bikes on the bus to the zoo, or at
least to Rodney Square, which is about 12 blocks from the station and four
from Brandywine Park. All our busses are equipped with bike racks these
days, and we don't have bike lanes in the city.

Mother Duck

Yes, you can get to BCSP by bicycle reasonably easily from Claymont Station. The shortest route is about 6 miles each way, though.   I take most of that route going from Talleyville to Arden occasionally. 
 
Now there's a letterboxing marathon if ever I heard one -- from Claymont to Cauffiel to Bellevue to Rockwood to Alapocas to Brandywine Park in town/the zoo and then home by way of the Wilmington Station.  It's about 15 miles without side trails for boxing.  For laughs, I checked the route on Google Maps. Throw in BCSP and Woodlawn you could double it.  Any cross-country riders out there?
