Knoebels Update

From: Nathan Brown | Date: 2008-07-25 19:37:18 UTC
Just wanted to update folks on the gathering.

Now in its forth year, I hope to see many familiar faces as well as some
new ones at Knoebels Groves in Elysburg, PA.

Why four years running now?

Because Knoebels is a great amusement park, and it is free to get in.
They have loads of food as well as a camping ground right next door.
The phoenix, one of their three wooden roller coasters, is constantly
rated one of the best in the world.

As usual, this is a very laid back event, bring all the event boxes you
care to, or none at all, it is up to you, just come and have some fun.
There will be a smattering of boxes hidden around the park, and some new
local boxes as well.

This year the gathering will be in pavilion P-3. This pavilion is
behind the Sklooosh, and we will have it all to ourselves, with plenty
of room for everyone. The gathering officially runs from 9AM to Noon,
but we have the pavilion all day, so you can set up there with lunch or
to just relax.

Two platform sites on the Texas Panhandle have already been reserved for
the gathering. Anyone wishing to camp with the group should reserve
sites there. There is one platform left and several sites. It is a
wooded, fairly secluded area, and a great place for our usual after
gathering camp fire.

The pre-gathering get together will be once again held at DeVitto's
Pizza in Ashland at 7PM on Friday night.

More information will be coming soon.

You can sign up for the gathering here:
http://www.atlasquest.com/events/event.html?gEventId=633

Sign up is not necessary though, just come on out and have a good time.

--
