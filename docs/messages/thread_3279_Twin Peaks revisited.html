Twin Peaks revisited

From: Suzanne Coe | Date: 2008-07-26 12:19:53 UTC
So the other day I'm on trail, looking for some new local boxes, and I realize that the one I'm working on is taking me onto the disc golf course.  Oh great, shades of Tyler....  I walk past the --what do you call it?  the hole? the flag?--anyway I walk past it, into the lee of a boulder, and I'm pulling the box out when fffwissh, a disc whips past me & lands about 5 feet away.  There's no way the guy's gonna miss me, so I pop out from behind the boulder with the box tucked under my arm, under my shirt (it's buggy, so I've got a long-sleeved shirt on over my Tshirt).
Startled the golfer, who had no idea I was there, went "upstream" a ways to stamp in & put the thing back after he left.

I get on trail for another box, same park.... This time not only am I back on the disc golf course (hearing "cha-ching" in the not-too-distance), but it's at the intersection of two wide, fairly straight trails--and I can see the box as I come up to it, so I'm going to need to recover it better....  I take it down the trail, stamp in,  tuck the box under my arm again, & grab a log  & some wide sheets of bark.

As I get back to the intersection, whom do I meet but the same disc golfer...who looks at me a bit funny as I stand there holding my log, waiting for him to play through....

Sheba
