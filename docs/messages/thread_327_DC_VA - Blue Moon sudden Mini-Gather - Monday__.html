Re: [lbma] Re: DC/VA - Blue Moon Mini-Gather - Success

From: The Doubtful Guests | Date: 2004-08-31 01:49:22 UTC

Little did I know this idea would turn into a mystery gather.

1.  It wasn't really a blue moon.

2.  The Desert Moon cafe no longer exists.

3.  A store, and an important clue in the local letterbox, has closed.

Fortunately Sam I Am already knew where the letterbox was.  When I arrived I could see her up there on the bench.  When I got up there she recognized Sowsowbizzy walking around way down in the food court.  Sam stayed, following her and pointing, while I went down to get her.  It was fun looking up 4 floors to Sam pointing down to the ground level for me.  When we got back Schizokeet and Night Owl found us. 

We all passed around It's a Holiday's new stamp, Sally the Rag Doll.  Sam I Am did a wonderful job coloring it.  Coloring really makes a stamp look better than it really is.  I don't want to hear any more complaints about simple stamps.  Any stamp can look great with coloring, even my early, crude stamps, as I found out in Firefly's logbook.

And Sowsowbizzy, this does not get you off the hook for carving a bigger Sally to go with  your  NMBC collection.  :-)

We left a note in the letterbox to find us in the American Cafe and repaired to dinner, a big blue moon drink in a orb glass, and fun conversation and comeraderie. 

We tentatively decided to go ahead with a Northern Virginia Halloween Saturday gather, perhaps in my front yard.  Details if I figure this one out. 

Oh, and yeah, everyone got to stamp into the Once in a Blue Moon postal letterbox.  Personally I think a postal is a good excuse for anyone to call for a party.  (As long as you don't delay it or use up too many pages.)

My humble apologies to anyone who could not figure out the mystery.  Many thanks to Sam I Am, Sowsowbizzy, Schizokeet and Night Owl for showing up and for a good time.

Scarab of the Doubtful Guests
