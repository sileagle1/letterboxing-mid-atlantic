Re: [lbma] wee ink pads

From: Otis' Friends | Date: 2008-07-29 02:08:27 UTC

Don't give up too soon on the markers.  For some stamps, there's nothing better.

 

Everyone has a different preference when it comes to ink.  My only recommendation is to avoid the METALLIC pigments unless you are the author and are placing them with the stamp because it really needs to be silver, or gold, or copper, etc.  Metallic pigments are very difficult to clean off and wind up getting on other people's pens - where they take forever to work their way off.

From: MaryAnn Lockard | Date: 2008-07-29 10:39:05 UTC
Both are pigment inks and take a little longer to dry. I like the way the catseye nest together, but when in a backpack they do need a rubberband around them to keep them together. I like both types of these inks for various artistic projects but have not purchased the small versions for use on the trail. Don't forget to scour the paper for coupons, with 40% off you can buy more.
