New Hope Box Report

From: Rainear, Tim TQO | Date: 2004-04-01 23:38:04 UTC

Hi all,

I have read about a number of "disappointing days" lately, so I am pleased to report a pretty darn good day.

This was almost a Connecticut-like day.

 

I went after and found all of the following boxes.....

 

  1. Bowman Hill Flower Reserve - this was a tough one to find and to get to.  Give yourself some extra time for this one.
  2. Jane Austen's - Persuasion: yes folks, despite what you may have heard, this box is alive and very well.  Nice and dry.
  3. Wincawhawchunk Box - not MIA as someone else suggested.
  4. Battle of Trenton - Colonel Moore's Insignia.  Definitely behind private property signs, but that seldom stops me.
  5. Battle of  Trenton - Victory: Not wet yet but the box is almost useless.  Great big hole in the side.

 

Happy hunting.

Raindear
