Well, I'm not all that thrilled tracking stuff on two different
websites. It's rather inconvenient, and I would have loved to have
merged the clues on LbNA with those on AQ (even more so early on when
there was practically nothing listed on AQ in the first place), but
that idea didn't seem like it would anywhere.

But hey, if you can make it happen on the LbNA end of things, I'd be
absolutely thrilled to develop a system where LbNA can do more talking
with Atlas Quest, so if the status of a box is changed on one site,
the other site can automatically be notified of the change and have
the change happen there. That sort of stuff would be enormously useful
for everyone, or to make it easier for people to list boxes on both
sites simultaneously. But I didn't see that idea getting off the ground.

Things could definitely be more efficient, though.
