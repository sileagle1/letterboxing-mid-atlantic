You don't have to log in your finds anywhere if you don't want to. It's a personal choice. I guess it's gotten to the point where almost everyone does log their finds on AQ but it's not "required". You can just keep track of them in your own logbook if you want to, the journal you keep your images in. The only things in this hobby that are "rules" (for lack of a better word) are things like respecting the boxes of other people, **rehiding well** (one of the most important things to remember) etc, respecting nature, learning what to do with hitchhikers, stuff like that, learning certain terms.
 
You should also join the newbie list on yahoo and look at Silent Doug's site because it has a good glossary of terms. It's at  
 
http://www.letterboxing.info/
 
It's a great hobby.....welcome to it and treat it well.
