This weekend, boxing in VA, and a new box planted

From: Kathryn Wolfe | Date: 2004-08-30 15:23:29 UTC
    This weekend, we braved the heat and scoured for the two boxes in Lacy Woods in Virginia (the 5133 box and the 2003 Chinese new year box -- both fun stamps).  Both were in fine condition, though we had to intently study the holly bushes a few times and pose as "researchers" to avoid the family in the nearby pavilion.  We've decided to walk around the trails with a ziploc bag full of a few random leaves in hand just to make it more convincing.
 
    We also planted a new box, The Educator, on Capitol Hill.  It's not quite a microbox, but it is still pretty small -- a minibox?  There's a surprise in there for the first finder.  I'm posting the clues now.
 
Longhorn and The Camel
 
PS, not sure whether I can make the mini-gathering tonight or not.  I don't usually get off until 6:30, but since it's recess, maybe I can sneak away a little earlier.
