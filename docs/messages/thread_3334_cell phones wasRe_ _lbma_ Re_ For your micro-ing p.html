It shouldn't be a problem.

1) The strips on credit cards are made to resist erasure to guard
against casual reprogramming. A really super magnet would be required.

2) The level of current in a cell phone wouldn't create a magnetic
field large enough. I have doubts it would even get through the case
of a cell phone.

doubts on even this. Maybe on your library or grocery store card.
