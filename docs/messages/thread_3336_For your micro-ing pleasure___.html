> Carving hint # 347: (if u don't already know it--<s>) If you want to
do a bigger stamp than will fit in the tube, carve one that will fit
if it's cut in half. Put several layers of clear packing tape on the
back. Some people like duct tape. I prefer the clear tape. Cut the
stamp in half until you get to the tape. You still get a great image
with no line down the middle. Holds up forever, so far. I've never had
one come off the clear tape. Somebody passed that little hint on to me
when I first started boxing.
