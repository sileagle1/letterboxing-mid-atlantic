A BRIEF HISTORY OF THE BUMPPO

For the record, my infamy comes from two (yes, only 2) rather public screwups.
One was a completely disasterous compass conundrum at the Rubbermaid Road Rally 2
where I - admittedly - failed completely. AND YET everyone found the box anyway.

The other incident related to a magnetic microbox I planted at the Live8 concert. I failed
to notice I had planted the box under a GIANT ELECTRIC TRANSFORMER which - in case
you don't know is HIGHLY MAGNETIC. Thus, throwing off all my bearings. Sue me.

There was an additional incident where I noticed that one compass I had was affected by
my body. It would skew my bearings. I later discovered it was because of the additional
cell density I have from being born on the planet Krypton.

So you can see how this group - who frankly has a low threshold for entertainment -
would get a huge chuckle out of my misfortune and name a syndrome after me.

In my defense, there is also quite a bit of whining going on from some who THINK they
have figured out a few clues that are -charitably - beyond their paygrade.

And now you know... The REST Of The Story.

Natt-
