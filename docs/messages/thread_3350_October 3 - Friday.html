From: Lea Shangraw Fox | Date: 2008-08-31 23:18:47 UTC
Hey all --

Amanda (from Seattle) and I will be in your neck of the woods for the Nancy Drew conference
this year - this time we are having it in PA Amish country (note Nancy Drew #33 is set in this
part of the country). There is a big field trip to Philadelphia planned for Friday 10/3, but
since both of us have already been on that tour, we've decided to set that day aside for
letterboxing.

We are staying in Bird in Hand, which is a few miles east of Lancaster. We are thinking that
maybe a meet and greet in the evening, at the hotel might work out best - anyone have any
better suggestions? Also, we will be boxing during the day, if anyone cares to join us.

Hope this works out - I look forward to seeing you all again - I have fond memories of my
trip to PA last year.

-- Princess Lea
