Fun story

From: Samuel Checker | Date: 2008-09-08 04:09:08 UTC
We took our two girls out to the Village of Goshen to work some ants
out of their pants. They tore off up the boardwalk and I followed, but
my wife was stopped by two ladies in a Mini. They wanted to know,
could she direct them to the trail near Foxcatcher?

Well, she put two and two together and asked if they had found the box
in Goshen yet. We had to check the logbook after they left - they had
the cutest "Mini" stamp and their ink was the same color as their car.
Hope they found the other box too!
