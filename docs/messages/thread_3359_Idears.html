From: Kristy Lucas | Date: 2008-09-10 18:17:46 UTC
I've sought several boxes with photos for clues.  One used a collage style with multiple photos on one large one, so I only had to print one photo.  They used a photo editing program to draw arrows and call attention to certain areas of the image.  Another reduced the size of the images so they only printed out about wallet size (all on one page).  The clarity wasn't great, but it gave me enough information to discern once I got to the starting point.  Black & White drawings might work as well even though that would leave a lot of room for "artistic license."

Sounds like fun to me,
