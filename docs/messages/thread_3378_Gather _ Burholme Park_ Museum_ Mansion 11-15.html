Due to those who expressed regrets about the date of 4/5/08, we are
redoing the event at Burholme Park Area for those newer folks & those
who couldn't make 4/5 (& any one else who wants to come).
Kind of a ltrboxing sampler w/ park, neighborhood, library, & museum
as backdrop.
We hope to involve letterboxes, art, outdoor fun, fall colors, good
scenery & company into a fun day.
About 12 boxes in mostly the same hides w/ a few new hand carved
stamps. Any input & suggestions are greatly appreciated. Boxes will
in place Sat. AM. to Mon. PM.
Loan of a few Burholme, pet, or Asian themed stamps or boxes would be
a help. We willhave about 12 hides (In small to quite small places).
Contact us if you want to help or place.

The Mansion, Museum & Library will be opened & free 10 to 4PM,
guided tours (also free) available .
The 68 acre free park offers picnic groves, garden, accessible rest
rooms (10-4PM), playground, hilltop views up to 30 miles, parking,
ball fields, woodland trail, streams, self guided walking history
tour, etc. Fees for mini-golf, driving range, snack bar, & batting
cages.
We plan to have a host table, on the mansion side lawn, near the 3
large porches. We can have a small meeting space in the mansion
available 12 to 3:30 PM if there is a need.
The Museum & Library do accept donations, should you desire.

A lawn chair would be a good idea, as would a sun shade or rain fly
if you want. WE WILL GO RAIN OR SHINE.
Due to park rules this will be RSVP only & limited to first 70 signed
up.
SEPTA Bus stops at park, regional rail line stops 150 yards from park.
