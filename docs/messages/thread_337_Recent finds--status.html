Hello,
Just touching base with recent finds and others:

"You Got To Have 'Em": Replaced bag in need of help, but box was
mostly in good shape.

"Seven Bends": This one is in good shape, dry and well-hidden.
There are a lot of trees down. This is located next to a great tower
in the mountains. It was a clear day, and you could see lots of
ridges in VA and WV. It would be good to see in the fall during
the "colors" and maybe even better after the leaves have dropped.

"Woodstock Tower Seedling": This is near the Seven Bends letterbox,
but I couldn't find it. Lots of trees down, so maybe it's there.
Anyone found it lately?

"2004 Campaign Trail Hitchhiker Race--Bush Hitchhiker": found
at "Be's Search For The Mushroom Kindgom". Nice stamp, and moving
soon to the Shenandoah Valley along I-81.

"Oh Deer!": What a great park this is in! This park is worth some
time and the $4 to get in. I looked for this tonight and found it
just before the rain started. The bad news is that the logbook is
completely ruined due to water. Most of the images are lost, and
remaining ones exist barely. The letterbox container is no longer
watertight. It was out in the open, although in this corner of the
park, you'd have been looking for the letterbox anyway. It's been
rebagged and stamp is wrapped in a papertowel now.

"Helping Hands Charity Box": This is in the same park as "Oh Deer!"--
but seems to be missing. It's obvious where it should have been, and
in the rain I waded 50' through grass (and thorns) 4-5' high. There
is no sign of it anywhere. Anyone seen it lately?

It was a good night though!
