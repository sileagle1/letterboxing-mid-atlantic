I have little or no patience for newbie questions. I'm listed as a member because I joined so I could post about my now defunct urban boxing list. I figured out most stuff for myself when I was new. It ain't rocket science. And I always figured anyone who has to ask questions like what should I carve, etc, should maybe go bowling instead, or take up some other less creative hobby.
 
And I choose the word "talent".
 
I figure unless one has the patience to answer endless/redundant/questions and the desire to help new people, why keep going there? I welcome new people who are serious about the hobby but have no desire to lead them in their quest for information. There are plenty of people who are so they don't need me. I've been know to refer people to Silent Doug's site for the glossary but that's about it.
