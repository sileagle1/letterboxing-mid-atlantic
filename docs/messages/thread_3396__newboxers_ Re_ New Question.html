From: Randy Hall | Date: 2008-10-16 12:00:47 UTC
> Any time you get any group of more than 2 people together
> for any length of time it will happen. :)

This statement is demonstrably false. (No offense,
its just the way I write :-)).

I'm in 2 other outdoor activities where it doesn't happen,
and have been so for years. There is a sense of community
in both. There is certainly a sense of rivalry in the field,
but no sense of rivalry amongst the persons, who mostly go
out of their way to be friendly and civil, and genuinely care
about each other as persons.

This hobby, by contrast, is characterized by nastiness,
cliqism, pettiness, one-upsmanship, and hypocrisy, as LB so
correctly demonstrated an instance of, and it is a shame
Cyclonic appears to have been the victim this time. I've
reviewed the messages. Absurd.

Brendan accurately points out a proximate cause inasmuch
as people will self-stand-in for the other in an 80%
communication vacuum, but I see that as only a proximate
cause. The ultimate cause, IMHO, of the nastiness and pettiness
in letterboxing is the quest for prestige -- to be the big fish
in the pond, regardless of how absurdly small and irrelevant
the pond happens to be. Message to the prestige seekers --
no one cares.

This list doesn't seem the place for this cat fight, and I
apologize to the moderator(s), but when you've been seeing
absurdity for years, and you feel Cyclonic warrants the
support in this case, it is difficult to hold one's peace.

As an aside, it is interesting that a credible source has
suggested to me in the distant past that certain messages
posted on the list I manage have been a deliberate attempt
to devalue and destabilize that list in favor of those run
by associate(s) of the poster(s) in question. Everyone loves
a good conspiracy theory, don't they? I don't care enough
about it to have an opinion one way or the other, but it
seems possible that such a suggestion could pass the Occam's
Razor test when considering all the bizarre and senseless
facts in toto.

Again, apologies to the moderator(s) and the rest of the
list. I'll rant elsewhere going forward. But some things
needed to be said.

Randy
