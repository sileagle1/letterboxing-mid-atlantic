When the going gets tough, the tough go to Joisey

From: Suzanne Coe | Date: 2008-10-18 05:55:15 UTC
In other words, I spent an afternoon boxing across the river the other day....  (Sorry, I know I'm s'posed to warn people when I'm going to do that, but it was a spur of the moment thing.)  I went after Spirits of the Celtic Cross' First Drop, & furthered my knowledge of history in a pretty cool, scenic location.  Mommo wouldn't have cared for the box =), but I found that I had to pay pretty close attention to the clues in order to find it, so I was impressed.  Would have gone after their other box if I'd done enough homework to find the location.... Instead I went to Mantua for Team Happilyeverafter's Stars & Stripes & Angel Wings.  Nice park, nice hike, and a very meaningful box. Both in fine condition, with quite a few local boxers stamped in (man, there's a whole lot of folks boxing over there that I don't know!  Might be nice to have a south Jersey gather again around this time next year?)

One other note, glad  to hear of the return of the Great Pumpkin to our sincere local pumpkin patch!  You've really got to love the hide on this one, not to mention the details on his new face.... If you can get there before he leaves for the season, it's worth the trip.

Sheba
