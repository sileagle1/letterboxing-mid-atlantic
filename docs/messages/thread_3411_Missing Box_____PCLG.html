Well...I guess it was bound to happen. Today I found that one of 'my'

am not expecting it......

Oooh that was a good one.! When Dave was on Active Duty and I was coming down to visit, Dave picked me up at the train station and we went to find it on the way back to Fort Dix. It was a great site. Do love Gazebo boxes. Maybe I should plant one of my own.
