There are plenty of neat boxes in the Pine Barrens, though most you have
to do a little research for before venturing out.

I highly recommend Pine Barrens Tree Frog, Just Passing Through, and Oh,
Be My Friend. All take you to very neat, mostly forgotten locations in
the Barrens. There are a few others out there, I just can't mention
them more specifically.

Some not so mysterious boxes include Terra-Cotta Tangram (highly
recommended), Ssssssss!, NJ Pysiography, Things to Do in the Pines
Series, and the Scary Vegetable NJ. These boxes are near Chatsworth
which is aneat little town in and of itself. Be sure to visit the
general store. Nearby is Whitesbog Village, a historic area with its
own neat general store.

Batsto Village on the South end of the Barrens is well worth a visit as
well. It is an old company town with a mansion, mill and the workings
of several Pine Barren industries. There are also plenty of boxes in
the area, including the Bridges of Burlington County.

Enjoy.
