Hope folks are thinking beyond the Big Bird and planning their trip to
Tyler where Songs will Fill the Air (did I get that right?) on Dec 6.

It is $21/nite. Call Jim or Susan to make your reservation.

Hostel address: Jim Whitmoyer, Box 94, Newtown PA 18940
Hostel phone: 215-968-0927
Park phone: 215-968-2021 Web site:
