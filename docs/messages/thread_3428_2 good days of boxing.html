From: mizscarlet731 | Date: 2008-11-30 16:10:47 UTC
Got in 2 good days of boxing this week-end. Went for 2 of Riversol's new boxes, sucess.
Next day no such luck. Nice clues, but I think the planters were one hit wonders. I did n't see
any other boxes planted by them. Clues ended in a rather high impact area with a set of
stone steps. Many little hidey holes but far too obvious.
On another note... Decided to clean out carving drawer, sort, finish things started, ect. Came
upon half carved stamp of Mastercarve or should I say Mastercrap. I now know why I didn't
finish the stamp in the first place. Yes it cuts like butter but way to easy to over shoot line or
undercut. Just orderd some PZcut grade B white.
