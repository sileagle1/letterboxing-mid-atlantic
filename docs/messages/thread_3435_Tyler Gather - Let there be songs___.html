Tyler Gather - Let there be songs...

From: Hikers & Hounds | Date: 2008-12-07 20:07:09 UTC
A heartfelt thanks to everyone who braved the cold to attend my annual "freeze you @$$ off" gather at Tyler State Park. Dude and I got to see so many of our dear friends and make new ones, which is always a huge kick for us! Thanks to everyone who brought food, wood and thier warm friendship to my little neck of the woods. A special shout out to Sowsewbizzy and the Red Hats who pretty much refused to let this gather not happen when I was feeling pretty blue and was thinking of cancelling. Next thing I know I see emails flying back and forth and who's bringing this and making that and I decided I guess I just better suck it up and attend my own gather! What a wonderful bunch of broads, the absolute best.
 
Good news is next year the gather will be at the Hostel in Tyler park, so that means indoors with HEAT and a fireplace! We'll work out parking and food logistics later.
 
So thanks again, turns out a day in the woods freezing my tookus off with the best people on the planet was just what the doctor ordered!
