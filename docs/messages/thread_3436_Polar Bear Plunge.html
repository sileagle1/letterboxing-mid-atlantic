Polar Bear Plunge

From: brendan_hickey13 | Date: 2008-12-08 15:41:44 UTC
Some folks were asking at Tyler - what is it about wind chills in
the teens that makes some folks think about swimming? - so it seems
OK to note that the Lewes Polar Bear Plunge is coming up again.

Sunday, 1 February 09 at 1:00 PM in Rehoboth Beach, Delaware. We
cleared $530,000 for Special Olympics Delaware in 2008 and the event
is a lot of fun - like a summer beach weekend, except for the
weather...and the attire...oh, parking is free, too.

Anyway, for a bit more information, you can visit my page at

http://www.firstgiving.com/brendanhickey

or the Special Olympics site at

http://www.sode.org


Naturally, I will come up with a stamp for anyone who donates or
makes the Plunge.
