Ft Miles mystery

From: brendan_hickey13 | Date: 2008-12-15 01:19:34 UTC
Ft. Miles Tower is back on active duty and available to anyone
visiting Lewes for, say, the Polar Bear Plunge, or the Lower Slower
Stagger.

Back in June, we received a report that the log book had been found
in the open and the remainder of the box found not at all. This kind
boxer mailed our log book back, which is nice to have from a dead box.

We guessed that some kind of critter had pulled the box apart and
carried it off, so we recarved and finally had a chance to replant.

The story gets a bit weird, though.

We found our original box under a different branch of the same tree,
wet but closed, with the stamp intact and two sheets of paper with
personal stamps on them.

OK, so the log somehow leaves the box and winds up on the sand...and
then other boxers find the box and create a temporary log...but the
box itself was never pulled apart, carried off, or destroyed, just
temporarily missing back in June.

If Natalie H or A. H. read this list or if anyone knows them, we'd
love to hear from them in the hope of rounding out this tale a bit.
