Where's BUMPPO?

From: nattybumppolbna | Date: 2008-12-19 15:50:55 UTC
Some of you have remarked about the conspicuous absence of Bumppo clues from LBNA
and I just wanted to update everyone at once to minimize emails.

After getting thoroughly rogered by AOL when they pulled out my ftp space from their
services, I determined to be self-sufficient on the web. Thus, I planned and launched
Bumppoville.com as a home for my letterboxing projects. Because of the way many of my
clues are included in other web sites, news groups etc. and contain graphics hosted on
other pages, etc. I needed to carefully extricate and remap everything before I could
launch Bumppoville properly.

Well between removing the clues and restoring them to Bumppoville, I discovered that I
have a small case of leukemia that needs to be dealt with. So I will be spending December
and January in the hospital kicking it's ass before I can get back to the home computer to
restore the clues. But don't worry, one thing this has given me is time to plot and plan
connive and scheme and I will be looking forward my full recuperation on the trails of
Montgomery, Bucks and Chester Cos.

Meanwhile I hope everyone has a happy healthy holiday season and Boxing Day.

All the best,

Natt-
