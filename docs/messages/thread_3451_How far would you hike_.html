How far would you hike?

From: mizscarlet731 | Date: 2009-01-04 12:39:08 UTC
Went on great winter's day hike with a group of intrepid boxers. We covered 11 miles over
the course of the day. Box count was 7 and 3 attempts. A few of the boxes were of a historic
nature, letterboxing history that is. Interesting to see clue development and box placement
morph over a period of years.
Would I have covered so much ground on my own? No way! We would have gone farther but
in theses days of winter the boxers day is cut short.
