Letterboxing penguin party in Schnecksville, PA this weekend

From: Magical Music Man | Date: 2009-01-08 20:42:24 UTC
The new African penguin exhibit is opening at the Lehigh Valley Zoo!

We are going to be there this Saturday, and if anyone is interested in
coming, you can meet up with me and I will have special clues to a day
only African penguin box. There are also lots of other neat animals to
see at the zoo, including Scimitar horned oryx and golden crowned
cranes, both endangered species. I also have my box in the zoo and
there are boxes on the nature trails that are on the way out.

This is the special box (and the stamp came out really well!)
http://www.atlasquest.com/boxes/clue/index.html?gBoxId=102457

I will have my PTs and if I have time, I will be planting a new stamp
that I just carved yesterday.

Please contact me if you are planning on coming so we can work out a
time and place to meet up in the zoo.

-Magical Music Man
