Barrack Obama Inaugural Letterbox

From: gallant_rogue | Date: 2009-01-15 15:18:45 UTC
All this discussion about the mysterious letterbox with 22 question
marks in the title that somehow
was ingeniously hidden under the Washington Monument on New Years Eve
has inspired me!

I have created a fantastic stamp & log and placed
them in a container fashioned to look like Abe Lincoln's Bible and
under cover of darkness I planted this very special very limited
commemorative letterbox under the temporary inaugural podium recently
constructed on the front steps of the US Capital Building. You should
hurry over to get this box because it will only be there till
Wednesday January 21st when crews will begin dismantling the stage.

You must be very discreet in logging into this box because the area
might be subject to extra security by the US Capital Police, Secret
Service, Virginia Maryland and DC National Guard and U.S.Army. Plus
there may be a few hundred thousand non-letterboxer types (darn
muggles!)in the area the next few days.

If security or anybody asks what you are doing please say "Sorry
Officer, My wife lost her keys... "

Click here for the photo clue:

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/lbma/photos/album/457082306/pic/list

The box is near the street lamp directly in front of the stage

Remember not to resist arrest when letterboxing on federal property!
