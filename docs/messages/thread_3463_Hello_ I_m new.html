The best site for a new boxer to find focused info is at Silent Doug's
website:

www.letterboxing.info

It is written by a writer who letterboxes. Also, Randy Hall's book,
"The Letterboxing Companion" is a valuable resource for letterboxers.

Both of these resources represent the more true side to letterboxing.

Cheers,

LB
