From: Lightnin Bug | Date: 2009-01-17 16:59:55 UTC
It all depends what type of boxing experience you wish. If you are a
hiker, any one of the PA State Parks offer many a box - longer hikes
with elevation gain can be found at French Creek near Pottstown, for
intance.. If you want a lot of boxes in a little time, try Tyler
State Park a little further from Doylestown from where you are headed
(which I kind of know) or Ridley Creek State Park west of Philly.
Closer to home, I'd say hit South Mountain - all 3 locations - lots of
known and unknown things there.

Once the stuff thaws, great stuff can be had at Hickory Run State Park.

Again, it all depends upon your preference for hiking or finding and
such. Please let us know what sort of adventure you prefer.

Cheers,

LB
