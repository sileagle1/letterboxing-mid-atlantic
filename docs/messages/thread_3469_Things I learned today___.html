Urushiol can get you at any time of year, no doubt - beware of
rubbish fires - the oil can become airborne and you can inhale it -
poison ivy in your lungs = hospital visit.

Hard to cover your tracks when finding a snowbound box - not sure
how to avoid leaving a trail that screams, "Hey! Something
interesting over here!" though concentrations of yellow snow seem
to be a deterrent....
