Where Did We Box This Weekend?

From: Nathan Brown | Date: 2009-01-20 03:02:30 UTC
The question was asked, but I figure it does deserve its own thread.

Schizokeet and I are happily (OK, very happily) moved in at this point,
but had been so busy we had not had the chance to really get out until
early last week.

On Tuesday we went looking for Ostrich Love, which we had no luck
finding, but we did find a mystery box in the same park, which had great
clues that seemed to just piece themselves together. I love it when
that happens.

Sunday we ventured into the cold and found Suburban Sanctuary. Nice
boxes, and we decided to complete the walk along the trail the boxes
were on. Had a great time, as did the two families playing hockey on
the frozen pond there.

Last night we meat with a few locals at a pub from some drinks, a great
meal, but no darts as the board was removed. We still had fun though,
and it was nice to greet Mt Scorpia from NC, she is some character.

Today we ventured once again into the cold and found Suburban
Recreation. Again, a nice walk, and Schizokeet also planted her 20th box.

Hope yous who have signed up for the mystery gathering are having fun
with the clues and boxes ;-) It should be a lot of fun, though I have a
lot of work to do this week.

--
Nathan Brown
