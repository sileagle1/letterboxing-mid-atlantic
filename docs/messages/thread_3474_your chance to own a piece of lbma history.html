Down here in the food challenged abyss that is Northern Virginia, the
local grocery store is Safeway. Of course, they have one of those
shopper club program things that is really meant to track your buying
habits (or is it buying hobbits?).

So, of course I don't sign up for it. In Philly I would go to Redner's,
which doesn't have that, or Giant, which would just put in a code for me
without even asking. But Safeway, nope, it is against policy, so they
won't do it, and they will fight with you about it (did I mention this
is also a service abyss). You can put in your phone number if you don't
have your card with you, so guess what, in goes Jenny's number, which is
in the system.

Becky says I should just get a card, that I am missing out on specials
and such, but I say no thanks, I don't buy six gallons of milk in a week
to get the free gallon or whatever it is, and besides, I enjoy messing
with the system.

Wonder what would happen if everyone started to use Jenny's number?
