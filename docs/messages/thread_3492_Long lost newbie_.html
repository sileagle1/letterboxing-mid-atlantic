Long lost newbie!

From: lunastrixae | Date: 2009-02-19 17:42:01 UTC
Hey ho everyone!

Some of you may have wondered where I wandered off to! At lest the
people I met in RL may be wondering. :)

I have a horrible heel spur that I started having issues with around
the same time I started boxing last year. Of course I ignored it and
kept boxing, biking, and running. This fall I could hardly walk a few
city blocks. This is why I bailed from the December Gathering- I KNOW
I wouldn't be able to take it easy with a zillion boxes all around me!
I hope lots of you found My Pink Floyd box, anyway.

So the good news is that I think I'm finally on the mend! Not quite
ready to chase after Bug at a full trot yet, but I'm optimistic I'll
be back this spring. Yay! I'm getting fat! I have a few carves ready
to plant, too.

I did manage to get my first two, out of the country stamps this fall
in Ontario. Sheba, I left that hitchhiker we found there, that's a
nice hitch distance from Delaware!!

Hope to see you again soon,

-Sherry (aka moonowl)
