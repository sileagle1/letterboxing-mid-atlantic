A Thief Unmasked

From: Otis' Friends | Date: 2009-02-22 01:01:00 UTC
Had an undeniable urge to put out a letterbox today.  Unfortunately, the site was much more tulgy than I anticipated (perhaps in part because I was not in the correct spot).  As a result, I'm thinking of making the clues more in the nature of directions.  Until that happens, here's the rough cut.
 
 
On April Fool's Day in the true first year of this millenium, a couple of meddling kids were playing in the woods not far from their apartment building (and the local police station) when they stumbled across a sealed PVC pipe in a cement drainage pipe.  Further searching by the authorities uncovered much, much more, ultimately inspiring at least two letterboxes.
 
This particular letterbox is in the woods not far from the police station, though not in precisely the same area as the original cache.
 
15 - now watch this drive
310 - home run
10 - Santa's crying
 
Inside a right angle.
