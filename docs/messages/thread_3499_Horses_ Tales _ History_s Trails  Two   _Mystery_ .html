Thanks to all for the interest, & ideas.

Responding to varied comments:  We are a work in progress.

We are going to have a 2nd event stamp for those who "solve" the "mystery" location. Three teams or folks have already solved it. If you solve location email us with your proof IE. why you think it is @____.  Mystery will need to be "solved" by 5/1 when websites & more info will be posted & emailed to all. You need not attend to get email copy of mystery stamp.

You can email for any or all info.

There will be a regular event stamp for all present  & 1 to 3 or more event boxes in the area (clues at event table).
If you want to help, plant or have clues available at event let us know how we can help.

There is group camping at a nearby Bucks Co. Park. If there is interest we can try to sign up @$3/per head, there are also cabins, trailer, & tent family camping @ Tohicken park.  There is closer camping, but this park seems much better.   http://www.buckscounty.org/government/departments/parksandrec/Parks/Tohickon.aspx

The burgers & horses will be center stage 2PM.

Horses may go to a rain date if field is too wet or in lightning. We need ideas on should we reschedule or just go letterboxing on 5/16.

We will have a dining fly at tailgating, chairs & other sun/rain flys will be helpful. You can sit on grass.

Next clue Tuesday.

Good hunting to all, M
