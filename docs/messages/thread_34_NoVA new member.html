I believe they're supposed to be different - I've personally never
seen two that were the same. A lot of people carve/buy stamps
according to the theme or location of the box. The FAQs can
probably help you some more - besides the main letterboxing.org
site, check out Silent Doug's www.letterboxing.info site - very
useful!

From: thedoubtfulguests | Date: 2004-04-12 20:57:06 UTC
I recommend that you try to carve your own personal stamp. You
would be surprised at what you create and then it's "your". Sure
the first one will be crude but who cares. Read the FAQs and hints
and ask questions and give it a go.

Meanwhile don't let that stop you. Grab a store bought stamp and go
hunting. Originally all letterbox stamps were storebought. Making
them yourself developed later and many people still use store
bought. The important thing is to "get out there."

Happy hunting,
Scarab of the Doubtful Guests
