Free Containers.....

From: June | Date: 2009-02-28 19:39:54 UTC

50 ml centrifuge tubes.........if you need a really small container. I have used these after buying some from catbead who stopped selling them *and* boxing I think. I also won some at a gathering. They are great for small boxes, totally waterproof and totally weatherprooof. Some of you know about them.

I was checking out an old site in my favorites and asked for two free samples and they sent them to me almost imemdiately. You usually have to buy them in bulk.
They're great for urban boxes but can be used anywhere. I put a picture in an album called 'Supplies' for those who may not know about them. You can use a cash register tape logbook or some other rolled up paper, really small stamps or split stamps backed with tape to be able to get a bigger image in it.

http://www.perf-sci.com/d_cent_tubes/cent_50.html

Hey, for free, you know?
