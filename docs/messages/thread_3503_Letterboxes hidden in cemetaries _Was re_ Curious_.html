I have planted perhaps a dozen in cemetaries and/or using cemetaries as
landmarks. One of my most memorable finds was Dixie's "The Mourning" in
a beautiful old cemetary in Ashville NC. I've always thought
Letterboxing in cemetaries could be done in a dignified and respectful
manner. That may have changed for me last week. There is an HBO movie
called "Taking Chance" that deals with the military protocol of
transfering remains. A real tear jerker. Since I can only control my
behavior and not that of anybody looking for my boxes, I may refrain
from such future placement.
