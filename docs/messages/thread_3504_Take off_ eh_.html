So yesterday, Shoeless and I were boxing up in Allentown, and we found some pretty stamps on the ground.  Chh hoo hah.
 
Anyway, I know LB has often lamented the lack of visitors up to the "frozen wastelands" of Lehigh County,* but I have a feeling that is about to change.  With the very talented PA Vintners joining Lightnin Bug and Magical Music Man in putting out high quality stamps,** I, for one, hope to spend more time up there.
 
 
*Of course, not as many people on this list may be active PA boxers as we think.  We just lost a Keystone State boxer to the Old Dominion.  Given the denominator, the number in the numerator may not be as small as it seems.
 
**And this doesn't count the new entries I didn't have time to get to, or the Vulture's legacy.
