From: Debbie Kotlarek | Date: 2009-03-30 04:38:51 UTC
Cheers!  We had a great time!  Even though it was a bit chilly outside, we had the cozy pubs to retreat to throughout the afternoon and those crazy RHBers and their games kept our brain cells from getting completely pickled. Our team pulled together to tackle the hardest one - the mixed drink crossword puzzle!  We had the privilege of being teammates of Gallant Rogue, El Inka, Psychiker & Syner, Grzzly Grrl, and ShadowDancer. The event clues and stamps were great and it was nice to meet even more Mid-Atlantic boxers throughout the day. After the official Pub Crawl ended, we gladly joined Gallant Rouge, Kibbles and El Inka for some night boxing.  El Inka called it quits early (not to retire, but instead to join the RHBers at their cottage).  It was a misty dark night, but the pole dancers were active on the boardwalk and Sister Mary Margarita was still swilling her drinks outside the church.  After finding all that were on our list, the remaining 4 of us made our way to the infamous cottage.  We were then treated to some fine hospitality, more adult beverages and some tasty treats, NOT to mention an amusing floor show!!  We finally called it quits at 1 am and made our way back to our hotel.
 
Many, many thanks to SewSowBizzy, Miz Scarlet, Hikers n Hounds, Pink Panther, Gerania, & Geckospot!!!
 
Wisconsin Hiker & Martini Man
 
P.S. We enjoyed the warmer weather today at Tuckahoe and found several boxes there before heading back home.  Yes, we did get new snow here over the weekend - Yuck!
