LSS - Last Shore Sighting

From: sewsowbizzy | Date: 2009-03-30 21:30:20 UTC
The last of the RHBr's departed Rehoboth Beach today at noon after finally re-planting the "One Hump or Two" pouch she had been carrying around all day Sunday (sorry Otis) when she had returned to ask the pubs to continue hosting their boxes.

Those who could not make it down for the crawl, can cry in their beer for all the fun they missed, or form their own belated team to solve & follow the street and crawl clues now posted on AQ/LB.org with link to         http://lowerslowerswagger.tripod.com

I am so glad all the hard work played off and the real rain abated so everyone could stamp in without runny ink.

A great big Thank You to the RedHatBar-tend'rs who really made it all possible and those who attended and made it all FUN.

ssb
