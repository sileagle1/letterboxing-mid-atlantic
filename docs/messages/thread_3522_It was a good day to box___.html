Indigo Vulture and I headed east to the land of the Sopranos. Met up with Jersey Trailblazer and hiked Turkey Mountain. All of the boxes are nicely in place, with a new one coming on line soon, dedicated to a volatile British Chef and a part of nearby Gotham. For those who like such things, a hitchhiker with a solution to the nearby Three Legged Dawg mystery has been planted in something with lots of steps.

The Tourne (great name for a thrash metal band) was a good next destination, with lots of interesting features and boxes.

Dawgs were barkin', not just the three legged kind.
