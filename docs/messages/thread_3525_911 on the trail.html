911 on the trail

From: Marleah | Date: 2009-04-09 23:41:56 UTC
Today we decided to go for Ice Age by Lotus. We stopped for the first box & my children were slightly ahead of us. I could hear them talking. I called them back & my daughter (6) came. My son (4 1/2) didn't. We started calling him, but he didn't answer. We took off up the trail, but couldn't find him & he didn't return our calls. After 30 minutes, I called 911 & was in the process of describing his clothes to the rangers, when he came on down the trail. What a scare!

I am very thankful to be home with 2 children. We set up some new rules for the kids after that. Still got 6/7 of the boxes...which is a crazy hard hike! No wonder it's listed as extreme letterboxing.

Marleah
