From: Lightnin Bug | Date: 2009-04-17 16:32:21 UTC
With Ken Lockwood - be forewarned - the road leading south out of Califon is closed about 3/4 mile from where the box it (really bad erosion of the roadbed). If you come up from the south (from the High Bridge area - go north from Cokesbury Road onto Raritan River Road), you can get parking right close to the box.

There is also a rail trail box on the rail trail that runs right through the Gorge. Again, approach from High Bridge. You'll need to walk a bit further for that one unless you climb up behind a train trestle (not somthign I recommend for an 84 yr old Dad!).
