Retiring a box

From: mizscarlet731 | Date: 2009-04-22 00:31:36 UTC
With great regret I have retired the Walking the Dog series on the Radnor trail. It had been out for almost 4 years intact. One box went missing and then another. It was the Ritchie box, I just didn't have in my heart to replace it now that he is gone.
This set of boxes was very popular with new boxers so I will be putting out new boxes on the trail in different locations.
I tend to get bored with maintaining boxes. If they are OK I'll leave them out but if they go missing I'm not inclined to replace.
