Rogue makes good beer, but we'll be talking about organic beer and other environmental issues related to beer - local beer is green beer (not what you get on St. Patrick's day) - plus beer styles and tasting notes and beer history.

Along with all of this talking? Yeah, we'll get to taste beer - the line-up is

(1) Twin Lakes Greenville Pale Ale
(2) Twin Lakes Winterthur Summer Wheat
(3) Twin Lakes Tweeds Tavern Stout
(4) Dogfish Head 60-minute IPA
(5) Victory Whirlwind Wit
(6) Victory Storm King Stout

plus a beer from Stewart's, to be determined tomorrow.
