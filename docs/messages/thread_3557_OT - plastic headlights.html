OT - plastic headlights

From: sewsowbizzy | Date: 2009-05-14 15:26:39 UTC
Any of you who have ridden with me at night and wondered "why doesn't she turn on her headlights" -- they are always on!  But being parked out in the elements for 7yrs and exposed to 130K miles, that plastic goes gray and cloudy.

When the service station doing the state inspection said they needed to be refinished and they could do it for $200 and 2 hrs of my $$$ & time -- I opted to wait until the next day when I had more time.  Thank goodness they passed me anyway %^) on the assumption I would return.

Home to the internet where I found
http://www.myheadlight.com/   (have sound on and watch the video)

So with $18 (product was on sale) and 20min I Can Now See!!!   Whoooo Hoooo
ummmm. I can see better anyway!!
NOTE: do not get Pouch #1 on the paint -- but then I just glossed over it with a touch of Pouch#2

sewsewbizzy
