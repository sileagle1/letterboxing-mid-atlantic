A few notes:
Driveway to Polo field is on N. end of park off Rt. 32 (River Rd.) Turn away from the river. Drive slowly or stop if horses are moving on road or near cars.

Stay behind the white chain, in front of the chain is in play & may be full of charging horses. Polo balls & horses are dangerous if one is coming at you DUCK don't try to catch the ball.

Children & pets must be safeguarded at all times.

Drinking water available at rest rooms behind Stover House on S. end of park 800' from polo field. 2 porta- rest units are @ field.

Sun shades & lawn chairs are a good idea, there is often some free seating under N. big top. Near center field.

We'll be set up @ Blue van, w/ stripped shade top. SW corner of polo field. If game is canceled we'll be in picnic area behind Stover House same gear.
