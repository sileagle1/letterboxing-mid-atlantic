Warcat 76 had a great weekend. We planted our first box in PA at our
son's college in Doylestown (Bucks County). There are so many new
boxes that it is no longer listed on the "new" list so please look it
up . This box is "squealing" for visitors.

Also on Monday we found 5 boxes in PA- Winahawcawchunk, Cheshire Cat,
Abstract Guitar (all in Washington Crossing) and Delaware Canal
Bridge & Bowman's Hill Tower. We actually met Silent Doug & Jennifer
on the trail and were thrilled to meet fellow letterboxers.

Closer to home, we were only 1 for 3. Found the first Campgaw Box
(NJ) but not the second. Also gave up looking for the Down Under
first box in the series due to the rocky terrain.
