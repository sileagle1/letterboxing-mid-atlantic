Re: fwd: Landowner Permissions

From: wood thrush | Date: 2009-05-27 12:23:45 UTC
What is significant about the document is that it was drafted either by or in cooperation with the Maryland Geocaching Society (MGS), a regional group in our area. I remember reading on the MGS web site (www.mdgps.org) several years ago how they were working with the DNR to come up with this type of agreement. That was the route the cachers were taking---approach the authorities and work something out.

Letterboxing, on the other hand, has been flying under the radar, and we don't have an official group the way the cachers do. It seems that the DNR is now aware of boxing for the first time and would like boxes to conform to the cache agreement. DNR, of course, does not realize there is no official voice of boxing. Probably from their perspective, contacting the LBNA webmasters = what they did with the cachers.

I'm going to lay low, review my clues, make them more vague regarding the locations of the boxes, and hope for the best.
