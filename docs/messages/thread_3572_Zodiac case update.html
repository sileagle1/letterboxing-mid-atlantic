Zodiac case update

From: detectivederekspendlove | Date: 2009-05-27 02:56:10 UTC
Before we start, let me introduce myself....I am Detective Derek Spendlove Of the Fairfax County PD. I am the new lead detective in the Zodiac case. I am in charge after the disappearance of Detective Tagaki. He has not been seen or heard of since Saturday, when he was out invesigating a double murder scene in Great Falls. I have been looking over his case files, and can confirm the double murder in Great Falls, the murder of Veronica, the disappearance of her sister Vanessa, and now the disappearance of BJ Tagaki.

I want to congradulate the SRIA task force that solved the first case, and Detectice Zorket for her work on the case. It seems it took many trips to the crime scene, and some great homework to solve the crime. Each of you solved it differently, so nice work. To the others that failed keep your head up high. These murders are related, and each one must be solved in order. It is impossible to solve the next murder without the previous.

There is not much to go on in the the latest letter from Zodiac. I will review it with my superior before releasing it to the detectives. The newest jogger to go missing is Eddie S. Park. We have no body, but the letter is all we have to go on. We can't solve it and request help. I will update here this week on progress.

Contact me at the AQ headquarters for info.

Detective Spendlove

From: Derek Spendlove | Date: 2009-06-07 23:02:31 UTC
Veronica Cabot is confirmed dead via a group of 3 detectives, all solving the case differently.
 
Her sister, Vanessa is confirmed missing...no new info at this time
 
Detective Tagaki is presumed dead.  Zodiac has written a letter confirming certain things about his body.  We have yet to learn more, but we believe he is murdered.
 
Eddie Park is still missing, and we have no new info to go on.
 
We have names on the missing counselor and camper in Great Falls.  Ms. Jeanie Johnson, 47 and Calvin Hampton, 14.  We are working the case.
 
And Zodiac, has claimed to have kidnapped and assulted another young girl in Northern Va.  He has just contacted us with this info, and claims to have her tied up somewhere close to another murder victim.  he is teasing us with info, and daring us to find her before she starves.  Be alert to everything you see.
 
I apologize to all those that have looked hard at the evidence...I just had a baby, and it has taken my time.  More info is coming this week.  The case is alive, and sure to play out.  Be alert.
 
Spendlove
