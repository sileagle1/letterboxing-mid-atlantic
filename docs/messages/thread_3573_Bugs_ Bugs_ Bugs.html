Directions for Use (from the website):

Batteries are included.

To prepare unit:
1. For Initial Use Only: Turn over unit and remove red tab from battery door to activate the two 1.5 volt AA alkaline batteries.
2. Remove one mosquito repellent refill from its packaging and REMOVE the peel-off foil seal to start the green use-up indicator.
3. Open front cover and insert refill into unit with orange side down; then snap the front cover latch closed.

To operate unit:
1. Slide the ON/OFF switch all the way to "+" until the fan turns on.
2. Clip the unit to your belt, pants/shorts waistband, purse or any other convenient location next to you.
3. When finished using the unit, slide the ON/OFF switch to "O."

Useful tips:

You can also place the unit next to you on a table, bench or other suitable hard surface (away from food and sources of heat, ignition or moisture). When placing unit on a surface, lay the unit on its clip to allow open circulation of repellent.

Within minutes, the unit provides head-to-toe protection for up to 12 hours. ******If you move, allow a few minutes for the unit to rebuild its protection. ******** (stars are mine!)

To prolong the refill's life between uses, close the unit by sliding the switch to the "O" position. Store the unit in a cool, dry place-never in a hot car, glove compartment or trunk.

Replace the refill when the green use-up indicator is gone (repeat steps 2 and 3).

For best results, replace refill 14 days after opening or after 12 hours total use. Only OFF!® Clip-On refills should be used in the unit.

When you no longer feel air blowing from bottom of the unit, replace with two 1.5 volt AA alkaline batteries.
