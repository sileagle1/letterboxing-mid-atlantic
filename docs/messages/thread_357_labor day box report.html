Re: labor day box report

From: Lightnin Bug | Date: 2004-09-07 19:29:59 UTC
Here's what was happening in the (formerly isolated) Letterbox Zone
of Allentown, PA on Labor Day...

The Cihlid Guy came for an exchange and hunted for the Pool Wildlife
Sanctuary series with Tom from Team LB. The old LB just watched and
chatted and checked the boxes to make sure they were OK.
Tom hid the Tom's Alien Spaceship Letterbox on South Mountain in
Allentown and we did some box maintenance and HH stocking in the
South Mountain H³.
Planted Seem Seed Farm - a weird little place.
Checked the following boxes and found all is cool - Rodale Bike Park,
General Trexler, Pass-Set-Spike and Rose Garden.

Thanks to all the folks stamping in and rehiding properly!

I also had an interesting, fulfilling encounter. I can see the
general area of Pass Set Spike from where I parked the mean green LB
machine (my small green car). When I was done checking the boxes, I
looked over and heard a kid scream and come running out. I thought
he had found the boxes and was running over excitedly to show his
folks. I jogged over and introduced myself and asked if they were
letterboxers and and had found the box. They said they were, but
instead of finding the box, the poor little guy found a bumblebee and
had gotten stung on the ankle. Luckily, I still had my backpack and
was able to provide Dad with some first aid stuff. This was their
second time out, so I showed them my books, stamps, etc and exchanged
with them. The kids and folks were excited and I hope that we have
some permanent new members in the Allentown area!

Lightnin' Bug
