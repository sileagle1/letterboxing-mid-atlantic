Saranac is the high-end line from FX Matt Brewery, Utica, NY. It's not quite a micro, more like a regional - a bit bigger than Victory but smaller than Samuel Adams, though Matt did/does contract brewing for Samuel Adams.

In any case, the beer is available in bottles.
