From: Rainear, Tim TQO | Date: 2004-04-07 02:20:50 UTC

H n H

Can't wait to look for your new boxes.

Your stamps are the best.

 

Per FdL's comment, one thing I continually remind myself when hunting a box is, it's always farther than you think.

I can't tell you how many times I have turned around because I figured I must certainly have passed the landmark, only to eventually turn back around again, retrace my steps past where I turned back before and found the next marker.

I'm not kidding.  This happens almost every time.

 

I somehow managed to find Jennifer's original Jane Austen mystery box today.

I completely missed the first turn, followed what I thought were the rest of the clues only to stumble upon the marker from the second clue.

From there I back tracked, re-connected with the clues and found the box.

Sometimes it's dumb luck.

Have fun!

Raindear
