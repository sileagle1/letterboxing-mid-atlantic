Re: Poison ivy question

From: brendan_hickey13 | Date: 2009-07-13 19:45:15 UTC
Supposedly, the urushiol can endure and retain its capacity to inflict misery for over a year - nasti and durable, what an evil (not in a good way) combination.

However, the reaction time frame is probably accurate - could even be less, depending upon your individual sensitivity, which tends to build - if you had poison ivy on your lower legs, for example, then your lower legs will react more readily to the next exposure. In my own case, I once picked up poison oak on my forearms from carrying fire wood and I am sensitive to poison ivy on those patches, even years later.

I think that other folks had good ideas about thinking of surfaces that get between your fingers (shoe laces, pet fur) and working backward from there.

Ivy Block and Tecnu are good products...

Psychiker
