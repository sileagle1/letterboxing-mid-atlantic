The key is water, water, and more water. Even if you are not thirsty,
you have to keep chugging that water.

Becky and I did a six mile hike on Wednesday to plant a box (it was a
nice hike too). By the time we were done it was 115° in the sun, which
most of the hike was. She got heat exhaustion, I felt fine. When we
compared what water we had left at the end of the hike, she drank far
less then I, I drank nearly a gallon (we took a lot of water with us).
So lots of water, and take a small snack for the trip and you will do
better.
