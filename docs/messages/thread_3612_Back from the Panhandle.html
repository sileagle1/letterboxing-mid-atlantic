Well, we are back from our honeymoon in Texas.

If you didn't know, Schizokeet and I were married on July 11. Somehow
she manages to still be in love with me. It was a great wedding and I
just wanted to thank everyone that helped make it such. We had a ton of
stamps from all over the place, and in the coming weeks I will be
reaching out to the folks that spent much of their time and effort to
help make our wedding something very special.

We had a great time on the honeymoon as well, got a bunch of
letterboxes, and even left a letterbox out there on a nice, hot, six
mile hike. We even passed through St. Louis on Saturday, where there
was some sort of gathering going on, but didn't have time, or the
ambition for that matter, to stop there. We did get a few nice boxes
there however.

So, now I will concentrate on Knoebels, I hope to make it a great event
this year, I have some neat stuff in mind for everyone to have some fun
with. Details will be coming for the gathering very soon.
