Jinxed Box: Chapter 4

From: Suzanne Coe | Date: 2009-07-25 05:28:12 UTC
(In Which Our Heroine Once Again Avoids Being Struck By Lightning & Trees)

So I was out looking for that mystery box again....the one I'm jinxed on. 

The one where the first time I went looking for it, I got caught in the rain & had to dry out with the hot air dryer in the nature center ladies room.

The one I was looking for about a month ago when the sky suddenly turned gray and in a matter of minutes there were sheets of rain coming in sideways and the lightning was crackling and the wind was snapping off trees thirty feet further down the trail.... and when I finally made it to the park office, they didn't have any power because the big tree out front had fallen across the road and taken down the power lines, so I had to wring out my tshirt in the sink and then squelch back to the car sopping wet.

The one I went looking for last week, and....it was a beautiful sunny day! but the only thing I found was a stinky turtle shell.

So today when I got done work & it looked like it was clearing, I figured I'd go take another look. Drove out, put on bug spray & hiking boots....just as I finished tying the laces, the first drop hit. Thought about going out on trail anyway....and decided maybe I would wait in the car until it blew over.

20 minutes later, it still hadn't blown over. In fact it was raining harder. Thunder was starting to rumble. I looked at the power line directly over my car....at the tall trees on each side....Decided this was not the place I wanted to be, so I drove back up the road & out of the park with the thunder getting louder.

As I was heading down the road, I had an inspired thought. Ice cream! I would stop & get ice cream, wait until the storm passed, and then perhaaaaps I could go back.... I pulled into the parking lot, looked at the perhaps 50 feet I'd have to travel to the door, looked at the rain, decided to stay in the car.... and then everything let loose.  Rain, wind, thunder,  lightning, a ball of blue light on the left (telephone pole transformer), hail....a crash & another sudden blue light from behind (tree coming down across the road, taking all the power lines with it, gee where have we heard this before....)  A good 15 minutes of being scared silly, wondering what was wrong with me that I didn't just go home and rest after work, and maybe take up crocheting. =)

So once again, no box. And no ice cream. =(  
I'm jinxed.
But at least I didn't get wet.
And it's supposed to be nice out tomorrow....

Sheba
