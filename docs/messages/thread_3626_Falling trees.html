When I was in VT this past June hiking alone and while stamping in -- there was a loud bang (snap?) and I looked ahead into the woods to see a huge branch fall - or maybe the whole tree? From: Jan | Date: 2009-08-12 18:13:04 UTC
I did two years ago doing some of your boxes at Ridley Creek. I was on the blue trail which made a big loop and I heard the creaking noise of a tree starting to lean as I was just starting the trail. The groaning continued as I worked my way through the several boxes on the loop. Then when I was 3/4 of the way around the loop and sitting on the ground stamping into a box I heard a much louder groan and watched an entire tree come down about 100 feet away from me. I have to admit that it terrified me to think that it could have hit me had I not stopped to stamp into a box.
