Friday Knoebels Treat

From: Nathan Brown | Date: 2009-08-16 23:42:18 UTC
For any folks planning on coming into Knoebels on Friday, September 11,
and camping for the gathering, there is a great treat planned for you.

True Indigo has let me know that she and the rest of the Crayola Posse
will be planting a night series at the nearby Conrad Wieser State
Forest. It will be planted on Friday night only, the clues for it will
be removed Saturday morning before the gathering.

The starting location for the series is the same location as for my Hike
It, Bike It series
(http://www.penncoasters.com/letterboxing/hikebike.htm). Directions and
a map can be found with the clues for this series. The clues for the
night series will be posted on the board at that parking lot.

This should be a lot of fun for those coming out early to camp from some
of the best boxers in the area.
