Re: Re-Inking Colorbox Stamp Pads?

From: Lightnin Bug | Date: 2009-08-19 18:58:02 UTC
Agreed. We have used the liquid ink refill inks that one can order online many times to re-ink our dabber duo pigment inks. I think you can order them from places like scrapbookers warehouse and such. The last time we ordered a bottle of each of our sig stamp colors was back in ealry 2005, so those little bottles last a long, long time.

Try these links:

http://www.blockheadstamps.com/ink_versacolor_reinkers1.html

http://www.stampandgo.com/cartVERSACOLOR.html

http://www.franticstamper.com/versacolor.htm

Years of ink for the price of a single marvy marker. LOL. Not flaming marvy's - some folks love them. I am just too cheap and would rather spend my $ on carving materials and containers.

Cheers,

LB
