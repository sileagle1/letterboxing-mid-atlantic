Re: DE letterboxing article...guerilla tactics

From: hexacat | Date: 2004-09-10 16:17:53 UTC
i can understand your frustration. more often than not people are
misquoted or misinterpreted all together. i too spent almost an hour
talking to Lauren (the reporter) and ultimately she decides what part
of the interview is used.
anywho, i guess it could be worse.

FYI. i know the box in Lums Pond she refers to and it was planted
with permission from the park rangers. the boxer wrote a letter to
the parks dept. detailing the hobby of letterboxing and where the box
will be planted (as to conform with their conservancy issues) then if
that was not enough to convince them the boxer physically took the
ranger out on the trail to show them where and how it will be planted.
the latest news is that particular box was vandalized last week!
presonally, i'm fine with guerilla (gorilla?) tactics it saves me the
headache.
