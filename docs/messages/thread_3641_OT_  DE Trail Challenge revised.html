OT: DE Trail Challenge revised

From: Heather Siple | Date: 2009-08-28 23:22:17 UTC
Anyone interested in completing the DE state parks trail challenge should take note that the program has been revised.  We started the challenge this week and found that while the parks are still stocked with the old cards, there are new cards and they list different trails.  The challenge now has 3 levels - 5, 10, or 15 trails - which is nice for those of us who have short-legged hikers along or those who really don't want to trek from one end of the state to the other multiple times.  However, not all the trails on the old card are still part of the challenge.  The new card is online and the program information can be found at :
http://www.destateparks.com/trail-challenge/
 
Also, New Castle County, DE has devised its own trail challenge.  There are no markers to find, just an honor system of logging when you walked a trail at this or that park on the list.  Cards can be found at the NCCo public libraries.  All of the county cards must be completed by December.
 
Mother Duck
