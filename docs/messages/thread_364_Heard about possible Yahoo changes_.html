From: Silent Doug | Date: 2004-09-09 16:55:05 UTC
I smiled while reading all your stats, Poison Ivy -- the discussion of
Indigo Vulture's F100 milestone also had me looking at my PFX count and
dates, too. And I started doing some quick calculations of my own, too:

I reached F500 this past labor day, which was 27.25 calendar months after
finding my first letterbox. That means that I've found 18.3 letterboxes
each month on average since I started.

I've planted 38, but I've also adopted 19 more, including rescuing a couple
of boxes from the letterbox basement and restoring a couple that had been
missing or damaged beyond use. We've also made a couple of attempts at
restoring some other abandoned boxes, which I figure is good for our P
karma, as well, so I think I'm well within in your range of 1 P per 10 F,
as well. As soon as Jennifer reaches F300 (only 7 more to go!), then we're
going to focus on planting more. (Of course, that also depends on her
getting over her, um, *obsession* with catching up to my F count, though
I'm not sure how that works since every time we go boxing together my F
count also goes up....)

:-)

The thing is, all numbers aside, the biggest thrill I get from boxing still
comes from a great hike, like restoring the Pinnacle and Pulpit Rock boxes,
or from the challenge of figuring out clues like the Nolde Forest series --
not from racking up a big F count.
