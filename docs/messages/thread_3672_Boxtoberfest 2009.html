From: Sara Negron | Date: 2009-10-19 12:30:23 UTC
I've been to several events, and as far as content, I think this was my favorite :)
 
The stamps were beautiful, boxes plentiful and the PirateTurtles, who were there with us, won a raffle and were VERY excited.
 
Was it raining? Celtic Cat and Lotus, thank you so much for providing so much to do that I didn't even realize it was cold or raining :)
 
Those night boxes were the coolest thing, as we'd never done that before. Until Saturday night, night boxing to me was wandering the woods in the dark, shining a flashlight (if i had one) on a piece of paper while I dodge smashing into trees, and while muttering "it was dark this early last time... thought I had plenty of time for this last one..." But Saturday night boxing took on a whole new meaning, and i love it.
 
Hope to see this event up again next year!
