Boxtoberfest 2009 food donation update

From: marybeth_mc | Date: 2009-10-21 00:41:35 UTC
A big thank you to everyone who attended the Boxtoberfest 2009 event, and who brought food to donated for our little food drive. All food items were delivered to the New Britain Baptist Church Food Larder on Monday morning. The Food Larder Volunteers were very grateful for the donations that were made.

When Wednesday Bowler and I arrived at the church there were several people standing in line, in the cold, waiting to be admitted to select their food items. I have to admit it made me a bit sad, and I did tear up some as I thought about how fortunate and grateful I am that we were able to make this food donation on behalf of the LBMA group.

Here is a link to the New Britain Baptist Church Food Larder:
http://www.newbritainbaptistchurch.org/food/index.html

Photos of the event have been posted by Handstwo, Travelin' Shutterbug and myself on the AQ Boxtoberfest 2009 event page, if you care to view them.

Thanks again,
Celtic Cat
