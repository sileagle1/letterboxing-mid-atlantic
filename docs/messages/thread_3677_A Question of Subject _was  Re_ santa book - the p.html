I don't see how this is AQ's fault! Just because it has bells and whistles such as LTC's, virtuals, and such, doesn't give anyone the indication that AQ leans towards the quick find. What I think has happened is when someone plants a drive-by, there is always a copy cat. Someone else has to plant a drive-by box too.
 
If I may use my state of New Hampshire as an example. I just planted a box on top of a mountain reaching 3,475 feet. You had better believe I got some flack for that. I gaurantee that there will be very little finds for that box.
 
It would seem that the more people that begin letterboxing, the more likely they are to wanna find quick boxes. A website is not going to encourage this, one way or the other. I have my share of drive-by boxes and no websites swayed me in that direction. It was inevidable that letterboxing was gonna grow and change some. When it did, no one was forcing you to come along. It was up to you to come along for the ride.
 
And I couldn't help myself but, for someone that doesn't care for the bells and whistles on AQ, you certainly found the block for your finds in your logbook. What are you hiding? Drive-bys? And look at all those blue-diamond plants! Must make you proud!
