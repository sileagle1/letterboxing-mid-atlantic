Lovely day for boxing

From: mizscarlet731 | Date: 2009-12-13 15:36:45 UTC
I have been know to box in the freezing rain, but not today. Next boxing trip will be this thurs. if weather is OK. In fact every thurs I have a standing hike with some gals from my spin class. We do a loop of my two favorite mountains. But alas no boxing. This week no one showed up but me. So I thought great I can snag that little mystery box I know is up here. No clues? No problem, or so I thought. Got to area, sooo much SPOR! Many great places to hide a box. Came up empty. Shared story with one of my hiking and spin buddies who said she would be interested in finding a box or two. So next week just might find this box. Will even bring clues!
MizS
