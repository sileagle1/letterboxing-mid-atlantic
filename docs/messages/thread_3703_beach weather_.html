Beach weather for Polar Bears! Yeah, I've made the Polar Bear Plunge with snow on the beach, so snow and sand naturally go together. To quote another bear, "The more it snows, the more it goes on snowing" - nothing that profound but I think that Winnie the Pooh is a Taoist.

Naturally, I will carve a stamp and give it to donors.
