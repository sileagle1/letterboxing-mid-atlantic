Identified my first casualty

From: R. Copeland | Date: 2010-02-23 17:44:12 UTC
Snowmageddon has claimed its first victim among my boxes--Pencil Pusher, a 2003 plant by La Chola that I've been looking after for a few years. It was located in a holly tree, which, like most evergreens around here, didn't fare too well in the blizzards. The good news is that it was still there, and I was able to reach in and nab it before the tree cleanup crews got here.

If you've got boxes associated with evergreens, especially in more cultivated areas, it might be a good idea to check on 'em when you can.
