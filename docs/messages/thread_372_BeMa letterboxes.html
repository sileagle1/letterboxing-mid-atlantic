Just a quick note about BeMa's letterboxes.

After multiple attempts to reach BeMa by myself and other
letterboxers, I'm adopting these boxes. If BeMa comes back for them,
great! If not, I want to keep them alive. All of them are fun and
it would be a shame to lose them.

BeMa will continue to be listed as the originator.

I've been to all of the letterbox sites in August, and 5 of them are
still standing, although some need work. "Oh Deer!" is in sad shape,
but the park is spectacular and worth the $4 to get in.

These have all been listed as MIA, for now:

Helping Hands
Curtain Up!
The Mole

In keeping with the spirit of the originals, does anyone have images
of these so that I could roughly replace the stamps? If so, please
contact me offlist so I can get photocopies or electronic images.
