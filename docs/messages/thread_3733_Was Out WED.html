Was Out WED

From: sewsowbizzy | Date: 2010-03-20 12:51:41 UTC
with Amanda & Ryan. We did Leaping the Dragon -- albeit the 2nd box required reverse hiking as the water was too high to get across. Both were great carves! The area is still brown and the water was moving swiftly.

After all those hills and rocks I needed a 'drive-by' and we went for the Water Wheel mystery . . . nuff said!

The actual goal was River Bend and we sought those starting at the Visitor Center Prking Lot -

Puddle Duck - was a find.

Treasure Map had a notable location - but alas it was missing. Looking downhill in case it had 'melted' away -- no luck. MIA

Muskrat Ramble II -- way wet but Ryan traversed while Amanda and I drove over to the Nature Center and came in the back way - was a find.

By now I was exhausted and those from Seattle had an early flight so we called it a day. 5 for 6 was a great day!

ssb
