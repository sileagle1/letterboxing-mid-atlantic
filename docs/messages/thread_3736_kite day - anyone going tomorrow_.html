I hope those who made it had a great time! I went to help out at the Kite Doctor tent around 2:00 and then when 4:00 was getting close figured I had better go get my stuff. I didn't take time to look at the log, just grabbed the box and packed it up. It was only later when another member mentioned helping someone who didn't see the "This is a clue" box under the bear in a chair that I looked at the log. I was so psyched!

I had explained Letter Boxing to several members (one who asked me to carve her a stamp) but unfortunately neither I or any of them was at the Wings Over Washington Kite Club site when the boxers came by. Hope it wasn't too awful. It was too funny listening to people all day see the box hidden in plain site and not have a clue what the "clue" was all about. Thanks so much, I wondered if I had been really dumb over this.
