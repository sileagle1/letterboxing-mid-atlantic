Pencil Pusher is back, in a slightly modified location that survived Snowmageddon.

Those considering the Columbia Petroglyphs series might want to hold off for a while. When construction is complete (probably some time in 2011) the boxes will be replanted in modified locations. Until then, only boxes 4 and 6 of this series are in place.
