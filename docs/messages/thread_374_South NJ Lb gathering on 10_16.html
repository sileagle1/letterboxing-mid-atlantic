i have been on the sending end as well as the receiving end of
evites. they are quite simple to send and you do not have to register
on the site to receive one. i belong to a local running club and have
sent evites to our members (400+) w/o any problems. of course i am
the membership director so i have access to everyone's email addy.

members email address or send post a message requesting such.
