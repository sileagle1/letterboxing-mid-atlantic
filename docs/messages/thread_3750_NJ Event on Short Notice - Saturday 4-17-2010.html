NJ Event on Short Notice - Saturday 4-17-2010

From: ed@bluarcher.com | Date: 2010-04-14 10:38:53 UTC

HikerGuyEd and Smiling Kate found the End of the Rainbow and were married on 3-17-2010!

Please help us celebrate our wedding and St Patty's Day by stamping in, collecting a special carving, having a bite and saying Hello!

We are having an Open House party and if you are in the area from 1:00pm to 11:00pm, please stop it whenever you like, stay as long as you wish, come just for the stamp!

 

There will be food and drink for all ages. Please don't bring gifts, just you and your stamps!

 

There are many letterboxes and geocaches in the area, so if you’re going to be in the area, please stop in!!

DIRECTIONS:

From 287 South:

287 South
Exit 10
As you get off the exit, follow to first light, turn Right onto Cedar Grove Lane
You will go 3 or 4 lights, there will be a gas station on the left with 2 lanes bearing to the left and 1 lane going straight, you want to go left onto 514 at that light
At the next light turn Right onto South Middlebush
Go a couple miles to the 2nd light, turn Left onto Claremont
Go about a half mile and turn Right onto Society Hill, at the end turn Left onto Stewart
At the 3rd road on the Right turn onto Veros
Look for my RAV4 on the right and note the License Plate for my personal traveler.
Continue past and turn left at the end, the club house will be on the left.

From Route 1:

In Kendall Park / South Brunswick ,
Henderson Road (610) West (North?)
At Route 27 ( Lincoln Highway ), there is a Quick Check on the right.
Go through light onto Stewart Avenue
Turn Left at the 2nd road onto Veros Lane
Look for my RAV4 on the right and note the License Plate for my personal traveler.
Continue past and turn left at the end, the club house will be on the left.
