From: Keystone | Date: 2010-05-25 10:19:59 UTC
Several of these questions were actually answered. i saw every episode, but have no idea what season they were in.
- The numbers were the numbers assigned to the candidates who survived.
- They did show the significance of all the people on the island.
- They never explained the baby problem. My guess would be that it had something to do with the electromagnetic properties.
- The reason Walt didn't come back is simple, he grew too much. They actually discussed it on the Jimmy Kimmel Live episode that followed. He made it back to the US and was living with his grandmother.
- The Black Rock was the slave ship that brought Richard to the island. I don't think they ever said why it was loaded with dynamite.
- Charles Widmore was the leader of the others who was kicked off the Island when Ben took over.

As for the rest of it, I have heard that the writers themselves couldn't figure out how to explain some of their mysteries in a congruent fashion. That is why there are some things that never got settled. Some of the characters were probably just flavor and "red shirts" to be sacrificed to plot development. Jack evidentally wasn't supposed to survive the pilot, so a lot of things evolved over the course of the show.
