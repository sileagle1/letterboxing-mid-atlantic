From: Lightnin Bug | Date: 2004-09-13 11:59:39 UTC
Team Lightnin' Bug planted another one in SW Lehigh County [we call
it our own little Connecticut! ;-) ], Kalmbach Park in Macungie.

We also carved a couple of stamps - one for the Emmaus Zuflucht Haus
(Shelter House), Tom's Hobo Village and a stamp for Mrs. Lightnin'
Bug, Called "Fly Killer" - She is deadly with the swatter!

Oh, and we also did a lot of stamping of the cards for the Great HH
Race. What that entailed was taking each of the stamps we received
thus far and stamping them onto index cards for everybody. 6/10 so
far, with 2 pre-done by a very nice mystery person.

It sill soon be time to work on Irene's Battle of da Bands - I'm
listening to one of the CD's now and somemore Lehigh County
oddities. These will actuallyt be (gasp!) further than 7 miles from
my house!

Lightnin' Bug
