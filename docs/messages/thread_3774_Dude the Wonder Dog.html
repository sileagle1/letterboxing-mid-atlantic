Dude the Wonder Dog

From: Irene | Date: 2010-06-11 13:15:09 UTC
Hello to old friends, it's be awhile since I've posted but life has been happening and my old boys arthiritis made it tough for him to hike anymore. Today I am posting to let you know that last night Dude the Wonder Dog was put to rest. He was 19 and hadn't been feeling that great the last month or so. My son called me at work to tell me to come home. I took him to the vet and they said it was time to let him go. I stayed with my boy while he went to sleep and they let me sit with him for a long time afterwards. For those of you who didn't know him, Dude was the best damn dog in the whole wide world and the whole reason I got into letterboxing. As his health declined we didn't go out too much. Besides letterboxing Dude was an avid camper, caver and a huge fan of McDonald's french fries. He and I hiked two of the highest paeks in West Virgina togther and have had so many adventures that I will cherish forever. To those of you who DID know him I thank you from the bottom of my heart for your kindess to him. To everyone who welcomed him into your home, you cars, your tents, who always asked how he was doing if I was at an event he couldn't attend. I thank you and Dude thanks you. Dude was 19 and my best friend in the entire world and I am just heartbroken. But I am so grateful we found each other and had so many great adventures together. This is the end of Hikers & Hounds. I will plant our team stamp somewhere but without Dude there is no Hikers & Hounds. I thank you and miss you all. Now back to your regularly scheduled programming...
