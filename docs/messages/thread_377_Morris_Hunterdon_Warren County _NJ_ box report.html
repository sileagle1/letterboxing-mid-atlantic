In my attempt to catch up to Silent Doug (ha!), on 9/11 I found...

Merrill Creek
Charmed for Sure
Ken Lockwood
Fisherman's Point

There were a few issues with boxes being rehidden and/or repackaged
well (a couple of the boxes were quite wet), but I cleaned them up as
best I could and rebagged where needed. Merrill Creek was beautiful
with the sun shining wonderfully on the reservoir.

Ken Lockwood was beautiful; my advice is to walk it and not try to
drive by. The drive had me gripping the wheel, and I completely
missed the tretsle. That's right, I drove under a trestle and didn't
notice. I got out and walked back when I got to the end and realized
what I had done (Silent Doug can attest that I was a little, um,
annoyed with myself), and it was much more enjoyable that way, anyway.

On 9/12, Silent Doug and I found:

Jockey Hollow
Physiography 102
Huts

The Huts stamp is awesome, really!
