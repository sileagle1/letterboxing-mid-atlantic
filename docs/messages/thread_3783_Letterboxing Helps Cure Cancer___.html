Letterboxing Helps Cure Cancer!!!

From: FishMan | Date: 2010-07-14 17:17:56 UTC
. . . and improves the lives of those dealing w/ this disease.

At least it CAN if you visit my Philly LiveStrong 2010 letterbox! For the 3rd year in a row, I'm riding in the Philly LiveStrong ride to benefit the Lance Armstrong Foundation. As part of my fundraising efforts, I have carved and planted 2 letterboxes in eastern PA. The stipulation is that in order to get the clues, you must make a small donation to my fundrasing page. You can donate as little as $5 online. Of course more is welcome. :) We're in the last month of fundraising and I'm trying to make a push now. All the details and links are at both Atlasquest.com and letterboxing.org. Click the clue link for my Philly Livestrong 2010 letterbox. And the carves aren't too shabby either. ;)

I'm sure that each and every one of us has be affected directly or indirectly by this awful disease. Please join me in helping to find a cure and assist those currently fighting the battle against cancer!

Thank you!

Pete Mandeville
(Fishman)
