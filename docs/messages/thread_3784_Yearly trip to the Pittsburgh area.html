Sorry I did not get back to you sooner.

The Brewery tour is awesome, a must do. Try and catch a weekday tour,
even better, the early tour, as it is not as crowded and they are in
full operation so you get to see everything at work.

I, sadly, cannot suggest any boxes, as when we were out there a few
weeks ago we were busy doing other things and found not Western PA boxes.

I can, however, suggest amusement parks, as we visited all of them :-)

Idlewild and Kennywood are fantastic parks, well run with great rides.
I would also recommend Conneaut Lake Park, which is nearest to Slippery
Rock. The park is run down and seen better days, but they are trying to
rebuild it as best they can. It has a few classic rides and a staff
that really cares about the park, along with a great bar :-)
