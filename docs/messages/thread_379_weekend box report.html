From: Lightnin Bug | Date: 2004-09-13 16:58:36 UTC
Dick Blick in South Mall Allentown (which you can see from the
Hostel) used to carry the little cubes. Now, you can find them at:

http://store.scrapbooking-warehouse.com/rubber-stamping-ink-pads.html

They are called "Versacolor" by Tsukineko

Blick used to sell them for about $2 a pop, but the website is $1.65
per. Since Blick is kind of running out (I wonder why? :-P ), I've
decided to go there. I actually got the site from She Who Plants in
Nashville, who had a PLB with the absolute coolest color stamp pad in
it. So, I bugged her for the place and found my favorite little
plastic cuboids!

Glad you like the hike and the HH's. I guess somebody got the
Blivet. Hope you dropped some in as well (the Hostel must remain
stocked!).

Lightnin' Bug

weekend box report

From: divinelotus73 | Date: 2006-04-16 11:50:00 UTC
Hi All--
So after two weekends of no boxing, I was beginning to develop the
shakes. Looking at tupperware in the fridge and starting to cry- I
thought about joining LMBAA to get some help, doing a twelve-step
program to seek help, but alas- i broke down and went boxing and now
everything is better!!

We were out in Reading, and we had some Nolde Forrest clues in my car
and decided to make a pit-stop, 7 hours, 12 1/2 miles later- we were
finished without finding all of the boxes there! What a beautiful
park, excellent hiking and lovely boxes. It is a real gem!!

Well the day is early, and Mr. Lotus has to work, so I guess I should
get my gear and find some local boxes that I have been working on--

Happy Respective holidays to everyone!!

Lotus
