From: Mike Brooks | Date: 2004-04-08 06:25:44 UTC
I hope it's "legal". I can't carve, period. Tried it, cut my finger more
than any hobby is worth. No reason to try again.

My signature stamp is store bought. The "stamps" in some of my boxes are
labels with photos or other artwork (usually relevant to the area) on them.
That's as close to art as I'm ever going to get. The only other option is
for me to plant other people's stamps, and I've been lucky enough to get to
do that a couple times. Actually, I could plant a store bought stamp too,
but I prefer to put an original creation of some sort out there.

I see nothing wrong with getting started by just signing in the log book,
with no obligation to return with a stamp. Just get out there and have fun.

Mike
