Lucky save

From: brendan_hickey13 | Date: 2010-10-14 16:24:14 UTC
Syner and I were in Lewes on 9/26 and hoped to check our Ft. Miles Tower box - hadn't been there in awhile - good for us that the rain kept us away.

Some folks found that box on 9/26 and, it seems, left it the open. I can't figure out how to include the picture here but maybe I will post it in the photos section.

At the time, we did not know that I would be back on 10/7. If we had checked the box on 9/26 I would not have looked when I came back, and if we had checked before the last finders then that box would probably have sat in the open for awhile.

When you find a box, please put it back where the clue says that it should be or, if you can't, then hide it safely nearby and tell the placer what happened.

Psychiker
