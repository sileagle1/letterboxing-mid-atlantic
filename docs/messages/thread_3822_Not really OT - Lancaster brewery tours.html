Re: Not really OT - Lancaster brewery tours

From: brendan_hickey13 | Date: 2010-11-14 23:06:16 UTC
Evilmommy has a box very close to Stoudt's - the only box that we tried to get - good box to seek at this time of year.

Stoudt's makes really good beer. I love the Fat Dog oatmeal stout - with coffee notes in the beer, it's like breakfast in a bottle.

As for the tour, well, nothing wrong with breaking a 500 year-old German food purity law, the Reinheitsgebot, unless you keep yapping about how the brewery still obeys the law. Oatmeal would be verboten under the Reinheitsgebot.

Seems like the Stoudts are merchants who happen to make beer, rather than beer geex who sell what they can't drink - doesn't make them wrong but it leads to stuff like the cheesy simulated German village of shops out back.

On the other hand, if we were in Lancaster then I'd probably be a regular at Lancaster Brewing...
