ZODIAC TIMELINE

From: Derek Spendlove | Date: 2010-12-01 11:00:54 UTC
May 19, 2009 - Janet Folly of Fairfax County calls the police to report her friend Veronica Cabot, never returned from her daily walk. Later that day police find clothing and a shoe belonging to the missing Ms Cabot on a nearby trail.

May 15, 2009 - Story is leaked by the media, and is feared tied to previous murders at Bull Run, and Wolf Rock, locations of long hikes. Locals in the DC region fear that a serial killer in now lurking in the suburbs.

May 19-22, 2009 - Locally hired detectives locate more evidence, leading to the body of Veronica Ann Cabot.

June 4, 2009 - Veronica Cabot's sister, Vanessa is reported missing...no new info at this time.

June 6, 2009 - Vanessa is found at a friend's house. After interviewing her, there is no connection.

July 4, 2009 - Detective Tagaki never shows up to HQ for work. His police car is found running unattended the same morning.

July 6, 2009 - Detective Tagaki is presumed dead after police receive a letter confirming certain things about his body. We have yet to learn more, but we believe he is murdered.

August 28, 2009 - We have names on the missing counselor and camper in Great Falls. Ms. Jeanie Johnson, 47 and Calvin Hampton, 14. They had strayed a little of trail to the river for a lesson in survival swimming techniques in the Potomac. We are working the case. Evidence found leads to a connection in the Zodiac cases.

October 25, 2009 - Letter to police contains cryptic message, and two pictures. Pictures leaked to public. Days later the photos and letter are determined to be fake and unrelated.

February 14, 2010 - Mr Park goes missing after leaving his wife and kids for a bike ride. Missing person poster is placed all around the county.

May 7, 2010 - Another note received by police. Loaded to our secure server. Note is infact from Zodiac. http://lh3.ggpht.com/_bskr121NusI/TO-0JkrJPsI/AAAAAAAAABY/s2AxdEGmEKI/zodiaccipher.jpg

July 4, 2010 - Pair of bicyclist gloves found placed in a clearing near where Mr. Park was last seen. Traces of Mr. Park's blood found on them, as well as cut marks in the palms. Cuts are typical of trying to defend an attack. Gloves appeared to be put there in hopes of being found. http://lh3.ggpht.com/_bskr121NusI/TO_NXFPbiwI/AAAAAAAAABo/gZR55RVES7c/glove.jpg

November 25, 2010 - Thanksgiving Day: call is received by police. Nothing is said except, 'Be joyful always; pray continually; give thanks in all circumstances, for this is God's will for you and Mr Park. Same morning Det. Spendlove finds a note on his cruiser. http://lh4.ggpht.com/_bskr121NusI/TO5piL-x9BI/AAAAAAAAAA8/wPTMxmER8yA/s512/ThanksgivingZodiacNote.jpg On the back is the handwritten phrase: Interficio Interfeci Interfectum


Now, I am off to see Ms Park this morning to see if she has one of the old missing posters of her husband. It contained the most recent picture of him. I will get that to you. It might be all we need to get the search party out there.

Spendlove
