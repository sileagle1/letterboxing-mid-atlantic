Mid-Atlantic Ice Breaker

From: Nathan Brown | Date: 2011-01-01 17:22:21 UTC
Well, it is just two weeks away, and I figure with the holidays pretty
well behind us, it is time to look forward to the return of this gathering.

This year it will be set in the sprawling suburbia of Sterling, VA. We
will start out at Joe's Pizzeria, in the heart of Sterling Park.
Afterwards we will move on to our place for some more food, drinks,
refreshment, a game of pool maybe, a fire, some indoor boxes, and some
night boxing outside. I have some wonderful Miz Scarlet stamps that
have seen very little use, so they should be great fun to hunt in our
ivy covered backyard.

It is also close to Schizokeet's birthday, so you can expect some cake
too :-)

So we invite all to come out to Sterling on the 15th, the fun starts at
5 PM, giving you plenty of time to explore local parks and boxes as
well. If you are traveling far we do have a place for you to stay if
you would like as well. Hope to see some new faces as well as some old
friends.
