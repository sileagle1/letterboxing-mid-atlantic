Checking my boxes on the Mall

From: Nathan Brown | Date: 2011-01-30 02:19:30 UTC
I had about an hour yesterday afternoon to roam the Mall and check on my
boxes.

Much to my amazement, "America's First Superhighway" still remains in
place, in a very populated location. It has proven to be a surprisingly
successful urban hide, as I was worried about the location. It has not
had a finder since May, which also surprises me considering the number
visitors we get asking for boxes along the Mall.

Also I thought I would look one more time for my missing box
"Naturally" The location was not busy so I could take my time and
really give a good search. Guess third time is a charm as it was
there. It was in the wrong location, but still alive and well. I
placed it back in the correct location so it should be back in business.

My website has been down for some time now, and now my desktop has taken
a dive (might be time for a new machine). All of my boxes for right now
should be considered word of mouth. Just email me and I will send you
the clues. Also, you can find clues right here in the files section of
the list.

Cyclonic
