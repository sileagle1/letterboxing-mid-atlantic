In Slower Lower for the Polar Bear Plunge, visited the Bethany Blues restaurant that is in Lewes - incredible place for BBQ.

We discovered that it's also a GREAT bourbon bar. You can get a 3 x 1 oz flight of samples, one per visit, 14 flights in all. Sample all 14 and get a t-shirt, bottle of BBQ sauce, and get your picture on the wall.

It's good to have goals.

We met the owner at the bar and he treated us to a round of Rittenhouse Rye 23 Year-Old - wow - amazing stuff. Kevin is cool.

I can't believe that I am old enough to have done any voluntary act for 14 consecutive years.
