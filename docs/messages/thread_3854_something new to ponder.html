Subject: Letterbox Located inside Petersburg National Battlefield
Author: CreekRanger       
Date: Mar 3, 2011 3:28 PM
The Park not interested in writing a ticket to the person(s) responsible for the box
placement, just to have them claim it and remove it from the listing. Since a good deal of time / effort was spent setting this up, I would like to see it re-used in the proper manner.
It is a good outdoor hobby but current National Park Policy Prevents this type of activity at this time w/o a special use permit.

I will leave it at our visitor center for pickup. Please leave us a message at: PETE_Superintendent@...

Thank you

The Ranger.
