Re: [lbma] trees in bloom

From: Suzanne Coe | Date: 2011-04-14 17:35:39 UTC
Well, it was a gorgeous day for the park! The blue of the sky, the white of the statues, the pink of the cherry blossoms.... Just a couple of folks walking dogs, so I sat down on the stone steps of the gazebo, reached casually under... there it was. Lovely stamp, clever hiding place. Stamped in, made sure everything was resealed, checked to make sure the dog walkers were headed away from me, went to push the box back into its hiding spot.... and suddenly I couldn't feel the edge of the container anymore--and I heard a "thunk."

Uh oh, I thought. That's bad.

Turns out the crevice the box was wedged into was actually a ledge about the width of a sandwich-sized Lock n' Lock. On the other side, a deep dark hole. How deep, I don't know, but when I put my hand in & felt around, I didn't touch bottom...or the box. So I am really, really sorry, you guys (especially Renee!) but I think it's gone. :( But it was a lovely box....

Sheba
