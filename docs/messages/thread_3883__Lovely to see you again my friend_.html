"Lovely to see you again my friend"

From: Dancing Bat | Date: 2011-04-27 13:46:16 UTC
to quote one of my favorite Moody Blues songs. (I do listen to something other than the Dead occasionally). Grateful Dawg, Tiny Terror and I had a really nice day on Monday catching up with some old friends..of mine at least. This was the first time the dogs met them. We caught up with the Pepe's and Lightnin' Bug during the day in Allentown. It was so good to see everyone, I was so happy to be able to take the day off and catch up with them. Then Miz Scarlet came up later in the day and eventually we all meet at a local pub for dinner where we were joined by several other area boxers and everyone seemed to have a great time. Many thanks to The Bug for taking time off to come out and lead the charge. to everyone else who showed up to provide a wamr PA welcome to the Pepes, and to Mark and Sue for just be themselves. I had a wonderful day, and so did the "kids"!
 
Dancing Bat, Grateful Dawg and the Tiny Terror.
