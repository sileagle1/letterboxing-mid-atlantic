North by NorthWest

From: sowbiz | Date: 2011-07-02 17:16:11 UTC
That would be ALASKA - where sewsewBZ TRVLN is headed for another PineCone Adventure. Our own LBMA Celtic Cat will be cruising along too!! It begins Monday July 4th when flying against time to Fairbanks in the land of the Midnite Sun -- there will not be any dark available for fireworks!! Even the 3hr layover in Denver is in late afternoon - but there will be a mini meet with Doublesaj & Old Blue (also on the adventure) and 11 others coming out to meet us. Actually I think they are coming because Amanda posted she is on layover there also and will try to come . . . bringing her special in uniform in airport stamp!!

With so many boxes to find (unlike back in 2007) and the 33 I carved this will be a very exciting trip - not to mention the Denali Tundra Tour and then the inland passage cruise to Vancouver BC.

Later Celtic Cat, Gamecock, DeniseRows and I will venture down to Kenmore WA for the Harry Potter event on Jul 16. Better brush up on my HP Lingo or I'll never understand the clues!!

Then there will be Sun-Thu with DeniseRows and I based out of Centralia WA tearing up the countryside . . . and I'll be getting my $10 LIFETIME National Parks Pass - and planting a box to celebrate.

With DR headed to CA I'll return rental car to Vancouver and train back south to a Mount Vernon WA B&B and another mini-meet day of boxing with locals.

On to Portland OR for a few days with Storm Rider taking me out and then my sister and I dining at any number of box locations!! Italian YUM Grilled Cheese YUM YUM French Pastry YUM YUM YUM

Now need to figure out how to carry the 10LBs of Clues and Google Maps!!

ssBZ TRVLN
