Re: [lbma] Re: Black Squirrel local sighting!

From: R | Date: 2011-08-06 16:23:51 UTC
Wow, I never knew that black squirrels were rare for some people. They're the most popular colour in my area (north of Lake Ontario). On any given day I will have 3 varieties of squirrels at my bird feeders - grey, black and red, including a black squirrel with a white-tipped tail. In Toronto there's a park (Trinity-Bellwoods) with white squirrels. My sister has had the good fortune to see a few of those. Here's a video of one of the Trinity-Bellwoods Park residents: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=E6YAyiQNWxo and here's and example of a black squirrel with a white tail http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bWmZQqJIgIA

Lone R
