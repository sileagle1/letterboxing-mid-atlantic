Charles Town Heritage Quest

From: Becki Zaglifa | Date: 2011-08-06 02:34:05 UTC
I am working with the Planning committee of the Charles Town Heritage Festival to put together a fun event that will appeal to letterboxes and Muggles alike!

All of the stamps included in this event will be hand carved by local letterboxes. If you would like to carve a stamp for this event, please contact me!

There is also an after-quest event planned. Join us! Event information can be found on AQ's Event listings.

Do you like discovering new things?  Do you fancy yourself clever?  Are you good at unraveling a mystery?  Then take a Quest, a journey into the past during Charles Town Heritage Days September 17, 2011.  We challenge the young, the old, the timid, and the bold to a search for history.  Participants will receive a Passport to their very own adventure.  In it will be questions and clues that will lead you to local businesses and different locations in Charles Town where hidden treasure awaits.  Once found, stamp your passport and claim your prize at the end of the Quest.  All participants will receive a ribbon and those who complete the Quest will receive the ulimate prize--Excitement to last a lifetime  Join as a family, a team, or an individual but please join us for this ultimate journey through time.  The Quest begins at 10:30 a.m. on September 17, 2011, and ends at 3 p.m.  For more information please
contact HeritageQuest2011@....
