You may want to consider Cunningham Falls State Park in Thurmont, MD.

The park has tons of hiking trails and is next to Catoctin Mountain Park
which has tons more. Also, obviously, it has a nice waterfall and lake
with swimming and boating.

Thurmont is halfway between Frederick and Gettysburg on Route 15, so you
are close to tons of historic attractions as well as shopping and
restaurants if you don't want to eat in camp the whole time. There are
a few boxes in the park, one that I know of at Catoctin, and a bunch
more in the area by various planters.
