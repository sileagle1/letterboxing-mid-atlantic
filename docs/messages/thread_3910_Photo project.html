Hi all,

 

I'm working on a photo project to improve my photography skills.  I'd like to do a gallery of letterboxers based on their trail names or a particularly interesting trail story.  It can be as straightforward as Sewsowbizzy working on a quilt or as offbeat as Gargoyle Bob perched on the side of a deck next to a gargoyle.
