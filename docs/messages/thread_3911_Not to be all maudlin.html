Not to be all maudlin

From: Otis' Friends | Date: 2011-08-16 15:40:22 UTC
I am retiring, or at a minimum cutting back dramatically.  Unlike politicians caught in a scandal, I really am doing this to spend more time with my kids, and to pursue new goals.

One of them is to get rid of the pile of crap I have accumulated over the years (as seen on A&E's "Hoarders").  This includes several big binders of clues, some of which I think I will just get rid of because I don't see myself going letterboxing in those places anytime soon, and don't want to go to the trouble and expense of moving them out of storage.  But I want them to go to good homes where they might be used.  Please let me know if you would be interested. (I also saved paper clues for the boxes we found, which I might just pitch.)  I may not get back to you immediately because I've removed this e-mail account from my phone.  However, I will likely be interested in delivering these things during August.

I think I'll keep all the maps, though.  Never know when those will come in handy.  Of course, as I say to my son from time to time, a map and compass will help you find your way, but won't tell you where you want to go.
