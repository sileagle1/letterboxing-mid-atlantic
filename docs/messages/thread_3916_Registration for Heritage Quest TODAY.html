Registration for Heritage Quest TODAY

From: Becki Zaglifa | Date: 2011-08-27 11:10:48 UTC
If you or anyone you know is interested in registration or information about the Charles Town Heritage Quest, stop by the farmers market this morning! Four if by Sea and myself will have a table with registration forms and information. We will also have our signature stamps ready to exchange!

Heritage Quest is a Downtown Scavenger hunt that is open to anyone but should be appealing to letterboxers! It will include Trivia type questions, stamps, scavenger hunt gathering and fun!

The Heritage Quest is being planned by letterboxers, but is being hosted by the city of Charles Town Heritage Festival and will be held on September 17 from 10:30-2:00.

Also, there is a letterboxer meet and greet after the Quest posted on AQ.


We hope to see you there!
