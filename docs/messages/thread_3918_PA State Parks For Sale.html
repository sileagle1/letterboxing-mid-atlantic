PA State Parks For Sale

From: nattybumppolbna | Date: 2011-08-31 12:25:58 UTC
Here is how it works:

1. The current administration sells our Parks to companies who will "manage" them.
2. Just coincidentally, those companies will make large political contributions to that same administration.
3. The companies will operate them as a profit-making enterprise. We will have to pay to visit the parks that our parents built and our tax dollars paid for.
4. A few years down the line, the parks closer to population centers will start losing money. Apparently you just can't make 20% quarterly profit on hiking and fishing. And the companies will get permission to turn the park into luxury home developments and golf courses.
5. A few years down the line, the parks farther away from population centers will start losing money. Apparently you just can't make 20% quarterly profit on hiking and fishing. Those will slowly be opened to logging and mining and drilling.

Protect our State Parks. Tell your Legislators this is a Pass/Fail vote for them.
