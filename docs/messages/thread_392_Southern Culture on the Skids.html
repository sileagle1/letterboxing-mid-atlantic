When I was in college the technical director played Flood in the
scene shop over and over again . Particle Man and Istanbul were
made into Animaniacs bits. HELLO NURSE! Wow, I forgot how much I
loved the Animaniacs!

I still listen to Flood on a regular basis. The words are so
bizarre I laugh as I sing them.

A woman came up to me and said I'd like to poison your mind with
wrong ideas that appeal to you though I am not unkind. She looked at
me I looked at something written across her scalp and these are the
words that I faintly read as I tried to call for help. (or something
close to that)

And now that we are on the topic of lyrics, let's all finally admit
that when we are alone in the car if we are singing along to a song
and don't know all of the words we just start making them up! It's
not embarassing, it's actually pretty funny! My mom thought "You're
the one that I love" (Grease) was called "You're a Wonderful Bug."
I almost wet myself laughing over that one! So sing nonsense, it's
fun and it might make your friend pee in your car which is always a
good thing.

TMBG Lunatic and General Miscreant

Tammy
ADG #1
