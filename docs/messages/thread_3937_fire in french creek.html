The power of SPOR! We could not check a lot of boxes because we had to be at Shed Rd by 1pm for a volunteer clean up we signed up for. I was happy my favorite area on the trail was spared. We did not check contents of every box because of the time constraints. We walked by 13 boxes and check only those in burn areas. Only one had a melt box, the rest were good. We did not get to check buzzard, red/blue or the area down near Lenape/Hopewell. That will have to be another trip.

S&R is complete. Of the 28 boxes in FC East, only one loss due to BUBAR (Burned Up Beyond All Recognition). Fortunately many escaped the fire's path.
