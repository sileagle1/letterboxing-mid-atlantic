Campin' and Stampin' Update!

From: Nathan Brown | Date: 2012-08-12 18:17:53 UTC
It is back, the Campin' and Stampin' Gathering.

It has been a few years, so I figured it was time to bring the gathering
back. It is staying in Maryland, but this time moving a bit west to
Cunningham Falls State Park in Thurmont, MD. There is a large
campground there, and plenty of hiking to be had, though not many
boxes. That will change, however ;-)

You do not have to camp in order to come to the gathering. There will
be a day portion to the gathering, on Saturday, September 8, from
10AM-2PM. We'll meet at the William Hauck Picnic Area in the park.
This area is at the end of the road leading to the lake, within the
road's loop. We'll be in the back of the loop. There is no pavilion,
we will just be gathering at a grouping of tables near the back of the loop.

A new series of boxes is being planted within walking distance of the
picnic area. Clues will be available to print off as part of a packet
before the gathering. A few packets will also be available at the
gathering.

These gatherings tend to be very low key, hang out if you like or hit
the trails, it is up to you!

Please, bring a dish to share.

For the campers...

Site 107 in the Deer Spring Branch loop of the William Hauck campground
will be our base camp. This site still has plenty of sites near it
open, so folks should book their sites now. The site also sits right
next to three cabins, for those that don't want to do tent camping.

From here we will have a Saturday night campfire and begin the night
boxing. The campfire and night boxing are open to all, even if you are
not camping (non-campers will need to park in overflow parking and walk
to the campsite.
