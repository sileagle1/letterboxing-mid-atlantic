School boxes - warning

From: brendan_hickey13 | Date: 2012-09-02 01:33:53 UTC
School is back in session, or will be very soon, so if you are seeking a box on or near school grounds, please do so on weekends/holidays or well after school hours.

A stranger poking around school property wearing a pack and looking at directions of some kind will definitely cause a school to go into "lock down," which is when all teachers and students huddle silently in darkened rooms behind locked doors.

It will also cause every police officer within several minutes of high-speed driving to come to the school, full of adrenaline and ready to rumble.

After you get arrested, the incident will become front-page and viral news, bringing lots of unwanted attention to our hobby. The same thing happened a few years ago to some hash-house harriers who marked a trail in Philadelphia with flour, which is a typical marking substance and completely benign - unless you put it on the sidewalk in front of the federal mint within recent memory of the anthrax scares.

If you have planted a box on school grounds, you might also give this some thought.

Thanks, and now back to the fun...
