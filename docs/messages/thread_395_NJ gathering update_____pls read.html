Bev,

this was mentioned before on a previous post: the use of
www.evite.com will help you build a RSVP list. Evite also has the
ability to list "need to bring items". it free too!
you can either post it as public event or create a private invite.
however, if you go the private route you will need a list of email
addresses of people in the lmba newsgroup.
with the help of the list owner or moderator you may be able to
obtain this list. good luck.

another option would be to generate a word document of member's email
addresses and post it under the Files section. anybody that does not
mind adding their email addy to the file can download it, edit the
doc and upload it back to the newsgroup. just a thought.
