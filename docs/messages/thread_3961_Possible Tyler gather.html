Possible Tyler gather

From: cwolf3030 | Date: 2012-10-29 17:29:27 UTC
I was kicking around the idea of hosting a gather at Tyler in late December and wanted to see if people were interested and or able to attend. I know it is always tough around the holidays with all of the family stuff going on so I just wanted to get a feel of what people thought.
I am hoping for Saturday, December 22 so we could celebrate the beginging of the 14th Baktun. The 29th would also work if too many people can't do the 22nd.

I have some stuff I'm working on to make the gather Mayan themed. I am not planning a ton of stuff and was figuring keeping things pretty low key like the other Tyler gathers (that I have been to). I did want to plant some stuff so the "Tyler veterans" have something to do if they choose.

I also wanted to make sure no one else was planning on doing a winter gather at Tyler this year. If someone was planning on doing something I would just drop the idea of the gather and just plant the boxes.

Let me know what you think, 22nd or 29th if interested at all.

Chuck
