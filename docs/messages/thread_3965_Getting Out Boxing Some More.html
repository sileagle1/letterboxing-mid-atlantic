Getting Out Boxing Some More

From: Nathan Brown | Date: 2012-11-21 00:32:00 UTC
Funny, it seems of late the only boxing we had been doing had been on
vacation, but in the last few weeks we had managed to get out a bit more
and enjoy some more "local" boxes.

It has been nice to get a few mysteries, including being the first
finder on one, finding a local Brownie troop's box (amazingly, first
finders there too) as well as a few old classics, like drazzuB Hill,
which has been well hidden for six years with very few finders. That is
a shame too, as it is on an excellent hike, just over three miles, that
includes a very nice waterfall and a great overlook.

It has all really whetted our appetite for more. Schizokeet wants to
get out Thanksgiving morning, I am thinking Into The Woods should be a
nice walk on the way to dinner at the folks place.

I think the mountains are once again calling, and there are boxes hiding
in them.

--

Yeah, we were traveling a lot for a couple of months and when we finally had a weekend at home we were thrilled to discover that 85 new boxes had been planted in the SE region of Wisconsin in the time we had been gone (half of them were in a park where a gathering had been held).  We've been enjoying getting out to find many of these local boxes over the past 4 weekends.
