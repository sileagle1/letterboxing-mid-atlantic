Letterboxes in Montgomery County PA Parks

From: drewclan11 | Date: 2013-03-13 18:11:36 UTC
Just a reminder that letterboxes planted in these parks must be registered with the good folks who preserve and maintain the land for the public. Please do not plant letterboxes illegally, and do not post illegal box clues to letterboxing.org. For the park policy in writing, and instructions on how to register your boxes, go to:

http://www.letterboxing.org/Policy.php#PA
