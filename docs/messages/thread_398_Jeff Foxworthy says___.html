I still get a smile when I remember back in January of
this year. I stumbled upon my first letterbox
accidently on a trail and posted to the list the
standard "Found my first box" yadda yadda. And several
people posted back saying Welcome, you're now
addicted, etc. And someone posted, I can't remember
who..."Soon you'll start planning your vacations
around where the boxes are". And I thought "These
people are NUTS! I'll never be like that! This is just
a nice little hobby to enhance Dude's and my weekend
walks..." Nine months later, I am flying back to NH to
go boxing, driving up to CT (although not on MMM
weekend) and spending pretty much every waking minute
thinking about where to hide or plant. Now I am one of
the nuts....but havin' a great time!
