RE: [lbma] Visit to Baltimore area

From: Heather Siple | Date: 2019-09-17 23:09:37 UTC

There are some letterboxes in First State NP, Beaver Valley Tract. I have it on my list to check on them, but I know there are at least two in good shape and one of them is a favorite of mine. NPS has been very nice about leaving them alone since they were there before the park service took over the property. Look for Big Crows Nest, which I know is there, and the Relics series, which I’m going to check this week. The Barrel Racer Series also goes through the park. Box 1 is definitely there, but it’s a much longer walk and I haven’t checked the other two yet.
