From: Lightnin Bug | Date: 2004-09-19 14:54:18 UTC
Now, I am sure that you have all had strange things happen to you
while you box, but how about tstrange things happening at home while
you are out boxing?

Team Lightnin' Bug had yet another strange encounter with insects!

Here's the tale...

My father in law (FIL) does wood carvings and wood turnings.
Collecters tend to value odd looking patterns in wood such as burls,
etc. He recently found a piece that had been invaded by forest ants,
the kind that can eat wood. So it had all sort of cool holes. He
usually turns these into bowls. Anyway, he figured out a way to 'de-
green' (dry) freshly cut wood by cooking it in a microwave after the
piece is carved. He had a particularly large piece that did not fit
in his microwave oven, so Mrs. LB offered to nuke this wood bowl for
him in our larger microwave. She'd zap it for 1 minute, then 2 and
so on to get a feel for the process. After about 1 hour of this she
notices that the holes in the wood started to have ants crawling out
of them. She tries to get them, but they crawl back in the hole
before she can grab one! It was kind of like playing 'whack a mole'
with ants in a bowl. We left the bowl out last night in the 45
degree cool post-Ivan weather to freeze em out.

I am not sure that there will be any more microwave treatments of the
bowl in the near future.

Arrgh! <-Obligatory Piratism for today

Lightnin' Bug
