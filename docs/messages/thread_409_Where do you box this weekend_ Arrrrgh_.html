From: zukeepr14 | Date: 2004-09-20 00:56:06 UTC
Today was an absolutely fabulous day to go letterboxing. I managed
to make my way down from Bergen County, NJ to Nockamixon Lake
inorder to search for the LoTR series. To The Raindears and H&H
wonderful series. Loved all the hikes and surroundings. And you
will be happy to know that all are alive and well and survived
Ivan. On my way down to Nockamixon I stopped by to pick up Rahway
River. First one for that box..woohoo. I was busy today and also
found "Jump for Joy" and "Hungry Termites". Both alive and well.
Can I just tell you how much I LOVED Cichlid Guy's stamp for "Hungry
Termites". I do have to say though "Hungry Termites " outer box was
a little damp but due to the woderful double bagging nothing was
damaged. I dumped out the water and tried to dry it as best I could
with my T-shirt. On my way back to "Joisey" I stopped off at the
NJAA Observatory to collect "Starry Night". Again alive and well.

All in all it was a very successful day today. Arrgh!

-zukeepr14
