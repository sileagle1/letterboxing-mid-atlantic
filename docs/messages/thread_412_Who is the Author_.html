We only found the first box in this series. When we were putting it back,
my 6 yo stumbled into a nest of bees/wasps and they took after her big time.
She got stung at least 7 times that I counted and was extremely hysterical
so we had to head home.

There was a warning about wasps in the log book, but being the usually
clueless person I am didn't put 2 and 2 together until my baby was screaming
and running away in a panic. I can't remember who planted this series
(sorry!), but would it be possible to place a warning directly on the clues?
The nest is in the ground and really isn't visible unless you are looking
for it.

I'm looking forward to going back - it sure looked like a nice walk!
