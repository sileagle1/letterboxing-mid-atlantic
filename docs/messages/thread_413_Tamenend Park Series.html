Tamenend Park Series

From: Elizabeth Grace | Date: 2004-09-20 21:38:56 UTC
What a great day to box - cool and dry!

Irene, The Brave Little Caterpiller series was a lot of fun. We did spend a
few (okay... about a half hour) trying to start from in front of the Farm
House instead of the office, but once we figured out the mistake we were on
our way. We were the first ones to find the series, just as you suspected.

We found #1 and #4-7 a-okay. #2 is definitely missing in action - all we
found was the burlap wrapped around the end of a stick. :(

I can't say that #3 is missing for sure, we were having a little trouble
with the clues. We couldn't find the rock with moss on it, but assumed we
should look on the path between #2 & #4. We saw two paths "with a small log
across it", but couldn't see "a large tree with grey-white bark". Guess we
should have brought that tree id book along as my son suggested - we don't
have any idea what a Birch or Ash tree looks like either. ;) We did look
around two trees that had logs at the base, but didn't find anything.

It was a completely successful hunt, though, in the kids eyes. The story
was wonderful and the stamps were cute. Their favorite by far was the
spider - I just finished reading Charlotte's Web out loud to my 6 yo. THANK
YOU!

Elizabeth
(aka The Mama Fishy)

Re: [lbma] Tamenend Park Series

From: Hikers_n_ Hounds | Date: 2004-09-21 00:20:01 UTC
Well, I went up to the park after work and "The
Raindears" box is gone. I am so sorry, Tim and girls.

"The Brave Little Caterpillar" box #2 is gone as in
now also box #7 with the log in it! This one I don't
get at all. It was there this afternoon for you guys
and all of the sticks and stuff are in place. I was
shocked that it wasn't there.

I am going to call the park Director tomorrow to see
if he has any clue where any of them might be.

In the meantime I am pulling the clues from the
website as the series makes no sense without box #7.
Box #3 is still there but I need to make the clues
clearer.

Of my 24 plants, Tim's box and box #2 are my first
ever to go missing. I honestly thought they were
pretty well hidden but I knew these boxes would be
tricky. I specifically planted them right off of the
trail so that the kids could find them easily and the
park would not complain about off-trail damage. I am
really surprised box #2 went missing as I thought that
was a pretty good spot.

Oh well. For now the series is pulled.

Thank you Elizabeth for all of your helpful feedback.
I hope your little one is okay again real soon.
