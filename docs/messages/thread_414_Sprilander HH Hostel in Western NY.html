We planted the Sprilander HH Hostel this morning and plan to take on
regular travellers as well as postal ones (meaning you can mail us any
hitchhiker you want and we'll either place it here in Western NY via

stay postal or should go into regular letterboxes. Also, since we're
in visual site of the hostel, if you let us know WHEN you're coming,
we can exchange while you're there!
