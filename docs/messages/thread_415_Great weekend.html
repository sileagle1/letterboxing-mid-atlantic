Great weekend

From: chilepepperletterbox | Date: 2004-09-21 01:53:20 UTC
Went 'boxing for the first time this weekend, and had a great time.
Golden's Brainteasers 1, 2 and 3 are alive and well, but we couldn't
find 4. I'm pretty sure we were in the right place, but it was cold
and raining and the four-year-old had had enough. Besides, a group
of foreign tourists showed up (why?) so we split.
We also walked the Noland Trail for the Mariners Museum series. We
found 1 (no stamp) and 2 (REALLY confusing clues--I must have done
something wrong) but couldn't find 3 or 4. 3 we probably walked
right by but we looked hard for 4--I wonder if it survived the
hurricane(s)? Anyway, we're hooked. Even the four year old, who
walked about four of the five miles of the Noland trail even without
her nap, is anxious to do it again!
