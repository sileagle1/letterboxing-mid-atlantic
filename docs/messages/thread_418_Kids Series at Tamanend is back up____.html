From: Hikers_n_ Hounds | Date: 2004-09-21 20:52:24 UTC
Greetings fellow LBMA-ers,

I replaced box #2 in a slightly different spot. Box #7
was all safe and sound and very well re-hidden by
Momma Fishy's brood. It was just a little beyond where
I had put it and between the darkness and the
camoflage I couldn't find it last night! Yes, I
actually couldn't find my own box. Tim's "Raindeers"
box is most definately gone. I take responsibility for
not hiding it well enough. Again, I was thinking about
little kids hunting. Mean people suck.

Remember, folks the kids series are store bought.
But's Tim's 2 remaining boxes, Dobby and Cat in the
Hat are very nicely carved by his girls. And it really
is a beautiful park.

Take a box to plant while you're there if you can but
I suggest you go well back on trail. There's lot's of
places in lower traffic areas. Just email Tony the
clues at parknrec@.... That way he'll know
they're there should anyone on his staff stumble
across it by accident.

H&H
