The week in boxing and a conundrum.

From: Cyclonic07@aol.com | Date: 2004-09-22 00:00:48 UTC
Is that how it is spelled?  Anyways, I've been doing some good boxing this week.
 
On Friday I took the long walk in Gibraltar and found "Exeter Scenic Trail Rocks."  I didn't find the trail all that scenic, but it is a good hike on level ground, and yes, it does take 90 minutes to do, and I was walking at a good clip.
 
Saturday got rained out.  I was going to go to Clementon Park and ride Tsunami, but Mother Nature had other ideas.  To all those who did box, you are a bunch of loonies (Yes, that is directed at you Irene :-P ).
 
Sunday, after work, I meet up with Irene (AKA Hikers and Hounds) and WarCat1976.  Dude the Wonder Dog was also in attendance.  WarCat (which are two people) boxed for the "What a Long Strange Trip" series, and then I joined them to find "Brandywine Blue Bird" and "Future Discovery Road Runner," which made no sense, While Irene when to plant her new box.  We then went for some food, and a good time was had by all :-)
 
Monday, work and more work.
 
Today, being Tuesday that is, after work I returned to the Mad Hatter's Hitchhiker Hostel, this time with a HH.  This is where the conundrum comes in.  This HHH is in fact a mystery box, one of Mapsurfer's, and thus has very few visits, despite it being the first HHH.  It is so bad that the HH I picked up was there for 18 months, yeah, it was left in March 2003.  I just see something wrong with this, I think that a HHH should be able to pick up a few more visits, so that a HH does not sit for that long. 
 
So, what I am getting at is, should I give away the location, or at least a less vague clue to the location, so that maybe some more folks can find this box.  I know some would frown upon that, but something has to be done, as HH are not usually meant to be out in the wild for that long of a time (the one I picked up was drenched, I actually poured the water out of it, and the log was soaked).
 
Thoughts?

Nathan Brown
AKA Cyclonic
