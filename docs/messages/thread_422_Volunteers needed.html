Volunteers needed

From: Cyclonic07@aol.com | Date: 2004-09-23 02:43:25 UTC
I am looking for some volunteers that would be willing to help out with an event at Greame Park, a very letterbox friendly PA historical site.
 
The park is having it's biggest fundraiser on Oct 29-30, Ghostly Gatherings.  It is a family friendly haunted trail.
 
So, what do we need?  We need folks to do all sorts of things, from playing characters on the trail, to working the concession stand, to helping with setup and cleanup.  Whatever you are able to help with, on any of the days, would be great.
 
To sweeten it a bit, anyone that volunteers will get clues to a spiecal thank you series that I am going to plant in the park.  It will be four boxes, and have some added bonuses thrown in.
 
If you are interested in helping out, you need to contact Beth, the park's volunteer coordinator.  You can call her at 215-641-0757, or email her at BethGPFriendsVP@....  Be sure to put Ghostly in the subject line.
 
Even if you can't help out, come on out to the event, it is a lot of fun.
