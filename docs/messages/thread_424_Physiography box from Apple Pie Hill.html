Over the past week, I've found:
 
Scudder Falls Bridge
Mint Eagle

All were in good shape, with one exception:
"Ben Franklin - Founder" was lying on the ground in the open, near the tree in which it was supposed to reside.  I placed it back where it belonged.  Also, there is a hole in the box...the Ziplocs were keeping everything pretty dry, but it might require some work.
