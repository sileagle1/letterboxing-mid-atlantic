We got out early today and found Rahway River (NJ).  We attempted to find "Uprising", a Mystery NJ box.  We know we were at the right site and were able to follow the clues half way through, but then we just didn't know where to go to for the last couple of clues.  We covered the entire place, inside & out but were stumped.  It was planted by Letterboxing Guild but I don't see a way to contact them.  Has anyone found this box?

Saturday Boxing

From: Art Vandelay | Date: 2004-10-10 02:01:45 UTC
What a perfect day to be out boxing!  We planted two new boxes (What Exit?) along the Garden State Parkway (if you're traveling along that route don't forget to bring the clues).
 
Went down to South Jersey and found the following:
Bayonet Farm
Hartshorn Woods Park- only found 4 out of 6
Rosehill Cemetary (box was wet, logbook very damp- outer bag had been chewed by an animal)  We repaired as best as we could.
 
We tried but had no luck in finding the Holmdel Arboretum box (not sure we were looking in the right place).
Also tried The Spy House- this seemed to be missing as we're sure we were in the right place.
In Hartshorn Woods one box appeared to be MIA but the other was probably there but eluding us- we couldn't find the landmark given.
 
All in all a great day.  Hope you enjoyed it as well.
 
Cat
