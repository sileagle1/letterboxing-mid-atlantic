This series is based on the history of Mountain Road (Used to be the
road from Richmond to Charlottesville if I remember correctly) and to
get people to visit the sites. Several of boxes are not really in
parks but historic sites so they are quick finds.

This series is great for kids. Simple directions, mostly flat, and
Crump Park has a historic farm house (with tours during certain
hours), animals, out buildings, a very small museum/gift shop (Tues-
Sat 12-4) and a modern playground on the site (so make this the last
stop and have some fun exploring and playing afterward.)
