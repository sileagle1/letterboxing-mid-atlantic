A little good news

From: Hikers_n_ Hounds | Date: 2004-09-28 10:28:58 UTC
Wow, I recevied 2 very exciting emails yesterday. The
first is the our "Infinity Knot" letterbox is safe and
sound, if somewhat soggy in it's spot in Erwinna! The
finders dried it out and put all new bags on
everything. They saved Carmen and I a trip up there to
confirm a previous MIA report and to do the
maintenance. The finders were Monkey gril and
Celtic...sorry can't remember the rest! Very cool.

Then after I had given it up for stolen, my Truckin'
PLB actually arrived at it's next donation! This has
been stuck in Austin Texas since 8/3. I emailed the
person, called them and they assured me it was on it's
way (on 8/16). I then sent pre-paid mailing envelopes,
called again with no answer and finally decided this
person was just going to keep the damn thing. Then, lo
and behold, the next person emails me yesterday that
they received it!

So all in all, a pretty good day for H&H boxes. Carmen
is working on something very, very cool for our next
postal that he is trying to have done in time for the
S. Jersey gathering. We'll see!

H&H
