From: gerania93 | Date: 2004-09-28 20:50:22 UTC
Yeah, right! The only box that I've put out. I was trying to sound a
little misleading up front there. It took a long time to get that
show on the road. I'll go over to the new boxers board and weep, wail
and moan about my multiple shopping trips for carving tools and
materials.

Being extremely forgetful didn't help either. I'd go to the craft
store for something, (and this place is not close) and not realize
till I'd gotten home that I forgot the gosh darn flippity wink (thats
not what I said) ink pad. Or, go to the supermarket and forget the
freezer bags.

All in all, I'm really lucky to have gotten this box assembled,
planted and the clues up in 2004. Some observent individual might
even notice that the box was planted on Friday the 24th and the clues
weren't available till today! Geeez.

I did a terrible thing. I made my first box (and who would do this?)
a mystery box. Remember the thrill of finding your first box, your
first HH, being first finder??? ...of putting your first box out into
the big, wet world? I've set myself up for a wait. No one is going
out at lunch tomorrow for my box. I know people like MapSurfer do
that. I'm not suggesting that my box is *that* good; he plants boxes
that no one might ever find. Actually, Letterboxingbee does that. I
stamped in at one of the boxes Bev put out months ago and found that
I was only the 4th person to visit. I guess that means that I'll have
to put out another box soon. Yeah, right!

It's called "Bought the Farm".
