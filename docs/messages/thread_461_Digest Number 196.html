Hey!

I just happened to be on and saw your question about a mystery box...

Most letterboxes have their location (if it is a park or whatever) in
the clues. For example, go to the Reading Pagoda...

With mystery boxes, you need to figure out either where the box is or
actual decipher the clues. For example, Go to the city with Japanese
Folly and ...

They can get pretty intense.

For a good resource on Mystery boxes, go to:

http://www.mapsurfer.com/boxes/index.html

These are some of the most difficult ones.

On www.letterboxing.org, you can find other mystery boxes by clicking
on the clues link and then going to you state of choice or mystery.

Most boxers consider finding them a bit of an accomplishment.

Take care,

Lightnin' Bug
