What A Weekend!

From: Art Vandelay | Date: 2004-10-03 22:25:36 UTC
Warcat76 just returned from the Monster Mash!  Had a fabulous time- The organizers did a wonderful job.  We managed to find just 34 of the 128 boxes planted for the event- we were too busy exchanging with the 250+ people in attendance!!  Since it was our first gathering, it was really overwhelming but worth every minute of it. So now with just 4 months of boxing we'd like to brag about our stats- 124 finds, 65 exhanges, 1 event and 2 hitchhikers! 
This is so much fun!  Hope to meet lots more of you from this group in the future.
 
Cat
