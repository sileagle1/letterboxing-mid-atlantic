MD-2 Crownsville boxes update

From: shaneodom2003 | Date: 2004-10-04 13:01:28 UTC
Just a quick note between the Ren Faire madness.
The Green Rose Labyrinth box is closed until further notice. I have
had to bar the labyrinth trail itself from public access due to some
large dangerous trees that have broken off and are hanging in the
canopy. I will remove them as soon as possible.
The Brass Dragon at the MD Ren Faire was washed away by the recent
rains. I was notified by a boxer that they found it laying by a post
on a Sunday next to wear it was velcro'ed, apparent having fallen
from the velcro. As they did not know wear the exact hiding point
was, they tucked it back next to the post, however the rains came
before I was able to visit. Sorry Scarab, I really wanted to maintain
them for ya. I hunted the slope over the rail but no sign. If ya
would like to re-place it before faire ends ya could mail me a backup
and I would hide it for ya.
Hope I see some more folks at the faire when ya come to enjoy the
park and visit our little "Face in the Trees".
Peaceful Trails,
Shane Odom
