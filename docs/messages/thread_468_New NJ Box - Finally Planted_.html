From: highlysung | Date: 2004-10-07 17:02:48 UTC
You're right - if you read the Ghost Towns of SJ List, there was a
very recent discussion of Saddler's Woods there. It's one of only two
old growth forests in SJ - if I'm remembering correctly - another was
a small patch in the pine barrens. I live about 2 blocks away - it's
been an interesting transition, as these woods sat neglected and
unprotected for many years until development threatened and their age
and significance was discovered. The conservation group is trying to
transition use of the area from local dirt bike riders to nature
walkers and education. They've done a good job so far, but cleanup
efforts remain very important. It sure is an amazing place to walk
through - some of the big trees are really quite amazing! They have a
really nice website at saddlerswoods.com (I especially enjoy the
Creative Corner with photos, art and poetry about the woods.)
