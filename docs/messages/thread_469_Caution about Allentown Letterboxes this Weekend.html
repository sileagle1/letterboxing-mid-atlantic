Howdy All,

not much good at haikus, so I've been quiet lately.

Anyway, if you are thinking of coming to Allentown to hunt General
Trexler's letterbox, Pass-Set-Spike or Allentown Rose Garden, be
aware that there are serious festivitiews going on in these parks
this weekend. It is the General Trexler Sesquicentennial
celebration. Sunday will be the more crowded day. Little Lehigh
park will be busy as well, for those of you looking into the Perfect
Circle Letterboxing Guild's "Fish in Outer Space" series or Robin
Hood's Hat. Lenape is far enough west to be away from the mayhem.

If you are coming to Allentown, I would encourage boxing on Saturday,
as Sunday will be "Harry's Day in the Parks"...
