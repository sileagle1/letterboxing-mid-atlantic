Letterboxing As It's Intended -- A Haiku

Letterbox for you.
To hike, Find and make your mark(s).
Or carve and Plant some.

"As it's intended"
Can not be applied to this.
None can claim to know.

1854,
and the thoughts of James Perrot,
are gone forever.

Do it for the hike.
Do it to share with fam'ly
Do it for the art.

Reap obsessively.
Attend all the gatherings.
Or box laissez-faire.

Make sly or dry clues.
Rhyme with reckless abandon
Use vague or plain tongue.

In it for the count?
Journey or destination?
The thrill of the hunt?

Whatever your goal,
the reason doesn't matter.
Letterbox for you.
