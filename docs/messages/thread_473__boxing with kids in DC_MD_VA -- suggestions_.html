letterboxing. We've found many new Montgomery County MD boxes to

gorgeous autumn weather.

particularly challenging. If you all say it's worth it and a

We also planted a box last week -- we love the White's Ferry area
and put one out there -- hope you'll look it up!

We went out for finds 6, 7 & 8 today and you beat us to all 3! So I'm

can't remember if I saw you in the logbook. If not, it has fun clues

in that same park! They weren't listed a few days before that. Now we
have to go back again! We've been hampered with vehicle problems.

around here!

From: The Doubtful Guests | Date: 2004-10-08 15:36:14 UTC
Boxes in Nern VA that aren't too long: 
 
(just because a box isn't listed does not mean it isn't good or appropriate, just that I'm not sure or don't know it)
 
Gulf Branch - great walk but clues tricky on the switch backs.  I need to rewrite them.
Ghost Castle of East Falls Church - drive bye, cool, woodsie, rocky hill for kids
Glencarlyn Dog Park
Vince Foster's Box - was not well hidden so don't be disappointed
Fort Marcy - mystery
Bunnyman Bridge - cool drive bye once you figure out where it is.
Bastion & A Flower for Mildred - drive bye mysterys
Jones Point Lighthouse
Huntley Meadows Park & other boxes there - Gorgeous park
Foxleigh Weigh - cute drive bye, no hiking
O&A RR - beautiful area
Bump on a Log
Who's on First - drive bye
The Bear Went Over the Mountain
Frying Pan Park
Puddle Duck - drive bye
 
 
Long hikes, fun stamps:
 
The Creature of Fountainhead Park
Riverbend Boxes
Seneca Boxes
Dead Authors
Tribute to the Woods
Goose Creek - first stamp missing, you can drive to the bridge near the 2nd stamp which is a great stamp.
Balls Bluff Civil War Trilogy
Froggy's Hot Date - disorienting clues but great stamps
The Pirates of Sunset Grace
First Manasas - not so long
Far Side of the Moo - not so long.  Fun stamps by Night Owl :-)
The Dangerous Spy - Great stamps by me :-) Second in thick undergrowth. 
The Sarah Special: A Triple Sized Feast of Fancy and Mystic - Very long hilly hike but great stamps.
 
 
There are a lot of good boxes and history in Fredericksburg, one in a really cool place.
 
Happy Hunting, Hope to see you on the trail,
Scarab

From: Lady Bug | Date: 2004-10-16 15:07:03 UTC
Lake Frank and Lake Needwood in Montogmety County have a couple of good boxes for kids.  Lake Frank Turtle would make a nice autumn hike, mostly on a trail.  We've tried to find the other Lake Frank box (Fungus) a couple of times with no luck. But also a nice walk...down a steepish semi-trail. 
 
There's a fun indoor one in the Olney Library.  And another personal favorite in Olney as well.
