Mansfield Monster Mash & week after

From: Marie & Bob | Date: 2004-10-08 21:36:54 UTC
MMM was a great success. Sounds like about 350 in attendance. Line to
sign in event book was long and slow. We spent over a hour in line.
Lots of food and goodies, more than enough to go around. Thanks to
all organizers and helpers.
We left home on Friday and started our boxing.

NJ-- The Place (mystery box) with Old Grandaddy HH inside (our second
find of Gramps) Moved him along to another state.
Audubon Nature Center (3 boxes)

CT-- Three Boxes (#1&2), CT Audubon Center at Glastonbury Presents (2
boxes), Sunshine & Daydream w/bonus box

MMM-- Mummy Dearest, Attack of the 50 foot Woman, My Precious, Cookie
Monster, Mr Waternoose, Mr Tooth Decay, The Thing, Uncle Fester,
Frankenstein, Wyvern, Yoda, Skeksie

MA-- Westville Dam, Broad Meadow Brook(MA Audubon)

NH-- Monson Villiage, Runnells Bridge, Otter Brook Dam, Chesterfield
Gorge (2)

VT-- Mountain View, Hip-Hop (our only miss)

NY-- Voldermort Strikes, Bowdoin Park

33 in all with one not found. All boxes in good condition generally.
Now to catch up on chores at home.
