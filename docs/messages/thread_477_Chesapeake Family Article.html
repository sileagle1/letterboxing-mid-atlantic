Chesapeake Family Article

From: shaneodom2003 | Date: 2004-10-08 22:58:35 UTC
Hello All,
Chesapeake Family Magazine, a free monthly for parents and such,
available at local grocery stores and other locations, just published
a story on boxing as a cover byline. The writer contacted me from the
board, just about the time some of the questions about articles and
their impact on the hobby were coming out. I discussed many of these
concerns with her and that printing any direct clues or comments on
actually boxes was not such a good idea. She took these to heart and
there is even a disclaimer in the article about asking permission.
She used a wonderful pic of my son finding a box, and allthough I had
orginally identified the location to her, I asked her not to print it
and she agreed. Nice little article, just the kind to encourage the
hobby but not overload it. We ourselves, found letterboxing thru an
article, and I think they are a good idea within certain parameters.
So anyone who wants to know what Team Woodwose looks like, well check
out the Oct. Issue, free in a store near you.
Much Peaceful Trails,
Papa Woodwose
