A very late Monday sign in...

From: Hikers_n_ Hounds | Date: 2004-10-12 00:35:58 UTC
Boogie Bob and I just got in from an all day family
funeral and wake. It was actually really great. My
brother-in-law Dan was the worlds biggest Deadhead. My
son Bob played Ripple at the graveside and we all sang
along. Pretty cool...

Anyway, Dude and I had a wonderful day yesterday
boxing with Celtic Cat and Wednesday Bowler, and
Janila and her 4 legged buddy Barney. After a not so
promising start, we found all 4 boxes there. We think
the picnic tables have been moved since the boxes were
placed and started out totally on the wrong side of
things, but we finally go on the right trails. A fun
day with some great folks. A personal note about
Wednesday Bowler, she had eye surgery today to correct
a detached retina caused by diabetes. She has been on
my mind all day and I hope all went well. Now she has
to stay face down for a month! I would go out of my
mind. So let's think good thoughts and I think
Marybeth, Celtic Cat, is going to try and make the
gathering on Saturday. I'll pick up a big get well
card for us all to sign that she can read when she
gets her eyesight back. See you guys on Saturday. I am
picking up Miss Moon from NYC at the train station so
that she can join in the fun too.

H&H
