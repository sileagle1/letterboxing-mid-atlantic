Thanks to Doug for the following instructions:

"For all you letterboxers attending the gathering from PA, here are
some driving directions: After passing through the toll booths on
the Tacony-Palmyra Bridge (there's no toll for NJ-bound vehicles),

highway to reach the nature center."

complex) make a left. Your going to go past the "toll house" and you
will be driving right along side the toll booths. You will be going
under the bridge and straight into the Palmyra Cove parking lot.
