Parking for Saturday's gathering

From: letterboxingbee | Date: 2004-10-14 22:56:26 UTC
Hey gang,


I am not sure how many parking spots Palmyra Cove's parking lot has,
but when you arrive if there happens to be no parking spots left
please come into the building (opens 10am) and ask the desk to have
the overflow parking lot, that is directly under the bridge, opened.


Then if the overflow lot happens to get filled there is extra parking
on Market Street and Temple Blvd but you will have to walk a short
distance back to the park. It's very easy and you don't have to drive
back onto Route 73. Go out the same road you come into the park on
and there will be parking on your right (Market Street) near the "toll
house". OR continue "straight" through the intersection at the Korman
suites, park on Temple Blvd. Do not enter or park in the apartment
complex.


Hope this helps.


--Bev
