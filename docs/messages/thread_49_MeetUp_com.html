There's the Camping Weekend in MD. Message repeated here from
earlier.

-----

De-mildew your tents, pack your duffel bags, and bring the whole
family to Patapsco Valley SP in Elliocot City, MD the weekend of May
22-23, 2004. Plan on friendship, merriment, and, of course,
letterboxing! The park has many campsites, cabins (with
heat/electric), and 17 letterboxes. Rates are $20/camp site per night
and $50/cabin per night. Pet friendly.

We are getting together a preliminary count of interested parties, so
please let us know if you plan on attending so we can block out the
correct number of campsites. We will firm up numbers once the event
gets closer at hand.

We hope to see you there!

Celtic Pride

http://www.dnr.state.md.us/publiclands/central/patapscovalley.html

P.S. I'm sure there are myriad hotels/motels around for those who
stay at Camp Holiday Inn.
