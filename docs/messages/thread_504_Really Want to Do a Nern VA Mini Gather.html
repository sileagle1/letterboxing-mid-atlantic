Re: Really Want to Do a Nern VA Mini Gather

From: Mike Brooks | Date: 2004-10-19 01:25:59 UTC
Since you mentioned Fountainhead...

That park should be great for an event. They treat my orienteering club very
well for our annual visits. The geocachers had a big event there in June.
The last time I counted, there were 7 letterboxes and 17 geocaches in the
park, so the park is OK with our hobbies. They can't "not know" about them
when there are that many. It's a big park, so there should be room to hide a
few more letterboxes. To my knowledge, no letterboxes nor geocaches have
been plundered at Fountainhead, knock on wood.

The park website with shelter rental info and such is at nvrpa.org. The big
problem with Fountainhead is that they lock the gates for the winter
starting November 13. After that, you can park on the entrance road and walk
in. That won't affect a 10/30 event, but those of us who miss it might
choose to wait until Spring to get the ones you plant for the event. If you
plant any on the MTB Trail, make sure that you are very alert when walking
that trail. The bikers ride very fast and don't look for hikers, since there
are plenty of hiker-only trails. Make sure that all your spots are good ones
well off the trail, as all the trails are very busy.

I doubt I could make such an event, but it would be a cool thing to offer.
Good luck if you try it.

Mike
