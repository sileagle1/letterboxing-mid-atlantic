Clue Series Update

From: ilikebeingbob | Date: 2004-10-20 21:10:13 UTC
Update

Weapons - Boxes 2 & 3 have been replaced and the rest are in good
shape.

Box 4 #9 - Directions may be confusing. May be better read
as 'At the intersection, look for a small path that goes off at 220
degrees.
#10 - Did not see any brown trail markers


Suspects - Box 5 - Mrs. White missing

Box 2 - After finding this box, you have to retrace your steps
(about 5) and then take the trail on the right that heads downhill
and toward the road.
Box 4 - Has been moved. After you are facing the tree after
step #4, take about 10 careful steps to the left. You should be near
a small group of rocks. The letterbox is between these rocks.
Box 6 -#8 These last few steps may be vague. The leaning tree
is very large.

Rooms - Did not check


Final Park
I tried to find these boxes but could not. I did not have the
directions with me but I had a good idea were they were.

Take this information however you want. These boxes were placed when
we were at the end of a several year drought. Now that we have had
two years of excessive rain, the path to the area of the letterboxes
is marshy. I don't think the last part of the directions to the
letterbox is actually part of the trail system but more of a cleared
area due to underground sewer lines. Heavy rain has also caused
several trees to fall and several ravines up to 20 feet deep to open
up in the immediate area of the letterboxes.

If you try the final part of the series, plan for a few inches of mud
and some bush wacking.

Bob2
