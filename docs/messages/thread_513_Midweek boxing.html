Midweek boxing

From: Cyclonic07@aol.com | Date: 2004-10-21 02:39:45 UTC
 
Did some midweek boxing today, with some good success.
 
I found Camp Conrad Wieser, in good shape, and with a very nice stamp.  While I like the view associated with the location, I didn't like the location itself.  There really isn't anyplace to park, and the folks at the camp did not know the box was there yet.  They would really like a call ahead of time too, as they would like to know who is on the mountain, so including the camp phone number in the clues would help.  On the plus side, the camp councilor that I talked to was very friendly and seemed very interested about letterboxing, and was happy to hear that there are several in nearby Blue Marsh.
 
Later in the day, Tommcat, Farmer Brown, and I went over to the Armtrout Preserve to find W.W. Holloween Fly By, and had much luck.  Since I read the clues, we found it right away (Tommcat confuses easily) ;-)
 
Yesterday, Farmer Brown went for some of the Perkiomen Trail boxes, with no luck.  I have to find out which ones were missing from her, she forgot right off the top of her head, I will update on them.
 
Looks like I will be busy this weekend, so no boxing for me.


Nathan Brown
AKA Cyclonic
