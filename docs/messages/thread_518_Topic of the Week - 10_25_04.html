From: hexacat | Date: 2004-10-25 16:04:47 UTC
FYI.
i am writing a database that will keep track of all your "finds"
and "plants". included with your planted boxes there will be a
maintenance section where you can insert recent comments and
conditions of that box(including any boxes you may have "adopted").
also included is a contact list that you can keep info on fellow
letterboxers with their phone, addy, email and a list of all their
boxes they planted. of course you will be able to search according to
specific infomation you select on the main page (ie. most recent
date).

i'm still working on this database and will be looking for beta
testers so i can work out the kinks before release. if anyone is
interested please let me know so i can add you to my distribution
list. thanks.

no, i'm not a nerd.
-hexacat
