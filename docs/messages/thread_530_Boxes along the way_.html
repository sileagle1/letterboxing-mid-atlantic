I-78 runs right through the greater Lehigh Valley. Here are some
within a 5 mile drive of I-78 exits:

Easy, Suburban Park type Boxes - trails are paved or close to it -
General Trexler, Pass Set Spike, Allentown Rose Garden, Rodale Bike
Park
Slightly more challenging with minor climbs, dirt trails - Pool
Wildlife Sanctuary, Robin Hood's Hat
Short, but one steep section - Lenape Museum, Tom's Alien Spaceship
Longer, challenging, Rocky - South Mountain Series w/ South Mountain
H³

If you are game to meet up and exchange stamps, I will be in the area
on Saturday (within a shout of Rodale Bike park up until 10:15 am),
then at home (1 mile from I-78) until 1-4:30 when I'll be hiking in
the vicinity of the South Mountain Series with a bunch of Boy Scouts,
then home after that.

Shoot me an emial if you want my Cell Number and to meet up with the
members of Team Lightnin' Bug!

Eagle Bob & Teapot Marie just placed one at the rest stop on 287N in
NJ. It's listed as such in Morris county on the lbna site. Funny
rest stop - the size is away above and beyond the demand.
There are other boxes in parks just to the west of 287 along the
Ramapo escarpment, but they're real hiking boxes. Shortest hike I
can think of would be the Tourne, although it's all uphill.
If you solve the clue, you might want to try for my "the Place" box,
but there's no facilities, just a great diner up the road.
