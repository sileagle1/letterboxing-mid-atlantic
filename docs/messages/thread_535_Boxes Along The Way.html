When I went through CT a few weeks ago, 84 was a mess in the Waterbury area
for some construction that looked to be settling in for a long project. I
don't think there is any fast way through CT, especially on a Friday
afternoon. You didn't mention a specific town that you were aiming for
anyway (or does "MG" refer to a letterbox gathering that I haven't paid
attention to).

If 84 does work for you, Tarywile Park #1 in Danbury CT might be manageable.
The whole series of four is a hefty bit of hiking, but the first box is real
close to parking. I recall it would be about 10 minutes from exit to
parking, 15 minutes to the letterbox and back, and 10 minutes back to the
exit. The stamp is good and the viewpoint just beyond the placement is good
too. Those reading along with more time and energy on a future trip should
consider the whole series; it's a fantastic park and hike.

Have a safe trip.

Mike
