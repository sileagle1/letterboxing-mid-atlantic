Battle of the Bands - Plantapalooza

From: Hikers_n_ Hounds | Date: 2004-11-07 02:15:15 UTC
Well, Plantapalooza was a heck of a lot of fun,
attended by a small but hearty band of boxers. The
Raindears, Team Little Dog, Team Sudden Turkey, Miz
Scarlet, Buckaroo Bonzai, Number 6 and Nevermore as
well as Hikers and Hounds. What a beautiful day! We
planted a mess of boxes. Miz Scarlet did a count of
some kind but I can't remember what the heck it was.
Plus Nattybumppo was planning to attend but had a last
minute change of plans. He had already planted "The
Police". We will be busy posting clues ASAP. There are
some GREAT stamps and more to come! Letterboxing Bee
is not feeling well so will mail me the boxes to plant
later, plus Fleur de Lis will be planting, or she can
mail as well.

A great day and some great boxes left behind for
everyone. Thanks to all,

H&H

Re: [lbma] Battle of the Bands - Plantapalooza

From: MaryAnn Lockard | Date: 2004-11-07 12:20:46 UTC
While enjoying the richly deserved hospitality of
Issac Newton's we tried to do a count of boxes planted
and boxes already in place and came up with about 15.
It would certainly be worth you while to check out
this park. Some of the stamps were awsome. I take that
back all of the stamps were awsome. Some people who
claim they can't carve put out some great work. Out of
the 15 boxes there I found 3 amidst the planing fury
so I will be going back. After you find 15 boxes
retiring to Issac's is the thing to do for refreshment
and warstories. Also in the parking lot is a little
box planted by Mizscarlet, Hikers and hounds and team
Sudden Turkey. I may not have lettetboxed at night but
now I have planted at night.
Mizscarlet

Here is some info to help, if you want to use it:

From the main park entrance exit by making a right
onto Swamp Road. Stay on Swamp Road approximately 1
mile to State Street, this is the light just after the
raised pedestrian footbridge. Left onto State Street.
At the second traffic light turn left (the Temperence
House will be on the right corner). Almost immediately
you will see the Municiple Parking lot on your left.
Turn in and Park. Isaac Newton's is in the Municple
Parking lot. To locate the box...
