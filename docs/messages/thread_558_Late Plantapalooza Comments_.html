Late Plantapalooza Comments!

From: highlysung | Date: 2004-11-12 18:45:16 UTC
Hi Everyone:

I've been so busy this week, but I wanted to be sure to check in and
let everyone know what a fun event it was last weekend at
Plantapalooza. Gwenllian and I really enjoyed seeing everyone again
and meeting new people. Tyler State Park is really lovely! We were
excited to find a great spot for our Steely Dan box. We can't wait to
go back and hunt down all the other great boxes. If you look for
Steely Dan this weekend, you'll be getting pretty basic directions to
the box. I'm hoping to come up with more creative clues by next week
sometime to improve the box experience and inject some Steely Dan
coolness to the whole hunt!

I especially loved meeting up for dinner at Isaac Newton's - what a
great idea that was! If you are heading out to hunt these boxes - do
not forget to plan your apres hunt meal at Isaac Newton's! While you
are there, look for Specific Gravity, planted by Mizscarlet in the
back of the parking lot.

Irene, thanks a ton for the excellent idea of Battle of the Bands,
and for all your work planting the boxes that were sent to you!

By the way, this is a belated announcement that I have planted a
limited edition bonus box at Saddler's Legacy - it will be available
until Thanksgiving. So, if you're headed to Saddler's Legacy, you can
get two for the price of one! Directions are in the logbook.

highlysung of Team SuddenTurkey
