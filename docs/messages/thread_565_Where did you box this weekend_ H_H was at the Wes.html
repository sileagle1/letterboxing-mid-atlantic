Where did you box this weekend? H&H was at the Western NY Gathering

From: Hikers_n_ Hounds | Date: 2004-11-15 13:17:05 UTC
Well after a whirlwind weekend trip to the 1st Annual
Western NY gathering I am back home in good ol' PA.
Friday after my daily grind I jumped a couple of
trains up to NYC and crashed out at Miss Moon's.
Saturday way-too-early she and I drove up through some
beautiful country and arrived at the gathering about
3:00PM. We met some great folks! It was wonderful to
put some faces to some of the names I've seen on the
various talk lists. Lemme tell you LBMA-ers, this was
an OUTDOOR gathering. These hardy folks had been out
in some serious cold since about 10:00 AM, exchanging
and boxing all day long. Sprite posted last night that
she couldn't get the smile off her face, that's cause
it's frozen in place! Anyway, great time, great
people, beautiful day. Several of us went out to
dinner which just extended the fun even more. Sunday
AM Miss Moon and I found a box right near our motel
then boogied about a hour north to Niagra Falls. Both
of us had never been there before so it was a real
treat. We walked around in Canada for awhile and then
found the Elba Onions "Goat Island" box before sadly
heading south again. But, thanks to the Northwoods
Adventure Team we rocked out to a live String Cheese
Incident show taped at the Fillmore, thanks SO MUCH
for the music guys! What a great way to spend a sunny
November day driving through your beautiful state.

I really enjoyed meeting everyone, their friendliness
and hospitality made the whirlwind trip a blast.
Thanks to Sprite and the Highlander and all who helped
put the gathering together. Also, special thanks to
Sprite who ran clues over to our motel room so we
could box on Sunday. And lastly, thanks to my
travelling partner, Miss Moon. It was fun travling
with someone other than Dude the Wonder Dog. Dude's
great but he's not enough of a conversationalist to
sustain a 7 hour drive. Thanks again!

I don't keep close track on my progress because a. I
am too lazy to count b. Only have 10 fingers and ten
toes and am easily confused by numbers over 20 c. Am
not particularly competitive. That being said during
our 7 hour drive home I did some counting and it seems
that I am at P27, F94, X97, HH10, PLB's 4. This
weekend Mr.Irene and I will be in Dewey Beach DE and
will be hunting Mz Scarlet's boxes so I am pretty sure
I'll pass F100. I've only been boxing since the end of
January of this year so I am pretty pleased with my
progress, especially since when I first started I
hunted twice as many as I found!

Thanks to all who make this hobby so much fun. Carmen,
Deb, Dude, Jasmine, Cody and I are having way too much
fun!

Hikers & Hounds
