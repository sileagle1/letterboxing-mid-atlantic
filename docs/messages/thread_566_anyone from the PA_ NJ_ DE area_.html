I'm tempted to reply but since I'm a newbie and have just eight finds
under my belt I'm afraid that I'll be able to show them the ropes of
how not to letterbox.
Lesson 1. That break in the trees that you passed three hours ago was
the trail you were supposed to take.
Lesson 2. At 5' tall you might as well forget about trying to go by
paces. Just start looking for the landmark instead
Lesson 3. Letterboxing will forever ruin a nice "walk in the woods".
You will spend all your time with thoughts in your head like "that
would be a great hiding spot" and "what would the best clues to here
be?"

mentioning Doug and Scarab. Everyone in this group

felt that our more senior boxers, yeah that's the
right phrase, Doub and Scarab are our Senior Citizen

experience and history. I know how hard Doug has
worked to restore some "historical" boxes in this
area. But there are lot's of long term boxers on this
