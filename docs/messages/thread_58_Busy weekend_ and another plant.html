Busy weekend, and another plant

From: Cyclonic07@aol.com | Date: 2004-04-20 00:02:50 UTC
 
I had a busy, productive weekend.  I should have, it was so nice out I had to get out some.  Besides the finds I have already posted, I found WINAHAWCAWCHUNK on my second try.  I missed it the first time out, but thanks to Google, I was able to find it with ease this time around.
 
Also, I found The Jane Austen Series: Persuasion.  It, as already reported, is not missing, and is right where it should be.  If you have not yet visited this box, give it a go, it has a very nice setting.

Finally, I planted my second box on Sunday.  It is Scudder Falls Bridge, and is planted in Yardley, PA.  As soon as I am able to, I plan to move this one though.  The original planned location is currently closed for construction.  PennDOT says it will be reopened after next weekend, so expect to see it take up permeant residence in the Scudder Falls rest stop very soon.  Right now you can find it close by at the Delaware River access area off river road in Yardley, just five minutes off I-95.
 
The clues are here:
http://www.letterboxing.org/BoxView.asp?boxnum=7886&boxname=Scudder_Falls_Bridge
 
Hope everyone else had a great weekend as well.
