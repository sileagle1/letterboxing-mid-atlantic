Hi All,

The Hairy Legged Boxers are happy to report on our boxing mischief at
Blue Marsh Lake....

Found: NW and NE of the "Stamp Collection" Series. NW was in good
shape, NE had been gotten to by a beast of some sort. The box was
wrecked, soaked and chewed upon. We need to email ShredMTB.

We also found "Like Chasing Rainbows", a box that eluded us in our
last adventure. Some nerdology helped here. Finally, we revisited
the scene of "Mobius Strip" to see if any new finds have occurred.

Hidden was Mothra and Little Alien's Older Brother, which will be
normal boxes, in an area laden with mystery.

Cooties also showed their ugly faces by some Killer Peas first
infesting Jan from Reading and then being transferred to Cichlid Guy
by our intrepid 10 yr old. I was also violated by receiving a slug
like cootie myself.

Our intrepid travelers included Cyclonic, Jan from reading, Cichlid
Guy (for the Mobius) and Thomas and Lightnin' Bug of Team Lightnin'
Bug.

Happy Trails,

The Hairy Leggers
