Weekend Boxing Report - Nockamixon

From: Hikers_n_ Hounds | Date: 2004-11-29 12:30:11 UTC
Saturday H&H, Celtic Cat and The Gamecock met up with the Cichlid Guy for a little while to hunt his wonderful "Hungry Termites" box. Then he boogied on to Blue Marsh while we stayed and found The Cabin by the Lake, Catch the Wind and The Lord of the Rings series. Didn't find "The One Ring" box but in light of my recent "Shiver Me Timbers" debacle I am not reporting it missing! But Tim, have you had any MIA reports on it?
 
We parted ways around 3:00 and I went to New Hope for my final Jane Austen box and actually found it! It's my favorite stamp in the series, although I have enjoyed them all.
 
Sunday was supposed to be boxing at Tyler with Miss Moon but we got rainied out. Even though it did stop raining the trails would have been horrible. So plan B was to drive down to MD for the F100 PLB mini-meet, but Carvin' Carmen's windshield wiper motor died and he needed to borrow the Dudemobile so I stayed home and worked on my log.
 
I guess no more boxing for a few weeks as I get ready for the holidays. At least that's the plan...
