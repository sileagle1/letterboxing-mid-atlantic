Nice day for a walk

From: Rainear, Tim TQO | Date: 2004-04-22 02:34:04 UTC

Today turned out to be nice despite the threat of rain earlier.

I managed to find "Scudder Falls Bridge" in the morning on my way to a customer visit and swung down towards Bensalem and went on a "Treasure Hunt" in the afternoon.  All boxes are in excellent shape. 

First finder on "Scudder Falls"

That gets me to something like F82. 

My first year anniversary is in August, so F100 within the first year is well within reach.

Thanks all.

Raindear

From: Hikers_n_ Hounds | Date: 2004-04-22 11:09:48 UTC
Tim,
 
Thanks for the update. I think I have to start working for you so that I can do more hunting! I am trapped in an office 5-60 hours a week so must do the "weekend warrior" thing! By the way all PA Letterbox Planters. This Saturday, April 24th, the PA Department of Conservation and Natural Resources is sponsering the "Great PA Clean-up". Many parks are scheduled for intense trash clean up by volunteers. Neshaminy State Park, where Treasure Hunt is, is scheduled for a shoreline clean up. Needless to say, I will be one of the volunteers so that I can keep an eye on things. I doubt anyone would find them who was justing looking for trash, but better safe than sorry. Also, those of you who plan to hunt on Saturday might see a few more people on the trails than is usual. I don't know which parks have volunteers. I read about this in my local paper. You might want to look into it if you have any concerns about your boxes or about hunting on Saturday.
 
Tim, congrats on your successes! And thanks for the update on the boxes.
 
Irene - Hikers & Hounds
