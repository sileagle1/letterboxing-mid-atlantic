While boxing at Nockamixon Lake on November 27th I found someone's
signature stamp in the first of the LoTR Series- Minas Tirith box.
The stamp is of animal tracks, carved on an eraser that was bought at
A.C Moore. It looks like it was stamped in hot pink, and the owner
stamped into Minas Tirith and the Cabin by the Lake boxes in early
November. Unfortunatly the owner did not sign a trail name.

If anybody knows who this stamp belongs to please e-mail me off list,
so we can make arrangements to get this stamp back to the proper
person.
