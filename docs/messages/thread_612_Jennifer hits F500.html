Jennifer hits F500

From: Silent Doug | Date: 2004-12-06 01:34:58 UTC
Jennifer reached F500 this evening, so be sure to congratulate her!

:-)

Here's our report for the day. First, I logged Vision Quest in Blairstown,
NJ, and then we hit the road, finding:
Harmony Methodist Church, Stillwater, NJ
NJ Physiography 101 (only one left in the series!), Culver's Lake, NJ
Stone Needle, Mystery NJ
Oak Plateau, Wantage, NJ
Shooting Star, Farmington, CT

All were in good shape. The last box was found in the dark, but fortunately
it was a driveby and not like the hike we did last week where we ended up
walking the last 30 minutes in complete darkness! Darn these short winter
days -- there aren't enough hours to find enough letterboxes!

Doug
