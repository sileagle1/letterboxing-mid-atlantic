Some southern boxes to report before I come back home. I was able to

drive by.

Should not be a problem for an 'enlightened' company.

St. Louis is about 170 miles from Paducah (Wacinton). KY Dam is
another 20-25 miles and Land Between the lakes another 20-25. Heck,
you could keep going and hit Nashville in a few hours. Needless to
say, things are a bit more spread out in the Heartland. Your can get
a one-way rental drop off!
