The first of the Goose Creek boxes is MIA.  The 2nd is a rather nice stamp,  You can closer to it by looking at the map and finding the bridge. 
 
The 2nd Froggy box, Eyes, may be MIA and 1 and 2 were both tricky to find even when it wasn't.  Nice stamps though.
