From: Silent Doug | Date: 2004-12-08 17:41:17 UTC
At 12:29 PM 12/8/2004, you wrote:
>Does anyone know if the canal path is still
>open-specifically between Upper Black Eddy and Tinicum
>park?

If you don't get an answer here, try
http://www.fodc.org/
or
http://www.dcnr.state.pa.us/stateparks/parks/delawarecanal.aspx

The PA State parks page says:

"As of November 5, 2004 two sections of the canal towpath are open. These
sections are: an eight-mile section of trail from Upper Black Eddy
(Bridgeton Hill Road) South to Smithtown Lock 15/16 and a three-mile
section of trail from Center Bridge South to New Hope Lock 11. These
sections are open in addition to the section of trail that was not damaged
by the storm, Rt. 532 in Washington Crossing South to Bristol. At this time
these are the only sections that are open for use by the public. The rest
of the towpath and canal will need to be repaired before it will be opened."
