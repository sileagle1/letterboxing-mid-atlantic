Re: [lbma] Pre weekend boxing

From: Cyclonic07@aol.com | Date: 2004-12-10 04:12:47 UTC
 
I have been doing some pre-weekend boxing as well, since the weekend will be taken up by coaster riding :-)
 
I was in downtown DC today, but I am getting ahead of myself.  I was up by 6 today (that is sleeping in for me), and found the three Suburban Saturday boxes.  Someone beat me to them, but that is OK.  All were in fine shape, though the previous finder did not hide them well, and the third box was clear out in the open, easily seen from the trail.
 
I hopped on the Metro and headed into town.  The first stop there was Roosevelt Island, where I found Ted E Bear in fine condition.  What a neat spot too, it is a memorial kinda hidden away, but very fitting for Teddy Roosevelt, being in the middle of nature like it is.
 
I walked across to the Mall (yeah, I had to make a hike out of these urban boxes), and found Orion the Hunter right where it was meant to be.  It is now in need of yet another log, I filled up the last little space left in it.
 
But, I did strike out on one as well.  After doing touristy things on the Mall all day, I went in search of Cornucopia, with no luck.  The bush it was supposed to be in were nearly bear of leaves, so I suspect it was muggled.
 
Well, off to TN tomorrow, can't wait.  On Monday I hope to find some of the boxes in the Great Falls area.  Hope everyone has a good weekend.
