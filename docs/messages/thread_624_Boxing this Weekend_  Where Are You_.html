Depending on how much time you have:

South Mountain Series - 2 hrs - 3 boxes, including the HHH.
Tom's Alien Spaceship - 20 minutes (add 40 minutes if you want to
find the HHH)
Pool Wildlife Sanctuary - 1 hour - 2 boxes
Trexler Park - 45 minutes - 1 box
Rodale Bike Park - 30 minutes - 1 box
Mystery Boxes - No help for you!
Kalmbach Park - 30 minutes - 1 box
Seem Seed - 30 minutes - 1 box
Zuflucht Haus - 15 minutes - 1 box - very interesting place
Robin Hood's Hat - 40 minutes - 1 box
Lenape Museum - 40 minutes - 1 box - Most rewarding view
Ironton Rail Trail - 90 minutes - 2 boxes - very weird spots

I know I am forgetting a few,
