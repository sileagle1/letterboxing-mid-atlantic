The DaVinci Code

From: nattybumppolbna | Date: 2004-12-14 20:20:26 UTC
Okay, I just finished the DaVinci Code.

Amateurs.

Anybody can clue in iambic pentameter. Try writing a clue in Seuss verse.

I would not put it in a tree
Or in a stump
Or in a cree-k
I would not place it here or there...

You get the idea.

You want some fun? Watch for the Tolkien Code - Coming to a web site near you in 2005.

I wish I had something more valuable to hide with it - like the Ark of the Covenant or the
Lindbergh baby. But I guess a rubber stamp will have to do.

Natty Bumppo

P.S. If you liked The DaVinci Code, try "Gospel" by Wilton Barnhardt. Much more fun.

Re: Mechanicsville VA

From: ilikebeingbob | Date: 2004-12-17 15:12:55 UTC
I don't know what you mean by good box clues but you can check out

-the Mountain Road Series in Henrico County - Most of them are drive
by. The purpose of this series is to visit the historic spots on
Mountain rd.

-The JRP series - If you are interested in going to the river there
are several letterboxes there. If you can only visit one, get the
Pumphouse boxes or the Belle Isle box

I recently checked these boxes so they should be in good shape.

Close to Mech., check the clothespin box(Hanover), in Richmond check
the Garden and Rebellion box.

Bob2
