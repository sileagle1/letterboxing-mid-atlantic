Funny, when this message arrived I had to do a doubletake. I founded the
Pocono Mountains Freecycle group last year, and Turtle McQ founded the
group in Ithaca, NY at the same time. Ours were among the first 50 or 60
local freecycle groups in the country -- today there are nearly 2,000!
Freecycling is a great idea, so I heartily recommend it for anyone who's
interested in doing their bit to help the environment. You can learn more
at http://www.freecycle.org -- chances are good there's a local group near
you where you can clean out your closets and garages and get rid of good
stuff that you no longer need.
