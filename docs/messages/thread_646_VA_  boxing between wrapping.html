VA: boxing between wrapping

From: thedoubtfulguests | Date: 2004-12-20 02:05:46 UTC
After deleting every message that said T-shirt or flame war in the
subject...;-). I finally managed, between Christmas shopping,
wrapping, and decorating, to get out to letterboxing again.

Replaced the log in DC's Orion the Hunter. I must have replaced
that log because it was full, four or five times now. It makes me
realize that most of my stamps around the area are my earlier, and
cruder.. um, simpler carvings. I've planted newer work in Cape Cod
and Florida but not so much close to home. Got to get working and
brighten up that earlier work with new boxes nearby.

So I am glad I managed to get the Evil Women series planted, and
posted on AtlasQuest. I have not yet vetted the clues so if anyone
tries them I am interested in feedback. I welcome your complaints
about my inclusion of the 'Controversial Entry' into the evil women
category. I should have asked people to vote, like cheese racing.

I've done something really odd with this. I have overlapped two
different series, in the same park, by including one stamp in both
series. Why? Don't ask me, but it seemed right. Only a couple
visitors had made it to the first series anyway. I am hoping that
evil women are a better draw. Who can resist Cruella DeVille?

Walker Bridge Troll - Much better than all that, Night Owl and I
were finally able to hunt for a new local Reston box. How come it
took us so long to look for it when it is in our own neighborhood?
Anyway we found it a very fine and fun stamp and an interesting
hide. Thanks for the fun to Jem Heist. Now we are anxious to head
to Springfield for some more.

Happy hunting out there,
Scarab of the Doubtful Guests
