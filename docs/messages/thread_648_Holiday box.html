Furiously carving a series of stamps for the X-mas holiday. The
12 letterboxes of Chrismas. Yes 12 boxes(13 including the log
box).I have decided to only put out one log at the end of the
series. It's cold and stamping in 12 times might get a little
tedious. I plan on planting tomorrow morning when I walk the
dog, always a good cover and the clues should be up by the
afternoon. The boxes will be on the new rails to trails trail in
Wayne, wheelchair and stoller acessable.
