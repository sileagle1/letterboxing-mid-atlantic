Boxing Day

From: May Day Lillies | Date: 2004-12-21 02:56:20 UTC
So it looks like the early whether reports are calling for a nice
clear boxing day for everyone to go boxing.

So here is a definition of letterboxing off of the internet:

The day after Christmas Day is known as Boxing Day, after the
tradition of opening the alms boxes placed in churches
over the Christmas season. The contents were distributed amongst the
poor of the parish.

December 26 is also known
as the Feast of St Stephen. It is often a day of outdoor sports and
horse racing and hunting [for letterboxes].




So is anyone else going out boxing in our of this event?
