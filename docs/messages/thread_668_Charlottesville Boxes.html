Charlottesville, VA
Date Placed     August 6, 2003
Difficulty    

Easy, drive-up if you can get close parking!
Directions    

The University of Virginia, near the Rotunda.

Clues    

With your back to the main door (south side) of the UVA Chapel, walk to the historical marker ahead and to the right. Note the last two digits of the year of the University's founding. Turn around, and follow the brick path as it passes to the right of the Chapel. It will curve to the right, and you will pass IMP, Z, and TJ. At the point where the path forks, turn left, and walk the number of steps from the year you noted above. Walk to the back of the large tree, and take 4 steps. You should be surrounded by bushes, and the box will be underneath the bush to your right, under some sticks. Please be discrete!


Charlottesville, VA
Date Placed     August 10, 2003
Difficulty    

Over 1 mile round trip along paved path. There are hills, as well as benches along the way.
Directions    

From Interstate 64 outside of Charlottesville, VA, take exit 121-A, Route 20 South, toward Scottsville. Go through one traffic light, and turn left at the flashing yellow light onto Route 53 East, Thomas Jefferson Parkway. Park in the first lot on the right.

Clues    

Start along the path at the left end of the parking lot. From the end of the wooden footbridge, walk 75 paces past the pond below, winding up with a very large and very small tree to your left. On your right, the box lies in a pile of logs and rocks at the base of a small bush a few steps up the hill.
