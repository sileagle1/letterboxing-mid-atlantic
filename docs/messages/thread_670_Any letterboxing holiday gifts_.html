From: Alan Marx | Date: 2004-12-29 01:27:24 UTC
Irene is giving me a huge compliment re: my comedic skills, and I appreciate it...   She and Mr. Irene are very good audience members, and that is half the battle. 
 
Yes, I got my son (new trail name: sonofcomic) his first Signature stamp, carved by dad and his first log book and compass.  We are going out together tomorrow on a search for the 12 days of Christmas by mzscarlet.  This is his second hunt and he is excited.
 
My girlfriend got me a trail backpack filled with EVERYTHING from pink stuff to ponchos,  a whistle, gloves and a first aid kit.  She really did her research and doesn't seem to have left me unprepared for any trail happening, surprise or otherwise.  She also gave me a lovely engraved brass compass.
 
She received a carving kit from me (as I dare not presume to carve her first stamp for her) and a log book and compass.  
 
My Brother in law, a true pirate in search of a treasure at heart, received his first signature stamp carved by me, a log book and compass, and an explanation of Letterboxing as a pursuit he will fall in love with, knowing his personality.  It was fun to watch because I gave him all the pieces before the explanation and he seemed absolutely dumbfounded for a minute until it all became clear.
 
Amazing how quickly this new found hobby of mine seemed to take over the gift giving at the Comedy Ranch!
 
Interested to see what others received.
 
Be good, box well!
 
Somecomic
