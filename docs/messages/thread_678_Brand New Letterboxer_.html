Brand New Letterboxer!

From: highlysung | Date: 2005-01-01 17:51:25 UTC
Hello letterboxing pals!

I've missed you all so much! I spent a little time this morning
catching up with the news on the list. Happy holidays to everyone, I
envy all your recent letterboxing exploits! But we'll be getting back
into the swing of things real soon!

Our new little letterboxer, Carley Jackson Ebbenga, was born on
Thursday, December 23 and came home on Christmas Day! She is
beautiful! We haven't finalized her trail name, but we'd better hurry,
as we are heading up to Palmyra Cove this afternoon for a very short
letterboxing excursion (I've missed it so much!)Somehow, we missed
Mizscarlet's Snail Mail box in October. Irene, if successful, that
will make Carley's first letterbox at 9 days old! How would that rate
with your caving club?

We can't wait to see you all at the Road Rally! Although the baby
might be a handicap for us!

Regarding the discussion on Letterboxing holiday gifts, I was the
lucky recipient and giver of letterboxing goodies: I gave my
sister-in-law (from TX) a treasure hunt themed stocking, and included
a carved personal stamp for her (a depiction of her beloved black lab,
Bailey), compass, stamp pad, log book, clue sheets to boxes near her,
and a HH in her honor to plant. (Along with some other fun items that
went with a treasure hunt theme, just in case she wasn't into it. She
did come with us to find Oh, Be My Friend in July and loved it.)

My father-in-law gave me the greatest stocking - all outdoor themed! A
box from LL Bean that included hiking sticks, really nice binoculars,
a cool nalgene bottle and a GPS unit!! Another SIL gave me a hiking
book and Smartwool socks (love 'em!)

Welp, a beautiful warm breeze is calling me - I can't wait to get out
of the house, even for a tiny drive-by!

highlysung of Team SuddenTurkey
