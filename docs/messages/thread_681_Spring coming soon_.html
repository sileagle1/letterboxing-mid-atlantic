Spring coming soon?

From: justacomic@comcast.net | Date: 2005-01-02 02:35:23 UTC
Happy New year everyone! I hope that 2005 is as beautiful and calm
as the weather was on the trails in the SE PA area this weekend for
all!

I ran out of sunlight today so I am anxiously waiting for the arrival
of spring so that hunts can continue until 8 or 9:00.

Winky has carved her first stamp, so she went with me this weekend.
We grabbed Natty Bumpo and Walker's "America's First Super Highway"
westbound box yesterday, her first find!

We spent New Years eve in Altoona PA, YIPPEE! and woke early to drive
to Lancaster PA area this morning by 11:30. We had a great day.
Weather was INCREDIBLE for january 1 and I told Winkie, I bet there
are a ton of boxers out today. While there we found "Leap Frog" by
Thumbs up. A great hunt with a neat clue. Awesome hike, hilly
wooded, neat location. From there we headed out for Thumbs
Up's "Underground Railroad" series. If you haven't gone after this
one yet, GO! A great series with a wonderful history lesson about
people that need to be remembered. I thought this was a great series
because of the history, the location and the relevance of the hand
carved stamps. Very moving!

OK, now the best part of the day. I gave my Brother in law a LB kit
for Christmas and today he went out with my sister for the first
time. He called me three times throughout the day to tell me what a
great time he was having and that my sisiter was climbing over rocks
and getting messy. He was so excited and fired up about Letterboxing
and I thought "oh oh, another addict is born". They ended up
finding four and now his wife, my sister is planning her first
stamp. How cool it was to hear him discovering the joy we all find
when out on the trail.

So, after today, my count stands at 35f 1HHp.

Happy New Year all.

Be good, box well.

Somecomic and Winky
