What a great tradition ...

From: pamelar123 | Date: 2005-01-03 23:59:03 UTC
Another beautiful New Years Day. Last year (2004) I was out in a
sweatshirt when the little vikings and I planted our Froggy Hollow
box. New Years Day 2005 didn't even require a sweatshirt! We
didn't plant a box, but checked on the White's Ferry box -- and were
pleased to see a few visitors since last check. Hopped on the ferry
and visited the boxed at Balls Bluff on the Virginia side.

Got so motivated by that excursion that the littlest viking and I
went out boxing today in Black Hills Park -- and came home to find
the postings about a regional gathering at the park -- great idea!
It's beautiful -- it was so nice to get on the paths and to
different areas of the park today.

Pam of the Vespa Vikings
