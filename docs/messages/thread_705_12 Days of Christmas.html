12 Days of Christmas

From: Winky | Date: 2005-01-09 23:35:08 UTC
Just got back from finding the 12 Days...you guys were not kidding
about all the foot trafic. I think I met everyone in Wayne
today...and their Mother, Father, Uncle, dogs....! I went with my
mother (she is now hooked in a bad way) and my almost 4 year old
daughter - thought she would be a good diversion. Oh yeah. I need
to teach her what stealth means! All day and at the top of her lungs
we heard..."Mommy - I didn't have a ball - I didn't drop my ball in
there" "What are you talking about Mommy - I didn't throw anything
in there?" "Are you looking for the buried treasures?" "Hurry -
there is no one coming but that man right there!" "You just told me
to take my gloves off - why do you have to put them back on me now?"
My mother and I laughed for the whole 3 hours!

Admist the hilarity we have the pleasure of meeting the infamous
Mizscarlet and #6. It was quite a pleasure! It was a great series
and I am really glad we took the time to find them.

Happy hunting!

Winky
