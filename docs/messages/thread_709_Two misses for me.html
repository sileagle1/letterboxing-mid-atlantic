Well,

I finally got a kitchen pass on Sunday to do some boxin’

I haven’t really been out for several weeks.

 

I went after “Old Hat” in Hatfield first.

Traipsed through a lot of mud.  Pretty sure I found the right tree and log.

Nothing there.

Maybe washed away in the rains? 

Anybody find this one lately?

 

Then I climbed the back side of Spring Mountain looking for Ski Bee.

I found (a) anchor rock.  Maybe there is another?

Not sure I found the right V tree.

Did not find an appropriate BIG rock with crevice.

Any chance this was already pulled for ski season?

 

So, Irene, no body finds a box every time they search.
