From: Tammy, Cat, and Sarah | Date: 2004-05-10 14:43:43 UTC
The ADG is a group of 3 crazy women. Cat and I live in Frederick,
MD and Sarah lives in Ashburn, VA. We have boxes hidden throughout
the tri-state area although most of them have fallen into
disrepair. We were extremely gung-ho when we started boxing in
August of 2002 and blanketed the area finding and placing boxes
until we ran out of steam about a year later.

We are slowly easing our way back into the scene and hope to hide
some new boxes this year. As soon as all of the weddings, birthdays,
and vacations slack off we will be making a beeline for the nearest
park.

Tammy
ADG #1
