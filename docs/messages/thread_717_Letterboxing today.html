Letterboxing today

From: Pamela | Date: 2007-06-25 01:53:50 UTC
Would like to tell you all what a beautiful day we had letterboxing.
Got a bit of a late start but we did find one of Mommo's boxes today.
We were first finders, got a surprise and found our first hitchiker. We
saw three deer on our outing. A baby ran across the street in front of
us driving to the site, still gangly legged with spots, then while
looking for one of the clue spots, looked up and a deer not too far
away and she / he politely stood while I snapped a photo. Then later
while walking back saw another sprint thru the woods in front of us.
Now we are originally from Ga. and have seen Deer on the side of the
road all the time but this was the first (for me anyway) out walking in
the woods. Also it was my Birthday and could not have asked for a
better day. Well I would have liked to have had more time to find more
boxes but hubby was taking me to dinner so.... The day was great, great
clues and boxes and am ready for the next outing. Thanks for letting me
share.

Cannonball and Handyman
Annapolis
