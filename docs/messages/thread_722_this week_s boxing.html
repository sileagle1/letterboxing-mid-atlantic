Inventory is FINALLY over--let there be great rejoicing, & dancing in the streets.  And boxing!
 
I celebrated Tuesday AM by going up to Washington's Crossing for Capt. Moore's Insignia, and on Thursday I found Myke's Lost Cats--all in good shape.  Then Thursday night my sisters & I went to see Somecomic perform--had a blast & got his special personal traveler (fortunately even a BAD joke counts if he hasn't heard it yet!).
 
Spent quite a while at the Wissahickon Public Library today but had to go home disappointed--there is one more clue I haven't figured out.
 
Hopefully tomorrow we will finish up the Chester County Hat Trick; then that will probably be it for the week.
