VA: Icebreaker "Clue" game challenge

From: thedoubtfulguests | Date: 2005-01-22 18:43:18 UTC
I do not know if I can actually get this together in time for the
gather on Saturday but I want to try.

CLUE! Exchange Mystery Game.

As you collect exchanges you will also collect Clue stamps. Figure
who, what, and where is missing and you win the prize.

As people arrive I will pass out simple (or not so simple) stamps
from the 22 Clue images to the first 19 people who agree to play.
Whenever they do an exchange they will also stamp their Clue stamp.
Anyone who leaves before the game is over must pass their Clue stamp
to someone else.

The first person to show all 19 accounted for stamps in their log,
and make a successful accusation, wins.

For those of you who want to bring your own Clue stamps, and I hope
there are many, I will keep a list of what people are doing so there
are no duplicates. If you agree to bring a stamp you must arrive
before noon please.

I know there is not much time. Stamps can be very simple. Since I
will make all the stamps no one else makes, mine will be pretty
simple (except a couple of them).

What do you think? Can we do this? Anyone want to carve a stamp or
two? Get snowed in and carve.

The prize will be a copy of Clue with all the stamps stamped on the
board. If there is interest I will turn the stamp set into a
traveling event set.

Scarab of the Doubtful Guests
write to thedoubtfulguests at yahoo dot com
