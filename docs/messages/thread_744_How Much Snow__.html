What to do in the snow!

From: Alan Marx | Date: 2005-01-24 02:58:42 UTC
After the shoveling and the Eagles game (sorry Pittsburgh), nothing left to do but finally, finally pack up my first letterbox.  It took a lot longer than I thought.  The stamp has been done for a few weeks, but to gather the FF cert, the logbook, the inserts, took longer than I imagined it would.  Now I have to stare at the darn thing until the snow is gone enough to plant it.  I love the stamp, but I don't feel bad about sending it out there since I know where it will be and I can visit it whenever I want to.  I have to admit, when I released my "Sailing out to See" HH, I felt a sense of loss.......    Weird huh?
 
OK, I'll announce when it is planted and the clue will go out.  Enjoy the hunt for my "Birdhouse" letterbox.
 
somecomic
