HH Question

From: JuneMcAllister | Date: 2005-01-27 15:29:04 UTC
Hey ya'll...........From what I always see on the big bb, it seems like most HH disappear into the Letterbox Black Hole, never to be seen again. I have a couple of stamps that I want to make into HH but as procrastination is my middle name, I have yet to do. But I really hate the idea of carving the stamp, setting the little guys out on their way, and having them just seem to go poof at some point. From the posts I see, most people are always looking for their HH that have gone missing.
 
Any opinions?
 
MissMoon
 

Re: HH Question

From: the_cichlid_guy | Date: 2005-01-27 16:13:46 UTC
Hi, I currently have planted 11 hitchhiker stamps. I have heard
something from all but two of those stamps. I would say that it is
true that the hitchhikers seem to fall into a black hole. But you
eventually hear something about them every now and then. It just
takes a really long time. There is that chance though that you will
never hear anything from them or the stamp. It is just the risk that
you take when starting a HH. Another reason could be that it sits
in a box for a couple months too. I found a hitchhiker called Paul
Bunyan and Babe that had been in the box for 9 months.

-Ryan
The Cichlid Guy
