Sorry I missed the Bedlam in DC, sounds like you guys caused quite a ruckus in the mail.
 
I was in Carlisle PA for 2 days.  Found "the line never ends" by Pink Panther.  Nice hunt even in the snow.
 
Was met tonight by MizScarlet and Hubby at a show for most of the oldest people in the world.  They were offended by the word boogers, Not MizScarlet and party, the old people.  They think boogers is offensive?  I have done the "clean" version of my show dozens of times, and never offended anyone.  I am glad I gave them the "clean" version, otherwise there little old heads would have popped right off. Great to meet more boxers though.  MizScarlet earned my personal traveler by telling me a joke, and we did an exchange.  It made the pain of the show easier to take. 
 
I was counting tonight although I really am not too concerned with my numbers, I more enjoy the collecting of the stamps and the hunt, but I was curious.  I have been boxing for exactly 2 months on Jan 29th and my count stands at:
 
P 2 1/2   F42   X4   HH4
 
42 finds?  When do we admit we are powerless over letterboxing and our lives have become unmanageable?  I definitely have a problem.  I am enjoying meeting nice folks and kicking around on the trails though so...  Here's to hitting 100 finds by the spring! 
 
Hope everyone else got out to box this weekend, and I am glad no one was arrested in DC.
