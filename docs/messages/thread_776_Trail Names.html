We're from South-central PA. I've just gotten started, though I read
about letterboxing years ago. There wasn't much available online then
and my daughter was little, so the idea sorta just simmered in my
subconscious for awhile. And though I'm no old crone :) a few years
ago I DID have a bum nearly as wide as a snow shovel ;) Now, thanks to
hiking and having a job that keeps me outdoors most of the time, my snow
shovel bum has shrunk, but I'll never be the 100lb "Slats" that I used
to be. Oh well...

I'm so excited because today I carved my first stamp! It's my Shovelbum
stamp and it isn't too bad, if I do say so myself. I discovered that I
really enjoy carving. Haven't come up with a stamp for the KniseGuys
yet...any suggestions?
