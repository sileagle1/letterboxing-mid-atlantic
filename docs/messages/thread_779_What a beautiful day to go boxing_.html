From: Hikers_n_ Hounds | Date: 2005-01-31 02:03:55 UTC
What a great weekend it was! Found out I'm not to old to cause trouble and still young enough to get bounced from places! Woo-hoo! Okay, so it was only a Mall food court but it felt like the 70's all over again. The MAN was bringin' me down, bummin' me out! But we had a blast and I got to put more faces with familiar trail names and stamps, plus catch up with some old friends. A great day. Today Cyclonic and I did a little boxing in Gaithersburg, MD then went to Great Falls on the Potomac. Found a box or two in Riverbend Park and left one behid for others to enjoy. A beautiful sunny, snowy day. Not too cold, no wind. Just a perfect day to be out and about. We didn't come even close to getting the boxes we had planned on but we sure did enjoy the day. Now back home to husband and kids and of course, the best thing of all, Dude!
