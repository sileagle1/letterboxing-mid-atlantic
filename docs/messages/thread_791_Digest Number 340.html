Okay, I'm really hooked now! I found my first letterbox today. It was
Go Fly a Kite in York Co., PA. I don't know how you guys manage to
stamp discreetly. All I can say is I'm glad its winter and no one else
was in the park because it seemed to take me FOREVER to sign in and
stamp and clean up and rehide. :) Hey, does everyone remember their
"first time"? It would be cool to hear some stories.

Shovelbum



My first (our first) was Puppy Prints in Fischer's Park near Lansdale PA.
It was 95 degrees. I dragged my whole family along.
We waded back into a big bunch of briars and finally found the box.
Only I didn't have a logbook or a stamp yet.
Didn't realize you needed those.
We went back a few days later with the proper equipment and logged our first.
