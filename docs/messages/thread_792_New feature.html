From: fleurdelislb | Date: 2005-02-01 20:29:12 UTC
Yes folks, if you've noticed, we now have the "Database" feature on
the left hand side. A certain LBNA member who shall remain nameless
(but who has a glowing behind) asked for this feature eons ago, and I
just never did it. I finally remembered today and had a spare 3
minutes, so I put it up. Please take a gander and use it at your
discretion.

Please note: It is only for boxes that are officially MIA, or at
least confirmed by more than 2 people (and hopefully the placer) that
it is gone. It is not because you could not find XYZ box buried under
2 feet of snow, so there's no way it could possibly be there! Please
be responsible with it or I will be forced to take it down.

Also, please note, the layout and wording were shamelessly stolen
from the LBSE group :)

Cheers!
