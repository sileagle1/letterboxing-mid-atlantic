That would be cool as well. Though, somehow I get the impression
that CA wineries tend to be more corporate, impersonal and uptight in
general. I have my doubts as to them actually hosting a letterbox
for those reasons, but it would be quite a lot of fun!

The PA wine industry is in its infancy and most are family operations
and very friendly folks. We happen to know the Vynecrest folks for

there with permission was easy. Some of the other wineries around
here have potential, especially the folks we know better.
