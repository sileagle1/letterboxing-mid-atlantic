Misadventures in French Creek

From: Lightnin Bug | Date: 2005-02-13 12:36:50 UTC
Hi all,

The Visigoths Return! The Snow has melted enough to allow for new
Mapsurfian Mischief.

Jan, Cyclonic and I were joined by Karl and Sandy from Jenkintown to
explore the wonderful world of French Creek State Park. We were able
to find a couple more Mapsurfer boxes, including Six Penny Diary and
an undisclosed box in an undisclosed location. We were unsuccessful
in a second undisclosed box. Karl and Sandy are veteran letterboxers
that had found the couple we were able to track down several years
ago. We have extended them an invite to the Road Rally.

Stay tuned for the next Hairy Legged Adventure...including the future
home of Beldin...
