Southern Journey

From: Marie & Bob | Date: 2005-02-18 21:49:02 UTC
Teapot & I headed to FL 2/3/05 for some R&R and some boxing along the
way. Weather was good with a high of 80 On Sanibel Island and a low
of 34 with frost in Ocala. Along the way with some free time we had
16 finds 4 misses 0 exchanges and 0 HH's. Lo and behold who did we
find a few days ahead of us but "WANDA". Hard to find a box she
hasn't been to. Will hasve to check the big list to see if she posted
her finds.
Finds included
NC
The Twins Letterbox
Daniel Boone's parents Resting Site

FL
Lovers Key
Runcible Spoon
The Land Shark
The Place
Siesta Key
Bayside Bounty
Fort DeSoto
Go Fly A Kite
The Pirate
Haslam's Books
Coffeepot Bayou
Spoonbill

GA
Fairchild Oak
Beware the Fish, Hawks and Fish Hawks

Misses
FL
Sunset OverThe Beach
Fish Out of Water
Octopus Vulgaris

GA
Abandoned WWII Airfield

All in all a great trip just to short.Two weeks fly by when
everything goes well. Visited family & friends and did an
Elderhostel.

Teapot & Eagle
