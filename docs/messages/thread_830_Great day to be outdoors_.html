Great day to be outdoors!

From: Hikers_n_ Hounds | Date: 2005-02-19 23:22:12 UTC
After two weekends with nary a hint of boxing, Dude and I finally poked our heads out the door this morning, sniffed the air (well, I did, Dude sniffed his butt) and decided we had to get outside. We headed downtown and had a great time finding "The Persistence of Winky" box. Ton's of people around, it was difficult being discreet but I think we managed to pull it off. We parked on the other side of Kelly Drive in Fairmount Park and took a leisurely stroll up to the Art Museum. Found the box, and hiked around for awhile. Great stamp by Winky!
 
Then we boogied back north and swung by Pennypack Park to find Somecomics' Birdhouse Box. Again, great stamp. Team Wink and a Smile are some wonderful carvers. Dude and I seemed to be the only ones in the park, it was so peaceful. As much as I enjoy getting together with my fellow boxers to go boxing, I must admit that my favorite hunts are when it's just me and my old mutt, taking a meandering stroll through the quiet woods. I can't describe the peace and contentment it brings me. We're not prolific hunters, only 2 boxes in 4 hours, but we sure do enjoy ourselves.
 
Hope everyone had a great day, however you spent it.
 
H&H
