Life is good.

From: Silent Doug | Date: 2005-02-22 02:29:07 UTC
Jennifer and I went out in the fresh snow today here in CT and did a bit of
winter boxing. We found 6 letterboxes and 2 hitchhikers, in total. Not bad
for 90 minutes! <g>

We had our snowshoes on, though, so we got some good exercise while we
enjoyed the wintery scenery. (Should we add "snowshoeboxing" to the glossary?)

On Sunday, we found five (plus a hitchhiker that we'd already found --
dontcha hate it when that happens?), but we were only out for part of the
afternoon....

Another couple of weekends like this and we'll have found all the closest
letterboxes to our home, and then we'll have to drive 10 miles -- at least!
-- in order to find a letterbox! Life is indeed rough here in the northern
parts....

:-)
