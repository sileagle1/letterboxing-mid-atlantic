Where did I box?? I'll tell ya...

From: czappit | Date: 2005-02-21 16:39:11 UTC
Brickman and I had to leave slowerlowerdelaware this weekend to have
our taxes done.. Yah/ My aunt is our CPA in Gettysburg. We did the
SunsetRocks lb in Michaux State park, If anyone of you get the
chance the hike is very nice, parking by a slightly snow covered
stream , and the hike to the ridge of the mountain, i have to say my
legs still hurt today, but once at the top beautiful rock
outcroppings overlooking the valley, its a must, one I would hike
again to bring friends to just for the mountaintop experience,
the box was tucked away just where it should be and a very great
bonus for the hike, not to mention the snow covered rock climb down
the mountain/ Brickman said I need to get in shape to do these
boxes, I said I'm getting in shape by doing these boxes,,, its
just so hard to practice mountainclimbing in Delaware... JC\
Brickman slowerlowerletterboxers.
