Brandywine Box

From: Suzanne Coe | Date: 2005-02-24 15:44:32 UTC
Just to let you know--I went out this morning and replaced the Brandywine Box that has been missing since 10/03 (since NeverEnuff says they have no plans to ever come back from the West Coast).  I haven't done anything on the site yet.  Clues are basically the same (the water fountains are missing but you can see where they were) EXCEPT the box is at the 2nd set of stairs (265 deg. from the Y-tree)--if you go down those stairs and look along the top of the wall, the box is between the 2nd & 3rd stones, behind 2 small stones.  Microbox so BYO ink.  There is not much traffic now; but it's still on the disc golf course so summer boxers will have to be discreet....  Also in the driving directions--Rockland Road is closed; best bet is to pick up Rte 92 W (from I95S or 202N)--after the 202 intersection keep following 92W to the stop sign at Rte 100, where you make a left onto Adam's Dam Road.
 
If this would be a major trip you might want to wait a couple of weeks--you will get more for your money....   ;)  Sheba
