Our New Name

From: chilepepperletterbox | Date: 2005-02-28 04:32:11 UTC
Hello one and all--
After several successful months of letterboxing, and reading
hundreds (thousands?) of posts from a variety of "veteran" boxers,
EEK, Krash and I have decided a few things:
1--There is little reason for us to stamp three different stamps in
every log we find.
2--We will probably never box without one another.
3--The names we chose somewhat hastily lack the pizazz of many of
the other names we've seen on the boards.
As a result, we have decided to combine into a single Team, although
we will still keep our original trail names for times when they're
appropriate, like gatherings or naming boxes (e.g. EEK's Butterfly
Box and EEK's Bunny Box).
I took a vote and everyone who didn't want to sleep on the couch
voted Aye. (It was unanimous.) So here we are: The Kucifers,
a.k.a. ChilePepper, Krash and EEK will henceforth be known as Team
Raucous Auk.
For a special bonus, figure out how we chose the name and then tell
me what a kinkajou is related to and how you know (hint:
procyonidae), and I'll send a clue to a WOM box.
Hope to see you boxing!
ChilePepper
