From: nattybumppolbna | Date: 2005-03-03 20:50:40 UTC
How would people feel about cloisonne (enamel) pins rather than patches? I realize there
is a whole hiking/camping/scouting tradition of patches wound through this hobby, but
pins are transferable from garment to garment. Price-wise I think they are comparable.

Also I think there is a bigger demand for items now and if you get stuck with excess
inventory it would be easier to get rid of it on BoxHeads.

Re: [lbma] Pins, Patches, T Shirts...

From: Art Vandelay | Date: 2005-03-04 02:01:14 UTC
Well, I collect pins so I would definitely buy one.  However, let me tell you why I love the patch!  One day Warcat 76 was on a PA towpath coming from a box.  A couple passed us but we didn't think much of it since lots of people were on the path that day.  After they passed us however, I turned around and spotted the LBNA patch on the backpack of none other than our own Silent Doug (Jennifer was with him).  Had it not been for that patch, I would have missed meeting them and getting the exchange.  So for that reason alone, I would like to have a patch for our backpacks.
Cat
