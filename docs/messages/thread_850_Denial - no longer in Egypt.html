Denial - no longer in Egypt

From: trainear@comcast.net | Date: 2005-03-04 18:56:30 UTC
I can't drive down the road without passing a wooded area and think, "is there a box in there somewhere?"
Or if there isn't, I wonder if it's private property and can I put one there.

I can't read a magazine or watch TV without thinking, I wonder if I can carve that?
Mental? I guess so.


Just how addicted to this insanely, wonderful hobby have you become? Here's my
most recent epiphany. I actually turned the stove on and cooked the other day
and there were some left overs. Being the slob that I am and knowing I have a
son who works 2nd shift I left them on the stove for him and/or the trash or
both. I woke up the next day and *gasp*!! SOMEONE USED ONE OF MY LETTERBOXING
CONTAINERS TO PUT THE LEFT OVERS IN!!! I was freakin' out and my husband looked
at me like I have 5 heads, which is just crazy 'cause I only have 2, and says
"Hon, that's what those things were MADE FOR". And I swear, for about 1/2 a
second I thought HE was nuts and stared at him as if he was speaking in tongues
when it hit me, OH YEAH! That's what I used to by these little plastic
containers for! I honestly had forgotten that little fact for a moment.

So how 'bout you?
