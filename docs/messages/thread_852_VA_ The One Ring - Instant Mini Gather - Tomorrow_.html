"I haaaave the Preeeeeciousssss!"
 
Donutz' fantastic postal letterbox arrived today:  The One Ring.  The most beautifully set up and interesting letterbox I have seen since Gollum at the Mansfield Monster Mash.  Really cool stamp too.  She graciously allows sharing.  I would not take this to an actual gather but the few people who might actually show up should be alright. 
 
I propose meeting at the most Shire like local place I know, The Old Brogue Irish pub in Great Falls, VA.  By the way, there are a bunch of boxes around Great Falls. 
 
Anybody game?
 
I am thinking 1:00pm to 3:00pm or something. 
 
The Old Brogue
760-C Walker Road, Great Falls
703-759-3309

From the Beltway near American Legion Bridge take Georgetown Pike west, a beautiful sunday drive, past Great Falls park to the town of Great Falls at Walker Rd. 
 
Write me at thedoubtfulguests at ya hoo dot com to confirm and plan.  And check this space for an update tomorrow.
 
I am open to other ideas and suggestions from anyone who could come sometime on Saturday.  Know any more convenient pub like places?
