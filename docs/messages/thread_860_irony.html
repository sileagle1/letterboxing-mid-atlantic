I went out for mystery boxes! Dn'A's Toxic Twosome, Too Much Trash
and Dead Fish boxes. They used 'unconventional' containers. Nice box
series and I wasn't even attacked by a rabid pack of rats. Not much

series. The first time, it was dark before I got there (by myself
with no flashlight) and I turned around and went home. The second
time I went to the wrong place, no time to regroup, and the third I
was rained out. I don't know if I could have brought myself to go
back there a fifth time.
