Patch Logo Contest

From: fleurdelislb | Date: 2005-03-09 21:48:06 UTC
Put on your thinking caps and get your creative juices flowing! The
patch logo contest is here and needs your input!

Since Irene has decided to be a masochist and willingly create more
merchandise for LBMA, we are holding a contest to design the logo
that will be featured on patches (and may be included on future
merchandise if Irene escapes from the nuthouse).

Specific information on the patch, as far as pricing, material,
delivery, etc., will be available AFTER the contest is over and is
not currently available.

The rules are as follows:

Rules:

* Entries must be placed in the "Patch Logo Contest" folder in the
Files section to the left, in jpg or bmp format.
* Colors cannot exceed 6 - which include black and
white. Multiple entries by the same contestant are
accepted.
* Entry deadline is March 20, 2005; voting commences
on March 21.
* Winning entry as of midnight EST on March 31, 2005
will be the winner.
* The winning entry is subject to final approval by a
panel of 3 judges.
* Participants understand that their logo may be used on merchandise
for distribution among fellow letterboxers and not for profit.


Cheers!
Fleur de Lis
