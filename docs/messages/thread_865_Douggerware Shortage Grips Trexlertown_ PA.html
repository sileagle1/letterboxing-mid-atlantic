From: nattybumppolbna | Date: 2005-03-11 03:03:43 UTC
Have you tried the Dollar Tree stores?

There is one on Hamilton and other Dollar Trees In Allentown (Tilghman St) Whitehall and
Bethlehem. All great sources of the coveted Holiday/Milan Classic Douggerware. If you do
go check them out, could you check for Be Sharp Vinyl erasers? They come in 4 packs: 1 x
1.5" x 3.75" and 3 x .75" x 2.5". If you find them I would be happy to trade you for SIlent
Douggerware (my Dollar Store still has a good stock.) Hell, I'll even bring them to you.

And to be clear... I haven't certified the new SureFresh boxes. The lids are made of a
"rubber" part and a hard plastic part which makes me suspicious of their longevity. What I
found interesting was that all of the boxes allowed air to escape - which may explain the
box condensation problems we have - even in high quality boxes. The more expensive
Rubbermaid version had the same problem.
