Where did YOU box this weekend? H&H was in DC.

From: Hikers_n_ Hounds | Date: 2005-03-14 11:18:53 UTC
H&H, in the form of Irene and Mr. Irene, headed down to our nations capital and had a wonderful weekend. Saturday, after getting a very late start, we searched for Mr. Jefferson's letterbox. The clues were great, the walk around the tidal basin was just beautiful. We parked at the Jefferson Memorial and hiked past the FDR memorial, which was really impressive, past the Pagoda, the Lantern, over the second bridge...and the clues just stopped. Nothing made sense anymore. The clues referenced a Library which we couldn't find. So we tried to work it out as best we could but nothing made any sense. I tried to not be so literal and broaden my interpretation of what a "library" could be, but still no luck. So we hiked back to our car and still enjoyed the wonderful walk. We were going to go after Orion the Hunter but were losing the light. Sunday we did find Poison Ivy's Li-ii-iii-iiiions. Another gorgeous walk on a beautiful day. The only problem was that there is a homeless person living exactly where the 3rd Lion is so we had to leave it alone. But we had a lot of fun and the stamps are awesome.
 
Have a great week everybody,
 
H&H
