This took about 20 minutes of walking time (at most a mile, probably a

avoid the wasp time. YMMV. I found all four in August. A caching friend
reported finding one by accident last month.

> Great Outdoors Series 2 (how long is the hike?)

From the description, it's a drive by if the parking lot is open, and less
than 200 yards if not. On my to do list.

> Banyan Box

250 yards or so each way. Take old shoes. Found in July.

> Mr. Moody Goes to City View Park

Found on Sunday. Maybe 400 yards total. Both in good shape. But you have to
be very athletic to avoid thorns on one of them.

> EEK's Bunny Box

My other Sunday find. Maybe 300 yards for this one. I was first letterboxer.
A cacher found it before me. I don't know why, the cache is too far away for
this to be a conflict (I do both and found both). His batteries must have
died. He never found the cache.

> VA Beach
and
> Munden Point Rail Line

Opposite sides of the same park. Maybe a half mile if you park your car
once. You can shorten that to 600 yards if you park twice. Found in August.

> John and Jackies Wacky Letterbox

and I didn't think it would survive a month in that spot.

> The Old Maid

description.
