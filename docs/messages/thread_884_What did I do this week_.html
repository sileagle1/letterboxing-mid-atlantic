What did I do this week?

From: nattybumppolbna | Date: 2005-03-19 02:55:49 UTC
Boxed in Boston as best I could. I found Head by the Charles, which was a fun little jaunt
to the river. I tried to leave a box behind but was foiled at every turn. I spent hours in
Boston Public Garden, but every place was too obvious, to populated, or too temporary.
I found what I thought was a good place actually inside one of the millions of
commemorative statues in Beantown. But as I walked away from the plant, one of the
town's resident, um... vagabonds(?) came on it and took it apart looking for valuables. I
recovered what was left and retired to the bar to celebrate St. Patrick's fine
accomplishments.

I did acquire a hitchiker from far abroad. Who knows where I will set it free?

Also I think I indoctrinated some converts from Maine. New blood for our downeastern
brothers and sisters. But I did warn them to stay away from Connecticut boxes - they are
hardly worth the effort.

Natt-
