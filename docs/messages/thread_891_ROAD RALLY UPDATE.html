ROAD RALLY UPDATE

From: rallymastr | Date: 2006-05-01 16:37:38 UTC
I will have coffee, juice, water, soda and donuts, muffins, etc for the morning and a couple
of 6 foot hoagies for the afternoon. If you would like something else, please feel free to
bring it along.

I will post your tentative start times today on the blog
http://www.roadrally.blogspot.com

Please review them ASAP. Positions were determined by your preference and the order in
which you registered. If you would like to change, I will do what I can until Wednesday.
After Wednesday, you will need to find someone to switch with yourself.

We do have a few more slots available for teams.

A reminder about your plans for Eeevil. You may not alter the boxes in any way that
proves to be a disadvantage to the teams that follow you. You can add to the box but you
may not move or remove any component of the box. Teams are only required to complete
the boxesplaced by the Rally. Participating in any additional Eeevil (much like life) should
be of the competitors free will.


An IMPORTANT REMINDER. Please review the rules on the blog. You will need to be
familar with them at rally time. There will be a test.

An other VERY IMPORTANT REMINDER. While this is a competition of speed - of sorts- you
must follow all the normal conventions of letterboxing. This includes retrieving and
replacing the boxes with stealth. Retrieving, replacing or stamping in in full view of
Noxers* is not permitted. You must make every effort to ensure that the box is replaced
properly and available to the team that follows you. This was not an issue last year in the
world's record deluge, but if the weather is nice, it could be a major factor this year.

If there are people around and you cannot replace the box unobserved, you must take one
for the team and wait. You are all ON YOUR HONOR to do this. We do not have the
resources to police every box and the rally cannot continue without your best efforts.

Grumpy Curmudgeon Hat is off now. I'm really looking forward to this year. It's gonna be
fun.

The Rally Master

* Noxers= Non-Letterboxers, ie civilians
