From: Hikers_n_ Hounds | Date: 2005-03-23 01:25:03 UTC
Timewise this might be a little far for you, but my favorite place in West Virginia is in the Seneca Rocks area. There is camping, caverns,and a gorgeous summit to hike or climb, supposedly with a box on top but it might be MIA. I didn't know about letterboxing last time I hiked it. Spruce Knob, the highest point in the state is not far and there are 4-5 boxes in it (2 of them mine!). It's just the greatest area.
 
Another nice place was Endless Caverns campground. Beautiful campground, great caverns on site to explore.
Anyway, just a few ideas.
