Mushroom Kingdom and Universal Language, Vienna, VA

From: EarlyBird | Date: 2004-05-21 03:26:17 UTC
This is my first post on the local list. Please let me know if you
think I should also post this on the main list. (I'm not sure folks
there have time to read about actual LB'g what with all the flaming
they've been doing lately!)

Found both these boxes today exactly as described. We (me and six
kids) had looked for Mushroom Kingdom a year ago and failed to find
it. Today we (me and ten million cicadas) looked in the right place
and found it with no trouble at all. (Do you think there could be a
connection?) Mushroom Kingdom is in fine shape, UL is also but is a
tiny bit damp and could probably use a dry out. The book is dry, but
the baggie is getting a bit beat up and could leak in a bad storm. (I
keep meaning to bring a box repair kit with me!)

BTW, I left a note in the book on the fascinating story of the
"amphitheater" at Universal Language. You can read more here:

http://www.co.fairfax.va.us/parks/resources/freedomhill.htm

Last note - both of these boxes had been found by non-LBers, who wrote
polite, confused notes ("I have no idea what this is") in the books
and carefully replaced them. Maybe BeMa has made two new LBers?

- Early Bird
