From: mothrnatr | Date: 2005-03-26 21:06:16 UTC
Hi,

I am new to this group but have been part of the newboxers and
letterboxers yahoo group for a while.

I am also a GS leader and have had to have patches made for Girl
Scouting events. Price is generally determined by three things: The
shape of the patch, (round and square much cheaper than funky sized
patches) the number of colors, we generally limit it to 4, and the
percentage of embroidery on the patch. Some of the ones that have
little background color require more embroidery (like the one with the
man and the trees). The ones with the black and blue backgrounds
could just be material as opposed to the embroidery.

A patch company tha tI have used before is called Design-it and they
do have a website and email service. Let me know if you need more
patch companies.

Sylvia
