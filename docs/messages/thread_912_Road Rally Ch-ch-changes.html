Patch Finalists - a new poll

From: Silent Doug | Date: 2005-04-01 20:50:24 UTC
Because we had a couple of patch ideas that were "variations on a theme,"
some of the votes were split among two or more choices that differed
slightly in color or text but were very similar. When we added together the
votes for each individual "group," we ended up with a three-way tie. We
figured that some people might prefer the patch concept on which they voted
no matter the color, so we're going to have a run-off vote of three
finalists. Two of the finalist groups contain two similar patches, and
we're assuming that a vote for that group is a vote for either, and we'll
base the final decision if one of those groups wins on issues such as final
cost, ability to reproduce in embroidery and other factors.

The file patch_finalists.jpg is in the Patch Logo Contest folder in the
Files section of the site:
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/lbma/files/Patch%20Logo%20Contest/
and the three groups are numbered 1 through 3. Take a look and then vote
for your favorite here:
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/lbma/polls

Voting will end on April 10th. Vote early and often!

Doug
