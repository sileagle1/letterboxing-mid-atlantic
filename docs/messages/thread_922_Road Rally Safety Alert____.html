Road Rally Safety Alert!!!!

From: Arthur Pewty | Date: 2005-04-01 14:15:23 UTC
A new wrinkle...

Our insurance company has officially notified us that
as we will be letterboxing in State Game Lands, all
participants will be required to wear blaze orange
vests and - get this - Kevlar Chest Protectors.

At first I thought we were sunk, but I have found a
Military Supply Company in the Lehigh Valley that will
provide us with (used) Kevlar (Bulletproof) vests
returning from Iraq. Bill Vincent, of DEFENS
Wholesale and Retail In Bethlehem has offered us a
rental on these vests at an incredible discount rate.

I know it is a late change, but we will lose our
insurance if we do not comply. DEFENS will have the
vests delivered to the park tomorrow morning in time
for the third run of the day., I am purchasing vests
for the first two teams in the rally. If nobody gets
shot, we should be able to return them.

What I need from everybody is to go to my web site and
give me size and quantity info. There will also be
info on how you can Paypal the fee to me. Everyone in
the vehicle must have a vest - even if they do not
plan to enter the State Game Lands.

I wish I had known about this earlier, but I just
opened the insurance letter last night. You can
imagine my shock and horror.

Here is the link to the site please send the info and
payment ASAP.

<a
href="http://www.members.aol.com/nattybumppolbna/kevlarvest.html">KEVLAR
VEST ORDER FORM</a>
