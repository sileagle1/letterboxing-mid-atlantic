Thanks so much to Mr. and Mrs. Rallymaster and the the whole family. I was overwhelmed by the amount of work, planning. clues, prizes and games that went into this wonderful Road Rally. I know I say this after every gathering but seriously, I can't remember when I have had more fun!! And I agree with Janila, the rain just made it more so. Sewsowbizzy and I careening around in her red Toyota, festooned inside with purple and red everywhere to represent our Red Hat Boxers. Judy yelling and holding clues going one way, me ignoring her, cursing and heading the other way, and LOL! both of us being wrong. We walked through swamps, fell in the mud, drove into peoples private drives, got lost, and lost, and lost again. And somehow still came in second place! The prizes were great, the contests a blast, the food hit the spot and the road rally was just perfect. I wouldn't have changed anything!! And Dave, I was telling Sewbizzy and Cyclonic that the rally length was just right. Too many boxes and we would have been exhausted, plus not have had time to socialize. This was just right.
 
Bravo Arthur and Deidre!! aka Mr. and Mrs. Smith, LOL!
