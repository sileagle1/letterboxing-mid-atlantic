Thanks for the great photos Jan! I am usually one to snap a lot of shots but I was so
overwhelmed with everything that I didn't get one of you 2, so sorry! We were together so
much on the trail there were a lot of great shots of you I could have gotten. How about us
sitting there looking blankly at that fence that closed the final stretch of road to the
second box? Or us passing each other going opposite directions looking for Foy Park "2
miles" down the road. Best one yet, I should have gotten you trying to replace that box up
in the tree in the pouring rain. Us vertically challenged boxers had a lot of fun retrieving
that one!

Despite distracting me from shooting lots of photos, being overwhelmed at the Road Rally
was a terrific experience. Overwhelming warmth from fellow boxers, careful planning on
the part of the Rally Master & family, rain from the skies, mud from the ground, exchanges
to be made, and generous prizes given out made this a day to remember.
