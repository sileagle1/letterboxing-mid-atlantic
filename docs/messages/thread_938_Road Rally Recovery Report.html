Road Rally Recovery Report

From: rallymastr | Date: 2005-04-05 15:12:16 UTC
I went back to the scene of the crime(s) to recover the boxes from the Rubbermade Road
Rally. I have recovered most of the boxes and they will soon be on their way home.

The bad news is Box #1 looks to have been under about 8 feet of water at one point. It
appears to have gone MIA. The whole area is covered with flotsam. Many trees came
down and many others were deposited there. I spent about an hour digging through logs
and branches with no joy. So the wonderful Jeep stamp by the MayDay Lillies is gone as
well as the cuckoo clue left by Janila. And here I thought we made it through without
casualties.

I will be back in the area in the next few weeks and will try again, but I am not hopeful.

In addition, would the owners of Camera Shy Owls and the Falcon Microbox please contact
me off-list.

My many, many thanks to everyone who helped make the Rubbermade Road Rally a
success, and to the incredible generousity of the participants.
