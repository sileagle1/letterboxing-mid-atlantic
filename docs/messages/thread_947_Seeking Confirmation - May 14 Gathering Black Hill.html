Hmmm. It was on the calendar. I guess it disappeared with the Yahoo
restructure! Here are the basics. I will be putting updates on the
AtlasQuest.com event board.

The LBMA Spring Gather will be held on Saturday, May 14, 2005.

Place: Black Hill Regional Park, 20930 Lake Ridge Dr., Boyds
(Germantown), MD
Time: 11 a.m. until 5 p.m.

Camping is available nearby at Little Bennett Regional Park.
Furry friends on leashes are welcome!

The park, including lake, is 1,843 wooded acres. Little Seneca Lake
is 505 acres, with 15.7 miles of shoreline, great for fishing.
Rowboats, canoes and kayaks are available to rent. There is also a
nice nature center.

There are over 3 miles of paved trails and 10 miles of unpaved
trails, accessible to hikers, mountain bikers and horseback riders.
There are currently 19 listed letterboxes hidden in the park and
many more within a short driving distance.

I have reserved a large pavilion with restrooms, playground, many
tables and grills. Charcoal fires and hotdogs will be provided (or
you may bring something to throw on a grill). Attendees are asked to
bring a potluck dish to share. There is a volleyball court and
horseshoe pit if letterboxing isn't enough for you (BYO).

We are planning to keep this a casual meet and greet with
intermittent letterboxing frenzy, but are open to suggestions. I
will need a rough headcount, so please email me (donnas28@...)
or rsvp on AtlasQuest.com if you plan to attend or if I can answer
any questions.

Stay tuned for the announcement of a special temporary series:
Boch's Circus, presided over by Phineas Boch, Ringmaster
extraordinaire!

Hope to see many letterboxing friends: old and new, from near and
far!!

Your co-hosts,
Donna (Tempus Fugit) and Scarab
