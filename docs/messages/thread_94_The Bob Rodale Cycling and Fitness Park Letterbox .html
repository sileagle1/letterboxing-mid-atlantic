The Bob Rodale Cycling and Fitness Park Letterbox

From Allentown, PA, take route 222 West. Continue on Route 222 for
0.2 miles after it crosses Route 100. Turn right on Mosser Road. A
sign will show this as the way to the Lehigh County Velodrome. The
Velodrome is on your right, and the park is on your left. A parking
lot is provided.

From the welcome sign at the parking lot, head 300° towards the
monument. Take a time to reflect and remember when Bob Rodale was
born and died. From the monument, head 318° on a path that curves
west and enter the woods. Be sure to obey the rules, remember that
phone number (abc) def- hijk and follow the command to turn left! By
now your heading should be 4 times the difference between the birth
and death year of Mr. Rodale. Head in the slow lane for about 1/4
mile. At this point, any self-respecting letterboxer would choose a
trail over a paved road! Your bearing should decrease by 10°. Take
the earth path from this part of the phone number: (abc) def-hijk -
baj° is your bearing. Along the path you will find all sorts of
cool things like a small birdhouse covered in corncobs. Your path
will now snake to bbh° and then 5a°, then due north then finally
k5°. You will come to a boardwalk, follow it through the swampy area
and past 3 totally ducky benches. At the end of the boardwalk there
will be a small stone monument to a Reading, PA bank that got gobbled
up by Core States which got gobbled up by First Union (hint: the name
started with M and describes a "a great circle on the surface of the
earth passing through the poles"). The box is so close you can smell
it! From the center of the end of the boardwalk, go forward 5 paces
at a bearing of 7f°, then look under a log at bjf°.

The first to find will get a special bonus of a rather chubby dawg!

To exit the part, go north on the gravel path that will emerge from
the forest. Pass McAfee's rock to another boardwalk that will return
you to the monument.
