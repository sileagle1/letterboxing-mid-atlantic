LB,
So the tree frog is there?  Good.
I plan to replace with a hand carved version later this spring.
Got a real nice Blue Butterfly carved, just need to run it out to the park.
I have been working on a 5 stamp project for my Adventure Guides group.
These will be (unfortnuately) located on private camp grounds and not generally available to the public.  I placed them yesterday, but may need to fine tune the clues for our younger seekers. Moral: you can't be too careful.  Suggest bug repellent of some variety.
 
I liked Planes, trains and autos too.  For anyone who has to travel much, it really hits home.  I'd be up for a joint planting project.
