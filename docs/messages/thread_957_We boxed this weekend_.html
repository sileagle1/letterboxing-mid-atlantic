We boxed this weekend!

From: highlysung | Date: 2005-04-12 02:56:45 UTC
We finally made it back out on the trail on Sunday afternoon! My mom
(Lunabar) joined Gwenllian, Lil'E, and me for the day. Iplaytunes is
in the UK right now. Unfortunately, he wasn't able to fit in any
letterboxing.

Found Messenger of Peace - the box is in great shape, but needs a new
logbook - we used the last page. We put some extra pages in the back,
but it will need a new log soon. Does anyone know how to reach Perfect
Circle Letterboxing Guild? I haven't figured out a way to contact them
to let them know.

Also found Ssssssss! - Great box, Bev! I wanted to keep going and
check in on "O Be My Friend", but the rest of the gang was tired.

Gorgeous day to be out boxing. Saw lots of folks near Messenger of
Peace who looked like they were out geocaching - they didn't seem to
be looking in the direct area of Messenger of Peace, though.

Lots of ticks out, though. I pulled one off of myself this AM, even
after checking when we got home yesterday. Yuck - I've been itchy all day!

highlysung of Team SuddenTurkey

Try this for pclg: yahoo@...
It had someone named Brian at the other end last summer when I needed
to contact them. Someone here knew an alternate way into the LbNA
database which revealed the email address from which clues get
submitted, and I can't remember who, or how they did it.
(Note to those using their own webpages for clues - include contact
information or button - something the equivalent of the LbNA "contact
the placer" button.)
The PCLG boxes are, IMO, some of the finest boxes, with the most
intriguing clues, in NJ. Unfortunately, it seems the group went on one
big planting/posting/finding blitz a few years back and then subsided,
leaving these boxes for the most part unmaintained. The website itself
was updated about a year ago, but the clues remain pretty static, and
don't always reflect true box status.
I'm glad M of P is still there and OK; it was my second find when I
started boxing.
