A rare mid-week boxing adventure! "Creature of Fountainhead Park"

From: gallant_rogue | Date: 2005-04-13 13:58:43 UTC
Hiking boots & backpack already in the car, at 5:00 O'clock Tuesday I
fled my government office building, stripping off my tie and heading in
direct pursuit of the Creature of Fountainhead Park. I've been anxious
to do this famous "Scooby Series" since I started this thing called
Letterboxing back in January, but had assumed it was off limits as the
park was closed for the season. Even by changing clothes at traffic
signals, I didn't reach the trail in time to find the whole series
before darkness chased me from the park. (And you have to enter and
exit through an old cemetery at that!) I did find Scooby, Velma &
Daphne. Fred and Shaggy will have to wait for another day.

But three finds on a weeknight is "ok"

Thank You Knights of Colombo!
