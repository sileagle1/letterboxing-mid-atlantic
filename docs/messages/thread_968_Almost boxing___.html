last week and this. I planted a box last week. Where are the clues?
Weeellll...there aren't any. I put one box in this "park" and went
back the next day to plant another only to find a gate across the
trailhead. Inquired inside the building and was told that there is now
a $5 fee for park use. Five! Zero to five? There are two short trails,
a small lake (to look at, no use allowed) picnic tables, etc. I paid
my money and picked up my box.

Thought I'd check out a little lake on the edge of a forest, paved
roads nearby. I drove along a road which approached the lake, the
pavement ended and by the time I realized it was too late...it was too
late. The road turned left, then right as it went downhill filled with
potholes and depressions as it narrowed. At 1/2 mile I reaced an
intersecting road. I couldn't bring myself to turn around and go back
the way I'd come so, onward it was. Sand, mud, puddles, small lakes,
the road was just barely above the surrounding flooded swamp in areas.
Three miles and about an hour and I was back in civilization. I've
never been so glad to see pavement before.

I went out yesterday to hund instead and, wanna guess, didn't find the
box.

Things will only get better. They have to, they couldn't get worse,
could they?
