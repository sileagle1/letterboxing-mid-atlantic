From: Lightnin Bug | Date: 2005-04-15 01:47:00 UTC
Thinking outside the Maryland Box, you can come up to scenic PA and
hunt for "The Pinnacle", "Pulpit Rock" and "Pulpit Rock Observatory"
near Hamburg, PA.

Everything a commando boxer could want.... 9 mile round trip, 1000 ft
elevation gain, some serious rocks, 3 letterboxes (and a hitchhiker as
of last August) and oh the views. Trust me, it is a favorite hiking
place of mine, boxes or no boxes.

Lightnin' Bug

From: Jan | Date: 2005-04-16 23:31:33 UTC
Seven miles?? That was only seven miles??? My body says it was fifty
six miles! We did it, though, we conquered the Pinnacle and are happy
to report that we found all three boxes and True Indigo and RGBisMe
also managed to find two geocaches. Pulpit Rock and Pulpit Rock
Observatory were in fine shape - we were first finders on the
Observatory box. Not so good news about the Pinnacle box though, it
had been damaged by critters and the log book was thoroughly soaked.
True Indigo had repair supplies stashed in her bag and we replaced the
log book and the damaged box.

It was an absolutely perfect day for hiking, wonderful hiking
partners, cool enough to keep a jacket on all day, beautiful sunshine
and absolutely stunning views. Walking to the Pinnacle has been an
item on my "100 things I must do in my lifetime" list and I am now
thrilled that I can check it off as completed. I have never been so
tired in my life but felt so good before.

Jan of Team Little Dog
