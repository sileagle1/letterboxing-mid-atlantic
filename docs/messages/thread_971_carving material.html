From: The Doubtful Guests | Date: 2005-04-15 16:35:17 UTC
I used to pray that Michael's would not be out of the pink stuff again, and they were often out because of me.  I still like it fine but I am now very fond of PZ-Kut from www.steampeaz.com.  (By the way they are closed until next week for a wedding.)  PZ-Kut is a little firmer and less wiggly than the pink stuff.  Also I think they treat the surface to give it a little texture to hold the ink better. 
 
You might try this link for the pink stuff:
 
http://www.atlasquest.com/marketplace/misterart/
 
Scarab

Re: carving material and Off Topic PWD

From: Lightnin Bug | Date: 2005-04-16 22:11:19 UTC
The fave of us frugal Hairy Leggers is the Grey Stuff from Dick
Blick. It is available in 11x17 sheets that are about 1/4" thick
(will need backing unless a small stamp) for about $12. It is
unofficiallyu endorsed by Cichlid Guy, Cyclonic and Lightnin' Bug.
It is said to impart Mapsurfer solving capabilities. ;-)

Off topic - Today Thomas (Kirby Rider or Thomas for those who have
met my little sidekick) did his last ever act in a Cub Scout Role.
He moved up to Boy Scouts in late March and had qualified for the
Trexler District Pinewood Derby by winning our Pack's race in late
Feb. His little Bro, Andy had come in 2nd. Well, at the District
Race (made up of all the op finishers from like 30 packs), Andy came
in 25th/72 and Tom came in 4th/72! Great Job Boys.

We celebrated by eating some Rita's and clearing some brush in the
backyard (a red state thing in this blue state) ;-).

Take care,

LB
