Douggerware Alternative

From: Judy B | Date: 2005-04-16 20:12:49 UTC
I have not found any of the Milan Classic at Dollar Tree Northern VA
(that'll be at the two stores I've looked in.) And I have bought
several other brands but they all leaked UNTIL yesterday, I got
TONTARELLI (made in Italy, they know the meaning of "don'ta spilla the
saucea")

It passed the submarine test (paper towel, close, pounce this way and
that in a vat of water) and the towel came out 100% dry. It is also
marked "-30 degrees C" so it might even hold up thru winter. The lids
are opaque white or blue, bottom is clear. The size is slightly
larger (6.5"sq x 3") than Milan (6"sq x 2.5") - so suitable for
housing HH's without jamming lid on.

There were not a lot at the store, so I'll have to go buy them all.
Although if I had a choice I would buy the Milan/Dougerware for the
size and pretty green lid.
