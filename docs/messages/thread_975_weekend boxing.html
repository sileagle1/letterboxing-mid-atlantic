weekend boxing

From: Suzanne Coe | Date: 2005-09-12 00:03:58 UTC
After suffering acute withdrawal symptoms from 2 weeks of no boxing, I was finally able to get out this weekend. Yesterday I found hexacat's latest, "Mr. Ed Wings the Blues"--definitely worth the hike necessary to "earn" the stamp.  Today (start of my 2nd year of letterboxing!) we took the Raindears' suggestion & headed up to Green Lane Park for the Scottish/Irish Festival, which was a LOT of fun.  While we were there I found 2 of their boxes, Golden Oak & Under the Sea.  Also tried for 2 more but came up empty: Green Tree Frog (could not find the right spot) and Caber Toss (appears to be missing?).

I spent yesterday AM at Possum Hill (Newark, DE) trying not to look like a deer--either my blaze orange worked or there were no archers in the wood, because nothing twanged by me and I found the Explorer series without any trouble.  Today I went to Willow Grove for PA Smith's latest Home for the Holidays stamp--that was fun!  Also tried to find Zwxtr #1 on the Powerline Trail, but either I was in the wrong place or it's gone missing--has anyone seen that one lately?
