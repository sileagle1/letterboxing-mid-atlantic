LBMA Spring Gather and Boch's Circus

Date: Saturday, May 14, 2005
Place: Black Hill Regional Park, Pavilion J
http://www.mc-
mncppc.org/parks/facilities/regional_parks/blackhill/index.shtm
20930 Lake Ridge Dr., Boyds (Germantown), MD
Time: 11 a.m. until 4 p.m.

I have reserved a large pavilion with easy access to restrooms and a
playground. Furry friends on leashes are welcome. (There is a dog
playground in the park.)

Charcoal fires and hotdogs/rolls will be provided (or you may bring
something to throw on a grill). Attendees are asked to bring a
potluck dish to share. If you would rather bring canned soft drinks
instead of potluck, please contact me! (Sorry, Poison Ivy, but
alcoholic beverages are prohibited.)

Black Hill Park is a few miles off Interstate 270, exit 18. In
addition to a large lake, there are over 3 miles of paved trails and
10 miles of unpaved trails, accessible to hikers, mountain bikers
and horseback riders. There are currently 29 listed letterboxes
hidden in the park and many more within a short driving distance.
Print out clues for Boyds, Germantown, Damascus, Gaithersburg,
Hyattstown (all in Montgomery County, MD).

I will need a rough headcount, so please email me
(donnas28@...) or rsvp on AtlasQuest.com if you plan to attend.

Camping is available nearby at Little Bennett Regional Park.
http://www.mc-
mncppc.org/parks/enterprise/park_facilities/little_bennett/index.shtm
Hotels in Germantown include Hampton Inn and Fairfield Inn.
Or check out this cool B&B:
http://www.bbonline.com/md/pleasantsprings/

SPECIAL ANNOUNCEMENT!
This event marks the premiere of Boch's Circus, presided over by
Phineas Boch, Ringmaster extraordinaire: The most amazing,
impressive, stupendous collection of exotic animals, feats of skill,
physical entertainment, side show wonders and magic ever to be found
hidden in the forests and parks around our country. After a limited
appearance at Black Hill, the Circus will pack up and move on to
other venues.

We are expecting over 50 attendees. Thank you all for your amazing
response to this event!
