MD Patapsco Camping Pictures up

From: thedoubtfulguests | Date: 2004-05-24 17:13:52 UTC
I loaded up in the photo section some pictures from my blurry Palm
of last weekends MD Patapsco Valley Camping Gather. If anyone wants
more name clarfication write and I'll edit them in.

I second Hiker & noHound's enthusiastic report on the weekend. Was
really glad to see Psychomommy, Tempus Fugit, Dead Duck Chuck and
Claire de Lune who didn't camp but stopped by.

Night Owl and I are determined to go back and find the cave
letterbox, site of the infamous bird attack. Somone want to tell
that story?

Was very interested to see Hall's stamp marked PFX200 in
Psychomommy's Rufus box.

Many thanks for the shared food as Night Owl and I only managed to
get out the door with a tent and a sleeping bag. Mesquite grilled
burgers and a grande breakfast.

Many thanks to Celtic Pride for getting us together.

Scarab of the Doubtful Guests
