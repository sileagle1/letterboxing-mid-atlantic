From: Lightnin Bug | Date: 2005-04-24 23:50:37 UTC
After Saturday's monsoon and postponement of Central PA
hairileggedness, Tom (Kriby Rider) and I set forth to Raindear
county (aka Montco, PA). Found in good shape were:

Midnight Moon - on the Perky Trail
Ski Bee - Very cool stamp by LetterboxingBee atop a double diamond
climb
Perkiomen Watershed - Douggerware rocks and laughs at any flooding
that may soggify other boxes! This box is in a flood plain and had
definate flooding sediment all about, but nothing but air and box
stuff inside!
Milky Way - A fine candy bar in Collegeville

By that time, Tom needed to get home and do some homework. So we
made the obligatory stop at Dairy Queen in Pennsburg as we cruised
back up highway 29, over the northern mountain to where our Southern
PA letterboxing cousins fear tread (except for a few hardy souls
from Perkasie, Sellersville, Harleysville and Cornwall Heights).

2 New HH's in the H³ atop South Mountain in Emmaus....

LB
