Power-Boxing Vacation

From: marybeth_mc | Date: 2005-04-25 03:33:41 UTC
Celtic Cat returned to PA on 4-24-05 after a Power-Boxing vacation
with friend (The Gamecock). The trip began on 4-16-05 in Atlanta, GA
and continued to Key West, FL. With this trip I am happy to say I
was able to reach, and well exceed, my goal of F-100.


The following is a list of LB's found, and not found.


Found:
4-16-05: 1) Lost World, Atlanta, GA
2) Land of the Lost, Atlanta, GA
3) Hidden Agenda, Atlanta, GA- where we had the
opportunity to meet Mookey and Dipper of Newnan, GA (formerly of NJ)
and get their exchanges
4) Easter Egg, Atlanta, GA- where we had the fortune
of running into Mookey and Dipper again , who helped turn us around
from the incorrect path we where and to find the correct path.


4-17-05: 1 & 2) OndBay AmpSway (found both LB's), Macon, GA
3) Goffee Culture, Gainesville, FL (where we found
the Fighting Owls Hitchhiker, created by Leafcutters and Lotus)
4) Jaguar, rest stop on I-75S in FL
5) The Hogtown Creek Letterbox, Gainesville, FL
(where Catchin' The Big One Hitchhiker was found)
6) My Old Stomping Ground Series, Box #3 only,
Gainesville, FL


4-18-05: 1) See Ya Later Gator, Thonotosassa, FL
2) Light in the Dark (Mystery Box), FL


4-19-05 1) Lover's Key, Ft. Myers Beach, FL
2) Land Shark, Ft. Myers Beach, FL
We also orientated The Pilot (of Chalfont, PA) to
letterboxing and I received his exchange


4-20-05: 1) Rainbow Bridge from Rhode Island to Marco Island, Marco
Island, FL
2) Boardwalk Bee-Bop, Evergaldes City, FL
3) Chokoloskee Big Fish, Evergaldes City, FL
4) Watch Out for Gators!, Ochopee, FL
5) Songbird Key, Key Largo, FL
6) Mermaid's Lagoon, Long Key, FL
7) Conch Republic Series #1, Key Largo, FL
8) Conch Republic Series #3, Key Largo, FL
9) Conch Republic Series #4, Long Key, FL
10) Big Pine Key, Big Pine Key, FL
11) Flea Market, Big Pine Key, FL


4-21-05: 1) Conch City, Key West, FL
2) Conch Republic Series #2, Key West, FL
3) Harry Truman-Little White House, Key West, FL
4) Key West For My Test, Key West, FL


4-23-05: 1) Hemingway, Key West, FL
2) Key West Letterbox, Key West, FL


Attempted/ Not Found:
4-17-05: 1) Flutterby 475, Bibb, GA
2) My Old Stomping Grounds box #2, Gainesville, FL


4-18-05: 1) Bitter Orange Cracker Shack, Hillsborough, FL
2) Grumpy Lazy Old Farts boxes #1 and #2 (Mystery
boxes)


4-19-05: 1) Dolphin's Smile-although we found boxes #1 and #2 these
boxes contained logs only, no stamp except in box #3, and we were
unsuccessful in finding box number 3.

4-20-05: 1) Key Largo Mangrove Forest, Key Largo, FL

4-24-05: 1) Paradise Cove, Big Pine Key



I also placed two new Hitchhikers during the trip.

Even with all of our Power-Boxing we did find time for some typical
tourist activities-such as shopping, snorkeling and the like.
However, with the boxing we were able to experience some atypical
activities. We were able to come up close and personal with a
curious Key deer who was attracted by our flashlight and head lamp
which we used for boxing at night- the deer stood less then two feet
away watching over my shoulder- we were very pleased she decided not
to charge at us! We also had to climb over a fence, as an escape,
after being locked in a park in Gainesville. And, with the boxing
had the opportunity to see some gators we would have otherwise missed
along our travels.
