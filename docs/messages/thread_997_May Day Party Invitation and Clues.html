May Day Party Invitation and Clues

From: highlysung | Date: 2005-04-29 13:00:55 UTC
Hi There LBMA'ers!
Did any of you see SpaceTraveler's post to the main list on Monday
about a May Day Party this coming Sunday? Well, I emailed Judy and
here is her response. She asked me to send along the invitation to
this list and included the first set of clues to get there. Here's
what she wrote . . .

"It's not so much of a letterbox gathering but an afternoon party
with Maypole, bonfire and a letterbox to find at my house near the
Lehigh Valley. I'll send clues out later this week. Party starts at
3PM and will go until probably about 8 PM. Bring munchies/drinkables
and plan to hang out with us.

Please pass my invitation on to the Mid Atlantic boxers.

Here is the first set of clues:

Find the Knechts Bridge and head West 2 miles.
Look for signs of May merry making on the right.
Query the dog

Judi"

Below is the original post on the main list . . .

From: "Judi Space" <Judi@...>
For any boxers out and about in Eastern PA, be advised that there is
a May Day Party with Event Stamp and letterbox on Sunday (May 1)
after 3 PM. Boxers are invited to hunt the box and join us for
burgers, dogs, bonfire, maypole etc. Please email me off list for
more info. SpaceTraveler
